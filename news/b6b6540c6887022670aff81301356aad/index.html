第九届进博会美国农业与食品行业专场交流会 9th CIIE U.S. Agriculture & Food Industry Seminar

上海美国商会 2026-03-16


Thursday, Mar 26

9:45 AM - 11:30 AM (GMT+8)

The Conference Room

黄浦区湖滨路168号无限极大厦27楼

27F, Infinitus Tower, No.168 Hubin Road, Huangpu District.




Swipe for English >>>


作为全球企业共享中国市场机遇的重要平台，中国国际进口博览会（CIIE）已连续多年为国际农业与食品行业提供展示与合作的窗口。为帮助美国农食企业更好地了解2026年第九届进博会的最新动态，上海美国商会将继续携手中国国际进口博览局共同举办本次专场交流会。


本次活动将重点介绍第九届进博会农食展区的筹备进展、参展政策及创新亮点，并邀请往届参展企业分享实战经验。与会者将有机会与进博局相关负责人、行业代表面对面交流，获取一手信息，拓展合作资源。


我们诚邀美国农业与食品行业的州政府、商协会和企业代表报名参会，共同探讨行业趋势与参展机遇。


As a key platform for global companies to access the Chinese market, the China International Import Expo (CIIE) has long connected international agriculture and food businesses with local opportunities. To help U.S. agribusinesses stay informed on the latest developments for the 9th CIIE in 2026, the American Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai and the CIIE Bureau are co-hosting this exclusive seminar.

The session will cover key updates on the Food and Agricultural Products section, including exhibition policies, new highlights, and practical insights from past exhibitors. Participants will have the chance to network directly with CIIE officials and industry peers, gain firsthand information, and explore collaboration opportunities.


We welcome all representatives from U.S. state governments, trade associations, and agriculture and food companies to join us!
