GSA丨2024年德国汉诺威工业博览会(HANNOVER MESSE)圆满落幕

贵州航天电器股份有限公司 2024-04-29


展会介绍
Exhibition

2024年德国汉诺威工业博览会（HANNOVER MESSE）于当地时间4月22日-26日在汉诺威展览中心成功举办。航天电器副总经理黄浩、刘兴中带队参加本届展会，与来自全球20多个国家和地区的专家与客户齐聚此地，共同探寻工业技术的最新动态与发展趋势，共襄盛举。

2024 汉诺威工业博览会以“为工业可持续发展注入活力”为主题，作为行业领先的互连互通、驱动控制解决方案提供商，航天电器携新能源、石油、通信等领域光、电互连和微特电机驱动与控制解决方案精彩亮相本届展会，在这个充满活力的展会上，航天电器展示了行业卓越的创新技术和系统级技术与产品解决方案。

HANNOVER MESSE 2024 was successfully held at Hannover Messe from April 22nd to 26th, local time. GSA Deputy General Managers, Mr. Huang Hao and Mr. Liu Xingzhong, led a team to participate in this exhibition, and gathered with experts and customers from more than 20 countries and regions around the globe, to explore the latest developments and trends of industrial technology, and to share the great success.

The theme of Hannover Messe 2024 is “Energizing Sustainable Industrial Development”. As a leading interconnect, drive and control solutions provider, GSA brings its optical and electrical interconnect and micro motor drive and control solutions for new energy, petroleum, and telecommunication fields to the exhibition, showcasing the industry's outstanding innovations and system-level technologies and product solutions at this dynamic event.

火热现场
The scene of the fire

展台前，航天电器的互联解决方案，凭借其卓越的创新性和实用性，成功吸引了众多客户的驻足交流。同时，航天电器技术团队也积极与客户们展开了深入的交流，不仅充分展示了自身在连接器、微特电机、光电、智能制造产业的专业实力，更赢得了客户们的高度认可与一致好评。

In front of the booth, GSA's interconnection solutions, with their excellent innovation and practicality, successfully attracted many customers to stop and exchange ideas. At the same time, GSA technical team also actively engaged in in-depth exchanges with customers, not only fully demonstrated its professional strength in the connector, micro-motor optoelectronics, intelligent manufacturing industry, but also won the high recognition and unanimous praise of customers.





本次展会航天电器先后对接了来自德国、意大利、法国等10余个国家上百名客户的来访交流，团队以专业、严谨、认真的态度呈现了航天电器在国际化业务中的能力与水平。顺应时代的趋势，与趋势同行，伴强者共赢，在国际化的道路上，航天电器愿与全球各地合作伙伴携手同行，共赢未来。

In this exhibition, GSA has docked hundreds of customers from more than 10 countries such as Germany, Italy and France to visit and communicate with each other, and the team has presented GSA's ability and level in internationalized business with a professional, rigorous and serious attitude. Conform to the trend of the times, walk with the trend, and win together with the strong, on the road of internationalization, GSA will walk hand in hand with partners all over the world and win together in the future.
