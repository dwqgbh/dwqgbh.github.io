热烈庆祝易特科在第十六届高新技术成果交易会获两项大奖

Anycheck安测 2014-11-21

安测健康服务：anycheck001

安测健康为慢性病、亚健康患者提供一站式健康管理服务，并为疑难杂症患者提供美国医学影像第二医疗意见服务。

2014年11月21日，在刚刚闭幕的2014年第十六届中国国际高新技术成果交易会上，易特科集团在本次高交会重点展出的安测O2O慢病干预系统和AnyCheck智能交互及心理慰藉机器人服务系统，荣获优秀产品大奖，感谢各位领导、首席科学家姚建铨院士、邹和群教授、李书芳教授、戴勇教授、Harry Holthofer教授以及所有客户和整个易特科团队。


本届高交会易特科集团展出的AnyCheck安测O2O国际慢性病干预服务，即集健康体检、疾病预警、养生指导、慢病干预为一体的现代化慢病干预，以线上、线下相结合的健康管理服务新模式，为慢性病人群、亚健康人群、院后需调理人群及延缓衰老人群提供专业、高端服务。同时，易特科集团还将以“军民融合、创新驱动”为主题，展出军民融合版的安测健康管理系统、AnyCheck虚拟养老院系统、LifeTelling交易平台、智能交互系统、糖尿病筛查预警与干预系统、社区医院管理系统、全科医生培训与认证系统、全科医生辅助诊疗系统、社康分诊与转诊系统、网络医院公共服务平台。


本次高交会易特科展台共计接待新老客户超过500个，接待现场观众超过6万人，包括深圳市市委常委福田区委书记张文同志、中信集团领导等。同时包括深圳电视台、新华社、深圳新闻网、经济日报等国内主流媒体12家。

November 21st, 2014, in the 16th 2014 China International Hi-tech Fair, E-Techco Group mainly presented O2O chronic disease intervention system and AnyCheck intelligent robot interaction and psychological service system in the Shenzhen exhibition which both won the Outstanding Product Awards. Thanks for every leader, chief scientist academician Yao Jian-quan, professor Zou Hequn, professor Li Shufang, professor Dai Yong, professor Harry Holthofer and the whole E-Techco Group team.
In this High-tech Fair, E-Techco Group presented AnyCheck O2O international chronic diseases intervention service, the online and offline new health management service model which consist of health body check, diseases alert, health maintenance guidance, chronic diseases intervention. The new service model is aimed at providing professional high-end service to people with chronic diseases, sub-health people, outpatients who need recuperation, elderly people who want to defer senility. At the same time, E-Techco Group displayed the military and civilian integration version health management system on the theme of “innovation promotes military and civilian integration”, AnyCheck virtual nursing homes, Life Telling exchange platform, intelligent interaction system, diabetes screening, alert and intervention system, community hospital management system, GP training and certification system, GP auxiliary diagnosis system, community hospital triage and referral auxiliary system, network hospital public service platform which got high praise and recognitions from leaders at all levels, the masses of users and medias.
In this year’s Shenzhen High-tech Fair, E-Techco Group’s booth has received more than 500 new and old customers, received more than 60000 people scene audiences , including the Shenzhen municipal committee comrade, Futian district party committee secretary Mr Zhang Wen, Citic Group leaders and etc. At the same time, including about 12 domestic mainstream medias, such as Shenzhen Media Group, Xinhua News, Shenzhen News, Economic Daily and etc.
