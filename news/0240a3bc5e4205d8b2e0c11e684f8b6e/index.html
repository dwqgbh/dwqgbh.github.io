重磅来袭 !2026智能制造、交通与物流自动化国际会议(iCaMaL2026)

浙江省自动化学会 2026-01-21

2026智能制造、交通与物流自动化国际会议(iCaMaL2026)将于2026年8月12日至14日，在中国杭州盛大召开。本次会议由浙江工商大学与浙江省自动化学会联合主办，聚焦前沿学术热点，搭建产学研交流平台，诚邀全球相关领域专家学者、企业代表及科研人员共襄盛会!会议将包含大会报告、专题小组讨论、研讨会、互动环节及特邀/专题会议等环节。

会议常规论文（Regular Paper）提交截止日期为2026年3月31日，专题分会论文（Special Paper）提交截止日期为2026年4月15日。







Conference History & Achievements

iCaMaL has been successfully convened multiple times, establishing itself as a vibrant platform for academic exchange in the fields of automation, manufacturing, transportation, and logistics. Some of the earlier editions were organized primarily as high-level academic events without paper submissions, focusing on in-depth discussions and knowledge sharing among experts from academia and industry.

The subsequent conferences held in Wuhan and Hong Kong introduced a paper track and achieved impressive academic outcomes. All accepted papers from these two editions have been indexed by EI Compendex, demonstrating the strong technical quality recognized by the research community. Moreover, several outstanding contributions were recommended for publication in reputable special issues, further enhancing the conference’s international visibility and scholarly influence.

Call for Relevant Content


1.Call for Special Sessions/Workshop

Interested attendees are encouraged to propose special sessions / workshops, each of which consists of five papers providing a focused discussion of new or innovative topics.   Each proposal must include the session/workshop title, description, organizers and affiliations. Proposals should be sent to icamal.2026@gmail.com.

2.Important Notes

Special session papers will undergo the same intense review process and submission is not a guarantee for acceptance. Special session papers must be clearly indicated when submitted with a copy sent to the designated special session organizer.

3. Paper Submission &Publication

  • Complete manuscripts must be electronically submitted through the conference submission website:   https://www.icamal2026.com/index/user/login.html.

  • Submitted manuscripts should be within six (6) pages, including figures, tables, and references. All submissions MUST be in PDF format. 

  • Accepted papers will be included into the conference proceedings according to the presentation and the authors’ choice, which will be published to the third party publisher and would be indexed by EI (Compendex), etc. 

  • Authors of selected qualified papers will be invited to submit revised and expanded version of their papers to special issues of international journals (SCI), such as IEEE T-ITS, T-ASE, FSMJ. Currently, the special issue of IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering (T-ASE) has been approved.
