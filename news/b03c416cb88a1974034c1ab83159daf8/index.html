2025年斯里兰卡工业博览会

斯里兰卡驻上海总领事馆 2025-04-09

With the success of the Industry Expo 2024, Sri Lanka is scheduling to hold the Industry Expo 2025 from 19th to 22nd June 2025 at the BMICH in Colombo. The Expo will be organized by the Ministry of Industry & Entrepreneurship Development, in collaboration with the Industrial Development Board (IDB).

2025年斯里兰卡工业博览会将于6月19日至22日在首都科伦坡BMICH会展中心举办 。博览会将由斯里兰卡工业和企业发展部与工业发展局（IDB）联合组织。


The primary objective of this Expo is to create a platform to bring together local and international stakeholders from diverse industry sectors, showcasing a wide range of products and services, as well as their innovation, technology and sustainable practices.

此次博览会的主要目的是创建平台，汇聚当地和国际各行业企业，展示多样产品和服务，及其创新、科技与可持续发展实践。


Welcome to participate in this Expo. For more information, please visit the official website www.expo.idb.gov.lk. 

欢迎感兴趣的企业和个人报名参加，登录官方网站www.expo.idb.gov.lk，获取更多信息。
