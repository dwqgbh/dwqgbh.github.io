展会介绍 Exhibition introduction

   

埃及位于非洲大陆东北部，北隔地中海与欧洲相望、西南部直通非洲大陆腹地，苏伊士运河又是连接地中海和红海、沟通大西洋和印度洋的重要通道。埃及有与欧、亚、非各国相连的海运、空运及同非洲各国相连的陆路交通网，交通便利。埃及人口约为8250万，辐射中东、北非等周边人口总量达到近6亿。区位条件优越，辐射功能较强。

为了迎合非洲和中东地区纺织市场的巨大发展潜力，第十九届埃及国际纺织服装机械及配件展览会将聚集全球生产商和当地代理商以及埃及当地和周边国家的参观商，届时将展示整个纺织品和服装价值链，提供最新的技术和发展趋势。展会旨在通过展示纺织和服装领域的工业技术、创新和发展，为参展商和参观者提供一个全面的贸易和技术平台。


  Egypt is located in the northeast of the African continent, facing Europe across the Mediterranean Sea in the north and directly connecting the hinterland of the African continent in the southwest. The Suez Canal is an important channel connecting the Mediterranean Sea and the Red Sea, and connecting the Atlantic and Indian Oceans. Egypt has a convenient transportation network connected with Europe, Asia and Africa by sea and air, and with Africa by land. Egypt's population is about 82.5 million, radiating the Middle East, North Africa and other surrounding population of nearly 600 million. Superior location conditions, strong radiation function.

In order to cater to the huge development potential of the textile market in Africa and the Middle East, the 19th Egypt International Textile and Apparel Machinery and Accessories exhibition will gather global manufacturers and local agents as well as local Egyptian and surrounding countries visitors, will showcase the entire textile and apparel value chain, provide the latest technology and development trends. The fair aims to provide exhibitors and visitors with a comprehensive trade and technology platform by showcasing industrial technologies, innovations and developments in the textile and apparel sector.

企业简介

大连华阳新材料科技股份有限公司（简称华阳新材）成立于2011年，专业从事聚酯等高分子聚合物纺粘法非织造材料的新工艺、新产品以及成套技术与装备的研发与产业化推广，以“交钥匙工程”方式提供定制化的整体工程解决方案，为客户提供技术咨询、产品及装备设计、工厂设计、成套技术装备制造和集成、安装调试及后续技术支持等总体化、系统化工程服务，以及双组份粗旦长丝非织造材料的开发、生产与销售。该新型材料可应用于高端防水卷材基布、土工增强和土工排水板膜材料、气体液体过滤材料和复合过滤材料、簇绒地毯基布、运动鞋补强材料等领域。

Dalian Huayang new material technology co., LTD. (hereinafter referred to as Huayang new material), founded in 2011, the specialty is engaged in the method of polyester and other polymer spinning spunbond nonwoven materials of new technology, new products and complete sets of technology and equipment research and development and industrialization promotion, to provide customized "Turnkey-project" way of the overall engineering solution, To provide customers with technical consulting, product and equipment design, factory design, complete set of technical equipment manufacturing and integration, installation and commissioning and follow-up technical support and other overall, systematic engineering services, as well as the development, production and sales of Bi-component coarse denier filament nonwoven materials. The new material can be applied to high-end waterproof roll base cloth, geo-reinforced and geo-drainage plate membrane material, gas liquid filter material and composite filter material, tufted carpet base cloth, sports shoe reinforcing material and other fields.
