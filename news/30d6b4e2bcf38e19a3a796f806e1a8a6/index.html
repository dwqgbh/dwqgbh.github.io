斯闻画廊Suen Gallery荣幸参加ART南京当代艺术博览会，将于展位呈现陈皎、陈红波、戈子馀、金罡、李晓奇、卢琳、毛艳阳、施卫及许发平9位艺术家的系列作品。


此次展览汇聚了多位艺术家的创作实践，呈现出丰富多样的形式与视角。作品既有对传统文化元素的当代转译，也有对材料语言与空间关系的深入探索，同时不乏以幽默与幻想营造出的独特叙事。通过这些多元的表达方式，传递他们对现实世界的观察与思考。


Suen Gallery is honored to participate in the ART Nanjing Contemporary Art Fair and will present a series of works by nine artists: Chen Jiao, Chen Hongbo, Ge Ziyu, Jin Gang, Li Xiaoqi, Lu Lin, Mao Yanyang, Shi Wei, and Xu Faping.


This exhibition brings together the creative practices of numerous artists, presenting a rich variety of forms and perspectives. The works feature contemporary interpretations of traditional cultural elements, in-depth explorations of the relationship between material language and space, and unique narratives crafted through humor and fantasy. Through these diverse expressions, they convey their observations and reflections on the real world.
