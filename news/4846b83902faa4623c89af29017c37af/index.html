2026 WBE Exhibitor Preview | Hengwei Electronics at Booth E117: Unlock Full-Scenario Measurement & Control Solutions for Boilers

世界锅炉展 2026-01-07

点击蓝字 关注我们

作为全球新能源热能与锅炉行业的顶级盛会，大会以 “技术赋能・零碳供热” 为核心主题，汇聚全球1000+领军品牌与5万专业精英，通过9大主题展联动构建全产业链平台，精准对接前沿技术、创新产品与市场需求，为产业协同创新、资源整合及全球化合作提供核心支撑，助力构建高水平产业生态，推动行业向零碳目标高质量升级。


Sept 16-18, 2026 | Canton Fair Complex! 

With "Empowering the Low-Carbon and Intelligent Upgrade of the Boiler Industry" as its core theme, the 2026 World New Energy Themal Equipement &Boiler Expo（WBE） focuses on the key needs of industry transformation. It builds a core platform for global practitioners to conduct technical exchanges and business matching, helping them seize the opportunities of industry development.


The expo will gather over 500 top-tier enterprises from more than 20 countries worldwide, covering core exhibits across the entire boiler industry chain. 


More details including nearly 20 technical forums and new product launch events will be held simultaneously, focusing on cutting-edge technology trends and accurately connecting high-quality resources.


Company Pofile

2026 WBE


Chengde Hengwei Electronic Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd.


Chengde Hengwei Electronic is an enterprise with years of experience in the fields of flow measurement and industrial automation. 


Adhering to the core mission of "Precise Measurement & Control, Empowering Industrial Development", Hengwei Electronics has built a solid customer base and earned an excellent market reputation in multiple industries including petrochemicals, electric power, metallurgy, and new energy, thanks to its robust R&D capabilities, stringent product quality control, and comprehensive after-sales service system.


Booth：E117

Date：Sept. 16-18, 2026

Venue：Canton Fair Complex, Guangzhou

Show Plan

2026 WBE

On the site of the WBE , Hengwei Electronics will grandly present its core products and customized solutions covering the full-process measurement and control needs of boilers at Booth E117, fully demonstrating the enterprise's "hard power" to address the personalized needs of industrial scenarios.


Hengwei Electronics will also showcase its customized measurement and control solutions. Tailored to customers’ specific production processes and equipment configurations, these solutions offer a full-chain service covering product selection, solution design, installation and commissioning, as well as post-maintenance, to meet customers’ personalized needs to the maximum extent.


Invitation Letter

2026 WBE

Currently, the intelligent and low-carbon transformation of the boiler industry has entered a critical stage. With a series of core products, Hengwei Electronics hopes to take advantage of this exhibition platform to conduct in-depth exchanges with global counterparts and industry partners on industry pain points, technological trends and cooperation opportunities.
