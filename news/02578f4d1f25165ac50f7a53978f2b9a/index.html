中国国际工业博览会（简称工博会），始创于 1999 年，每年秋季在上海举办，是国内历史最悠久的国家级工业盛会。


展会聚焦新工业、新经济，汇聚行业精英与创新资源，构建开放包容的产业生态，贯通智能绿色制造全产业链，涵盖基础材料、核心零部件、高端装备及系统解决方案，为国内规模、阵容、覆盖面及国际化水准领先的工业大展。


工博会集展示、交易、评奖、论坛于一体，深耕专业化、市场化、国际化、品牌化发展，紧扣国家战略、赋能实体经济，已成为中国工业面向全球的展示窗口与经贸合作平台，形成西有汉诺威、东有中国工博会的行业格局，是享誉全球、国内极具影响力的工业品牌展。



China International Industry Fair (CIIF), founded in 1999 and held annually in Shanghai in autumn, is China’s longest-running national-level industrial exhibition.


Focusing on new industries and the new economy, CIIF gathers industry elites and innovative resources to build an open and inclusive industrial ecosystem. It covers the entire industrial chain of intelligent and green manufacturing, including basic materials, core components, high-end equipment and overall solutions, ranking top in China in scale, exhibitor scale, industrial coverage and internationalization.


Integrating exhibition, trade, award selection and forums, CIIF adheres to professional, market-oriented, international and branded development. Aligned with national strategies and empowering the real economy, it serves as a key global showcase and trade cooperation platform for China’s industry. It has established the renowned pattern of “Hannover Messe in the West, CIIF in the East” and become a world-famous and highly influential industrial brand event in China.
