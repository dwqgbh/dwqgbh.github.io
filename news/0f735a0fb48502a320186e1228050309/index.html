2025第二十一届上海国际汽车工业展览会:再登高展位人气火爆,诚邀您莅临!

再登高科技 2025-04-28


导语
2025第二十一届上海国际汽车工业展览会盛大启幕，作为汽车行业的标志性盛会，吸引了来自全球的专业观众和参展商。天津再登高科技股份有限公司（GetonAgain）携一系列创新成果惊艳亮相，展位号2.2H馆2BG001，为行业呈现了一场视觉与技术的盛宴。


The 2025 The 21st Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition opened. As a landmark event in the automotive industry, it has attracted professional visitors and exhibitors from around the world. Tianjin GetonAgain Technology Co., Ltd. (GetonAgain) made a stunning appearance with a series of innovative achievements at Booth Hall 2.2H,2BG001, presenting a feast of vision and technology for the industry.


再登高展位现场
GetonAgain Booth Live

4月25日，展会首日，再登高的展位迎来了大量来自不同地区的专业客户。本届展会，再登高展示的多款设备，包括全自动高速垫板式冲孔机、全自动任意转单针皮革缝纫机、平板扫描数字化仪、裁片视觉检测仪。凭借设备高效、精准的性能表现成功吸引了众多专业观众驻足观看和咨询。客户们纷纷与工作人员进行深入交流，详细了解设备的各项参数和实际应用场景。

On April 25th, the first day of the exhibition, GetonAgain's booth welcomeda large number of professional clients from various regions. At this year'sevent, GetonAgain showcased a range of equipment, including Automatic High-Speed Leather Perforating Machine，Automatic Arbitrary Rotary Single Needle Leather Sewing Machine，Flatbed Scanning Digitizer，Cutting Piece Vision Inspection Equipment . The high efficiency and precision of these devices successfully attracted numerous professional visitors, who stopped to observe and inquire.Clients engaged in in-depth discussions with the staff, thoroughly exploringthe equipment's specifications and practical application scenarios.

展会现场气氛热烈，销售人员和工程师们以饱满的热情和专业的态度，为每一位到访者提供了优质的服务，确保他们能够充分了解再登高的产品和技术优势。从设备的操作演示到技术细节的探讨，从合作方案的初步洽谈到达成共识的握手，每一个瞬间都记录着再登高在此次展会上的成长与收获。许多客户对再登高的设备给予了高度评价，并表达了进一步合作的意向。

The exhibition site was buzzing with excitement as sales representatives and engineers, with full enthusiasm and professionalism, provided top-tier service to every visitor ensuring they gained a thorough understanding of GetonAgain’s product and technological advantages. From live equipment demonstrations to in-depth technical discussions, from initial partnership talks to handshakes sealing agreements, every moment captured GetonAgain’s growth and achievements at this exhibition. Numerous clients spoke highly of the company’s equipment and expressed strong interest in further collaboration.

再登高期待在接下来的展会日子里，继续与更多新老客户深入交流，共同探索汽车生产领域的创新解决方案。我们将持续关注行业动态，紧跟技术发展趋势，不断推出更优质、更高效的产品和服务，为家具行业的繁荣发展贡献更多力量。

GetonAgain looks forward to engaging in deeper exchanges with both new and existing clients in the coming days of the exhibition, jointly exploring innovative solutions for the Automotive Production Field. We will continue to stay attuned to industry trends, keep pace with technological advancements, and consistently introduce higher-quality, more efficient products and services. Our commitment remains strong in contributing to the thriving development of the furniture industry.

展品一览
Exhibition Overview
 1

全自动高速垫板式冲孔机（Per）

Automatic High-speed Plate Mount Perforation Machine
各种类型及不同厚度的真皮、超纤皮革的冲孔加工。适用于汽车座椅、航空座椅、家具皮饰、皮革箱包、鞋子、服装等行业。

Holes punch processing for various types of genuine leather, microfiber leather with different thickness, suitable for industries such as car seat, aviation seats, leather furniture and upholstery, leather bag & shoes, clothing industries, etc.



 2

全自动任意转单针皮革缝纫机

Automatic Arbitrary Rotary Single Needle Leather Sewing Machine

适用于汽车内饰、汽车座套、汽车门饰板、飞机座椅、皮革家具内饰等领域的花样自动化缝制。

Suitable for automated leather pattern sewing in the fields of automotive interiors, car seat covers, car door panels, airplane seats, and leather furniture upholstery.



 3

平板扫描数字化仪

Flatbed Scanning Digitizer

适用于服装、家具、汽车、家纺、箱包、制鞋、玩具、医疗等行业，对各类纸样和实物版型的数字化录入。

Applicable for clothing, furniture, automotive, hometextile, bags & suitcases, shoe-making, toys, medical industries, etc. all kinds of paper pattern and physical product's panel shape digital input.



 4
裁片视觉检测仪

Cutting Piece Vision Inspection Equipment 

主要适用于汽车内饰皮革、复合皮革等裁片的扫描检测。

Mainly suitable for the scanning and inspection of cut pieces such as automotive interior leather and composite leather.
