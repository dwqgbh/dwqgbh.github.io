智造赋能,产业跃迁——2026济宁纺织服装产业发展峰会暨中捷智能制造应用交流会圆满落幕

中捷缝纫机官方 2026-01-30


1月30日，由中捷科技联合济宁市相关行业机构共同举办的“2026济宁纺织服装产业发展峰会暨中捷智能制造应用交流会”在济宁市汶上县国际会议中心成功举行。

On January 30, the "2026 Jining Textile and Apparel Industry Development Summit and ZOJE Intelligent Manufacturing Application Exchange Conference," jointly organized by ZOJE Technology and related industry institutions in Jining City, was successfully held at the Wenshang County International Conference Center in Jining City.


 



本次峰会汇聚了中国服装协会专职副会长杜岩冰，山东省纺织服装行业协会副会长王淼，东华大学教授、挂职新疆大学纺织服装学院副院长杜劲松，中捷科技副总经理兼营销中心总经理郑学国等行业协会领导、地方政府代表、学界专家、产业链伙伴及数百家服装企业负责人，共同探讨在数字经济时代下，纺织服装产业的智能转型与精益未来。

The summit brought together leaders from industry associations, representatives of local governments, academic experts, partners across the industrial chain, and heads of hundreds of garment enterprises, including Du Yanbing, Full-time Vice President of the China National Garment Association; Wang Miao, Vice President of the Shandong Textile and Apparel Association; Du Jinsong, Professor at Donghua University and concurrently Vice Dean of the School of Textile and Clothing at Xinjiang University; and Zheng Xueguo, Deputy General Manager of ZOJE Technology and General Manager of the Marketing Center. Together, they explored the intelligent transformation and lean future of the textile and apparel industry in the era of the digital economy.






济宁市工业行业发展服务中心党委书记、主任牛伟东为峰会致辞，肯定了济宁纺织服装产业的发展成效，直面产业结构性挑战，明确了高端化、智能化、品牌化的发展规划。

Niu Weidong, Party Committee Secretary and Director of the Jining Industrial Industry Development Service Center, delivered an opening address at the summit. He acknowledged the development achievements of Jining's textile and garment industry, directly addressed its structural challenges, and outlined a clear development plan focused on moving the industry toward higher-end, intelligent, and branded development.



山东省纺织服装行业协会副会长王淼指出，纺织服装业是山东支柱产业、济宁传统优势产业，并表示协会将发挥桥梁纽带作用，通过搭建交流平台、对接政策资源、赋能品牌出海，助力全省纺织服装产业高质量发展。

Wang Miao, Vice President of the Shandong Textile and Apparel Industry Association, stated that the textile and apparel industry is a pillar industry of Shandong and a traditional competitive industries of Jining. He added that the association will leverage its role as a bridge and liaison to help the high-quality development of the province's textile and apparel industry by building communication platforms, connecting policy resources, and empowering brands to expand overseas.


中国服装协会专职副会长杜岩冰肯定了济宁纺织服装产业的深厚积淀与各项成效，并强调“科技、时尚、绿色” 是行业高质量发展核心方向，智能制造是关键路径。


他同时高度认可中捷作为行业领军企业的创新担当与技术实力，提及中捷连续五年赞助 “中国服装品牌大奖”、发布 “线迹大师” 平缝机等成果。


他期待以本次峰会为纽带，深化产业协同与供需对接，为纺织服装产业智能化升级注入新动能。

Du Yanbing, Full-time Vice President of the China National Garment Association, affirmed the profound积淀 and various achievements of Jining's textile and garment industry. He emphasized that "technology, fashion, and green development" constitute the core direction for the industry's high-quality development, with intelligent manufacturing being the key pathway.

He also highly commended ZOJE, as an industry leader, for its innovative commitment and technological strength, specifically mentioning accomplishments such as ZOJE's five consecutive years of sponsoring the "China Apparel Brand Awards" and the launch of products like the "StitchMaster" lockstitch machine.

He expressed the hope that this summit would serve as a vital link to deepen industrial coordination and supply-demand dock, injecting new momentum into the intelligent upgrade of the textile and garment industry.



中捷科技副总经理兼营销中心总经理郑学国带来《中捷智造：携手济宁服装，共赴精益未来》主题分享。


他指出，中捷扎根缝制设备领域32年，作为行业首家上市民营企业，始终与服装产业同频共振。


面对产业 “小单快反”“降本增效” 的转型需求，中捷已从产品供应商升级为整体解决方案服务商。


郑总表示，中捷愿与济宁企业及生态伙伴紧密协作，深入车间量身定制升级方案，共享前沿技术与管理实践，合力打造可落地、可见效的智慧工厂标杆，推动济宁纺织服装产业链向高端化迈进，共创精益智造新未来。

Zheng Xueguo, Deputy General Manager of ZOJE Technology and General Manager of the Marketing Center, delivered a keynote presentation titled "ZOJE Intelligent Manufacturing: Partnering with Jining's Apparel Industry for a Lean Future."

He pointed out that ZOJE has been deeply rooted in the sewing equipment sector for 32 years. As the first listed private enterprise in the industry, it has always progressed in sync with the apparel industry.

In response to the industry's transformation needs for "small-batch, quick-response" production and "cost reduction and efficiency enhancement," ZOJE has evolved from a product supplier into a comprehensive solutions service provider.

Mr. Zheng expressed that ZOJE is willing to collaborate closely with Jining's enterprises and ecosystem partners. This involves going deep into the workshop to develop customized upgrade plans, sharing cutting-edge technologies and management practices, and jointly establishing practical and effective smart factory benchmarks. The goal is to drive Jining's textile and apparel industry chain toward higher value-added development and co-create a new future of lean and intelligent manufacturing.




在主题演讲环节，多位行业专家聚焦企业核心痛点，带来针对性解决方案。

During the keynote presentation session, several industry experts focused on enterprises' core pain points, delivering targeted solutions.



东华大学教授、挂职新疆大学纺织服装学院副院长杜劲松围绕“少人与无人智能缝制工厂设计”，为企业在多变市场中实现快速响应，提供了专业的理论框架与可落地的实践路径。

Du Jinsong, Professor at Donghua University and concurrently Vice Dean of the School of Textile and Clothing at Xinjiang University addressed the topic of "Design of Labor-Light and Unmanned Intelligent Sewing Factories." He provided a professional theoretical framework and actionable implementation pathways for enterprises to achieve rapid responsiveness in a volatile market.



济宁晟徕缝纫设备有限公司总经理张涛分享《服装工厂节能减排、降本增效》，针对生产中的高耗能、高损耗问题，提出设备升级与管理优化双轮驱动的解决思路。

Zhang Tao, General Manager of Jining Shenglai Sewing Equipment Co., Ltd., shared insights on "Energy Conservation, Emission Reduction, Cost Reduction, and Efficiency Improvement in Garment Factories." In response to high energy consumption and high loss issues in production, he proposed a dual-driven solution combining equipment upgrades and management optimization. 



中捷科技国内营销总监张立桥，对旗下线迹大师智慧平缝机、全自动激光开袋机等核心爆品，展开了全方位的专业讲解。

Zhang Liqiao, Domestic Marketing Director of ZOJE Technology, provided a comprehensive and professional explanation of the company’s core standout products, including the Stitch Master smart lockstitch sewing machine and the fully automatic laser pocket welting machine.






峰会现场，战略合作签约仪式隆重举行。



中捷科技与济宁市服装纺织行业协会正式签署战略框架合作协议。

签约落地，标志着济宁纺织服装产业与智能制造领域的合作，将迈入更紧密、更深入的全新发展阶段。

ZOJE Technology and the Jining Textile and Apparel Industry Association officially signed a strategic framework cooperation agreement.

The signing of the agreement marks that the collaboration between Jining's textile and apparel industry and the field of intelligent manufacturing will enter a new stage of closer and deeper development.





会场外序厅的 “中捷爆品体验区”人气爆棚。中捷线迹大师、K6300B 全自动激光开袋机等多款明星设备一亮相，就牢牢抓住了所有人的目光。

从稳定性到智能化，嘉宾们亲手操作体验后，纷纷点赞叫好。


The "ZOJE  Explosive Products Experience Zone" in the outer lobby was a resounding success, drawing massive crowds. Star equipment such as the ZOJE StitchMaster and the K6300B fully automatic laser pocket-welting machine instantly captivated all attention the moment they were unveiled. From stability to intelligence, guests who personally operated and experienced the machines unanimously offered rave reviews and high praise.





当日上午，与会嘉宾一行走进山东如意毛纺集团，沉浸式探访 “智慧门店”，零距离触摸智能高定未来工厂的前沿模样。

That morning, the attending guests visited the Shandong Ruyi Woolen Group, immersing themselves in an exploration of the "Smart Store" and experiencing firsthand the cutting-edge model of an intelligent high-end custom future factory.


本次峰会既是行业思想盛宴，更是智能技术与传统产业深度融合的生动实践。

未来，中捷将秉持 “赋能服装产业，推动精益生产” 的使命，携手更多合作伙伴，以创新与服务助力济宁乃至全国纺织服装产业高质量发展，共织智能、高效、绿色的产业新未来！

This summit was not only a feast of industry ideas but also a vivid demonstration of the deep integration of intelligent technology with traditional industry. 

Moving forward, adhering to its mission of "Empowering the Garment Industry, Advancing Lean Production," ZOJE will join hands with more partners. Through innovation and service, it will assist in the high-quality development of the textile and garment industry in Jining and across the nation, working together to weave a new industrial future that is intelligent, efficient, and sustainable.
