关于2022华南国际工业博览会延期通知


尊敬的参展商、观众及合作伙伴：


鉴于目前深圳疫情防控形势严峻，为最大限度保障广大展商、观众与合作伙伴的身体健康和生命安全，华南国际工业博览会组委会经过慎重考虑后决定：


原定于2022年9月14日-16日在深圳国际会展中心（宝安新馆）举办的华南国际工业博览会（SCIIF 2022）将延期举办，举办地点不变，具体日期将另行通知。


因展会延期给您造成的诸多不便，我们深表歉意！组委会将密切关注疫情的发展，积极协调展会的各项工作，并第一时间与您分享最新消息。


华南国际工业博览会一直以来的发展离不开您的亲力参与和鼎力支持。在此，我们衷心感谢各方的理解与支持。在本轮疫情结束后，我们仍将为所有展商和观众带来一场华南地区高质量高水准的工业盛会。


特此通知。


汉诺威米兰展览（上海）有限公司

上海工业商务展览有限公司

2022年9月1日

Notice of 

Postponement of SCIIF 2022


Dear exhibitors, visitors and partners:


Considering the current epidemic prevention and control of Shenzhen Municipality is still severely ongoing, in order to guarantee the health and life safety of exhibitors, visitors and participants, after prudent consideration, the organizing committee of South China International Industry Fair regret to make the following announcement:

 

The South China International Industry Fair (SCIIF 2022), originally scheduled to be held on 14-16 September 2022 at Shenzhen World Exhibition and Convention Center (Bao’an New Hall) will be postponed until further notice. The show venue remains the same.

 

We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused by the postponement. The organizing committee will closely monitor the epidemic situation, actively coordinate with all parties and keep you well-informed of any further news as soon as possible.

 

The development of South China International Industry Fair has been inseparable from your personal participation and support. We would like to express our sincere gratitude to all parties for your understanding and support. As long as the COVID-19 situation gets better, we will still bring a high quality and high level industrial event in South China for all exhibitors and visitors.



Hannover Milano Fairs Shanghai Ltd.

Shanghai Industry & Commerce Exhibition Co., Ltd.

 1 September, 2022
