01

第 23 届亚太国际塑料橡胶

工业展览会·合肥站

The 23rd Asia Pacific International Plastics  and Rubber Industry Exhibition

亚太橡塑展不仅是中国领先的塑料橡胶工业展览会之一，而且还是亚洲乃至全球橡塑行业的一项重要盛事，被誉为“中国橡塑行业北方第一展”“亚太第二大橡塑工业展”，对整个亚太地区乃至全球橡塑行业都有广泛的辐射作用。

The Asia-Pacific Plastics and Rubber Exhibition is not only one of the leading plastic and rubber industry exhibitions in China, but also an important event in the rubber and plastic industry in Asia and even globally. It is known as "the first exhibition in the northern region of China for the rubber and plastic industry" and "the second largest rubber and plastic industry exhibition in the Asia-Pacific region". It has a wide-ranging influence on the rubber and plastic industry in the entire Asia-Pacific region and even globally. 

2026 年 3 月 12-15 日，【AP-RUBBERPLAS 亚太橡塑展】将再度在长三角的核心引擎城市合肥重磅举行。借助合肥及安徽、长三角的产业优势及市场辐射能力,为世界各地橡塑科技供应商提供一个展现创新技术、加强国际商贸合作的理想平台。

From March 12th to 15th, 2026, the 【AP-RUBBERPLAS Asia-Pacific Rubber & Plastics Exhibition】 will be grandly held in Hefei, a key engine city in the Yangtze River Delta region. Leveraging the industrial advantages and market influence of Hefei, Anhui Province, and the Yangtze River Delta region, this event will provide an ideal platform for rubber and plastic technology suppliers from all over the world to showcase their innovative technologies and strengthen international business cooperation.

02

2026 合肥国际工业自动化

暨机器人展览会

2026 Hefei International Industrial Automation and Robotics Exhibition

合肥国际工业自动化暨机器人展立足安徽产业基础，辐射长三角地区，集中展示智能制造及工业自动化领域的最新技术和解决方案。

The Hefei International Industrial Automation and Robotics Exhibition is based on the industrial foundation of Anhui Province, covering the entire Yangtze River Delta region, and showcases the latest technologies and solutions in the fields of intelligent manufacturing and industrial automation. 

展会将聚焦高端智能装备、自动化与机器人、工业互联网、人工智能与数字化工厂等技术，并为国内外制造商和采购商搭建业务洽谈和学术交流的平台。加快技术制造与产业模式转型，聚集产业“智’向发展，融合新技术及新产品赋能工业自动化产业发展新生态，助力产业链优化布局。

The exhibition will focus on technologies such as high-end intelligent equipment, automation and robotics, industrial internet, artificial intelligence and digital factories, and will provide a platform for business negotiations and academic exchanges for domestic and foreign manufacturers and purchasers. It aims to accelerate the transformation of technology manufacturing and industrial model, gather the "intelligence" of the industry for development, integrate new technologies and new products to empower the development of the industrial automation industry ecosystem, and help optimize the layout of the industrial chain.

03

JM2026 金诺·第3届合肥

国际机床展览会

Jinnoc·The 3rd Hefei International 

Machine Tool Exhibition

安徽作为中国中部崛起第一大省，坐拥万亿级装备制造规模和世界级汽车产业集群。2026 金诺·第 3 届合肥国际机床展览会，立足安徽万亿级制造产业风口，将于 3 月 12-15 日在合肥滨湖国际会展升级启幕。

Anhui, as the largest province in China's central region's rise, boasts a manufacturing scale of trillions and a world-class automotive industrial cluster. The 3rd Jinno International Machine Tool Exhibition in Hefei, scheduled for 2026, will take place from March 12th to 15th at the upgraded Hefei Binhu International Exhibition Center, leveraging the momentum of Anhui's trillion-dollar manufacturing industry. 

本届展会规划展出面积 100000 平方米，设置六大展区，金属切削/数控机床展区、激光科技/热冲压技术与设备/金属成形展区、工业自动化/机器人展区、橡塑机械与制品、数字制造/高端装备展区以及机床附件/模具/压铸/机床供应链展区，大会深度聚焦数字化转型背景下智能工厂建设、新能源汽车及零配件柔性生产、智能化切削加工、轨交/航空航天、家电定制化生产、3C 电子等 100+家行业细分领域，近千家展商全面展示机加工上下游产业先进制造技术和装备，提供智能化落地解决方案。

This exhibition is planned to cover an area of 100,000 square meters and will feature six exhibition areas: the Metal Cutting/ CNC Machine Tools Area, the Laser Technology/ Hot Stamping Technology and Equipment/ Metal Forming Area, the Industrial Automation/Robot Area, the Rubber and Plastic Machinery and Products Area, the Digital Manufacturing/High-end Equipment Area, and the Machine Tools Accessories /Molds /Injection Molding /Machine Tool Supply Chain Area. The conference focuses deeply on intelligent factory construction under the background of digital transformation, flexible production of new energy vehicles and their components, intelligent cutting processing, rail transit/aerospace, customized production of household appliances, 3C electronics, etc. 100+ industry sub-sectors are covered, and nearly 1,000 exhibitors comprehensively showcase advanced manufacturing technologies and equipment in the upstream and downstream of the machining industry, providing intelligent implementation solutions.
