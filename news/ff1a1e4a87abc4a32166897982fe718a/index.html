2022中国国际复合材料工业技术展览会延期举办的通知!!

每日E展 2022-11-25

关注每日E展公众号，最新展会信息先知道


去看展

     为您提供国内外最新的展会资讯，一机在手，轻松看展，致力于打通线上线下展会信息服务痛点，增强商户间更紧密的融合，占领展会平台经济新制高点。

尊敬的参展商、观众及各界同仁：

近几日南京国际博览中心所在地建邺区发现多个确诊病例及疫情风险点，南京全市高风险地区升至26处。11月21日晚，组委会接南京市防疫及会展主管部门紧急通知，要求延期举办相关展会。依据《南京市大型展览会议活动疫情防控工作指引》要求，目前已不具备常态化疫情防控条件下正常举办展览及会议的客观条件。
为巩固国家防疫成果，执行地方防疫政策，保障每一位参会者的健康和利益，经过审慎研判与多方沟通，组委会决定：原定于2022年11月30日-12月2日在南京国际博览中心举办的“2022中国国际复合材料工业技术展览会”延期举办，时间和地点待确定后另行通知。
自1995年创立以来，以推动复合材料行业的繁荣发展为使命，“中国国际复材展”与行业共同成长，为企业开拓市场、增进交流提供了巨大机会，受到行业广泛认可，成为亚太地区规模最大、影响力最广泛的复合材料专业技术展览会。
对于本届展会的延期，我们深表歉意！一直以来，特别是在展会无法正常举办的近两年，对于所有参展商、观众及合作伙伴的理解、支持与配合，对各参展企业克服重重困难全力支持本届复材展举办，我们表达衷心地感谢！我们将全力以赴推进展会后续恢复及筹备工作，为展商及观众提供更好的体验和服务。
冬日的蛰伏只为春天的绽放！山水有相逢，来日皆可期！

Important Notice on the Postponement of CCE2022

In recent days, more than a few confirmed COVID-19 cases and risky traces were spotted in Jianye District, where Nanjing International Expo Center is located, with the number of high-risky regions across the city of Nanjing increased to 26. On the night of November 21, the organizing committee of CCE2022 was urgently informed by local pandemic prevention and control authority as well as exhibition supervision body to postpone the exhibition event. Despite already being approved by the government, according to Nanjing’s Guideline of Pandemic Prevention and Control for Big-size convention and Exhibition, external conditions for the staging of convention and exhibition under regular pandemic prevention and control status is fading.

In order to safeguard the hard-won achievements across the country in the battle against the virus, implement local policies, and guarantee the health and benefits of every attendee, the organizing committee of CCE2022 has decided hereby that: China Composites Expo 2022, set to be staged from November 30 – December 2, 2022, will be postponed. New dates and location will be announced later.

Since founded in 1995, China Composites Expo, targeting the development and prosperity of composite industry as its mission, has been growing together with the composite industry, providing opportunities for composite enterprises to expand market and enhance communication, thus widely recognized and further becomes the biggest and most influential composite exhibition in Asia-pacific region.

We feel deeply sorry for the inconvenience arising from the postponement of this year’s edition. Meanwhile, we would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude for the understanding, support and cooperation given all the time by every exhibitor, visitor and partner, especially in the past two years when CCE can’t be normally held. Our gratitude also goes to every exhibitor who has overcome tremendous difficulty to participate in this year’s event. We will make all efforts out to restore the event and restart the preparation work, and ensure that improved experience and services can be enjoyed by exhibitors and visitors.

The silence in winter only awaits the blossom of spring. We will expect to see you again very soon!

The organizing committee of CCE
