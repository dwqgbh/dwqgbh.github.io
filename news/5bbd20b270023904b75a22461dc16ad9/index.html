第138届广交会已于2025年10月15日开幕

第138届中国进出口商品交易会（广交会）于2025年10月15日至11月4日分三期举办，每期展览主题及展区设置如下：

第一期（10月15日—19日）：先进制造

聚焦工业制造领域，设19个展区，包括：

1.家用电器、电子消费品及信息产品

2.工业自动化及智能制造、加工机械设备

3.新能源汽车及智慧出行、车辆及汽车配件

4.新材料及化工产品、新能源、五金工具等。

第二期（10月23日—27日）：品质家居

围绕家居生活主题，设15个展区，涵盖：

1.日用陶瓷、餐厨用具、家居装饰品

2.建筑及装饰材料、卫浴设备、家具

3.钟表眼镜、编织及藤铁工艺品等。

第三期（10月31日—11月4日）：美好生活

侧重消费品与健康领域，设21个展区，包含：

1.男女装、内衣、运动服及纺织面料

2.医药保健品及医疗器械、食品、宠物用品

3.玩具、童装、孕婴童用品、乡村振兴特色产品专区等。

第138届广交会展览总面积达155万平方米，参展企业超3.2万家，创历史新高，并首次设立智慧医疗专区和服务机器人专区，吸引手术机器人、智能监测及可穿戴设备等47家企业参加。引入46家行业领先企业，展出具身机器人、机器狗等。

中国进出口商品交易会展馆地址：中国广东省广州市海珠区阅江中路382号

欢迎参加第138届广交会。您可下载广交会APP、浏览展会官方网站或通过电话及邮件咨询获取更多信息。

The 138th Canton Fair opened on October 15, 2025

The 138th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair) will be held in three phases from October 15th to November 4th, 2025. The exhibition themes and exhibition area Settings for each phase are as follows:

Phase 1 (October 15th - 19th) : Advanced Manufacturing

Focusing on the industrial manufacturing field, 19 exhibition areas are set up, including:

1. Household appliances, electronic consumer goods and information products

2. Industrial automation and intelligent manufacturing, processing machinery and equipment

3. New energy vehicles and smart mobility, vehicles and auto parts

4. New materials and chemical products, new energy, hardware tools, etc.

Phase 2 (October 23rd - 27th) : Quality Home Furnishing

Centering on the theme of home life, 15 exhibition areas are set up, covering:

1. Daily-use ceramics, kitchenware, and home decorations

2. Building and decoration materials, bathroom fixtures, furniture

3. Watches, glasses, weaving and rattan and iron handicrafts, etc.

Phase 3 (October 31st - November 4th) : A Better Life

Focusing on consumer goods and health fields, 21 exhibition areas are set up, including:

1.Men's and women's clothing, underwear, sportswear and textile fabrics

2. Pharmaceuticals, health care products and medical devices, food, and pet supplies

3. Toy, children's clothing, baby and children's products, and special product zones for rural revitalization, etc.

The total exhibition area of the 138th Canton Fair has reached 1.55 million square meters, with over 32,000 exhibitors, setting a new historical record. For the first time, it has established a smart healthcare zone and a service robot zone, attracting 47 companies including surgical robots, intelligent monitoring and wearable devices to participate. 46 leading companies in the industry were introduced to showcase embodied robots, robot dogs and more.

The 138th China Import and Export Fair address: No. 382, Yuejiang Middle Road, Haizhu District, Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province, China
