长安汽车在2025年巴基斯坦车展发布三大品牌及十一款全新车型

CPEC 2025-11-16

长安汽车巴基斯坦有限公司（MCML）在2025年巴基斯坦车展（PAPS）上达成重要里程碑，推出三大品牌及十一款全新车型，包括该国首款全散件组装（CKD）增程式电动SUV深蓝S05。此次发布被该公司称为巴基斯坦迄今为止规模最大的新能源汽车（NEV）展示活动。

 

MCML同时揭晓了深蓝品牌的全新愿景——“驱动新一代出行”，将智能可持续出行置于未来战略的核心位置。

 

本次展示的亮点是巴基斯坦首款CKD增程式电动汽车（REEV）深蓝S05。这款C级SUV兼具纯电驾驶体验与长续航能力，由长安联合全球企业宁德时代（CATL）和华为共同研发。基于EPA1纯电平台打造，其综合续航里程超1000公里，搭载车载增程器可实现纯电驱动体验。

 

S05由大众集团前全球设计总监克劳斯·齐乔拉操刀设计，采用太空舱灵感造型、无框车门及高端智能内饰，拥有同级别最长轴距，座舱空间比标准C级SUV多出23%。舒适配置包括零重力副驾座椅，安全与便捷性方面则配备20余项L2级高级辅助驾驶系统（ADAS）功能，含遥控直进直出泊车。

 

MCML宣布S05的首发价为999.9万巴基斯坦卢比，现已在全国开启预订：2月可锁定限时优惠价，长安或深蓝经销商将为早期订车客户免费赠送3.3千瓦时便携式充电器。

 

展会上同步亮相的还有深蓝E07，这是一款基于多用途车型（Multi-Truck）概念打造的超豪华E级SUV，融合高端设计与实用属性。其搭载双电机四驱系统，最大功率590马力，零百加速仅3.9秒，配备空气悬架与零重力座椅，在巴基斯坦的定价为1900万卢比，预订金为400万卢比。

 

MCML进一步宣布阿维塔（Avatr）品牌即将登陆巴基斯坦，这是由长安、华为与宁德时代联合研发的全新超高端电动品牌，旨在为巴基斯坦电动汽车市场带来先进智能出行方案与未来感设计。

 

长安汽车巴基斯坦有限公司首席执行官达尼尔·马利克在活动中表示，公司正进入转型阶段：“在长安汽车，我们秉持全新理念，重新定义巴基斯坦的出行方式。未来两年内，我们将推出三大品牌及十一款新车。凭借深蓝S05增程式电动车、Hunter K50增程式电动皮卡以及即将上市的阿维塔等创新产品，我们正弥合传统燃油出行与电动出行的差距，将新能源技术带入各个细分市场。”

 

“Master Changan Motors Limited (MCML) marked a major milestone at the Pakistan Auto Show (PAPS) 2025 as it introduced three brands and eleven new models, including the country’s first CKD range-extended electric SUV, the Deepal S05. The launch formed part of what the company described as Pakistan’s most extensive New Energy Vehicle (NEV) showcase to date.


MCML also introduced Deepal’s new brand vision, Moving the New Generation, which places intelligent sustainable mobility at the centre of its future strategy.


The highlight of the showcase was the Deepal S05, Pakistan’s first CKD Range-Extended Electric Vehicle (REEV). The C-segment SUV pairs electric driving with long-range capability and has been co-developed with global firms CATL and Huawei. Built on the EPA1 Pure Electric Platform, it offers a combined range of more than 1,000 km along with a fully electric driving experience supported by an onboard range extender.


Designed by Klaus Zyciora, former global design chief of the Volkswagen Group, the S05 features spacecraft-inspired styling, frameless doors and a premium intelligent interior. It also offers the longest wheelbase in its category and provides 23 per cent more cabin space than standard C-segment SUVs. Comfort features include a Zero Gravity co-pilot seat, while safety and convenience are supported by more than twenty L2 ADAS functions including Remote Straight In and Out Parking.


The launch formed part of what the company described as Pakistan’s most extensive New Energy Vehicle (NEV) showcase to date.

MCML announced an introductory price of Rs9,999,000 for the S05. Bookings have opened nationwide with a limited price lock in February and a complimentary 3.3 kWh portable charger for early customers at Changan or Deepal dealerships.


Also unveiled was the Deepal E07, an ultra-luxury E-segment SUV based on a Multi-Truck concept that blends high-end design with utility. It offers a dual-motor AWD system producing 590 hp, acceleration from zero to 100 km/h in 3.9 seconds, air suspension and Zero Gravity seating. The model was introduced in Pakistan at Rs19,000,000 with bookings starting from Rs4,000,000.


MCML further announced the arrival of Avatr, a new ultra-premium electric brand co-developed by Changan, Huawei and CATL. Avatr aims to bring advanced intelligent mobility and futuristic design into Pakistan’s EV landscape.


Speaking at the event, Danial Malik, chief executive officer (CEO) of Master Changan Motors Limited, said the company was entering a transformative phase.


“At Master Changan we represent a new mindset that reimagines how Pakistan moves. Over the next two years we will have three brands and eleven new vehicles. With innovations like the Deepal S05 REEV, the Hunter K50 REEV pickup and the upcoming launch of Avatr 11, we are bridging the gap between conventional and electric mobility and bringing new-energy technology to every segment.””
