在数字化、智能化的新时代,企业差旅及出行是企业运营的重要组成部分，全球商务旅行协会（GBTA）数据显示全球商旅市场展现出强劲的增长势头，中国排名第一，到2028年，全球商旅支出预计会突破2万亿美元，为推动企业商旅更大范围新业态、新模式，专门为企业商旅设立的专业展会，将集中展示企业商旅最受市场欢迎、最有代表性的国内外新技术、新成果、把握企业对商旅采购需求，探索数字化商旅的最新动向，本届展览会将以国际视野和产业高度，为国内外商家合作搭建平台，为企业商旅产业提供交流及展示平台及新机遇，为企业商旅产业更快更好发展而共同努力。

With the advent of the digital and intelligent era, enterprise travel and mobility have become a vital component of business operations. According to GBTA, the global business travel market demonstrates robust growth momentum, with China ranking as the world’s largest market. By 2028, global business travel expenditure is projected to exceed US$2 trillion. To facilitate innovative business travel models and practices on a broader scale, this dedicated professional exhibition will showcase the most popular and representative new technologies and solutions from Chinese and international businesses. The upcoming expo will address corporate travel procurement needs while exploring the latest trends in digital business travel. With an international perspective and industry-leading standards, this expo will establish a collaborative platform for global enterprises, create unparalleled opportunities for exchange and demonstration, and drive faster and higher-quality development of the corporate travel industry.

2025深圳国际企业商旅峰会--未来共同唱响，受惠于企业对商旅服务的强劲需求增长，商旅行业从发展上也面临着转型升级和提质增效的问题，2025深圳国际商旅峰会、将邀请一众行业主管单位、行业研究机构、企业行政后勤差旅采购，商旅供需双方、行业协会、产业链上下游、金融服务机构、国内外资深商旅专家学者等、探讨以共创共享商旅生态圈、为参会代表传递最新的行业发展趋势，帮助企业更好的把握未来发展方向。

为期三天的“2024第二届深圳国际企业行政后勤采购博览会”和“2024深圳国际企业商旅专题展览会”及“2024第二届企业非产采购峰会”于2024年12月19-21日在深圳会展中心顺利举办，吸引了来自全国32个城市12000多位企业行政后勤差旅采购专家和产业链专业人士莅临参观、交流；其中大中型企业的行政采购差旅采购专业观众占到了总人数的60%以上。

The three-day Shenzhen International Enterprise Administrative Logistics Procurement Expo (the “Expo”) 2024, Shenzhen International Enterprise Business Travel Exhibition 2024, and the 2nd Corporate Non-Production Sourcing Summit 2024 were successfully held at Shenzhen Convention and Exhibition Center from December 19 to 21, 2024. More than 12,000 enterprise administrative logistics and business travel procurement specialists and industry chain professionals from 32 cities across China attended the events and exchanged insights at this industry gathering. The administrative and business travel procurement professionals of large and medium-sized enterprises accounted for over 60% of the attendees.


The Expo 2024 showcased cutting-edge innovations in office ecosystems, including the latest intelligent management systems and the most flexible office and business travel solutions in the field of administrative logistics and business travel in China and abroad. The event provided a platform for enterprise administrative logistics professionals and suppliers to demonstrate their services and solutions and exchange insights, thereby advancing the digital and intelligent transformation of enterprise administrative logistics and business travel management.


Enterprises from first-tier Chinese cities including Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Shanghai, and Beijing formed a joint delegation to exhibit at the Expo 2024.
