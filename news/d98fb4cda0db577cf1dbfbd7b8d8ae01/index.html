PART.01

展会概述

Exhibition overview

2026年德国汉诺威工业展，定于4月20日至24日在德国汉诺威国际展览中心举办，作为始创于1947年的全球顶级工业盛会，被誉为“世界工业发展晴雨表”，一年一届，影响力覆盖全球150多个国家和地区。
Hannover Messe 2026 will be held 20–24 April 2026 at the Hannover Exhibition Center, Germany.Since 1947, it has been the world’s leading industrial event, widely regarded as the global benchmark for industrial trends, with influence spanning more than 150 countries and regions.

本届展会规模进一步扩容，展览面积预计超40万㎡，汇聚全球6500余家参展商，吸引20万人次以上专业观众，新增初创企业专区和碳中和技术专区，聚焦工业4.0向工业5.0过渡，重点展示自动化、数字生态、氢能技术等前沿领域成果，是企业拓展国际市场、对接优质资源、洞察行业趋势的核心平台。
The 2026 edition will cover over 400,000 sqm, with 6,500+ exhibitors and 200,000+ professional visitors.It will feature new zones for startups and carbon-neutral technologies, focusing on the transition from Industry 4.0 to 5.0.Key exhibits include automation, digital ecosystems, and hydrogen technologies.
