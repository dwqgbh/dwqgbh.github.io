China International Fair for Trade in Services 2022 (CIFTIS) will be held in Beijing from August 31th-September 5th, 2022. The theme of CIFTIS 2022 is "Cooperate for better development, innovate for a greener future," which is co-organized by the Ministry of Commerce and the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and supported by the World Trade Organisation, the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development, and the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development.


2022年中国国际服务贸易交易会（简称“服贸会”）将于2022年8月31日-9月5日在北京举办。本届服贸会由中华人民共和国商务部和北京市人民政府共同主办，世界贸易组织、联合国贸易和发展会议、经济合作与发展组织为支持单位。2022年服贸会主题为“服务合作促发展 绿色创新迎未来”。


This year, Germany Trade & Invest (GTAI), together with the economic promotion agencies of the three German federal states (Hamburg, North Rhine-Westphalia and Thuringia) and two German enterprises (FIEGE Logistik and EasyMotionSkin Tec GmbH) with the support of AHK Greater China, jointly represent Germany at a German national pavilion at CIFTIS.


今年的服贸会德国联邦外贸与投资署携手3个德国联邦州的经济促进机构（汉堡、北威州和图林根州），以及两家德国企业（飞格集团和EasyMotionSkin Tec GmbH），在德国海外商会联盟·大中华区的支持下，共同代表德国在服贸会设立德国国家展团。
