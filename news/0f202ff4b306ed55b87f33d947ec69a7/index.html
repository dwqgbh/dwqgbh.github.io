费米电气（舟山）有限公司

Formitek Electric (Zhoushan) Co., Ltd.


费米电气是以第三代功率半导体技术为核心的高科技电源公司，秉持 “技术垂直整合 + 产业协同发展” 战略，以自主研发为驱动，汇聚顶尖团队推动功率半导体应用技术国产化突破与全球化竞争，创新与市场竞争力稳居行业前列。


依托雄厚技术实力，品牌累计拥有近 70项发明专利及160余项实用新型专利等知识产权，通过ISO 9001与IATF 16949双质量体系认证，斩获国家级高新技术企业、省级潜在独角兽等多项重磅荣誉。


费米电气深耕智能船舶电气系统与立体海洋海工能源管理与利用体系，其中船舶电子、双向电源、工业充电等获市场好评，同时持续研发新型船舶供电与控制设备，以 “高效、可靠、智能” 理念，凭借全产业链优势助力电力电子行业创新发展。


Formitek is a high-tech power enterprise focusing on the wide bandgap semiconductor technology. Adhering to the strategy of "vertical technological integration + industrial collaborative development" and driven by independent R&D, we have assembled a top-tier team to pioneer the localized breakthrough and global competition of power semiconductor application technologies, securing a leading position in the industry for innovation and market competitiveness.


Endowed with robust technological strength, we hold nearly 70 invention patents and over 160 intellectual property rights including utility model patents. We have obtained the dual quality system certifications of ISO 9001 and IATF 16949, and won numerous prestigious honors such as National High-Tech Enterprise and Provincial Potential Unicorn Enterprise.


Formitek devotes itself to R&D and production of intelligent high-power-density power supply products. Our offerings in marine electronics, bidirectional power supplies and industrial charging systems have earned high market recognition. We also keep developing new marine power supply and control equipment. Guided by the philosophy of "Efficiency, Reliability and Intelligence", we leverage our whole-industry-chain advantages to boost the innovative development of the power electronics industry.
