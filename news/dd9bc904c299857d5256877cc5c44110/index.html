日本医疗介护及医疗设备展2026MEDICAL JAPAN 

Are you familiar with MEDICAL JAPAN? It’s one of Japan’s largest and Asia’s leading B2B trade shows for medical, elderly care, and preventive healthcare.

Japan’s healthcare market continues to grow, driven by an aging population and strong reliance on overseas technologies. Demand for international medical solutions is steadily increasing. However, building effective connections with local buyers and distributors remains a key challenge for international suppliers.

MEDICAL JAPAN provides a practical, proven platform to address this.

MEDICAL JAPAN 2026 TOKYO Overview

Dates: October 7 - 9, 2026 

Venue: Makuhari Messe, Japan 

Scale: 17,000+ professional visitors / 650+ exhibitors

Visitors: Key decision-makers, including hospital directors, clinic managers, distributors, importers, and industry leaders from across Japan and Asia.

Exhibitions: Seven specialised exhibitions covering medical devices, healthcare IT, elderly care equipment, pharmacy solutions, and more.
