JONHON丨2025日本汽车工程展览会圆满落幕(AEE 2025)

中航光电 2025-07-18

7月18日，日本汽车工程展览会（Automotive Engineering Exposition）在名古屋爱知县国际展示场圆满落幕。作为全球汽车技术领域最具影响力的专业展会之一，本届展会吸引了来自世界各地的300余家展商，携新能源汽车核心部件、智能驾驶系统等前沿技术成果集中展示。到场的上万名专业观众，通过深度技术交流与思想碰撞，不仅精准捕捉到产业发展趋势，更为汽车行业的创新突破与跨界合作注入了强劲新动能。
July 18th marked the successful conclusion of the Automotive Engineering Exposition at the Aichi Sky Expo in Nagoya. As one of the most influential professional exhibitions in the global automotive technology field, this exposition attracted over 300 exhibitors from all over the world, who showed their cutting-edge technological achievements such as core components of electric vehicles and intelligent driving systems. Tens of thousands of professional visitors presented, through in-depth technical exchanges, not only accurately captured the industrial development trends, but also injected strong new momentum into the innovation and breakthroughs.
中航光电携全场景新能源汽车整车互连解决方案精彩亮相本次展会。展会期间，其展示的高压连接器、智能网联解决方案等核心产品，凭借前沿技术特性成为现场焦点，持续吸引整车制造商及核心零部件供应商驻足展位、参观交流。现场技术人员通过系统化的产品展示与专业化的细致讲解，全方位展现出中航光电在高可靠互连技术领域的深厚技术积淀与强劲持续创新能力。
JONHON made a splendid appearance at this exhibition with its full-scenario electric vehicle interconnect solutions. During the exhibition, its displayed core products such as high-voltage connectors and intelligent network solutions became the focus of the venue due to their cutting-edge technical features, continuously attracting vehicle manufacturers and core component suppliers to stop by the booth for visits and exchanges. On-site technical personnel, through systematic product displays and professional in-depth explanations, comprehensively demonstrated JONHON's profound technical accumulation and strong continuous innovation capabilities in the field of high-reliability interconnect technology.
