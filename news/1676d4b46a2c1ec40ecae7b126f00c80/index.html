这里是四川 | 第三章:构筑向西开放战略高地④

Panda Voice 2026-07-01


创新开展重大开放活动


Innovatively Carrying Out Major 

Opening-up Activities

四川积极参与共建“一带一路”，建强开放平台，举办重大活动，全方位推进开放合作，在展现中国式现代化万千气象中提升影响力、集聚力、辐射力。

Sichuan actively participates in joint construction of the Belt and Road Initiative, strengthens open platforms, hosts major events and promotes open cooperation in all directions, enhancing influence, cohesion and outreach while showcasing the myriad aspects of Chinese-style modernization.



第二十届中国西部国际博览会



The 20th Western China 

International Fair

2025年是西博会创立25周年，也是中国实施西部大开发战略25周年。本届西博会以“深化改革增动能 扩大开放促发展”为主题，首次设置双主宾国、双主宾省，邀请匈牙利、老挝为主宾国，浙江、青海为主宾省。吸引来自境外62个国家（地区）和国内27个省（区、市）的3000余家企业参展，其中境外及境外在华企业近300家。西部各方与国内外投资者共签约投资合作项目416个，投资金额3543亿元。

2025 marked the 25th anniversary of the Western China International Fair, as well as the 25th anniversary of China’s implementation of the Western Development Strategy. The 20th Western China International Fair, themed “Deepen Reform for More Momentum, Expand Openness for Greater Growth,” and for the first time, set dual guest countries of honor and dual guest provinces of honor, with Hungary and Laos invited as guest countries of honor and Zhejiang and Qinghai as guest provinces of honor. The event attracted over 3,000 enterprises from 62 countries (regions) abroad and 27 provinces (autonomous regions and municipalities) in China, including nearly 300 overseas and foreign-invested enterprises in China. Various parties from the western part of China signed 416 investment cooperation projects with domestic and foreign investors, with a total investment of 354.3 billion yuan.



2025中国产业转移发展对接



活动


（四川）



2025 China Industrial Transfer & 

Development Matchmaking Activities (Sichuan)

中国产业转移发展对接活动是全国具有重要影响力的产业转移示范平台，本次举行的四川专场活动是我省第二次举办，由工业和信息化部、四川省人民政府主办。活动以“共享战略新机遇，同筑圈链新生态”为主题，举办1场综合活动和5场专题活动，围绕“15+N”重点产业链开展特色产业推介、实地考察、产业协作、项目签约等活动。

The China Industrial Transfer & Development Matchmaking Activities is a nationally influential demonstration platform for industrial transfer. This Sichuan special session marked the second time the province hosted the event organized by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the People’s Government of Sichuan Province. The event, themed Sharing Strategic New Opportunities and Building a New Ecosystem of Industrial Clusters Together, included one comprehensive session and five specialized sessions, featuring activities such as promotion of characteristic industries, on-site visits, industrial cooperation and project signings, focusing on the “15+N” key industrial chains.



2025中外知名企业四川行



The Well-known Chinese and Foreign Enterprises’Visit to Sichuan 2025

“中外知名企业四川行”创办于2014年，是四川招商引资的首选平台和对外开放的重要窗口。2025年活动吸引逾400名中外知名企业及商（协）会代表参加，共签约项目340个，投资总额达2630.6亿元。

The Well-known Chinese and Foreign Enterprises’Visit to Sichuan event was established in 2014 and serves as the premier platform for investment promotion and an important window for opening up to the outside world. The 2025 event attracted more than 400 well-known Chinese and foreign enterprises and chambers of commerce (associations), with a total of 340 projects signed and a total investment of 263.06 billion yuan.



2025世界显示产业创新发展大会



2025 World Conference on Innovation and Development in the Display Industry

在成都举办的2025世界显示产业创新发展大会，是全球规格高、规模大、影响力广的新型显示产业领域盛会，大会以“显示无处不在·AI点靓视界”为主题，在开幕式上投资总额157.4亿元的13个新型显示产业合作项目集中签约。

The 2025 World Conference on Innovation and Development in the Display Industry is a high-level, large-scale and influential event in the field of new display industries. This conference, themed Displays Everywhere, AI Lights Up the World, saw the collective signing of 13 new display industry cooperation projects at the opening ceremony, with a total investment of 15.74 billion yuan.



2025世界动力电池大会



2025 World Power Battery Conference

在宜宾举办的世界动力电池大会，已成为全球动力电池产业增进理解互信、深化交流合作的重要平台。2025世界动力电池大会以“新视野·新生态·新机遇”为主题，突出科技创新引领、动力电池产业建圈强链、关联产业融合发展，助力全球动力电池产业高质量发展。

The World Power Battery Conference held in Yibin has become an important platform for enhancing understanding and trust, as well as deepening communication and cooperation in the global power battery industry. The 2025 World Power Battery Conference, themed New Vision, New Ecology, New Opportunities, emphasized leading technological innovation, building a strong power battery industry chain, integrating related industries and promoting high-quality development of the global power battery industry.
