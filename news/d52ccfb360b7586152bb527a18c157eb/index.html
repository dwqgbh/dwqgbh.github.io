2026春晚:人形机器人“大阅兵”,中国智造开启“家庭元年”A ＂Grand Parade＂ of Humanoid Robots

WelcomingANNA 养老机器人用户群 2026-02-22

今日主题：2026春晚：人形机器人“大阅兵”，中国智造开启“家庭元年”


Today’s Theme: 2026 Spring Festival Gala: A "Grand Parade" of Humanoid Robots, as Made-in-China Usher in the "First Year of Home Adoption"


作为建立了两年多的机器人主题群，

当然要说说今年春晚的人形机器人。

当钢铁之躯在春晚舞台上演“空翻”与“共情”，

一场属于我们每个人的科技红利正在悄然降临。


As a robot-themed community established for more than two years,

it is only fitting that we talk about the humanoid robots at this year’s Gala.

As steel frames perform backflips and convey "empathy" on the Spring Festival Gala stage,

a technological dividend for each one of us is quietly arriving.

🤖🎉



01 | 春晚舞台的“硬核”阅兵：四家品牌，三大技术突破

2026年的春晚，不再是简单的歌舞升平，

开启了中国智造的“硬核”阅兵。

四家顶尖人形机器人品牌，

用三场风格迥异的表演，

向全球展示了中国科技的“肌肉”。


01 | A "Hardcore" Parade on the Gala Stage: Four Brands, Three Major Technological Breakthroughs

The 2026 Spring Festival Gala is no longer just a showcase of songs and dances;

it has launched a "hardcore" parade of Made-in-China intelligence.

Four top-tier humanoid robot brands

used three distinct performances

to showcase the "strength" of China’s technology to the world.

💪🌐



1.1 宇树科技（《武BOT》）：


上演“钢铁武者”对决。

机器人身着红袍，

与河南塔沟武校少年同台演武，

完成长棍、醉拳、双截棍及剑术的高难度演绎。

技术核心：高动态运动控制。

机器人实现了亚毫秒级同步（误差<0.1秒），

核心关节扭矩密度提升30%，

支撑空翻、跳跃等高爆发力动作，

是运动能力的极致展现。



1.1 Yushu Technology(Wu BOT):


A showdown of "steel warriors".

Clad in red robes,

the robots performed martial arts alongside young athletes from the Tagou Wushu School in Henan,

executing difficult routines including staff, drunken fist, nunchaku, and swordplay.

Core Technology: High-dynamic motion control.

The robots achieved sub-millisecond synchronization (error < 0.1 second),

with a 30% increase in torque density at the core joints,

supporting high-explosive movements such as backflips and jumps,

representing the ultimate expression of athletic capability.🥋⚙️



1.2 松延动力（《奶奶的最爱》）：


聚焦家庭场景。

在小品中携4款机器人参演，

主打温情陪伴与生活化服务。

技术核心：仿生交互。

机器人以1:1比例复刻蔡明形象，

头部40个自由度

实现了细腻的面部微表情与唇形同步，

成功跨越“恐怖谷效应”，

让机器人有了“人情味”。



1.2 Songyan Power (Grandma’s Favorite):


Focused on home scenarios.

Four robot models appeared in a skit,

specializing in warm companionship and daily-life services.

Core Technology: Bionic interaction.

The robots replicated the image of Cai Ming in a 1:1 ratio,

with 40 degrees of freedom in the head

enabling delicate facial micro-expressions and lip-syncing,

successfully overcoming the "uncanny valley effect"

and giving robots a "human touch". 👵❤️



1.3 魔法原子（《智造未来》）：


工业智造与艺术融合。

为陈小春、易烊千玺等伴演，

呈现工业机械臂与人形躯干设计的融合。

技术核心：多机协同与场景适配。

机器人完成连续倒地起身、

下腰旋转等特技动作，

兼顾观赏性与稳定性。


1.3 Magic Atom(Intelligent Future):


The fusion of industrial intelligence and art.

The robots performed alongside Jordan Chan, Jackson Yee, and other artists,

presenting the integration of industrial robotic arms and humanoid torso design.

Core Technology: Multi-robot collaboration and scene adaptation.

The robots executed stunts such as consecutive falls and get-ups,

backbends, and spins,

balancing visual appeal and stability.🎭🔄



1.4 银河通用（贺岁微电影）：


自主决策的“大脑”。

在微电影中自主完成“盘核桃”等生活化动作。

技术核心：具身大模型。

机器人不再依赖预设脚本，

可实时感知环境并调整行为，

实现了从“执行”到“思考”的跨越。


1.4 Galaxy General (New Year Microfilm):


The "brain" of autonomous decision-making.

In the microfilm, the robots autonomously performed daily actions such as "twisting walnuts".

Core Technology: Embodied large model.

The robots no longer rely on pre-set scripts;

they can perceive the environment in real time and adjust their behavior,

achieving the leap from "execution" to "thinking".🧠🔍



02 | 2026：从“实验室”到“舞台”的井喷元年


2026年，被业内称为人形机器人的“商业化元年”。

人形机器人不再是PPT上的概念，

而是真金白银的订单与实实在在的落地。


02 | 2026: The Explosive First Year from "Lab" to "Stage"


2026 is known in the industry as the "first year of commercialization" for humanoid robots.

Humanoid robots are no longer just concepts on PPT slides,

but tangible orders and real-world implementations.

🚀📈



2.1 规模放量：


2025年国内出货量约1.8万台，

而2026年预计将攀升至6.25万-20万台，

同比增长翻倍甚至数倍。

这意味着，机器人正从“稀有物种”变成“量产商品”。


2.1 Volume Surge:


Domestic shipments reached approximately 18,000 units in 2025,

and are projected to surge to 62,500–200,000 units in 2026,

representing a doubling or even several-fold year-on-year growth.

This means robots are shifting from "rare specimens" to "mass-produced goods".📦⚡



2.2 成本下探：


核心零部件国产化率超95%，

成本较三年前下降60%。

随着规模化生产，

机器人价格正从“奢侈品”向“耐用品”靠拢，

为进入家庭在扫清价格障碍。


2.2 Cost Reduction:


The localization rate of core components exceeds 95%,

with costs dropping by 60% compared to three years ago.

With large-scale production,

robot prices are moving from "luxury goods" to "durable goods",

clearing price barriers for entry into households.💰📉



2.3 场景落地：


从工业巡检、物流搬运，

到家庭陪伴、教育展示，

人形机器人的应用场景正以3倍以上的速度扩张。

我预测，2026年，

我们有望看到第一批大胆采购人形机器人的家庭用户出现。


2.3 Scenario Implementation:


From industrial inspection and logistics handling

to home companionship and educational demonstrations,

the application scenarios of humanoid robots are expanding at more than three times the speed.

I predict that in 2026,

we will witness the first batch of bold household users purchasing humanoid robots.🏠🌱



03 | 普通人的“入局”指南：拥抱产业链红利


面对这场科技盛宴，

我们普通人如何介入？

直接购买整机或许还为时过早，

但我们可以通过拥抱产业链核心环节，

分享时代红利。

核心逻辑：

人形机器人的核心在于“关节”和“大脑”。

谁掌握了核心零部件，

谁就掌握了产业链的“命脉”。


03 | A Guide for Ordinary People: Embrace the Dividend of the Industrial Chain


Faced with this technological feast,

how can ordinary people get involved?

It may still be too early to buy a complete robot directly,

but we can share the dividends of the era

by embracing the core links of the industrial chain.

Core Logic:

The heart of a humanoid robot lies in its "joints" and "brain".

Whoever controls the core components

holds the "lifeblood" of the industrial chain. 🔗💡



3.1 三花智控：


执行器总成龙头，

相当于机器人的“肌肉”和“骨骼”，

是运动能力的基石。


3.1 Sanhua Intelligent Control:


A leading manufacturer of actuator assemblies,

equivalent to the "muscles" and "bones" of robots,

serving as the foundation of motion capability.

🦴💪



3.2 双环传动：


减速器领域的隐形冠军，

负责将电机的转速转化为精准的力矩，

是机器人“稳准狠”的关键。


3.2 Shuanghuan Transmission:


An invisible champion in the reducer sector,

responsible for converting motor speed into precise torque,

the key to robots’ stability, accuracy, and efficiency.⚙️🎯



3.3 柯力传感：


传感器专家，

相当于机器人的“触觉”和“感知”，

让机器人能感知环境、避免碰撞。


3.3 Keli Sensing:


A sensor specialist,

equivalent to the "touch" and "perception" of robots,

enabling them to sense the environment and avoid collisions.👆🛡️



3.4 五洲新春：


精密轴承供应商，

是关节传动中不可或缺的“润滑剂”，

决定了机器人的寿命和精度。


3.4 Wuzhou New Year:


A supplier of precision bearings,

an indispensable "lubricant" in joint transmission,

determining the lifespan and precision of robots.🔩🛠️



3.5 绿的谐波：


谐波减速器龙头，

技术壁垒极高，

是机器人关节实现轻量化、高精度的核心部件。


3.5 Leader Harmonic Drive:

A leader in harmonic reducers,

with extremely high technical barriers,

a core component for lightweight, high-precision robot joints. ⚖️✨


3.6 峰岹科技：


驱控芯片专家，

相当于机器人的“神经中枢”，

负责指挥每一个动作的精准执行。


3.6 Fortior Technology:


A drive and control chip specialist,

equivalent to the "neural center" of robots,

responsible for commanding the precise execution of every movement.🧠⚡



⚠️ 风险提示：

以上仅为产业分析，并非投资建议。

人形机器人产业仍处于早期阶段，

技术路线、市场竞争格局存在不确定性，

投资需谨慎，切勿盲目跟风。


⚠️ Risk Warning:

The above is only industrial analysis and shall not be construed as investment advice.

The humanoid robot industry is still in its early stage,

with uncertainties in technological routes and market competition landscape.

Invest with caution and do not follow trends blindly.⚠️📊

2026年的春晚告诉我们，

人形机器人不再是冰冷的钢铁，

成为这场科技革命的见证者与受益者。

The 2026 Spring Festival Gala shows us
