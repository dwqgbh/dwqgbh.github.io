重点展会亮点解读
Exhibition Navigation System


✦

开年重磅·中东双星（1月-2月）

卡塔尔多哈海事防务展 (DIMDEX)：聚焦海军、海事安全与沿海防御，是进入海湾国家高端防务市场的首选平台。

沙特世界防务展 (World Defense Show)：在沙特“2030愿景”下，展会规模与采购预算连年攀升，是接触沙特及周边国家陆军、空军及综合防务决策者的核心场合。

亚太机遇·电子与防务并举（4月、10-11月）

香港春秋季电子产品展：亚洲规模最大的电子贸易盛会，是发布新品、联络全球买家的必选平台。

马来西亚DSA & 印尼Indo Defence：东盟地区最具影响力的两大防务展，是开拓东南亚快速增长的安全防务市场的关键跳板。

中国国际航空航天博览会（珠海航展）：世界五大航展之一，集中展示中国航空航天及防务领域最高水平，国际影响力巨大。

巴基斯坦IDEAS：巴基斯坦国家级防务展，是深化中巴合作、辐射南亚市场的重要窗口。

新兴市场·聚焦非洲与欧亚交汇（5月、9月）

土耳其SAHA Expo：依托土耳其独特的欧亚枢纽地位，是连接欧亚市场的战略性展会。
非洲空军论坛 & 埃及航展 & 南非AAD：三大展会共同构成开拓非洲新兴航空与防务市场的黄金组合，覆盖北非、西非及南部非洲需求。


The Heavyweight Opening: Twin Stars of the Middle East (Jan-Feb)
DIMDEX：Focusing on naval, maritime security, and coastal defense, it is the premier platform for entering the high-end defense market of the Gulf states.
WDS：Driven by Saudi Arabia's "Vision 2030," the exhibition's scale and procurement budget continue to grow annually, making it a core venue for engaging with decision-makers from the Saudi and neighboring nations' army, air force, and comprehensive defense sectors.
Asia-Pacific Opportunities: Electronics and Defense in Tandem (Apr, Oct-Nov)
Hong Kong Electronics Fair (Spring & Autumn Editions)：As the largest electronics trade event in Asia, it is the essential platform for launching new products and connecting with global buyers.
DSA Malaysia & Indo Defence Indonesia：The two most influential defense exhibitions in the ASEAN region, serving as critical springboards for tapping into Southeast Asia's rapidly growing security and defense market.
Airshow China：Ranked among the world's top five airshows, it centrally showcases the highest achievements in China's aerospace and defense sectors, wielding significant international influence.
IDEAS Pakistan：Pakistan's national-level defense exhibition, serving as a vital gateway for deepening China-Pakistan cooperation and accessing the South Asian market.
Emerging Markets: Focus on Africa and the Eurasia Junction (May, Sep)
SAHA Expo Istanbul, Türkiye：Leveraging Türkiye's unique position as a Eurasian hub, it is a strategic exhibition for connecting European and Asian markets.
Africa Airforce Forum, Egypt International Airshow & AAD: ：Together, these three exhibitions form a golden portfolio for exploring Africa's emerging aviation and defense markets, covering demands in North, West, and Southern Africa.
