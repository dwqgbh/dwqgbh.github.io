RivieraEvents 2022-03-15


在后疫情时代，线上活动和线上线下结合活动及时地解决了人们因无法亲临现场而产生的问题，有效地弥合人、地和组织之间的距离。然而，线下活动在建立至关重要的企业关系和获取潜在客户、开发利益方面仍然与以往一样重要。


In a post-pandemic world where virtual and hybrid events continue to help bridge the gap between people, places and organisations, offline exhibitions are still as relevant as ever when it comes to making those all-important corporate connections and reaping lead generation benefits.

中国国际进口博览会（CIIE）作为中国继续推动贸易自由化和全球化的经济举措的一部分，一直备受期待。它为国家、地区和品牌提供展示其产品与服务，并开展业务和加强全球贸易发展的机会。


As part of China's continued economic initiative to liberalise trade and promote globalisation, the highly-anticipated China International Import Expo (CIIE) is one such event that provides countries, regions and brands with the opportunity to showcase their products and services, do business, and strengthen the development of global trade.
