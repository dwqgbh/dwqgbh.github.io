青岛高科系列产品闪耀2025汉诺威工业博览会

青岛高科通信股份有限公司 2025-04-01

       2025汉诺威工业博览会的主题是“赋能工业可持续发展”，聚焦人工智能、能源转型等议题。    

   The theme of this year's HANNOVER MESSE 2025 exhibition is "Empowering Sustainable Industrial Development", focusing on topics such as AI and energy transition.

   作为服务于全球的新能源产品专业供应商，青岛高科通信参加本届汉诺威工业博览会，在12号展馆向世界各地区与国家的客户展示公司近年在配网自动化领域的科技创新与产品迭代，吸引并得到了海外诸多客户的认可与关注。

   As a professional supplier of new energy products serving the global market, Gaoke commiucation is participating in this exhibition. In Hall 12, we will showcase their recent technological innovations and distribution automation system in the field,It has attracted and received recognition and attention from many overseas customers.

   青岛高科通信拥有智能电网电力营销系统系列产品、电网自动化系统系列产品、新能源电动汽车充电系列产品、数字能源系列产品、电力通信系列产品的知识产权，专利技术。

  The company owns intellectual property rights and patented technologies for a series of products including smart grid power marketing systems, grid automation systems, new energy electric vehicle charging products, digital energy products, and power communication products. It has the capability for technological research and development as well as intelligent manufacturing for these product series. Currently, the company is engaged in global product marketing, technical engineering services, with its entire product line meeting the standards of China, Europe, North America.

   配网自动化系统由配网自动化主站、配电子站、配电远方终端和通信网络组成。配电主站位于城市调度中心，配电子站部署于110kV/35kV变电站。配电远方终端包括馈线终端FTU、集中式站所终端DTU、分散式站所终端DTU。

      A distribution automation system consists of a distribution master station, several distribution slave stations(optional), remote distribution terminals, and a communication network. The distribution master station is located in the city dispatch center, while the distribution slave stations(optional) are deployed at 110kV/35kV transformer substations. Remote distribution terminals include FTU, centralized DTU, and decentralized DTU.
