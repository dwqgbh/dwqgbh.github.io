EMKA 2024中国国际工业博览会精彩瞬间

EMKA爱姆卡 2024-09-28

      2024年9月24日-28日国家会展中心（上海）；EMKA携其创新产品和前沿技术精彩亮相，成为展会上的一大亮点。

September 24-28, 2024, National Convention and Exhibition Center (Shanghai); EMKA showcased its innovative products and cutting-edge technologies, becoming a major highlight at the exhibition.






   


      亲爱的朋友们随着时光的流转，2024 年中国国际工业博览会即将缓缓落下帷幕，至此已圆满结束。在这一场汇聚全球智慧与创新的盛会中，EMKA公司深感荣幸能够参与其中，与各界精英共同交流、探索和进步。回顾这一段精彩的历程，我们展现了EMKA的卓越实力，收获了无数宝贵的经验和友谊。那些充满激情的交流、精彩绝伦的展示以及令人难忘的合作瞬间，都将成为我们前行路上的璀璨星光。

      然而，离别是为了更好的重逢。虽然 2024 年的博览会即将结束，但我们满怀期待，相约 2025 再相见！在未来的日子里，EMKA公司将继续砥砺前行，不断创新，以更优质的产品和服务，在 2025 年的中国国际工业博览会上再次绽放光彩！

      让我们共同期待 2025 年再相聚，续写辉煌篇章！

Dear friends, as time goes by, the 2024 China International Industrial Expo is coming to a slow end and has come to a successful conclusion. EMKA is honored to participate in this grand event that gathers global wisdom and innovation, and to communicate, explore, and progress together with elites from all walks of life. Looking back on this exciting journey, we have demonstrated EMKA's outstanding strength and gained countless valuable experiences and friendships. Those passionate exchanges, stunning displays, and unforgettable moments of collaboration will all become the shining stars on our journey forward.

However, parting is for a better reunion. Although the 2024 Expo is coming to an end, we are full of anticipation and promise to meet again in 2025! In the days to come, EMKA company will continue to forge ahead, innovate continuously, and shine again at the 2025 China International Industrial Expo with higher quality products and services!

Let's look forward to meeting again in 2025 and continuing to write a brilliant chapter!
