参展GO 2026-05-19

关于2027成都国际工业博览会举办时间的通知

尊敬的参展商、合作伙伴及业界同仁：


感谢您长期以来对成都国际工业博览会的积极参与和大力支持！


在各方共同努力下，2026成都国际工业博览会已于3月13日在中国西部国际博览城圆满落下帷幕。作为“十五五”开局之年中西部地区首场工业盛会，本届展会以“创链新工业，共碳新未来”为主题，汇聚全球550余家参展企业，展览面积超50,000平方米，吸引专业观众50,372人次。展会期间，工业自动化、数控机床与金属加工、机器人、新一代信息技术及四川工业链博馆等六大展区全景呈现，同期举办20余场高端行业活动，充分展现了智能制造与绿色转型的最新成果，获得了参展企业、专业观众及行业各方的高度评价。


为积极响应广大展商的强烈要求，并进一步助力中西部制造业高质量发展，经主办方审慎研究决定，2027成都国际工业博览会定于2027年3月2-4日在中国西部国际博览城举行。


我们将继续秉承高质量办展理念，持续优化展会组织与服务水平，为各方搭建更为高效、专业的展示、交流与合作平台。关于2027年展会的最新筹备进展及参展相关事宜，敬请关注官方后续通知。


期待与您再度相聚蓉城，共拓西部工业新机遇！


顺颂商祺！


东浩兰生会展集团上海工业商务展览有限公司

汉诺威米兰展览（上海）有限公司

四川天府国际会展有限公司

四川鹏璨文化传媒有限公司


2026年5月18日


NOTICE


Dear Exhibitors, Partners and Industry Colleagues,


Thank you for your long-term participation and strong support for the Chengdu International Industrial Fair (CDIIF).


This year’s show – the Chengdu International Industrial Fair 2026- thanks to the joint efforts of all parties, concluded successfully on 13 March at the Western China International Expo City. As the first major industrial event in central and western China in the opening year of the country’s 15th Five-Year Plan, CDIIF 2026 was held under the theme of "Innovate Industry Chain, Make a Low-Carbon Future." The exhibition covered over 50,000 square meters, brought together more than 550 exhibitors from around the world, and attracted 50,372 professional visits. During the event, six themed exhibition areas – Industrial Automation, CNC Machine Tools and Metalworking, Robotics, Information and Communication Technology, and the specially established Sichuan Industrial Chain Exhibition – were presented in full. More than 20 high-calibre events were held concurrently, fully demonstrating the latest achievements in smart manufacturing and green transformation, and receiving high praise from exhibitors, professional visitors, and all sectors of the industry.


In response to the strong request of many exhibitors and to further support the high-quality development of central and western China’s manufacturing sector, the organizers have carefully decided that the Chengdu International Industrial Fair 2027 will be held from March 2 to 4, 2027 at the Western China International Expo City.


We will continue to uphold the philosophy of a high-quality exhibition, constantly improve our organization and services, and build a more efficient and professional platform for showcase, exchange and cooperation. For the latest preparatory progress and matters related to exhibiting at CDIIF 2027, please stay tuned to our subsequent official announcements.


We look forward to meeting you again in Chengdu and exploring new opportunities in western China’s industrial development!


Yours Sincerely,


Shanghai Industry & Commerce Exhibition Co., Ltd.

Hannover Milano Fairs Shanghai Co., Ltd.

Sichuan Tianfu International Conference & Exhibition Co., Ltd

Sichuan Pengcan Culture Media Co., Ltd
