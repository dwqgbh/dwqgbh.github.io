延期通知 | 2022中国国际复合材料工业技术展览会

玻璃钢商情 2022-08-01



尊敬的参展商、观众及合作伙伴：


　　综合当前的疫情防控形势和地方政策，通过与相关政府部门及属地场馆持续不断地沟通与确认，在充分尊重参展商意见并考量各方面条件的前提下，为确保本届展会顺利举办，组委会经过审慎研究决定：


　　原定于2022年9月27日-29日在上海世博展览馆举办的“2022中国国际复合材料工业技术展览会”将延期到2022年11月30日-12月2日举办，举办地变更为江苏省南京市的南京国际博览中心(南京市建邺区江东中路300号)。


　　对于此次展会时间和地点调整给您带来的不便，我们深表歉意。我们将竭尽全力，克服一切困难，做好展会各项准备工作，为大家提供更好的参展服务和观展体验，办好这场久违的、全行业翘首以盼的高品质盛会！


中国国际复合材料工业技术展览会组委会

                                   2022年7月28日


Important Notice on the Rescheduling of CCE2022


Based on current situation of COVID-19 pandemic control and prevention, as well as relevant local policies, following sustained communication and confirmation with government authorities and venue operators, with full respect to opinions from exhibitors and conditions on all sides, in order to guarantee the success of the exhibition this year, the organizing committee has decided after serious consideration that:


China Composite Expo 2022, previously scheduled from September 27th-29th, 2022 and staged at Shanghai World Expo Exhibition and Convention Center, will be rescheduled from November 30th-December 2nd, and relocated at Nanjing International Expo Center (NIEC, address: No.300, Middle Jiangdong Road, Jianye District, Nanjing, Jiangsu)


We feel deeply sorry for the inconvenience arising from such rescheduling and relocation. We will spare no efforts and overcome all the difficulties to advance the preparation work in all respects. Surely, we will present better exhibiting service and participating experience to all attendees, so as to ensure another edition of high-quality event long delayed but earnestly expected.


The organizing committee of China Composites Expo
