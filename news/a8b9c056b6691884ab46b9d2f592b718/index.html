爱尔兰国家馆将再次亮相中国国际服务贸易交易会

爱尔兰驻华大使馆 2025-09-02


爱尔兰国家馆将再次亮相

中国国际服务贸易交易会（2025服贸会）。

Ireland will once again participate in the China International Fair for Trade in Services (CIFTIS 2025) with a dedicated Ireland National Pavilion.


中国一直是爱尔兰在亚洲最大的贸易伙伴。2023年，双边贸易总额达到367亿欧元；2024年，双边货物贸易额进一步增至219.1亿欧元，同比增长7%。爱中经贸关系保持良好发展态势。


China has long been Ireland’s largest trading partner in Asia. In 2023, total bilateral trade reached €36.7 billion; in 2024, bilateral goods trade further rose to €21.91 billion, a year-on-year increase of 7%. Ireland-China economic and trade relations continue to demonstrate robust growth momentum.


在服务贸易领域，服贸会始终是促进双边合作的重要平台。自2017年首次参展以来，爱尔兰已成为服贸会的常驻国家。2021年，爱尔兰以主宾国身份亮相服贸会，获得广泛好评，并荣获当届“最佳展台”奖。2025年，爱尔兰将再次设立国家馆。届时，爱尔兰驻华使馆将携手多家爱尔兰国家机构，从医疗科技、高等教育、优质农产品与食品、投资机遇、旅游资源及独特文化体验等多个维度，全方位展示爱尔兰的国家形象和产业实力，向参展嘉宾和观众呈现“翡翠之岛”的独特魅力。

In the field of trade in services, CIFTIS has consistently served as an important platform for promoting cooperation between the two countries. Since Ireland’s first participation in 2017, it has become a regular presence at CIFTIS. In 2021, Ireland participated as the Country of Honour, earning wide recognition and winning the “Best Pavilion” award. In 2025, Ireland will once again have a National Pavilion. The Embassy of Ireland in China, together with a number of Irish state agencies, will present a comprehensive showcase of Ireland’s national image and industrial strengths - from healthcare and med-tech innovation, higher education, world-class agri-food, investment opportunities, and tourism attractions, to unique cultural experiences - highlighting to visitors the distinctive charm of the Emerald Isle.

爱尔兰高等教育

Ireland’s Higher Education


爱尔兰的教育是全球最优质的教育之一，自中世纪以来一直以其高质量和可及性而闻名。卓越的教育传统在世界各地得到广泛认可。爱尔兰的高等教育体系涵盖了各种学科，如软件和信息工程、金融、医学、生物学等等。爱尔兰是一个英语的国家，有着安全友好的学习和生活环境。爱尔兰支持创业创新，持学习签证毕业后可继续在爱工作两年，这吸引了来自世界各地的人才。爱尔兰国立科克大学和OnCampus Ireland两家机构将参展本届服贸会，期待探索更多国际教育合作机遇。


Education in Ireland is among the best in the world and has been renowned for its high quality and accessibility since the Middle Ages. The tradition of excellence in education is widely recognized around the world. Ireland's higher education system includes ICT, engineering, finance, medicine, biotechnology,  to name a few. It is an English-speaking country with a safe and friendly study and living environment. There is a focus on entrepreneurship there, and the study visa supports 2 years of work time post-graduation. This has attracted talent from all over the world. University College Cork and OnCampus Ireland will participate in this year’s CIFTIS, looking forward to exploring further opportunities for international educational cooperation.



先进的医疗科技 

Advanced Medical Technology


爱尔兰独特的协作生态系统孕育了全球最具创新性、集成化和国际化的医疗技术中心之一。大学科研、政府支持的研发中心以及商业合作正不断推动爱尔兰医疗科技创新。经过40余年的发展，爱尔兰已积累了深厚的监管和合规专业知识，现已成为欧洲第二大医疗科技产品出口国，并在医疗科技人均就业方面位居欧洲首位。本届展会上，多家爱尔兰医疗科技企业将亮相国家馆，集中展示其在医疗技术领域的卓越实力。其中，ORIEL Seasalt Limited还将在服贸会期间举行成果发布仪式，宣告其爱尔兰深海离子镁产品在亚洲市场的正式落地与首次亮相。


Ireland’s uniquely collaborative ecosystem has fostered one of the world’s most innovative, integrated, and globalized MedTech hubs. University research, government-supported R&D centers, and business collaboration are continuously driving MedTech innovation in Ireland. With over 40 years of accumulated regulatory and compliance expertise, Ireland is now the second-largest exporter of MedTech products in Europe and the leading employer of MedTech professionals in Europe on a per capita basis. At this year’s exhibition, a number of Irish MedTech companies will be present in the pavilion to showcase their capabilities and excellence in the medical technology sector. Among them, ORIEL Seasalt Limited will host a launch event during CIFTIS to announce the official debut of its deep-sea ionic magnesium product in the Asian market.



中欧地理标志认证产品-

优质的爱尔兰食品和饮品

China-EU Geographical Indication Product-High Class Food and Drink


农业是爱尔兰最重要和知名的产业，农副产品出口到全球180多个国家和地区，在对外贸易中发挥着重要作用。爱尔兰生产的食品85%都用于出口， 目前，中国是爱尔兰五大食品出口市场之一。步入后疫情时代，中国消费者的消费观念发生了转变，他们更加追求健康的生活方式和生活理念，对食品的溯源、品质及安全也更加重视——而天然的原料、优异的产品品质和严格的食品安全管控，正是爱尔兰整个农业板块的优势，也是爱尔兰长期以来对传统农业进行持续科技创新、同时在环保及可持续发展领域不断加码的成果。此次爱尔兰国家馆将为大家展示有代表性的爱尔兰乳制品，以及两款中欧互认地理标识认证产品—爱尔兰威士忌和爱尔兰奶油利口酒。


Agriculture/Food is the most important indigenous industry in Ireland with 85% of Ireland's food products exported to more than 180 countries and regions around the world, acting as a pillar of the country's overall foreign trade. China is one of Ireland's top-five food export markets. Entering the post-pandemic era, we see obvious changes in Chinese consumers' consumption habits, they are more in pursuit of a healthy lifestyle and raising expectations on traceability, quality, and safety of food. Natural raw materials, excellent product quality, and strict food safety control are the advantage of the agri-food sector in Ireland and that is also a result of Ireland's long-term continuous scientific and technological innovation, as well as its continuous efforts in environmental protection and sustainable development. This year the Ireland National Pavilion will  showcase representative dairy products and  two Geographical Indication products: Irish whiskey and cream liqueur.



爱尔兰民族音乐展示

Irish Folk Music Performance


爱尔兰音乐有鲜明的特点。节奏，或长或欢快，总是传达丰富而真诚的情感，充满了坚忍的力量和淡淡的悲伤。演奏时使用的乐器并不多，传统乐队通常会使用爱尔兰小提琴(Fidil或veidhlin)、木笛(Fliuit或Feadog mhor)或哨子(Feadog stain)、手风琴和菩提兰。在聆听传统音乐时，观众往往能感受到爱尔兰独特的吸引力: 高山的绿色，风在脸上，海浪拍打着岩石，溪流在山林中跳跃着轻快的音调。爱尔兰民族强大、浪漫的一面也体现在所有这些美好的想象中。在现代，许多古典管弦乐器也出现在爱尔兰音乐中，甚至原声吉他也经常被使用。用哨子和风笛演奏的著名电影《泰坦尼克号》原声带感动了无数人。


Irish music has distinct characteristics. Rhythms, either long or cheerful, always convey rich and sincere emotions, and are full of stoic power and light sadness. Not many kinds of instruments are used when paying, a traditional band usually incorporate an Irish Fiddle (Fidil or veidhlin) , a wooded flute (Fliuit or Feadóg mhór) or whistle (Feadog stain) an accordion and a Bodhrán. When listening to the traditional music, the audience can often feel the unique attractiveness of Ireland: the lush green landscapes, the sea breeze, the waves crashing against the shore, the stream flowing through the mountain forest. The strong, romantic side of the Irish nation is revealed in this beautiful imagery. In modern times, many classical orchestral instruments also appear in Irish music, even acoustic guitars are often used. The famous soundtrack of the movie Titanic played by whistles and bagpipes have touched millions of people.
