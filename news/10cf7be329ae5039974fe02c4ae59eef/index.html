广东斑马受邀出席担任2022年广交会设计创新奖终评评审

斑马工业设计 2022-08-31

点击蓝字 关注我们


8月23-24日，2022年广交会设计创新奖（简称CF奖）终评评审在广交会展馆落下帷幕。广东斑马工业设计有限公司首席设计总监Nicola Bariol先生受邀出席担任其评审，着重输出设计趋势、用户体验、产业方向等专业意见。

From 23 August-24,August, the final evaluation of the 2022 Canton Fair Design Innovation Award (CF Award for short) came to the end at the Canton Fair Complex. Mr. Nicola Bariol, chief design director of Guangdong Zebra Industrial Design Co., Ltd., was invited to attend the evebt as a reviewer, focusing on outputting professional opinions on design trends, user experience, and industry directions.


据悉，CF奖由中国进出口商品交易会（广交会）于2013年创办，以“树立品质标杆，引导设计创新”为宗旨，甄选兼具设计和商业价值的前沿精品。数据显示，本年度共有来自1074家单位的2040件产品参评，企业和产品数量均创历史新高。

It is reported that the CF Award was founded by the China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair) in 2013. With the purpose of "setting a quality benchmark and guiding design innovation", it selects advanced products with both design and commercial value. Data shows that  a total of 2,040 products from 1,074 units participated in the evaluation this year and both the number of the companies and products have reached to the highest point since 2013.


此次CF奖的终评共有9位评委，其中包括享誉中外设计界的湖南大学设计艺术学院教授、博士生导师何人可教授、世界设计组织（WDO）前主席Srini R. Srinivasan教授、南方科技大学创新创意设计学院教授Christine M. Herr、杭州工业设计协会会长周立钢、Lplus Design品牌首席设计师李凤朗、广东斑马工业设计首席设计总监Nicola Bariol等，以创新性、功能性、品质、美感性、环保性为标准，通过直观触摸、深入观察、实际应用、经济及行业发展模式解构等，甄选出兼具设计审美、品质和市场认可度的获奖产品。

There are 9 judges in the final evaluation of the CF Award, including Professor He Renke, a professor and doctoral tutor of Hunan University School of Design and Art, who is well-known in the design circles at home and abroad, Professor Srini R. Srinivasan, former chairman of the World Design Organization (WDO), and Southern University of Science and Technology. Professor Christine M. Herr of the School of Innovation and Creative Design, Zhou Ligang, President of Hangzhou Industrial Design Association, Li Fenglang, Chief Designer of Lplus Design, Nicola Bariol, Chief Design Director of Guangdong Zebra Industrial Design, etc. With innovation, functionality, quality, beauty, environmental protection as the standard, through intuitive touch, in-depth observation, practical application, economic and industry development mode deconstruction, etc., to select the award-winning products with design aesthetics, quality and market recognition.
