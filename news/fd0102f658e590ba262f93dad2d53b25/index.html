我会携会员企业代表参加2025中韩智能制造洽谈会

深圳市汽车电子行业协会 2025-10-30

点击蓝字↑ 关注我们

SZAEIA Participates with Member Company Representatives in the 2025 China-Korea Intelligent Manufacturing Conference

为促进中韩两国在智能制造领域的协作与发展，应大韩贸易投资振兴公社深圳贸易馆的邀请，2025年10月29日，深圳市汽车电子行业协会携比亚迪、航盛电子等多家会员企业代表，共同参与了2025中韩智能制造及机械设备类企业贸易洽谈会。

To advance collaboration and development in smart manufacturing between China and South Korea, the Shenzhen Automotive Electronics Industry Association, along with representatives from member companies including BYD and Hangsheng Electronics, participated in the 2025 China-South Korea Smart Manufacturing and Machinery Trade Fair on October 29,2025. The event was organized by the Shenzhen Trade Pavilion of the Korea Trade-Investment Promotion Agency (KOTRA).

会场内气氛热烈而有序。在高效的一对一洽谈环节中，中韩企业代表们围绕技术优势与市场需求进行了深入交流，积极探寻潜在的合作契机。

The atmosphere in the meeting room was warm and orderly. In the efficient one-on-one negotiation session, representatives of Chinese and Korean enterprises had in-depth exchanges on technological advantages and market demand, and actively explored potential cooperation opportunities.

协会代表应邀与韩国三星精密株式会社会谈，双方进行了务实且富有建设性的沟通，重点探讨了在汽车电子领域潜在的合作空间。三星精密代表方对协会主办的IAEIS国际汽车电子产业峰会表现出浓厚兴趣，并表达了积极的参与意愿。

The association's representative was invited to hold talks with Samsung Precision Co., Ltd. The two sides engaged in pragmatic and constructive discussions, focusing on potential collaboration opportunities in automotive electronics. Samsung Precision's representatives expressed strong interest in the IAEIS-International Automotive Electronics Industry Summit hosted by the association and demonstrated willingness to participate actively.

本次活动不仅是一场高效的商贸对接，更是一次友好的深入交流。与会双方企业代表均表示本次对接会收获颇丰，并期待以此次会议为起点，开展后续交流，共同开拓更为广阔的国际市场。深圳市汽车电子行业协会将继续构建中外产业交流的坚实平台，积极推动与他国汽车电子及相关零部件企业开展互访交流，拓展多层次、多形式的国际合作，共谋产业发展新篇章。

This event was not only an efficient business matchmaking session but also a friendly in-depth exchange. Representatives from both participating companies expressed that the matchmaking meeting yielded substantial benefits, and they are looking forward to using this meeting as a starting point to initiate follow-up discussions and jointly explore broader international markets. The Shenzhen Automotive Electronics Industry Association will continue to build a robust platform for cross-border industrial exchanges, actively promote reciprocal visits and exchanges with automotive electronics and related component enterprises worldwide, expand multi-level and multi-form international cooperation, and jointly chart a new chapter in industrial development.
