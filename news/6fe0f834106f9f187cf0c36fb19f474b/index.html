GTT参展2022中国国际服务贸易交易会

中国国际LNG峰会暨展览 2022-09-07

GTT ATTENDING CONFERENCE OF 2022 CHINA INTERNATIONAL FAIR FOR TRADE IN SERVICES(CIFTIS)


@北京，中国 – 2022年9月

@ BEIJING, CHINA – SEPTEMBER 2022

中国国际服务贸易交易会（简称服贸会）由中华人民共和国商务部和北京市人民政府主办。是国际服务贸易领域传播理念、衔接供需、共享商机、共促发展的重要平台，现已发展成为全球服务贸易领域规模最大的综合性展会和中国服务贸易领域的龙头展会，是国家对外开放的“金字招牌”。

China International Fair for Trade in Services (CIFTIS) Conference is an influential event hosted by China Ministry of Commerce and Beijing Municipality. The event is as an important platform for international trade in services to spread ideas, link supply and demand, share business opportunities, and jointly promote development, has grown into the largest comprehensive exhibition in the field of global trade in services and the leading fair for trade in services in China.

服贸会开幕当天，中国第一副总理韩正先生驻足参观了GTT展台。GTT 中国为韩正副总理介绍了GTT近年来为中船集团在LNG运输船领域和为北京燃气集团在陆上LNG储存系统领域做出的重大贡献。GTT中国向副总理汇报：北京燃气天津LNG接收站采用GTT薄膜技术建造8座超大薄膜型全容罐，是拥有全球单体容量最大且薄膜型全容罐数量最多的超级工程。该项目是中法先进低碳创新技术合作的典范。中央电视台（CCTV）和中国国际电视台（CGTN）做了报道。

On event opening day, First Vice Premier of China Mr. HAN, Zheng visited GTT booth. GTT China introduced GTT’s significant contributions made to CSSC Group in LNG carrier new-building business and Beijing Gas Group in onshore LNG storage system in recent years. GTT China highlighted that the implementation of GTT membrane technology for Beijing Gas Tianjin LNG Terminal is a role model of China-France cooperation in the area to find low-carbon state-of-art technology together. The news has been widely broadcasted on CCTV and CGTN.
