倒计时10天!第33届华交会最全攻略强势来袭,助您快速入场、优先掌握商机 !

AustChamShanghai 2025-02-19

中国外贸新春第一展——第33届华交会（华东进出口商品交易会）即将于2025年3月1日至4日在上海新国际博览中心举行！

The first major foreign trade exhibition of the year in China—the 33rd East China Fair (ECF)—is set to take place from March 1st to 4th, 2025, at the Shanghai New International Expo Centre (SNIEC)!

展会始终坚持立足华东、辐射全国、面向世界的办展目标，助力中国出口企业开拓海外市场，助力中国企业打造品牌、培育创新意识，助力长三角一体化发展，更是国际市场的晴雨表和我国进出口走势的风向标。

The fair has always adhered to its mission of rooting itself in East China, radiating across the nation, and facing the world, helping Chinese export enterprises expand into overseas markets, fostering brand building and innovation awareness among Chinese companies, and promoting the integrated development of the Yangtze River Delta. It serves as a barometer for the international market and a vane for China's import and export trends.
上届华交会（第32届）汇聚境内外共计3422家参展企业。吸引了来自111个国家和地区的采购商，到会境内外采购商超过4万人，其中包括近100名澳洲企业相关人士，展会意向成交额达22.1亿美元。
The 32nd ECF（the last edition） attracted over 40,000 domestic and foreign buyers from 111 countries and regions (including nearly 100 Australian Buyers), and achieved $2.21 billion in total transactions. 

重要信息全知晓！为方便采购商及专业观众入场参观、交流对接，特别给大家准备了一份观展指南，祝您畅行无阻、先人一步~预祝各位逛展愉快。
To ensure a smooth and efficient experience for buyers and professional visitors, we’ve prepared a comprehensive exhibition guide to help you navigate the event with ease and stay ahead of the competition. Wishing you a productive and enjoyable visit!

【场馆附近交通指引及接驳车安排

Transportation Guide & Shuttle Arrangements】

一、交通指引 Transportation Guide

地铁 Metro：
1）搭乘7号线至花木路站5号口从2号入口大厅入场，可搭乘馆内接驳车。

Line 7: Get off at Huamu Road Station (Exit 5) and enter through Entrance Hall 2. Shuttle services are available inside the venue.

2）搭乘2号线至龙阳路站，步行约10分钟至1号入口大厅入场（近E1馆）。

Line 2: Get off at Longyang Road Station and walk approximately 10 minutes to Entrance Hall 1 (near Hall E1).


自驾Self-driving：
1）SNIEC位于龙阳路、罗山路交汇处，易于通行。

SNIEC is conveniently located at the intersection of Longyang Road and Luoshan Road.
2）从市中心横跨南浦大桥、杨浦大桥可直达。

Easily accessible from downtown Shanghai via Nanpu Bridge or Yangpu Bridge.


可停在P4、P5、P6停车场（每小时8元，单日64元封顶；费用供参考，具体以展馆为准）从1号或2号入口大厅入场。

Parking is available at P4, P5, and P6 parking lots (¥8/hour, ¥64/day max; rates are for reference only, subject to venue regulations). Enter through Entrance Hall 1 or 2.

二、短驳车安排 Shuttle Services

*为方便观众参观，组委会安排了馆内短驳车，具体时间安排请参考现场接驳时刻表。


To facilitate visitor mobility, the organizing committee has arranged on-site shuttle buses. Please refer to the shuttle schedule at the venue for specific times.

本届华交会使用上海新国际博览中心10个展馆（E1-E5馆，W1-W5馆），展览总面积11.5万平方米，设立四大专业子展（服装服饰展、纺织面料展、家庭用品展、装饰礼品展）和两大专业展区（跨境电商展区、境外展区（包括澳大利亚））。展位数共计5143个，参展企业3250家，其中境外展商约360家。

Exhibition Highlights

  • Scale:

  • 10 exhibition halls (E1–E5, W1–W5)

  • Total exhibition area: 115,000 square meters

  • 5,143 booths

  • 3,250 exhibitors, including about 360 overseas exhibitors

  • Exhibition Categories:

  • Four professional exhibitions:

1. Garments

2. Textiles

3. Home Products

4. Gifts

  • Two professional exhibition areas:

1. Overseas Products (Including Australia)

2. Cross-border E-commerce


本届华交会除展览外，还将于3月1日举办开幕式和跨境电商高层论坛。开幕式将以“识变应变，拓展外贸新空间”为主题，邀请各省市商务主管部门、境内外商协会、行业代表、资深专家学者，围绕新时期规模经济与技术进步、数字化出海新赛道、中美贸易及关税政策变化等热点议题展开深度研讨，共同探讨新形势新格局下外贸发展的新机遇。跨境电商高层论坛则以“AI赋能：跨境电商创新发展新路径”为主题，聚焦上海跨境电商发展新思路，旨在推动人才、服务、金融等跨境电商要素集聚，探索“人工智能+跨境电商”融合新路径，为外贸高质量发展注入数智新动能。

In addition to the exhibition, the 33rd East China Fair will also feature an Opening Ceremony and a Cross-border E-commerce Summit on March 1st.

The Opening Ceremony, themed " Adapt to Changes and Expand New Horizons in Foreign Trade," will bring together representatives from provincial and municipal commerce departments, domestic and international trade associations, industry leaders, and renowned experts. They will engage in in-depth discussions on hot topics such as economies of scale and technological advancements, new digital export opportunities, and changes in U.S.-China trade and tariff policies, collectively exploring new opportunities for foreign trade development under the evolving global landscape.

The Cross-border E-commerce Summit, themed "AI Empowerment: New Pathways for Cross-border E-commerce Innovation," will focus on new strategies for the development of cross-border e-commerce in Shanghai. The summit aims to drive the convergence of talent, services, and financial resources in the cross-border e-commerce sector, while exploring the integration of artificial intelligence and cross-border e-commerce to inject digital intelligence into high-quality foreign trade growth.

精准对接，促进实效。本届华交会还将设置采购对接会专区，包括欧美专场、服装服饰专场、纺织面料专场、家庭用品专场、装饰礼品专场、日本专场和东南亚专场对接会，为采购商和参展商搭建交流平台。

Precise matching and promotion of practical results. The 33rd China Fair will also set up Business Matching Activities including sessions for Europe and America, Garments, Textiles, Home Products, Gifts, Japan and Southeast Asia, providing a communication platform for buyers and exhibitors.

华交会是中国服装、纺织、家居用品等领域举办规模最大、最具影响力的展览会之一，是为专业观众和买家提供观摩样品、洽谈合作的高效平台。向各地采购商展现优质新颖的高质量产品，为买家和生产商构建洽谈贸易和互通资讯的理想平台，解决选品难题，加速抢占市场。期待大家莅临参与，即刻行动锁定商机！

The East China Fair is one of the largest and most influential exhibitions in China for Garments, Textiles, Home Products, Gifts, and more. It serves as a highly efficient platform for professional buyers and visitors to explore samples, negotiate deals, and stay informed about the latest industry trends.

By showcasing high-quality, innovative products, the fair provides an ideal platform for buyers and manufacturers to connect, exchange information, and overcome product selection challenges, enabling them to accelerate market penetration.

Don’t miss this opportunity to explore new business prospects and stay ahead in the competitive market. We look forward to your participation—act now and secure your spot!
