第一轮会议通知


大会主题：AI赋能工业工程创新发展

2026华人学者工业工程国际年会（CSIE2026）暨第十六届全球华人工业工程与物流管理院系主任联席会（CIEDH2026）将于2026年8月7-9日在浙江省温州市温州大学召开。本次会议由中国机械工程学会、台湾工业工程学会和全球华人工业工程院系主任联席会主办，中国机械工程学会工业工程分会和温州大学机电工程学院承办。

CSIE&CIEDH 2026 will be held during August 7-9,2026 in Wenzhou, China. The conference will be hosted by Chinese Mechanical Engineering Society, Taiwan Industrial Engineers Institute, and Chinese Industrial Engineering Department Heads Association and organized by Chinese Industrial Engineering Institution, CMES, and Wenzhou University. 

第十六届全球华人工业工程与物流管理院系主任联席会旨在荟聚世界各地相关领域的专家、学者及行业精英，在大数据、人工智能等技术加速重塑工业体系的背景下，共同探讨人工智能如何推动工业工程创新发展。会议将聚焦行业变革带来的新机遇与新挑战，精准识别工业工程人才培养的新需求，分享最新研究成果与实践经验，推动我国工业工程学科的创新发展与国际交流。同时，会议将进一步促进工业工程在智能制造、绿色制造、智慧物流等关键领域的产学研深度融合，为产业升级和高质量发展提供强有力的技术支撑。

The CIEDH 2026 aims to gather experts, scholars, and industry elites from related fields worldwide to explore how AI can drive the development of Industrial Engineering in the context of big data reshaping industrial systems. The conference will focus on emerging opportunities and challenges brought by industrial transformation, accurately identify new requirements for talent cultivation in industrial engineering, share cutting-edge research achievements and practical experiences, and promote innovative development and international exchanges in China's industrial engineering discipline. Meanwhile, it will further facilitate deep integration of industry-academia-research collaboration in critical areas such as smart manufacturing, green manufacturing, and intelligent logistics, providing robust technological support for industrial upgrading and high-quality development.

华人学者工业工程国际年会是全球华人学者交流工业工程最新研究成果的重要学术平台，也是工业工程及物流管理领域极具影响力的国际会议。第十六届华人学者工业工程国际年会(2026年)将齐聚海内外华人专家学者，并诚邀国内数百所工业工程及物流管理院系的院长和系主任共襄盛会，共同推动新一代智能技术与工业工程的深度融合，为全球制造业和供应链管理的高效、智能、可持续发展贡献智慧与力量。

The CSIE serves as a vital academic platform for global Chinese scholars to exchange the latest research achievements in industrial engineering and remains a highly influential international event in the fields of industrial engineering and logistics management. In CSIE 2026, both overseas and domestic Chinese scholars will gather alongside deans and department heads from hundreds of industrial engineering and logistics management institutions across China. Participants will jointly advance the deep integration of next-generation intelligent technologies with industrial engineering, contributing wisdom and strength to the efficient, intelligent, and sustainable development of global manufacturing and supply chain management.
