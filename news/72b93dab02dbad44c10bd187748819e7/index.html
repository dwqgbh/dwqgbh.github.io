展会时间：2026年4月14-17日

展会地点：广州琶洲国际会展区（广交会）-海珠国际会展中心

展会周期：春季4月14-17日、秋季10月14-17日(一年两届与广交会一期同期举办)

联合主办单位：大湾区汽车产业出口联盟、汽车出口共创联盟(筹)、广东易之行网络科技有限公司

回顾 2025 年 10 月举办的首届展会，其亮眼表现为行业树立了标杆。展会汇聚超 100 家汽车出口企业、100 余个主流汽车品牌，展示出口热销车型超 500 台，吸引了 30000 人次专业观众及海外客商到场，最终实现现场成交车辆 5000 + 台、成交金额超一亿美元的斐然战绩。不仅获得广州市商务局、市贸促会等政府部门的高度认可，更得到广东省人民政府门户网、南方日报等官方媒体的聚焦报道。一汽大众、广汽进出口、仰望汽车等行业领军企业的倾情参与，进一步印证了展会的行业号召力与平台价值。

本届展会将在传承首届成功经验的基础上，打造了五大核心亮点，为参展商与采购商搭建高效对接桥梁。展会创新性地将闭馆时间延长至晚间 20:30，与广交会闭馆时段无缝衔接。借助广交会 30 万 + 全球采购商资源，参展企业可实现品牌曝光度与市场渗透率的双重跃升，塑造全球化品牌形象。

在观众邀约方面，展会精准锁定决策层人员占比近 65%，覆盖全国及东南亚、中东、欧洲等汽车出口成熟与新兴市场。展会期间，除了专业的产品展示与贸易洽谈，还将举办汽车产业出口论坛，邀请政府主管部门负责人、行业领袖及专家共同探讨行业发展方向与技术贸易措施，为行业发展注入新思想、新动能。

2026（春季）第二届广州国际汽车产业出口展览会，以资源整合为纽带，以专业服务为支撑，以贸易成交为目标，正蓄势待发。诚邀全球汽车产业相关企业齐聚广州，共享广交会千亿级流量红利，共拓汽车出口海外新市场，共创行业发展新未来！

Looking back at the inaugural exhibition held in October 2025, its outstanding performance set a benchmark for the industry. The exhibition brought together over 100 automobile export companies and more than 100 mainstream car brands, showcasing over 500 popular export models. It attracted 30,000 professional visitors and overseas buyers, ultimately achieving impressive results with 5,000 vehicles sold on-site and transaction volumes exceeding 100 million USD. It not only received high recognition from government departments such as the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Commerce and the Municipal Council for the Promotion of Trade, but also garnered focused coverage from official media including the Guangdong Provincial Government Portal and Nanfang Daily. The enthusiastic participation of leading industry enterprises such as FAW-Volkswagen, GAC Import & Export, and Yangwang Automobile further confirmed the exhibition's industry influence and platform value.


Building on the successful experience of the first edition, this exhibition has created five core highlights to provide an efficient platform for exhibitors and buyers to connect. Innovatively, the exhibition will extend its closing time to 8:30 PM, seamlessly aligning with the closing hours of the Canton Fair. Leveraging the Canton Fair's network of 300,000 global buyers, participating companies can achieve a dual boost in brand exposure and market penetration, shaping a global brand image.


In terms of audience invitations, the exhibition precisely targets decision-makers, accounting for nearly 65% of attendees, covering mature and emerging automotive export markets across the country as well as Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Europe. During the exhibition, in addition to professional product displays and business negotiations, an Automotive Industry Export Forum will also be held, inviting government officials, industry leaders, and experts to jointly discuss industry development directions and technology trade measures, injecting new ideas and new momentum into the industry's growth.

中国汽车出口共创联盟是由国内汽车主机厂、改装厂、出口商等组成，聚焦推动中国汽车产业高质量出海，构建全球化汽车出口服务生态。下设10大专业委员会，覆盖乘用车、商用车、供应链等多个细分领域，涵盖3000余家企业及20000名个人成员，并提供供应链金融、车源供给、全球服务网络及数字化赋能服务，并通过展会和论坛，社交媒体等形式为全球企业提供中国汽车对外贸易全方位支持。


The China Automobile Export Co-Creation Alliance is composed of domestic automakers, modification shops, exporters, and other entities, focusing on promoting the high-quality global expansion of China's automotive industry and building a global automotive export service ecosystem. It has 10 specialized committees covering various segments such as passenger vehicles, commercial vehicles, and supply chains, encompassing over 3,000 enterprises and 20,000 individual members. The alliance provides supply chain finance, vehicle sourcing, global service networks, and digital empowerment services, and offers comprehensive support for global enterprises in China's automobile foreign trade through exhibitions, forums, social media, and other platforms.
