贵州航天电器股份有限公司 2025-05-12

展会介绍
Exhibition Profile

2025第十一届世界雷达博览会将于5月17日至19日在合肥滨湖国际会展中心举办，该博览会是由中央军委装备发展部批准指导的综合性国际雷达专业展会。本届博览会旨在搭建国际交流合作平台，展示全球雷达及相关电子信息领域的新技术、新产品新应用、新趋势、新动能，推动军民融合、技术创新和产业创新的融合发展。航天电器将携高速/光电/射频/低频/混装等类型连接器、全光链路元器件及组件、电子组件、机箱等产品精彩亮相。

The 11th World Radar Expo 2025 will be held at Hefei Binhu International Convention and Exhibition Center from May 17 to 19, 2025. Approved and guided by the Equipment Development Department of the Central Military Commission, this expo is a comprehensive international professional exhibition in the radar industry. It aims to establish a global platform for exchange and collaboration, showcasing new technologies, products, applications, trends, and driving forces in the global radar and related electronic information sectors. The event seeks to promote the integrated development of civil-military integration, technological innovation, and industrial innovation.GSA will showcase an impressive lineup of products at this exhibition, including high-speed, optoelectronic, RF, low-frequency, and hybrid connectors, all-optical link components and modules, electronic assemblies, and chassis systems.

Part of the highlights

聚焦雷达及电子信息领域热点趋势，推动雷达互联产品技术融合创新及产业化应用，此次展会航天电器将以“星瞰万象，智领未来”为主题，围绕25G光电、微波无源、全光链路、微封装及微系统、液冷散热五大电子领域雷达先进技术发展方向互联解决方案精彩亮相智能电子装备及产业链展区A236展位。航天电器诚邀产业链合作伙伴共赴盛会，共探技术发展新趋势，共享市场增长新机遇！

Focusing on the latest trends in radar and electronic information technologies, and promoting integrated innovation and industrial applications of radar-interconnected products, GSA will participate in this exhibition under the theme "Observing the Universe with Stars, Leading the Future with Intelligence". At Booth A236, we will showcase cutting-edge interconnect solutions across five key radar technology fields: 25G photoelectric technology, microwave passive components, all-optical links, micro-packaging and micro-systems, and liquid cooling solutions. GSA cordially invites partners across the industrial chain to join this grand event, explore new technological development trends, and share emerging market growth opportunities!
