-公司简介-

Introduce

我们公司是一家有超过25年经验的制造商。在2000年的时候，我们公司就已经有能力制造出优良产品，我们的目标是给顾客提供更优质的产品。为了达到这一目标我们一直专注于提升制造创新能力，在公司体系管理方面也从不松懈。

我们公司已连续六年被评为高新技术企业，公司的产品远销迪拜、土耳其、俄罗斯、非洲、印度、埃及、英国、意大利等国家，并且在韩国我们有自己的代理。多年来，我司给美国配套单位做代工，拥有ODM和OEM加工经验。

我们创立工厂的愿景是:为客户提供最可靠的产品，让客户更安心使用。为了实现这一目标，我们努力研究了许多行业安全标准，并专注于改善我们的生产，制度和管理。现在，我们是中国领先的汽车零部件和灯具供应商之一。

欢迎新老客户洽谈合作，共同发展！

Our company is a manufacturer with over 25 years of experience.

Back in the year 2000, we were already capable of producing high-quality products, and our goal was to provide customers with even better products.

To achieve this, we have consistently focused on enhancing manufacturing innovation capabilities and have never relaxed our efforts in corporate system management.

Our company has been rated as a high-tech enterprise for six consecutive years. Our products are exported to Dubai, Turkey, Russia, Africa, India, Egypt, the United Kingdom, Italy and other countries, and we have our own agents in South Korea.

For many years, our company has been doing OEM and ODM processing for supporting units in the United States.

Our vision for establishing the factory is to provide customers with the most reliable products, ensuring they use them with greater peace of mind.

To achieve this goal, we have diligently studied numerous industry safety standards and focused on improving our production, systems, and management.

Now, we are one of China's leading suppliers of automotive parts and lighting fixtures.

Welcome new and old customers to negotiate cooperation for common development!
