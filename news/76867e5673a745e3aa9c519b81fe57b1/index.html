第25届

中国国际工业博览会

2025.9.23-27



News


9月23-27日，第25届中国国际工业博览会在上海开幕，博览会上，四川代表团全方位展现四川省制造业智能化转型成效、新质生产力培育成果，助力四川工业提升国内外影响力与核心竞争力。


本次工博会，四川馆不仅展现了单点技术突破，更通过全链条的协同展示，让“四川智造”智能化、融合化的高质量发展新图景变得清晰可见。


From September 23rd to 27th, the 25th China International Industry Fair (CIIF) opened in Shanghai. At the fair, the Sichuan delegation comprehensively showcased the province's achievements in the intelligent transformation of manufacturing and the cultivation of new quality productive forces, helping to enhance Sichuan's industrial influence and core competitiveness both domestically and internationally.


At this year's CIIF, the Sichuan Pavilion not only demonstrated breakthroughs in individual technologies but also, through a coordinated display of the entire industrial chain, made the new high-quality development landscape of "Sichuan Intelligent Manufacturing"—characterized by intelligence and integration—clear and visible
