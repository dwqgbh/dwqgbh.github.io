展会概况

GENERAL SITUATION


在全球基建浪潮奔涌向前、新兴市场对工程机械需求持续攀升的大背景下，第27届Constructo国际建筑工业展览会如期举行。

Against the backdrop of a surging global infrastructure wave and continuously growing demand for construction machinery in emerging markets, the 27th Constructo International Construction Industry Exhibition was held as scheduled.

作为拉丁美洲规模最大、专业度最高的工程机械垂直展会，Constructo汇聚了来自世界各地的顶尖设备制造商、建筑商和行业专家，是拉美地区工程机械与建筑机械的顶级盛会。

As the largest and most specialized vertical exhibition for engineering machinery in Latin America, Constructo brings together top equipment manufacturers, contractors, and industry experts from around the world, making it a premier event for construction and engineering machinery in the region.


Show Focus | At the Forefront

现场回顾 | 直面前沿

在深度的交流中聚焦新突破


就在刚刚落幕的2026年墨西哥工程机械及建筑机械展览会（CONSTRUCTO）上，我们带着最新的制砖设备技术与全套自动化生产线解决方案，跨越太平洋，完成了一场收获颇丰的“拉美之约”，同时也为我们深耕全球新兴市场写下了浓墨重彩的一笔。

At the just‑concluded 2026 Mexico Construction and Engineering Machinery Exhibition (CONSTRUCTO), we crossed the Pacific Ocean with our latest blockmaking equipment technologies and complete automated production line solutions, fulfilling a highly fruitful "Latin American rendezvous" while also adding a brilliant chapter to our deep cultivation of global emerging markets.




现场快闪 / REVIEW


技术驱动，品质为本

近年来，面对高品质、高效率、自动化制砖装备的迫切需求，以及大规模工程与个性化定制之间的现实矛盾——

站在本次展会的新起点上，以“更快捷、更智能、更耐久”为使命，唯暻机械持续打磨优化产品性能、完善全球定制化服务网络，把更优质的制砖装备、更完善的整线解决方案带给全球更多地区的客户。

让每一次的机遇，都是我们践行使命的底气。

In recent years, facing the urgent demand for high-quality, high-efficiency, automated brickmaking equipment, as well as the real-world contradiction between large-scale projects and customized production —

Standing at this new starting point of the exhibition, driven by the mission of "Faster, Smarter, More Durable", VINKING Machinery continues to refine and optimize product performance and enhance its global customized service network. We are committed to bringing better brickmaking equipment and more complete turnkey solutions to customers in more regions around the world.

Every opportunity we seize gives us the confidence to live up to this mission.



不止于展会，更在于连接

The energy at our booth went far beyond the roar of machinery. Through intensive conversations, we truly felt the pulse of the Latin American market. Each heavy vote of trust has further strengthened our vision.


Beyond the exhibition — 

it's about connection.

三天展会虽已结束，但我们携手共进的旅程才刚刚启航，衷心感激每一位莅临展位的新老客户。我们将带着市场的期许，持续精进技术、优化服务，为全球用户提供更专业的设备支持与服务体验。
