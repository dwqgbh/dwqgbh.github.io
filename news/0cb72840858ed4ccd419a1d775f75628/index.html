天阳油墨 2026-04-03

2026 年工业标识变革：从单一喷码到智能追溯的油墨进化论

在工业4.0 浪潮下，传统 “喷码即着色” 的认知已被彻底颠覆，油墨正从单一标识工具，进化为承载可追溯、防伪、智能交互的核心载体。上海天阳作为日立油墨官方代理，正携手日立技术，推动这场标识领域的深度变革。

过去，油墨仅用于打印生产日期、批次号，信息孤立且易篡改；如今，日立功能性油墨搭载微缩编码与光学防伪因子，配合智能喷码系统，可将产品全生命周期数据嵌入标识中—— 从原料采购、生产加工到物流配送、终端消费，每一步都能通过扫码精准溯源，实现 “一物一码” 的全链路可视化管理。

在防伪层面，日立油墨的特殊光敏、温变特性，让造假者无从下手：仅在特定光源下显现的隐形码，或随温度变化的变色标识，成为产品真伪的“数字身份证”，有效打击窜货与假冒行为，守护品牌价值。

同时，智能油墨的高稳定性与兼容性，使其能无缝对接工业物联网设备，为MES、ERP 等系统提供实时数据支撑，助力企业实现生产效率提升与质量管控升级。从 “被动标识” 到 “主动交互”，油墨已成为工业数字化转型的关键基础设施。

上海天阳将持续以日立尖端技术为依托，推动油墨从传统着色材料向智能功能载体演进，为中国制造业的智能化、透明化发展筑牢标识根基。

2026 Industrial Marking Evolution: The Ink Revolution from Simple Coding to Intelligent Traceability

Amid the wave of Industry 4.0, the traditional perception that "coding is merely coloring" has been completely upended. Ink is evolving from a single marking tool into a core carrier supporting traceability, anti-counterfeiting and intelligent interaction. As the official agent for Hitachi inks, Shanghai Tianyang is joining hands with Hitachi’s technologies to drive this in-depth transformation in the marking industry.

In the past, inks were only used to print production dates and batch numbers, with isolated and easily tampered information. Today, Hitachi functional inks are embedded with micro-codes and optical anti-counterfeiting elements. Paired with intelligent coding systems, they integrate full lifecycle product data into markings—from raw material procurement, production and processing to logistics distribution and end-user consumption. Every step can be accurately traced via scanning, enabling full-chain visual management under the "one item, one code" model.

In terms of anti-counterfeiting, the special photosensitive and thermochromic properties of Hitachi inks leave counterfeiters with no room for manipulation. Invisible codes visible only under specific light sources, or color-changing markings that react to temperature changes, serve as a "digital ID" for product authenticity, effectively curbing product diversion and counterfeiting to safeguard brand value.

Meanwhile, the high stability and compatibility of intelligent inks allow seamless connection with industrial IoT devices, providing real-time data support for MES, ERP and other systems, helping enterprises improve production efficiency and upgrade quality control. From "passive marking" to "active interaction", ink has become critical infrastructure for industrial digital transformation.

Leveraging Hitachi’s cutting-edge technologies, Shanghai Tianyang will continue to promote the evolution of ink from a traditional coloring material to an intelligent functional carrier, laying a solid marking foundation for the intelligent and transparent development of China’s manufacturing industry.
