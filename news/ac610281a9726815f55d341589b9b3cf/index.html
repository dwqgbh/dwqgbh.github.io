第31届华南国际印刷工业展览会暨2025中国国际标签印刷技术展览会

三信国际集团 SANSIN GROUP 2025-03-07

2025年3月4日-6日，由中国对外贸易中心集团有限公司和雅式展览服务有限公司共同主办的第31届华南国际印刷工业展览会暨2025中国国际标签印刷技术展览会（简称“华南国际印刷展/标签展”或“华南展”）在广州·广交会展馆A区隆重开幕。
On March 4-6, 2025, the 31st South China International Printing Industry Exhibition and 2025 China International Label Printing Technology Exhibition (hereinafter referred to as "South China International Printing Exhibition/Label Exhibition" or "South China Exhibition"), co-sponsored by China Foreign Trade Center Group Co., LTD and Ya-style Exhibition Service Co., LTD., was grand opened in Area A of Guangzhou Canton Fair Exhibition Hall.
作为开年首场印包行业盛会，华南展携手同期举办的中国国际包装展及包装制品展，四展联动，汇聚参展商2100+家，展出面积达15万平方米，为行业呈现印刷、标签、包装前沿解决方案。展会首日，专业观众络绎不绝，人潮涌动，盛况空前。

As the first printing bag industry event in the opening year, South China Exhibition joined hands with the China International Packaging Exhibition and packaging Products Exhibition held in the same period, the four exhibition linkage, bringing together 2,100 + exhibitors, with an exhibition area of 150,000 square meters, presenting printing, labeling and packaging cutting-edge solutions for the industry.

On the first day of the exhibition, the professional audience was in an endless stream, the crowd was surging, and the grand situation was unprecedented.

三信国际集团组织了泰国、越南、马来西亚、印尼、菲律宾采购团200余人参加此次展会。
Sansin International Group organized a purchasing team of more than 200 people from Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Indonesia and the Philippines to participate in the exhibition.
印刷包装行业瞬息万变，中国的智能化技术抢占了市场先机，三信采购团深深被中国制造的智能化所吸引，驻足参观智能化设备运作的同时，也纷纷感叹。
同时我们一大部分的意向客户也在展会现场签单！三信国际集团“签单送手机”活动在展会现场也是如火如荼进行...
The printing and packaging industry is changing rapidly, China's intelligent technology has seized the market opportunity, Sansin purchasing team is deeply attracted by the intelligent manufacturing in China, stop to visit the operation of intelligent equipment, but also lamented.
At the same time, a large number of our intended customers also signed orders at the exhibition site! Sansin International Group's "signing and sending mobile phones" activity is also hot at the exhibition site...
3月4日晚，青岛润之诚展览公司与三信国际集团举办的”珠江夜游“晚宴在鹅潭印象号上盛大举行。此次晚宴行业协会、技术专家、行业媒体和国内外采购商等共同出席，度过了一场“聚势东方，时代同行”的邮轮盛宴。

On the evening of March 4, the "Pearl River Night Tour" dinner held by Qingdao Runzhicheng Exhibition Company and Sansin International Group was held grandly on the etan Impression ship.

The dinner was attended by industry associations, technical experts, industry media and domestic and foreign buyers, which opened a cruise feast of "gathering power in the East, travelling with The Times".

在珠江的璀璨夜色中，游轮缓缓启航，标志着“聚势东方·时代同行”活动正式拉开序幕。而此次珠江游轮活动，正是基于双方共同的愿景——搭建一个国际化、专业化的平台，让行业精英们能够相聚一堂，共谋发展。
In the bright night of the Pearl River, the cruise ship slowly set sail, marking the official opening of the activity of "gathering power in the East and walking with The Times". The Pearl River Cruise event is based on the common vision of the two sides - to build an international and professional platform, so that the industry elite can meet and seek common development.
