2025印尼车展回顾:中国新能源车型的盛宴(1)

星火燎南洋 2025-08-13

五菱与名爵在2025年印尼国际汽车展推出全新创新车型

New Innovations from Wuling & MG at GIIAS 2025


2025年7月23日，Tangerang —— GIIAS（印尼国际汽车展）2025 再次成为创新车辆发布的主要舞台。今年，五菱和名爵带来了重磅惊喜：全新车款融合先进技术、高效能以及现代风格，专为印尼消费者打造。


Tangerang, 23 July 2025 — GIIAS (Gaikindo Indonesia International Auto Show) 2025 once again becomes the main stage for the launch of innovative vehicles, and this year, Wuling and MG brought a major surprise: new variants that combine advanced technology, efficiency, and modern style for Indonesian consumers.


五菱与名爵在2025年GIIAS车展上凭借各自最新的电动车型成功吸引了关注——五菱大胆推出未来感十足的纯电车型，名爵则带来动感且科技感十足的新款，充分展现了两大品牌对满足印尼日益增长的智能与高效出行需求的持续创新承诺。


Wuling and MG drew attention at GIIAS 2025 with their latest electric models—Wuling’s bold, futuristic EV and MG’s sporty, tech-forward variant—highlighting both brands’ commitment to innovation for Indonesia’s growing demand for smart, efficient mobility.


背景


“作为五菱进入印尼八周年庆祝活动的一部分，我们自豪地推出最新的MPV车型——五菱Cortez Darion。秉持‘在印尼，为您打造’的理念，‘Darion’这一名称灵感来源于‘来自印尼’的短语。此外，‘Darion’在希腊语中还有‘礼物’的含义。而‘Cortez’则源自西班牙语单词‘Corteous’，意为威严、自信。自2018年以来，‘Cortez’这一名称一直与五菱的中型MPV密切相关。” 五菱汽车市场运营总监Ricky Christian如是表示。


"As part of the celebration of Wuling’s 8th anniversary in Indonesia, we are proudly unveiling our latest MPV, the Wuling Cortez Darion. In line with the spirit of ‘Built in Indonesia for You,’ the name Darion is inspired by the phrase ‘From Indonesia.’ Additionally, ‘Darion’ also means ‘Gift’ in Greek. Meanwhile, the name Cortez is derived from the Spanish word ‘Corteous,’ which means authoritative. The name Cortez has been associated with Wuling’s medium MPV since 2018.” explained Ricky Christian, Marketing Operation Director of Wuling Motors.

五菱 Cortez Darion 的推出源于印尼深厚的家庭价值观，作为一款采用可再生能源驱动的 MPV，专为家庭出行打造，承载着每段旅程的意义与故事。这款车型延续了五菱在 MPV 领域的传承，并通过电动化创新迈向新高度。


The Wuling Cortez Darion was inspired by Indonesia’s strong family values, offering a renewable energy-powered MPV designed for meaningful family journeys—continuing Wuling’s MPV legacy with next-level electrification.


五菱 Cortez Darion 拥有宽敞且豪华的车内空间，配备自动舒适滑动门与电动天窗等高端配置。该车型提供两种版本：纯电动（EV）车型搭载 150 千瓦电机，续航里程最高可达 540 公里；插电式混合动力（PHEV）车型则结合 1,500 cc 发动机与 145 千瓦电动马达，在纯电模式下续航达 125 公里，总续航超过 1,000 公里。两种版本皆以舒适性、高效能与先进驾驶技术为核心，为用户带来全方位的优质驾乘体验。


The Wuling Cortez Darion boasts a spacious and luxurious cabin, enhanced by features like Autocomfort Sliding Doors and an electric sunroof. It comes in two variants: a fully electric (EV) model with a 150 kW motor and a range of up to 540 km, and a plug-in hybrid (PHEV) version combining a 1,500 cc engine with a 145 kW electric motor, offering over 1,000 km in total range and 125 km in EV-only mode. Both variants focus on delivering comfort, efficiency, and advanced driving technology.


“印尼是名爵的重要市场之一，我们致力于推动出行创新，以支持国家汽车工业的发展。MG4 EV Max和全新Cyberster版本的发布，展现了我们将全球创新带入印尼、并贴近本地消费者需求的承诺。这不仅仅是一场新车发布，更是我们迈向更智能、更愉悦、更可持续出行未来的重要一步。”名爵印尼首席执行官Jason Huang表示。


“Indonesia is one of MG’s key markets, and we are committed to delivering mobility innovations to support the advancement of the national automotive industry. The launch of the MG4 EV Max and the new Cyberster variant reflects our commitment to bringing global innovations that are relevant to Indonesian consumers. This is not just a product launch, but a concrete step in our journey toward a smarter, more enjoyable, and sustainable mobility future,” said Jason Huang, Chief Executive Officer of MG Motor Indonesia.





MG4 EV Max

MG4 EV Max 配备了 64 千瓦时电池，续航里程最高可达 540 公里（NEDC 标准），搭载最大功率 203 马力的电动马达，带来迅捷的加速响应。支持直流快速充电和交流充电，配备三种模式的动能回收系统（KERS），并搭载名爵的 i-SMART 智能互联系统，实现远程监控与操控。该车型按照全球安全标准打造，配备多项先进驾驶辅助功能，包括 360 度全景影像、自适应巡航控制、碰撞预警等系统。V2L 外放电功能还可为外部设备供电，提供更多灵活性。


The MG4 EV Max features a 64 kWh battery offering up to 540 km of range (NEDC) and a 203 PS electric motor for responsive acceleration. It supports fast DC and AC charging, includes a 3-mode KERS for energy recovery, and offers MG’s i-SMART technology for remote control and monitoring. Built to global safety standards, it also provides advanced driver assistance features like a 360° camera, adaptive cruise control, and collision warning systems. The V2L feature adds flexibility by allowing the car to power external devices.



MG Cyberster

名爵还推出了MG Cyberster的新配色：珍珠米色车身搭配红色对比车顶。此版本纪念这款电动敞篷跑车在印尼上市一周年。Cyberster不仅外观惊艳，且能在约3.2秒内完成0–100公里/小时的加速，带来令人振奋的性能表现。


MG also introduced a new color option for the MG Cyberster: Beige Pearl with a contrasting red roof. This edition marks the one-year anniversary of the electric roadster’s launch in Indonesia. With 0–100 km/h acceleration in just around 3.2 seconds, the Cyberster not only delivers a striking appearance but also offers thrilling performance.


五菱与名爵亮相 2025 年 GIIAS 展会，充分展现了双方在推动印尼汽车产业转型方面的共同愿景。通过推出如 Cortez Darion 和 MG4 EV Max 等先进的电动与混合动力车型，以及智能科技和本地化创新，两大品牌不仅积极响应印尼市场对可持续交通工具日益增长的需求，也全力支持政府在电动化和碳减排方面的战略蓝图。这些举措明确传递出一个目标：为印尼消费者提供更清洁、更智能的出行解决方案，同时助力本土产业发展、技术转移以及国家绿色经济的长期投资。


The participation of Wuling and MG at GIIAS 2025 underscores their shared vision to actively contribute to the transformation of Indonesia’s automotive landscape. By introducing advanced electric and hybrid vehicles like the Cortez Darion and MG4 EV Max—alongside smart technologies and localized innovations—both brands are not only responding to the growing demand for sustainable transportation but also supporting the government’s roadmap for electrification and carbon reduction. These strategic efforts highlight a clear objective: to empower Indonesian consumers with cleaner, smarter mobility solutions while fostering local industry growth, technology transfer, and long-term investment in the nation's green economy.
