2025 China (Ruian) Auto Parts Exhibition Wraps Up Successfully!

赢龙winbtb 2025-04-07


3月26日-28日，2025中国（瑞安）国际汽摩配展览会在“中国汽摩配之都”瑞安市会展中心成功举办。本届展会由全国工商联汽车摩托车配件用品业商会主办，温州国际会议展览中心有限公司展览分公司、全联(温州)展览服务有限公司承办，瑞安市国际商会、瑞安市汽车摩托车配件行业协会协办。


From March 26 to 28, 2025 China(Rui'an) Int'l Automobile & Motorcycle Parts Exhibition was successfully held in Rui'an Convention and Exhibition Center, "the capital of China's automobile and motorcycle parts". This exhibition is sponsored by the China Federation of Industry and Commerce of Auto & Motorbike Parts Chamber of Commerce.It branch of Wenzhou International Convention and Exhibition Center Co., Ltd. and Alliance (Wenzhou) Exhibition Service Co., Ltd.,co-organized by Rui'an Automobile and Motorcycle Parts Industry Association and Rui'an International Chamber of Commerce.


（展会开幕仪式）

（Opening Ceremony of the Exhibition）


展会面积20000平方米，标准展位约600个，汇聚300余家优质供应商，展品涵盖40个系列、5000多个品种，重点聚焦汽车智能“三电”系统、智能驾驶辅助系统等。众多国内外汽车产业展商、采购商、专家学者齐聚瑞安，与中外专业买家开展对接。


The exhibition area of 20,000 square meters, about 600 standard booths, gathering more than 300 high-quality suppliers, exhibits covering 40 series, more than 5,000 varieties, focusing on automotive intelligent "three power" system, intelligent driving assistance system. Many domestic and foreign auto industry exhibitors, buyers, experts and scholars gathered in Rui'an to connect with domestic and foreign professional buyers.


（展会现场）

（Exhibition Venue）


赢龙会展通过即时通信、邮件营销、定向数字营销等手段，精准邀约40位国际专业采购商于3月27日到达现场，参加供采对接会。


Through instant messaging, email marketing, targeted digital marketing and other means, Winbtb accurately invited about 40 international professional buyers to arrive at the scene on March 27 to participate in the sourcing matchmaking meeting.


（客商们积极参与展会）

（Business visitors actively participate in the exhibition）


供应商们精心准备了全彩产品手册，并用中英双语详细标注。来自各国的采购商们也展现出高度的专业性，针对采购需求、质量标准等关键要素进行详细问询。


对接会如火如荼地展开，不少采购商都兴奋地表示这次展会收获颇丰，期待以后能和瑞安更多企业深化合作，共同扩展国际市场。


The suppliers carefully prepared full-color product manuals, and detailed annotations in both Chinese and English. Buyers from various countries also demonstrated a high degree of professionalism, conducting detailed inquiries on key elements such as procurement needs and quality standards.


The matchmaking meeting is in full swing, and many buyers are excited to say that the exhibition has been fruitful, and look forward to deepening cooperation with more enterprises of Rui'an in the future to jointly expand the international market.


（客商积极与供应商洽谈合作）

（Business visitors actively discuss collaboration with suppliers）


本次展会为汽摩配产业链上下游搭建了高质量的商贸合作平台。未来，瑞安将以此次展会为起点，通过“展会+论坛+对接”的立体化模式，加速技术、资本、人才等要素集聚，书写“以展兴产”的新篇章，打造“瑞安制造”新标杆，开辟国际化发展新路径。


This exhibition has built a high-quality business cooperation platform for the upstream and downstream of the auto and motorcycle parts industry chain. In the future, Rui'an will take this exhibition as a starting point, accelerate the gathering of technology, capital, talent and other elements through the three-dimensional model of "exhibition + forum + docking", write a new chapter of "production by exhibition", create a new benchmark of "Rui'an manufacturing", and open up a new path for international development.
