CIIF 2025 Pre-registration Guide

2025中國工博會預登記指南


CIIF 2025 Overview | 概覽









CIIF 2025 will be held at the National Exhibition and Convention Center (Shanghai) from September 23rd to 27th. As a window for promoting international cooperation and exporting Chinese solutions, it is expected to attract nearly 3,000 high-quality enterprises from 30 countries and regions around the world to participate. There are 9 Theme Shows under CIIF: Metalworking and CNC Machine Tool Show (MWCS), Industrial Automation Show (IAS), Robotics Show (RS), Energy Show (ES), Information & Communication Technology Show (ICTS), Future Transportation Industry Show (FTIS), Green Low-Carbon Show (GLCS),  Scientific & Technological Innovation Show (STIS), New Material Industry Show (NMIS).

第25屆中國國際工業博覽會將於9月23日至27日在國家會展中心(上海)隆重舉行。作爲推動國際合作、輸出中國方案的窗口，預計將吸引來自全球30個國家和地區的近3000家優質企業參展，設數控機牀與金屬加工展、工業自動化展、機器人展、智慧能源展、新一代信息技術與應用展、智行未來展、綠色低碳展、科技創新展和新材料產業展9大主穎展區和一批創新專區，凸顯工業體系全面性和產業配套的強大能力。


Adopting the "1+10+N" conference and event system, we will create 1 globally watched opening ceremony, 10 high-level industry summits and hundreds of major events in specific fields. Introduce the "1+3+Z" award system, featuring one CIIF Special Award to showcase excellence, three benchmark awards to set industry benchmarks, and Z Incubation and innovation awards to explore cutting-edge potential.

採用"1+10+N"會議活動體系，打造一場全球矚目的開幕式、十場高規格行業峯會及數百場細分領域重磅活動。推出“1+3+Z"獎項體系，設1項CIF特別大獎彰顯卓越，三大標杆獎項樹立行業典範，並設立Z個孵化與創新獎挖掘前沿潛力。
