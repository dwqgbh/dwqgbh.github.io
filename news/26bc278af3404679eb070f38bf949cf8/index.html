    Instead of 32 teams being divided into eight groups, this year will see 12 groups of four. The top two teams in each group, plus the eight best third-placed teams, will advance to the round of 32. The new format means the team that triumphs in the final on 19 July will have played eight games to be crowned champions, one more than at Qatar 2022, after the inclusion of an additional round of 32 knockout phase.
