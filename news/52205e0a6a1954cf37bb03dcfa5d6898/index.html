PIX-LINK is a high-tech enterprise that integrates research and development, production, sales, and service! The company's headquarters is located in Wanzhou District, Chongqing; There are two modern plants in Guanlan, Shenzhen and Wanzhou, Chongqing, with an annual output value of 300 million RMB.
