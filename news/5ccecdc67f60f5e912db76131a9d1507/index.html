启迪出海招募 | 直通进博会!启迪之星面向全球招募创新企业参展

启迪出海 2026-06-13



第九届中国国际进口博览会将于2026年11月在上海举办。本届进博会创新孵化专区将继续汇聚来自全球的创新企业、前沿技术与创业项目，为科技创新企业提供展示成果、链接资源、拓展合作的重要窗口。


自第四届进博会设立创新孵化专区以来，启迪之星与专区建立了长期合作关系。过去几年，启迪之星累计推荐60余家海内外科技创新企业亮相进博会，项目涵盖人工智能、智能制造、生物医药、新材料等多个前沿领域。



这背后，是启迪之星持续构建的全球创新网络，不断连接国际创新项目、产业资源、科研机构与市场场景，帮助科技企业在更广阔的创新生态中寻找合作机会。同时，依托自身孵化服务体系，启迪之星已孵化及服务两万余家企业，持续陪伴企业从创新展示走向市场连接。


2026年，启迪之星将继续作为进博会创新孵化专区合作伙伴，依托启迪全球创新网络，面向全球招募优质企业参展。我们期待帮助更多创新企业走进进博会，展示创新成果，链接产业资源，探索市场机遇；也期待更多海外创新企业通过进博会了解中国市场，寻找产业合作伙伴，共同开启创新发展的更多可能。


The 9th China International Import Expo will be held in Shanghai in November 2026. As part of CIIE, the Innovation Incubation Zone brings together startups, emerging technologies and high-potential ventures from around the world, creating a platform for companies to showcase their solutions, meet partners and explore opportunities in the Chinese market.


Since the zone was first launched at the 4th CIIE, TusStar has worked closely with the Innovation Incubation Zone as a long-term partner. In recent years, TusStar has introduced more than 60 technology companies from China and overseas to CIIE, covering sectors including artificial intelligence, intelligent manufacturing, biomedicine and new materials.


TusStar’s participation is supported by its global network of innovation platforms, industry partners, research institutions and market resources. Through its incubation services, TusStar has supported more than 20,000 companies, helping startups and growth-stage businesses move beyond visibility and build real market connections.


In 2026, TusStar will continue to partner with the CIIE Innovation Incubation Zone and invite companies from around the world to participate in CIIE.


进博蝉联“最佳组织奖”


作为进博会创新孵化专区长期合作伙伴，启迪之星已连续多年组织海内外科技企业参展，并连续荣获创新孵化专区“最佳组织奖”。


这些企业来自不同国家和地区，既有成长中的创业团队，也有在细分领域持续深耕的科技企业。对于许多企业而言，进博会不仅是一场展会，更是一次与全球创新资源交流碰撞的机会。在这里，创新成果被看见，合作机会被发现，新的连接不断产生。



As a long-term partner of the CIIE Innovation Incubation Zone, TusStar has supported technology companies from China and overseas in joining CIIE for several consecutive years. Its contribution has been recognized with The Best Organization Award in the Innovation Incubation Zone.


These companies represent different markets, growth stages and technology sectors, ranging from early-stage startups to specialized companies with deep industry expertise. For many exhibitors, CIIE is more than a trade fair. It is a gateway to visibility, partnership opportunities and new connections within China’s innovation ecosystem.


往届回顾


从展台亮相到资源对接，从媒体曝光到合作推进，进博会创新孵化专区的价值，已在往届参展企业的实践中得到验证。



进博会期间，启迪之星推荐企业拉普拉斯在展前便通过活动对接到富策信息，并建立合作关系。单次展期5天内，企业累计对接约300位有效观众及潜在合作方，并收集到50余份具有合作意向的反馈问卷。


启迪之星推荐企业可立点科技携家庭陪伴机器人亮相展区。凭借创新产品设计和真实应用场景，企业获得央视新闻、东方卫视等主流媒体关注，让更多人看见科技创新企业的探索与实践。


The CIIE Innovation Incubation Zone has become more than a showcase. For previous exhibitors, it has created real opportunities to meet potential customers, connect with partners, gain media exposure and explore follow-up cooperation.


Laplace AI Lab, a TusStar-recommended company, connected with FuCe Information through a pre-show event and began cooperation before the exhibition opened. During a five-day exhibition period, the company met around 300 qualified visitors and potential partners, and received more than 50 expressions of interest for potential cooperation.


Another TusStar-recommended company, ClickMobot, presented its home companion robot at the Innovation Incubation Zone. With its innovative product design and clear real-life use cases, ClickMobot attracted coverage from major Chinese media outlets including CCTV News and Dragon TV, expanding its market visibility and brand recognition in China.


数据展示


除了企业个案，进博会的传播声量和专区成长，也在持续放大创新企业的参展价值。第八届进博会总传播量超60亿次，全网相关信息量达246万余条；2025年全年进博会相关境外媒体报道信息超过4.5万条，触达近100个国家和地区的近12亿海外受众。


展期还会举办多种配套活动，包含了创新创业大赛、项目路演、论坛研讨等活动。海外科技型人才聚集，诸多首发新品和爆款产品亮相。



自第四届进博会设立创新孵化专区以来，专区规模实现倍数级增长：参展主体从71家企业拓展至第七届360个来自30多个国家和地区的创新项目，展示面积由超1000平方米扩大至超4000平方米。这一快速增长，也让专区成为全球创新企业展示成果、链接产业、进入中国市场的重要窗口。


2026年，启迪之星将继续依托进博会创新孵化专区，帮助更多创新企业在展示中获得关注，在交流中找到伙伴，在真实场景中探索新的增长可能。


Beyond individual company cases, CIIE’s strong media reach and the continued growth of the Innovation Incubation Zone have further amplified the value of participation. The 8th CIIE generated more than 6 billion impressions, with over 2.46 million online mentions. In 2025, CIIE-related overseas media coverage exceeded 45,000 reports, reaching audiences in nearly 100 countries and regions, with a potential overseas reach of almost 1.2 billion.


The exhibition will also feature a variety of supporting activities, including innovation and entrepreneurship competitions, project roadshows, forums and seminars. It will bring together overseas tech talents, and see the debut of many new products and bestsellers.


Since the Innovation Incubation Zone was launched at the 4th CIIE, its scale has expanded rapidly. The number of participating exhibitors and projects has grown from 71 exhibitors to 360 innovation projects from more than 30 countries and regions at the 7th CIIE, while the exhibition area has increased from over 1,000 square meters to more than 4,000 square meters. This growth has made the zone an important gateway for global innovators to showcase their solutions, connect with industry resources and explore opportunities in the Chinese market.


In 2026, TusStar will continue to support innovative companies through the CIIE Innovation Incubation Zone, helping them gain visibility, meet the right partners and explore new growth opportunities in real-world application scenarios.
