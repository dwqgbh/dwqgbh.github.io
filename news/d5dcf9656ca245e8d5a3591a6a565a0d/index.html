会议主题

本次会议以“双碳目标下韧性城市建设”为主题，对近年来新理念、新技术在重大基础设施、岩土与地下工程、海洋工程设计、施工、运维等阶段的研究和应用进展进行深入交流，为青年岩土工作者提供学习和交流平台。会议涵盖但不限于以下专题内容：

1、基础设施韧性设计与建造

2、岩土与地下工程智能建造、监测与预警

3、极端环境下海洋工程韧性建设与服役

4、极端气候下基础设施的安全保障与韧性提升

5、大湾区地下空间开发新进展与挑战



Forum Topics

The theme of this forum is "Resilient City Construction under Dual Carbon Goals." It facilitates in-depth exchanges on recent research and application advances in new concepts and technologies across the design, construction, operation, and maintenance phases of major infrastructure, geotechnical and underground engineering, and marine engineering. This event serves as a learning and communication platform for young geotechnical professionals. The forum will cover, but is not limited to, the following topics:

1. Resilient Design and Construction of Infrastructure

2. Smart Construction, Monitoring, and Early-Warning in Geotechnical and Underground Engineering

3. Resilient Construction and Service of Marine Engineering in Extreme Environments

4. Ensuring Infrastructure Safety and Enhancing Infrastructure Resilience under Extreme Climatic Conditions

5. New Advances and Challenges in Underground Space Development in the Greater Bay Area
