中国东盟博览会CAEXPO 2025-09-03

第22届中国—东盟博览会将于2025年9月17—21日在中国南宁盛大举办。本届盛会以“数智赋能发展，创新引领未来——以中国—东盟自贸区3.0版新机遇助建命运共同体”为主题，突出人工智能唱主角、企业为主体。在此期间，将举办超80场的各类投资贸易促进活动，其中由中国商务部、广西壮族自治区人民政府和中国工商银行共同举办的2025东盟—中日韩产业供应链对接大会因其“聚焦自贸区3.0版核心方向（数字经济、绿色经济、供应链互联互通）、整合AI技术与产业供应链场景应用、覆盖东盟10国及中日韩RCEP成员国超1500家产业链企业参与”等特色，自启动报名以来便成为本届东博会备受期待的重点活动，诚邀您报名参会：


The 22nd China–ASEAN Expo (CAEXPO) will take place in Nanning, Guangxi, from September 17 to 21, 2025. With the theme of “Digital Intelligence and Innovation Empower Development—Leveraging China-ASEAN FTA 3.0 New Opportunities For An Even Closer China-ASEAN Community With A Shared Future”, this CAEXPO will be a enterprises-centered event，highlighting AI and focusing on ASEAN. The 2025 ASEAN Plus Three Industrial and Supply Chains Partnering Conference, jointly organized by the Ministry of Commerce of China, the People's Government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, has become a highly anticipated key event of this CAEXPO since it was launched due to its focus on the core directions of the Free Trade Zone 3.0 version (digital economy, green economy, supply chain interconnection), integration of AI technology and industrial supply chain scenario applications, and coverage of over 1500 industrial chain enterprises from the 10 ASEAN countries and RCEP member countries.We sincerely invite you to register for the conference.



2025东盟—中日韩产业链供应链对接大会

供需对接邀请函

Invitation to the ASEAN Plus Three Industrial and Supply Chains Partnering Conference

为进一步促进东盟—中日韩经贸往来，加强区域产业链供应链联通，中国商务部、广西壮族自治区人民政府和中国工商银行将在第22届中国—东盟博览会期间于2025年9月18日（周四）上午9:00在广西南宁国际会展中心共同举办“2025年东盟—中日韩产业链供应链对接大会”。

To further promote economic and trade exchanges between ASEAN and the People's Republic of China, Japan and the Republic of Korea, and to strengthen the connectivity of regional industrial and supply chains in this region, the Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China (MOFCOM), the People’s Government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC) will jointly organize “ASEAN Plus Three Industrial and Supply Chains Partnering Conference” on the morning of September 18th (Thursday) 2025 during the 22nd China-ASEAN Expo at the Nanning International Convention & Exhibition Center, Guangxi, China.


大会现场将设置涵盖先进制造、清洁能源、绿色农业、健康生活、数字科技、智能汽车、跨境金融、供应链服务等八大对接板块，邀请东盟及中日韩的政府机构、知名企业及金融机构等代表出席，聚焦“加强区域协同，推动产业链供应链可持续发展”主题交流讨论，并开展产业链供应链供需对接活动。


The Conference will feature eight dedicated matchmaking sections, covering advanced manufacturing, clean energy, green agriculture, healthy living, digital technologies, intelligent vehicles, cross-border finance, and supply chain services. Representatives from government agencies, leading enterprises, and major financial institutions of ASEAN as well as the People's Republic of China, Japan and the Republic of Korea will be invited. The discussions would focus on the theme of “Enhancing Regional Synergy for Sustainable Industrial and Supply Chains Development”, accompanied by supply and demand matchmaking activities along regional industrial and supply chains.


同时，大会在线上同步开通供需对接平台，诚邀贵司参与大会线上供需对接匹配，并出席9月18日上午在广西南宁国际会展中心举办的线下活动。携手拓展业务合作机遇，共同擘画区域产业链供应链互利合作新蓝图。


At the same time, an online supply-demand matchmaking platform is launched prior to the Conference. We sincerely invite your esteemed company to participate in the online matchmaking activities, and to attend the on-site ASEAN Plus Three Industrial and Supply Chains Partnering Conference on the morning of September 18th at the Nanning International Convention & Exhibition Center, Guangxi. Together, we look forward to exploring new business cooperation opportunities and jointly mapping out a blueprint for mutually beneficial collaboration in regional industrial and supply chains.
