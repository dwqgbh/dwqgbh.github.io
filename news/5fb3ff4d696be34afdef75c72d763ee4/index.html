展会预告 | 汉科医疗、韦科医疗即将参加2025上海医疗器械展览会!

汉科医疗HantechMedical 2025-09-19



2025年9月24日至26日，汉科医疗将携旗下子公司韦科医疗在上海世博展览馆参加2025上海医疗器械展览会。汉科医疗展位：2M307，韦科医疗展位：1H110，恭候您的光临！

Hantech Medical and its subsidiary Wexford Medical will participate in Medtec China 2025 at the Shanghai World Expo Exhibition & Convention Center, from September 24 to 26, 2025. Our booth numbers are 2M307 (Hantech) and 1H110 (Wexmed). We look forward to seeing you there!
