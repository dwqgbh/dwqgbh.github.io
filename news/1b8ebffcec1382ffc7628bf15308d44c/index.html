01

为什么这么“香”？

自1987年首届举办以来，迪拜国际航展（Dubai Airshow）一路开挂，成为中东地区最具影响力的航空航天展之一，也跻身全球三大航展（仅次于巴黎航展和莫斯科航展）。迪拜航展不仅是中东地区航空工业的重要展示窗口，也是全球航空航天企业交流合作的重要平台。在这里，全球航空巨头齐聚一堂，最新黑科技、创新产品和解决方案轮番亮相——想不出圈都难！

Why is the Dubai Airshow so Irresistible?

Since its inception in 1987, the Dubai Airshow has become one of the most influential aerospace exhibitions in the Middle East, ranking among the world’s top three airshows (after Paris Airshow and Moscow Airshow). It serves as a crucial showcase for the Middle East’s aviation industry and a global platform for aerospace collaboration. Here, aviation giants from around the world gather to unveil cutting-edge technologies, innovative products, and revolutionary solutions—truly unmissable!

Highlights of Exhibits
