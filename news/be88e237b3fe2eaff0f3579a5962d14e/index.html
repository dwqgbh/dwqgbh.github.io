2025广东旅博会 | 乌干达:CITIE 2025上的非洲明珠,众多精彩活动等你来!

CITIE广东旅博会 2025-09-08

乌干达，这片景观对比鲜明的土地，既是尼罗河的源头，是赤道上的雪山，也是栖息着世界上一半山地大猩猩的原始森林。在这里，自然与文化完美交融，带来真实而难忘的旅行体验。

Uganda is a land of contrasts — the source of the Nile, snow-capped mountains at the equator, and forests home to half of the world’s mountain gorillas. A destination where nature and culture blend seamlessly, offering visitors experiences that are both authentic and unforgettable.

布温迪国家公园（Bwindi Impenetrable Forest）




01

跨越时空的文化传承

Living Heritage


在乌干达，文化不是表演，而是生活。无论是在熙熙攘攘的街头流传的古老棋类游戏Omweso，还是诉说世代故事的传统舞蹈与鼓声，乌干达的文化传承是一种跨越时空的交流。

Culture in Uganda is lived, not staged. From the ancient board game Omweso, played in bustling town squares, to traditional dance and drumming that tell stories of generations, Uganda’s heritage is an invitation to connect.





在街头玩Omweso棋的人们



02

原始自然的奇迹

Pristine Wilderness


乌干达的风光从火山高地到雨林草原，层次丰富：

Uganda’s landscapes stretch from volcanic highlands to lush savannahs and rainforest.



塞穆利基温泉：地热奇观，见证地球的力量。

Semuliki Hot Springs: geothermal wonders bursting with the earth’s energy.



布恩迪不可穿越森林：山地大猩猩的家园，一生必有的邂逅。

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest: home of the mountain gorilla, a once-in-a-lifetime encounter.



鲁文佐里山：非洲神秘的“月亮山”。

Rwenzori Mountains: Africa’s mystical “Mountains of the Moon.”





03

无限探索之旅

Boundless Adventure


冒险精神在乌干达无处不在 —— 在维多利亚湖上骑水上自行车、在尼罗河上体验漂流，或走进壮观的安科勒长角牛群。这里可以聆听珍稀鸟类的鸣唱、与山地大猩猩面对面，或只是静静地望着卡乔佩湖上云雾缭绕，山与天空交汇。乌干达属于那些不仅想“看世界”，更渴望“感受世界”的人。

Adventure flows through Uganda — ride water bikes on Lake Victoria, raft the rapids of the Nile, or stand among the majestic Ankole long-horned cattle. Discover the call of rare birds, meet the great mountain gorillas, or simply watch the clouds drift and gather over Lake Kachope, where mountains and sky become one. Uganda is a destination for those who don’t just want to see the world, but to feel it.

04

非洲明珠的醇香滋味

Flavors of the Pearl


乌干达是罗布斯塔咖啡的发源地，也是优质阿拉比卡的故乡。在CITIE，欢迎品尝乌干达咖啡! 这种独特风味由火山土壤、热带阳光和世代传承的技艺共同孕育而成。

Uganda is the birthplace of Robusta coffee and home to some of the world’s finest Arabicas. At CITIE, visitors are invited to taste Uganda’s coffee that has a rich flavour shaped by volcanic soils, tropical sun, and generations of craftsmanship.
