国际展会丨医佳宝精彩亮相Arab Health 2025阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会

医佳宝 2025-02-05

·


第五十届阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会（Arab Health 2025）于1月27-30日在迪拜世界贸易中心顺利举行。阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会是中东地区展览规划最大、展品类别较为完全、展览作用最为杰出的世界专业医疗职业博览会。此次展会吸引了来自约180个国家和地区的4000多家企业齐聚一堂，共同展示全球最前沿的医疗技术与极具前瞻性的创新产品，成为全球医疗领域的焦点。

The 50th Arab International Medical Equipment Exhibition (Arab Health 2025) was successfully held at the Dubai World Trade Center from January 27 to 30. Arab Health 2025 is the world's professional medical expo with the largest exhibition planning, the most complete exhibit categories, and the most outstanding exhibition function in the Middle East. Arab Health 2025 attracted more than 4,000 companies from about 180 countries and regions to gather together to showcase the world's most cutting-edge medical technologies and forward-looking innovative products, becoming the focus of the global medical field.

医佳宝携骨科植入、外科创护、医学美容三大核心业务板块产品，重磅亮相阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会，全面展示了公司产品研发与创新的雄厚实力。

YiJiABiO brought Orthopedic Implants, Wound Nursing and Medical Cosmetology products to Arab Health 2025, demonstrating the company's strong strength in product research, development and innovation.
