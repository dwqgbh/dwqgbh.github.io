公司介绍

Company profile  


苏州柯尼洛净化科技有限公司

SUZHOU CLEANROOM Technology Co., Ltd.

苏州柯尼洛净化科技有限公司是一家集洁净车间设计，安装，洁净设备设计生产一站式供应商，位于江苏省苏州市吴江经济开发区。

我司可承接百级-百万级净化工程（制药药厂洁净车间，新能源洁净车间，电子公司洁净厂房，洁净实验室），等项目的设计和安装！净化洁净设备供应：洁净风淋室、洁净传递窗、负压称量室、FFU机组、百级洁净棚、洁净医用水池、洁净钢质门、医用PVC电动移门、百级洁净工作台、高效送风口系列、过滤器系列、不锈钢衣柜系列、不锈钢鞋柜系列。

我们拥有专业生产基地，专业技术人员和优秀业务团队，关于售后方面我们有一批高素质、经验丰富的工程施工服务队伍，为客户提供优质的工程与售后服务。

SUZHOU CLEANROOM Technology Co., Ltd. (One-Stop Supplier for Cleanroom Design, Installation, and Cleanroom Equipment Manufacturing)

Located in the Wujiang Economic Development Zone, Suzhou, Jiangsu Province,

Our company specializes in providing comprehensive solutions for cleanroom projects, ranging from Class 100 to Class 1,000,000.     We offer design and installation services for various industries, including pharmaceutical cleanrooms, new energy cleanrooms, electronic company cleanrooms, and clean laboratories.

Cleanroom Equipment Supply: Clean Air Showers, Clean Pass Box, Negative Pressure Weighing Rooms, FFU unit, Class 100 clean shed, Clean medical pool, Clean steel door, Medical PVC electric sliding door, One hundred clean workbench, High efficiency air supply port series, Filter series, Stainless steel wardrobe Series, Stainless steel shoe cabinet

With a dedicated production base, professional technical staff, and an outstanding sales team, we are committed to delivering high-quality engineering and after-sales service.  Our team of highly skilled and experienced engineering service professionals ensures that our clients receive top-tier support throughout the project lifecycle.
