2025年巴基斯坦工业博览会将展示中巴工业合作成果

CPEC 2025-09-18


萨戈达大学（UoS）与珠穆朗玛峰国际公司合作，于周二宣布，将于2025年9月27日至29日在拉合尔会展中心举办第九届巴基斯坦工业博览会。该活动将再次成为中巴工业合作的重要平台。

 

萨戈达大学校长凯撒·阿巴斯教授博士在拉合尔的一场新闻发布会上表示：“过去八年来，巴基斯坦工业博览会已成为促进工业合作、技术转让和商业伙伴关系的中心。”他补充称，举办2025年展会，体现了该校致力于加强产学研联系，并通过与中国合作伙伴开展创新与合作，为巴基斯坦经济发展作出贡献的决心。

 

该博览会自2017年启动以来，已发展成为南亚最具影响力的工业展示活动之一，吸引了来自机械、能源、科技、建筑材料和工程等领域的数百家中巴参展商。每年有数千名参观者出席，其中包括投资者、企业家和学生。

 

2025年，珠穆朗玛峰国际公司将再次为参展商和参观者提供针对性的对接服务，以确保实现切实的商业成果。主办方强调：“该博览会是开展工业交流、创新和全球合作的最具权威性的平台。”珠穆朗玛峰国际公司管理合伙人阿尔穆甘·穆克姆欢迎行业领袖们探索“科技、制造、能源、工程和贸易领域的无限机遇”，并着重指出该博览会在彰显巴中友谊、拓展创业视野方面的作用。

 

萨戈达大学一直支持该博览会“连接产业与学术”的愿景。去年，2024年第八届巴基斯坦工业博览会吸引了来自300多家中巴企业的参与者。2023年举办的第七届博览会也备受关注，有超过250家企业展示了在科技、贸易和制造领域的合作机遇。

 

2025年博览会预计将促进企业间的直接联系，增加贸易额，并将先进的中国技术和投资模式引入巴基斯坦的工业领域。

 

“LAHORE, Sept. 17 (Gwadar Pro) - The University of Sargodha (UoS), in partnership with Everest International, announced Tuesday that it will host the 9th Pakistan Industrial Expo from September 27–29, 2025, at the Expo Centre, Lahore. The event will once again serve as a major platform for China-Pakistan industrial cooperation.


“The Pakistan Industrial Expo has become a hub for fostering industrial cooperation, technology transfer, and business partnerships over the past eight years,” said UoS Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Qaisar Abbas at a press conference in Lahore. He added that hosting the 2025 edition reflects the University’s commitment to strengthening industry-academia linkages and contributing to Pakistan’s economic development through innovation and collaboration with Chinese partners.


Since its launch in 2017, the Expo has grown into one of South Asia’s most prominent industrial showcases, attracting hundreds of Chinese and Pakistani exhibitors from sectors such as machinery, energy, technology, construction materials, and engineering. Thousands of visitors—including investors, entrepreneurs, and students—attend each year.


For 2025, Everest International will again provide targeted matchmaking services between exhibitors and visitors to ensure practical business outcomes. “The Expo is the most prestigious platform for industrial networking, innovation, and global partnerships,” the organizers emphasized. Everest International Managing Partner Armughan Muqeem welcomed industry leaders to explore “endless opportunities in technology, manufacturing, energy, engineering, and trade,” underscoring the Expo’s role in celebrating the Pakistan-China friendship and expanding entrepreneurial horizons.


The University of Sargodha has consistently supported the Expo’s vision of linking industry with academia. Last year, the 8th Pakistan Industrial Expo 2024 featured participants from over 300 Pakistani and Chinese companies. The 7th edition in 2023 also drew strong attention, with more than 250 companies showcasing opportunities for cooperation in technology, trade, and manufacturing.


The 2025 Expo is expected to facilitate direct business-to-business linkages, boost trade volumes, and introduce advanced Chinese technologies and investment models into Pakistan’s industrial landscape.”
