第八届进博会证件注册指南The 8th CIIE Certificate Registration Guide

广东跨采 2025-09-30

第八届中国国际进口博览会（以下简称“进博会”）将于2025年11月5日至10日在国家会展中心（上海）举办。展客商证件注册通道已全面开放，现在登录进博会官网即可办理。


【证件注册要点速览】


重要提醒：10.27-11.10展馆封闭管理，必须持证入场。证件实名注册，严禁转借冒用。


『Part1.人证办理相关』


第八届进博会国家综合展参展国代表、企业商业展展商、人文交流活动人员、交易团专业观众等注册通道均已开放，注册截止日期为10月20日。


配套活动人员注册自9月18日启动，注册截止日期为10月31日。


温馨提示：已办理过第五、六、七届进博会证件，且证件保存完好的，建议优先选择"旧证复用"功能，更加高效便捷。


『Part2.车证办理相关』


场内停车实行配额管理，请根据分配停车区域及有效期按需申请车辆证件。外围停车请关注后期上海市交通部门进博会停车预约有关通知。


『Part3.证件服务相关』


  • 证件寄递服务：可选寄递领证，足不出户即可取证。


  • 电子证件应急入场：忘带实体证可通过移动端申请电子证二维码由指定通道入场。


  • 证件补办服务：线上申请补办更高效，减少排队等待时间。

『Part5.展会介绍』


中国国际进口博览会（CIIE）自2018年首次举办以来，已经走过七届。作为全球首个以“进口”为主题的国家级展会，它不仅是中国主动向世界开放市场的重要平台，也是各国企业进入中国市场的快速通道。


2025年第八届进博会在上海国家会展中心举办，延续了“新时代，共享未来”的宗旨，并强调数字化、绿色、责任共赢三大主题。继续由国家综合展、企业商业展、虹桥国际经济论坛、配套活动和人文交流活动等组成。企业商业展设立医疗器械及医药保健（健康中国，美好生活）、汽车及智慧出行（出行，无限可能）、技术装备（创新与可持续未来）、消费品（更高品质，更好生活）、农食产品（从全球农场到中国餐桌）、服务贸易（数字、互联、绿色）六大展区及创新孵化专区（在中国，切入新赛道），将进一步创新展览题材，丰富展览内容，更加聚焦全球领先的新产品、新技术、新服务，加大力度挖掘关键核心技术和产品。同时，还将继续举办贸易投资对接会、重要采购商选品会、采购商“长廊”等展期活动，多维度打造展客商双向对接平台，促进展客商深度交流与合作。


本次进博会专业观众证件费用，仍参照往年进博会采取市交易分团统一支付的形式，报名专业观众无须单独支付证件费用。

The 8th China International Import Expo (hereinafter referred to as "CIIE") will be held from November 5th to 10th, 2025 at the National Convention and Exhibition Center (Shanghai). The registration channel for exhibitor credentials has been fully opened, and you can now log in to the official website of the Expo to apply.


• Quick Overview of Key Points for Document Registration


Important reminder: The exhibition hall is under closed management from October 27th to November 10th, and entry must be with a valid permit. Real name registration of identification documents is strictly prohibited, and unauthorized borrowing and use are strictly prohibited.


• Part 1. Personnel certification processing related


The registration channels for representatives of participating countries, business exhibitors, cultural exchange personnel, and professional visitors of the 8th CIIE National Comprehensive Exhibition have been opened, and the registration deadline is October 20th.


The registration of supporting activity personnel will start on September 18th and the registration deadline is October 31st.


Reminder: For those who have already obtained the documents for the 5th, 6th, and 7th CIIE and whose documents are well preserved, it is recommended to prioritize the "old certificate reuse" function for greater efficiency and convenience.


• Part 2. Vehicle license processing related


On site parking is subject to quota management. Please apply for vehicle identification documents as needed based on the allocated parking area and validity period. Please pay attention to the notice regarding parking reservations for the Shanghai Municipal Transportation Department's CIIE in the future for peripheral parking.


• Part 3. Certificate Services Related


- Document delivery service: optional delivery and collection of documents, can be obtained without leaving home.


- Emergency entry with electronic ID: If you forget to bring your physical ID, you can apply for the QR code of the electronic ID through your mobile device and enter through the designated channel.


- Document reissuance service: Online application for reissuance is more efficient and reduces waiting time in queues.

• Part 5. Exhibition Introduction


The China International Import Expo (CIIE) has been successfully held for seven sessions since its inception in 2018. As the world's first national-level exhibition themed on "imports," it serves as a crucial platform for China to proactively open its market to the world and a fast track for international enterprises to access the Chinese market.


Scheduled to take place at the Shanghai National Convention and Exhibition Center, the 8th CIIE in 2025 will uphold its guiding principle of "New Era, Shared Future," with a reinforced focus on the three core themes: digitalization, sustainability, and shared responsibility for mutual benefit. The Expo will continue to feature National Trade and Investment Comprehensive Exhibits, Business Exhibitions, the Hongqiao International Economic Forum, a range of supporting activities, and cultural exchange events.


The Business Exhibitions will be organized into six thematic zones and an Innovation Incubation Zone:Medical Equipment & Healthcare Products (Healthy China, Better Life), Automobiles & Smart Mobility (Travel, Infinite Possibilities), Intelligent Industry & Information Technology (Innovation for a Sustainable Future), Consumer Goods (Higher Quality, Better Life), Agricultural Products & Food (From Global Farms to Chinese Dining Tables), Trade in Services (Digital, Internet, Green).


The 2025 CIIE will further innovate its exhibition themes, enrich the display content, and place greater emphasis on globally leading new products, technologies, and services. Increased efforts will be made to showcase key core technologies and products. Concurrently, the Expo will continue to host events such as Trade and Investment Matchmaking Sessions, Key Buyer Product Selection Meetings, and the Buyer "Corridor," creating a multi-dimensional platform for two-way engagement between exhibitors and buyers to foster in-depth communication and cooperation.


Similar to previous editions, the registration fee for Professional Visitors for this year's CIIE will be covered by the respective city trade delegations. Registered Professional Visitors will not be required to pay the fee individually.
