Smartgen论坛 | 热烈祝贺2026年众智科技工程机械控制技术交流会成功举办

众智SmartGen 2026-06-25




在能源结构转型与工业智能化升级的双重驱动下，工程机械行业正迈向高效、节能、智能、可靠新征程。为深化技术交流、共探行业新路径，众智科技于6月24日举办以“变频赋新能·增程拓新篇”为主题的2026工程机械控制技术交流会，聚焦空压机一体化变频控制、增程发电智能控制等核心技术，与行业专家、合作伙伴共谋工程机械装备新发展。




会议伊始，众智科技总经理崔文峰先生致欢迎辞，向远道而来的嘉宾表示热烈欢迎。他强调：海内外新型基础建设步伐加快，公司柴驱非道路工程机械产品从初代单色液晶控制器，升级为如今大屏彩屏成套系统，产品线持续丰富，配套解决方案日趋多元。伴随国内新能源产业高速发展，工程机械电动化、智能化、增程化升级成为行业发展的大趋势，众智科技将紧跟市场需求，继续深耕、持续创新，为非道路工程机械产业发展赋能。


众智总经理崔文峰

接下来，众智科技销售总监姚关保就企业发展历程、业务板块、智能制造、核心技术研发成果作详细介绍，同步解读产品质量管理、厂区精益运营管理两大体系，直观体现公司在控制器领域持续深耕形成的综合竞争力。


众智销售总监姚关保-《众智科技公司简介》


与会期间，各位嘉宾走进众智科技现代化展厅、智能生产车间与产品测试中心，近距离感受众智科技的研发实力与制造水平。




接下来众智科技研发经理张双洋、研发主任张威、产品线总监周晓伟、研发工程师毕非凡、工程机械事业部总监王满、玉柴芯蓝新能源动力科技有限公司配套技术高级主任王鑫浦、利星行机械(昆山)有限公司应用主任卢卫波围绕会议主题、行业趋势依次展开技术分享。

SmartGen Forum | 2026 Construction Machinery Control Technology Seminar Held Successfully



Against the backdrop of global energy transition and accelerating industrial digitalization, the construction machinery industry is entering a new era defined by higher efficiency, lower emissions, smarter operation, and greater reliability. To foster technical exchange and explore emerging trends, SmartGen held the 2026 Construction Machinery Control Technology Seminar on June 24 under the theme: “Empowering New Energy with VFD, Expanding Horizons with RE Tech”. The seminar focused on key technologies including integrated VFD control for air compressors and intelligent control solutions for range-extended power systems, bringing together industry experts and partners to share insights and discuss the future development of construction machinery.




At the opening of the conference, Mr. William, General Manager of SmartGen, delivered a welcome speech and extended a warm greeting to all guests. He noted that as infrastructure development continues to accelerate both domestically and internationally, SmartGen's diesel-powered off-road machinery control products have evolved from first-generation monochrome LCD controllers to today's fully integrated large-screen color display systems. Meanwhile, the company's product portfolio and supporting solutions have continued to expand and diversify. With the rapid growth of the new energy sector, electric, intelligent control, and range-extended technologies are becoming key development trends across the construction machinery industry. SmartGen will remain market-oriented, continue investing in innovation, and leverage its expertise to support the off-road machinery industry's ongoing transformation.


William Cui, General Manager of SmartGen


Following the opening remarks, Gabriel Yao, Sales Director of SmartGen, presented an overview of the company's development history, business sectors, intelligent manufacturing capabilities, and major R&D achievements. He also introduced SmartGen's product quality management system and lean operation practices, showcasing the company's comprehensive strengths built through years of dedicated expertise in the controller industry.



Gabriel Yao, Sales Director of SmartGen - Overview of SmartGen


During the seminar, guests toured SmartGen's modern exhibition hall, intelligent manufacturing workshop, and product testing center, gaining first-hand insight into the company's R&D capabilities, manufacturing excellence, and quality assurance processes.




The technical session then featured presentations from several experts: Herr Zhang (SmartGen R&D Manager), Wayne Zhang (SmartGen R&D Leader), Xiaowei Zhou (SmartGen Construction Machinery Product Line Director), Frank Bi (SmartGen R&D Engineer), Manco Wang (SmartGen Construction Machinery Division Director), Xinpu Wang (Senior Technical Leader of Yuchai Core Blue New Energy Power Technology Co., Ltd.), and Weibo Lu, (Technical Application Leader of Lei Shing Hong Machinery (Kunshan) Co., Ltd.). Speakers shared their latest technologies, solutions, and practical applications related to the seminar theme and evolving industry trends.

Manco Wang, Director of SmartGen Construction Machinery Division – Introduction to Diesel-Driven Air Compressor Controllers



Looking ahead, SmartGen will continue to advance construction machinery control technologies while further strengthening its VFD and range-extended solutions. By staying aligned with the ongoing trends of energy transition and industrial intelligence, SmartGen remains committed to delivering innovative products and solutions for the industry. We look forward to working hand in hand with our partners to push technological boundaries, accelerate the adoption of low-carbon, intelligent, and high-efficiency equipment, and jointly create a smarter future for the construction machinery industry.
