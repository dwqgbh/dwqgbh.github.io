长春航展与中国航天征程

The Changchun Air Show is always an exciting event! From September 19th to 23rd this year, it was held at Changchun International Aviation Expo City. People enjoyed amazing flight shows where planes showed their great skills in the sky. They also got close to famous planes like the J20 and Y20. 长春航展总是一场令人兴奋的盛会！今年 9 月 19 日至 23 日，航展在长春国际航空博览城举行。人们欣赏了精彩的飞行表演，飞机在天空中展现出高超技艺；大家还近距离接触到了歼 - 20、运 - 20 等著名机型。
This air show is not just for fun. It also tells us how much China's aerospace industry has developed. Let's look at China's great space achievements! 这场航展不只是为了娱乐，更向我们展现了中国航空航天事业的飞速发展。让我们一起回顾中国了不起的航天成就！
In 1970, China launched its first satellite, Dongfanghong I. This was a big start for China's space dream. Then in 2003, Shenzhou 5 sent Yang Liwei into space. China became the third country in the world to send a person to space. Everyone felt proud of Yang Liwei! 1970 年，中国发射了第一颗人造卫星 ——“东方红一号”，这是中国航天梦的重要起点。2003 年，“神舟五号” 将杨利伟送入太空，中国成为世界上第三个独立载人航天的国家，所有人都为杨利伟感到骄傲！
China's lunar exploration is also wonderful. The Chang'e missions have done many great things on the moon. In 2019, Chang'e 4 was the first to land on the far side of the moon. In 2020, Chang'e 5 brought back lunar samples. These help us learn more about the moon. 中国的探月工程同样精彩。“嫦娥” 系列任务在月球上取得了多项突破：2019 年，“嫦娥四号” 首次着陆月球背面；2020 年，“嫦娥五号” 带回了月球样本，帮助我们更深入地了解月球。
Mars exploration is another big step. In 2020, Tianwen 1 was launched. It sent the Zhurong rover to Mars. This shows China's ability to explore deep space. 火星探测是另一大跨越。2020 年，“天问一号” 探测器发射升空，并将 “祝融号” 火星车送上火星，这充分展现了中国的深空探测能力。
Now, the China Space Station is like a "home" in space for astronauts. They do interesting experiments there and learn more about space. 如今，中国空间站已成为航天员在太空的 “家”。他们在这里开展有趣的科学实验，探索更多太空奥秘。
All these achievements are big steps for China's space dream. Maybe one day, you can join in China's space adventures too! 这些成就都是中国航天梦的坚实步伐。也许有一天，你也能加入中国的太空探索之旅！
