国际展会丨医佳宝精彩亮相WHX Dubai 2026阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会

医佳宝 2026-02-12

第51届WHX Dubai 2026阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会于2月9-12日在迪拜会展中心顺利举行。WHX Dubai是中东地区医疗展览规模最大、展品最全、展览作用最为杰出的世界性专业医疗博览会之一。自 1975 年首次举行以来, 展会规划、参展厂商和观众人数逐年扩展，在中东国家的医疗机构和医疗器械代理商中享有盛誉。

The 51st WHX Dubai 2026 was successfully held from February 9 to 12 at the Dubai Exhibition Centre. WHX Dubai is one of the world's largest, most comprehensive, and most influential professional medical exhibitions in the Middle East. Since its inception in 1975, the exhibition's scale, number of exhibitors, and visitor attendance have expanded year by year, earning a high reputation among medical institutions and medical device distributors in Middle Eastern countries.

医佳宝携骨科植入、外科创护、医学美容三大核心业务板块产品，重磅亮相2026阿拉伯国际医疗设备展览会，全面展示了公司在产品研发与创新领域的雄厚实力。

YiJiABiO showcased its products across its three core business segments-orthopedic implants, wound nursing, and medical cosmetology-at WHX Dubai 2026, comprehensively demonstrating the company's strong capabilities in product research, development, and innovation.
