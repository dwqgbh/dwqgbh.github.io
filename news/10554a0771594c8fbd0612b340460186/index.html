2026春季广交会圆满结束 | 聚力前行 共拓全球新商机

JIESUTE 捷速特 2026-04-27

       为期多日的2026年广交会已圆满落下帷幕。

       After several intensive days, the 2026 Canton Fair has successfully concluded.

       本次广交会期间，我们满载诚意与实力赴约，携全线核心新品、升级工艺方案、全链条出口配套服务亮相现场，面对面迎接来自全球各地的采购商、合作伙伴及行业同仁。

During this Canton Fair, we attended the event with full sincerity and solid strength. We brought our full lineup of new core products, upgraded technical solutions, and one-stop export supporting services. We welcomed global buyers, long-term partners and industry professionals face to face at our booth.


      我们与新老客户深入沟通海外市场需求、定制化合作方案、批量供货周期、合规出口流程，真实展示工厂产能、品控体系与交付实力。
We had in-depth communication with new and existing clients regarding overseas market demands, customized cooperation solutions, bulk order delivery schedules, and full export compliance procedures. We fully demonstrated our factory production capacity, strict quality control standards, and stable delivery capability.

      未来，我们将继续坚守品质为本、诚信为先、稳定交付、合规出口的经营理念，持续优化产品创新、升级生产体系、完善外贸全流程服务。
In the future, we will continue to adhere to our core business philosophy: quality first, integrity-oriented, stable delivery, and export compliance. We will keep optimizing product innovation, upgrading production management systems, and improving full-process foreign trade services.
