2024年秋季广交会:把握经济新机遇舞台

杺言看世界 2024-10-15

    2024年10月15日，全球商业的焦点再次聚焦于中国广州，迎来了第136届中国进出口商品交易会（广交会）的盛大开幕。作为世界最大规模的综合性商品交易会，广交会不仅是中国对外开放的经贸平台，更是全球经济交往的重要窗口。在这个充满商机和挑战的时代，广交会为全球企业搭建了一个展示实力、寻求合作的大舞台。

一、新质生产力：经济新动能的集中展现

本届广交会，新质生产力成为了一大亮点。近40%的广州参展企业属于新质生产力企业，这些企业展示了中国在高端制造、绿色能源、智能制造等领域的最新成果。电子及家电、新能源、新能源汽车及智慧出行、工业自动化及智能制造等题材展位规模超8700个，涵盖了“新三样”（电动汽车、光伏产品、锂电池）、数字技术、智能制造等相关题材的企业约3600家。智慧生活、工业机器人、智能制造生产线等专区吸引了近400家企业参展，一批人形机器人、智能设备、无人驾驶产品等创新产品首次亮相，展示了中国在技术创新领域的强大实力。

这些新质生产力的涌现，不仅推动了中国经济结构的优化升级，更为全球经济带来了新的增长点。广交会作为连接中国与世界的金桥，将这些创新成果推向全球，为全球企业提供了合作与共赢的新机遇。

二、数字化转型：外贸企业的必然选择

在数字经济时代，数字化转型已成为外贸企业的必然选择。广交会作为外贸企业的重要展示平台，也见证了这一趋势的加速发展。多个外贸CRM提供商为广交会提供了强有力的技术支持，帮助企业提升营销和销售管理的效率。通过打通外贸营销的全触点，包括邮件、WhatsApp、网站、Facebook、企业微信等，为外贸企业提供了线上数字营销的整体解决方案。

数字化转型不仅提高了外贸企业的运营效率，更帮助企业更好地把握市场趋势和客户需求。在广交会上，众多外贸企业通过数字化手段展示产品、洽谈合作，实现了更加精准和高效的营销。这种数字化转型的趋势，将推动外贸企业向更高质量、更高效率的发展阶段迈进。

三、开放与创新：广交会的永恒主题

广交会一直以来都坚持开放与创新的精神，这也是其能够持续引领全球贸易发展的重要原因。本届广交会以“服务高质量发展，推进高水平开放”为主题，展示了中国在推动经济全球化、促进国际贸易自由化便利化方面的坚定立场和积极贡献。

在广交会上，各国商人和企业家汇聚一堂，共享商机、共谋发展。广交会不仅为中国企业提供了展示实力、拓展市场的平台，更为全球企业提供了合作与交流的机会。通过广交会，中国企业得以了解国际市场需求和趋势，学习国际先进技术和管理经验；而外国企业则得以了解中国市场的潜力和机遇，寻找合作伙伴和投资机会。

四、挑战与机遇并存：广交会的未来展望

尽管广交会为全球企业带来了无数商机，但同时也面临着诸多挑战。如何在海量的信息中准确把握商机？如何在繁多的客户中精准定位？如何在激烈的竞争中脱颖而出？这些都是外贸企业必须面对的问题。

然而，正是这些挑战孕育着新的机遇。随着数字技术的不断发展和全球贸易的持续深化，广交会将迎来更加广阔的发展空间和更加多元的合作机会。未来，广交会将继续发挥其作为全球经济交往重要窗口的作用，推动中国经济与世界经济更加紧密地联系在一起。
结语
2024年秋季广交会不仅是一场商贸盛会，更是一次全球经贸文化交流的大聚会。在这个舞台上，每一个企业都有机会展现自己的实力、获取商机；而在这个舞台背后，则是中国经济持续向好的强劲动力和全球经济复苏的无限可能。我们期待广交会在未来的日子里继续为中国和全球的经济贸易发展贡献更大的力量！

Canton Fair Fall 2024: Seizing the Stage for New Economic Opportunities

On October 15, 2024, the focus of global business was once again on Guangzhou, China, for the grand opening of the 136th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair). As the world's largest comprehensive commodity fair, Canton Fair is not only an economic and trade platform for China's opening up to the outside world, but also an important window for global economic exchanges. In this era full of business opportunities and challenges, Canton Fair has set up a big stage for global enterprises to show their strength and seek cooperation.

I. New Productivity: A Concentrated Display of New Economic Dynamics

At this year's Canton Fair, new quality productivity has become a major highlight. Nearly 40% of Guangzhou's exhibitors belonged to the new quality productivity enterprises, which demonstrated China's latest achievements in high-end manufacturing, green energy, intelligent manufacturing and other fields. The scale of booths for electronics and home appliances, new energy, new energy vehicles and smart mobility, industrial automation and intelligent manufacturing exceeded 8,700, covering about 3,600 enterprises in the “New Three Kinds” (electric vehicles, photovoltaic products, and lithium batteries), digital technology, intelligent manufacturing and other related topics. Intelligent life, industrial robots, intelligent manufacturing production lines and other areas attracted nearly 400 exhibitors, a number of humanoid robots, intelligent equipment, driverless products and other innovative products debut, demonstrating China's strong strength in the field of technological innovation.

The emergence of these new productive forces not only promotes the optimization and upgrading of China's economic structure, but also brings new growth points to the global economy. As a golden bridge connecting China and the world, Canton Fair pushes these innovations to the world and provides new opportunities for global enterprises to cooperate and win-win.

Second, digital transformation: the inevitable choice of foreign trade enterprises

In the era of digital economy, digital transformation has become an inevitable choice for foreign trade enterprises. As an important exhibition platform for foreign trade enterprises, Canton Fair has also witnessed the accelerated development of this trend. Several foreign trade CRM providers have provided strong technical support for the Canton Fair to help enterprises improve the efficiency of marketing and sales management. By opening up the whole touchpoints of foreign trade marketing, including email, WhatsApp, website, Facebook, enterprise WeChat, etc., they provided foreign trade enterprises with a total solution for online digital marketing.

Digital transformation not only improves the operational efficiency of foreign trade enterprises, but also helps them better grasp market trends and customer needs. At the Canton Fair, many foreign trade enterprises displayed their products and negotiated cooperation through digital means, realizing more accurate and efficient marketing. This trend of digital transformation will promote foreign trade enterprises to move towards a higher quality and more efficient development stage.

Openness and Innovation: The Eternal Theme of Canton Fair

Canton Fair has always insisted on the spirit of openness and innovation, which is also an important reason why it can continue to lead the development of global trade. This year's Canton Fair, with the theme of “Serving high-quality development, promoting high-level opening up”, has demonstrated China's firm stance and positive contribution in promoting economic globalization and facilitating the liberalization of international trade.

At the Canton Fair, businessmen and entrepreneurs from all over the world come together to share business opportunities and seek common development. Canton Fair not only provides a platform for Chinese enterprises to show their strength and expand their markets, but also provides opportunities for global enterprises to cooperate and exchange ideas. Through Canton Fair, Chinese enterprises can understand the international market demand and trends, learn international advanced technology and management experience; while foreign enterprises can understand the potential and opportunities of the Chinese market, and look for partners and investment opportunities.

Challenges and Opportunities: Future Prospects of the Canton Fair

Although Canton Fair has brought numerous business opportunities to global enterprises, it also faces many challenges. How to accurately grasp the business opportunities in the vast amount of information? How to precisely locate the customers in a wide range of customers? How to stand out in the fierce competition? These are the problems that foreign trade enterprises must face.

However, it is these challenges that breed new opportunities. With the continuous development of digital technology and the deepening of global trade, Canton Fair will usher in a broader development space and more diversified cooperation opportunities. In the future, Canton Fair will continue to play its role as an important window for global economic exchanges, and promote closer ties between China's economy and the world economy.

Conclusion

Canton Fair Fall 2024 is not only a trade event, but also a big gathering for global economic, trade and cultural exchanges. On this stage, every enterprise has the opportunity to show its strength and obtain business opportunities; behind this stage, there is the strong momentum of China's economy continuing to improve and the infinite possibilities of global economic recovery. We look forward to the Canton Fair continuing to contribute more to the economic and trade development of China and the world in the days to come!
