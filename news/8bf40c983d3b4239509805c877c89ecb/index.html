2025年第92届CMEF秋季会┃镜影科技系列报道之12:镜影科技CMEF秋季会完美落幕,11月进博会与您再相约!

镜影科技 2025-09-29


秋意正浓，盛会有期。为期四天的2025年第92届CMEF 秋季会于今日在广州缓缓落下帷幕，镜影科技与全球医疗同仁的这场深度对话，也在此刻画上圆满的句号。回首这四天，镜影科技的展台始终人流如织，热情不减。此刻，我们满载而归。

As autumn's essence reaches its peak, grand gatherings await. The four-day 92nd CMEF Autumn Exhibition in 2025 has just concluded in Guangzhou, marking a successful end to EndoVista's profound dialogue with global healthcare professionals. Looking back over these four days, our booth remained bustling with visitors throughout, their enthusiasm unwavering. Now, we return with a fruitful harvest.



展区盛况，产品引燃专业市场热情。

Exhibition Area Highlights,and products ignited enthusiasm in the professional market.


在镜影科技的展台区，专业观众通过亲自操作系统演示，亲身感受AI赋能内镜带来的智能诊疗，该系统凭借“全呼吸道覆盖、AI实时检查质量控制、病灶识别及智能化报告”等功能，引发众多专业观众的浓厚兴趣，洽谈区始终座无虚席。

At the exhibition area of EndoVista, professional visitors experienced firsthand the intelligent diagnosis and treatment powered by AI in endoscopy by operating the system demonstrations. The system's capabilities, including "full respiratory tract coverage, real-time AI inspection quality control, lesion recognition, and intelligent reporting," sparked strong interest among many professional visitors. The consultation area remained fully occupied throughout.

    Over 400 domestic and international professional visitors engaged in in-depth exchanges.

  • 累计200+场深度技术答疑。

    Over 200 in-depth technical Q&A sessions.

  • 完成100+次产品深度演示。

    Complete 100+in-depth product demonstrations.




商业成果，全球代理招商与OEM合作招募，共创产业新价值。

Business Achievements，Global Agent Recruitment & OEM Partnership, Co-creating New Industry Value.


展会期间，镜影科技开启全球市场的渠道合作以及针对行业伙伴的OEM合作，旨在通过灵活多元的合作模式，将这一领先的“AI+内镜”方案推广至全球市场，更广地应用于全球临床，并用AI赋能国内外优秀内镜厂商，共同打造更具竞争力的整合解决方案。

During the exhibition，EndoVista initiates global market channel partnerships and OEM collaborations with industry partners. Through flexible and diversified cooperation models, the company aims to promote this leading "AI+Endoscope" solution to global markets, facilitate its wider application in global clinical settings, empower domestic and international excellent endoscopy manufacturers with AI capabilities, and jointly create more competitive integrated solutions.

  • 收获80+代理商与OEM厂商的高质量合作意向，远超预期目标。

    Achieved high-quality cooperation intentions from over 80 agents and OEM manufacturers, far exceeding expected targets.

  • 与20+代理商和OEM厂商达成战略合作共识，将在会后做进一步合作洽谈。

关于镜影 About EndoVista：

镜影科技专注于AI全呼吸道内窥镜系统的研发与生产，用自研AI软件搭配国产内窥镜为患者提供精准诊疗。团队拥有丰富的出海经验，通过与顶尖医院合作，开发全球首创AI内窥镜系统，致力于推动中国医疗技术全球化。

EndoVista focuses on the research and development for Full Respiratory AI Endoscopy System,provides precise diagnosis to patients using self-developed AI software with domestic endoscopes.The team has rich overseas experience,by collaborating with top hospitals,it has developed the World's first AI Endoscopy System and committed to promoting the globalization of Chinese medical technology.
