学校简介

About the University

德国亚琛工业大学（德语: Rheinisch-Westfälische Technische Hochschule Aachen, 英语: RWTH Aachen University, 简称RWTH），成立于1870年，是世界顶尖的理工大学之一，理工科实力强劲，被誉为“欧洲的麻省理工”。2026QS世界综合大学排名全球第105名。


亚琛工大设有260个研究所，9个联合研究中心，现为：

► 11所“德国精英大学”联盟成员之一

► 德国TU9工科大学联盟成员之一

► 欧洲著名理工科大学联盟IDEA联盟成员之一

► TIME欧洲顶尖工业管理者高校联盟德国7所高校之一



RWTH Aachen University, founded in 1870, is one of the world's leading technological universities, renowned for its expertise in science and engineering. In the 2026 QS World University Rankings, it ranks 105th globally.


The university comprises 260 research institutes and 9 collaborative research centers. It currently holds the following affiliations:


  • One of the 11 Universities of Excellence in Germany

  • A member of the TU9, an alliance of leading universities of technology in Germany

  • A member of the IDEA League, an alliance of five leading European universities of science and technology 

  • One of the seven German universities in the T.I.M.E. (Top International Managers in Engineering), a network of leading technical universities and engineering schools.

申请要求与费用

Requirements & Fees

1.广州国际校区相关专业本科生，具有良好的政治和专业素养，身心健康。

Undergraduate students at GZIC, possessing sound political and professional qualities, and in good physical and mental health.


2.派出前必修课无不及格科目。

With no failing grades in required courses prior to departure.


3.英语语言能力：英语通过六级，或四级不低于485分，大一学生高考分数不低于120分

Language Proficiency: Passing CET-6, or CET-4 score of 485 or above; For freshmen, the English score of the National College Entrance Examination (Gaokao) shall be no less than 120 points. 


项目费用：3190欧元/人

费用包含：教学相关资源及活动安排，亚琛住宿（2人间），周一至周五早餐和午餐，亚琛当地公交车票，集中机场接送，相关行政服务与支持等。

Programme Fee: €3,190 per person

Fees include teaching and scheduled activities; accommodation in Aachen (twin-sharing room); breakfast and lunch on weekdays; local bus tickets; group airport pick-up and drop-off; related administrative services and support.
