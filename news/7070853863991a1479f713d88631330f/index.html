由西部国际传播中心和重庆卫视联合制播的重庆首档国际传播融媒栏目《渝见》第141期（特别节目）《2025世界智能产业博览会：走进重庆智造，连接数字未来》于9月13日在海内外媒体同步播出。

The first all-English international broadcast program in Chongqing, the 141st episode of "Let’s Meet", (Special Episode) titled “WSIE 2025: Chongqing Smart Manufacturing: Connect to the Digital Future”, jointly produced by the Western China International Communication Organization and Chongqing Satellite TV, was aired on both domestic and international media on September 13.


新加坡如何“智”造未来

How Singapore Builds the Smart Future



新加坡作为亚洲首个发布国家AI战略的国家，正在医疗、教育等多个领域全面推进人工智能转型。在本届2025世界智能产业博览会现场，我们走进了新加坡展区，与新加坡资讯通信媒体发展局助理局长布伟立，以及人工智能企业Sanbo和Neeuro的代表展开对话，捕捉新加坡在AI前沿的创新动态。


Singapore, the first Asian country to launch a national AI strategy, is advancing AI transformation across healthcare, education, and more. At the Expo, we visited the Singapore Pavilion and spoke with the Assistant Chief Executive of IMDA, along with representatives from AI companies Sanbo and Neeuro, capturing Singapore’s latest innovations in AI.


特斯拉如何应对中国市场挑战

Tesla Accelerates Localization Efforts in China

 


面对日益激烈的市场竞争，美国知名电动车企业特斯拉不仅推出了六座SUV新车型，还积极推进"品牌下乡"策略。本期《陆海财经》，我们采访到了特斯拉的重庆代表，介绍他们本地化战略的最新进展，了解特斯拉如何在中国这个全球最大的新能源汽车市场应对新挑战。


In the face of increasingly fierce market competition, the renowned American electric vehicle company Tesla has not only launched new six-seater SUV models but has also actively promoted its "brand localization" strategy. In this episode of "Bridging News," we interviewed Tesla's representative in Chongqing to introduce the latest progress in their localization strategy and to understand how Tesla is responding to new challenges in China, the world's largest NEV market.


从云端到现实：挡不住的物理人工智能

The Dawn of Physical AI: the Era of Embodied Intelligence



科技界正在迎来一个全新的概念——"物理AI"。特斯拉近日宣布从电动汽车全面转向，将人工智能带入物理世界。英伟达CEO黄仁勋也宣称"下一波浪潮将是物理AI"。在2025世界智能产业博览会召开之际，本期《对话世界》将为您深入解析这场从云端走向现实的AI革命。


The tech world is witnessing the rise of a new concept — "physical AI." Tesla recently announced its full shift from electric vehicles to bringing AI into the physical world. NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang has also declared, "The next wave will be physical AI." At the 2025 WSIE, this episode of "Dialogue " provides an in-depth analysis of the AI revolution that is moving from the cloud to reality.


智能产业的创新魅力

Innovation in Smart Industries



最后，我们将回顾本届2025世界智能产业博览会的高光时刻，感受智能产业的创新魅力，并了解一批重大项目的集中签约，共同见证这一国际盛事带来的合作与机遇。


Finally, we will review the highlights of this year's WSIE, experiencing the innovation charm of smart industries and learning about a series of major projects being signed.
