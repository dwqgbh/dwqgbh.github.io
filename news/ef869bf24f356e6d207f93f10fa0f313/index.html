中墨商会将参加第27届深圳高交会 MEXCHAM will attend the 27th China Hi-Tech Fair

多彩墨西哥 2025-11-11



INVITATION

第二十七届中国国际高新技术成果交易会（简称高交会）将于2025年11月14日-16日在中国深圳举办，中国墨西哥商会（MEXCHAM）将由四家企业（LEDEC Group, XPD Global, SLP Global and Terra Regia）代表参加，展馆号：13，展位号：13-A40-3。MEXCHAM诚邀您来我们的展位参观，与我们一同交流，共洽商机！


The 27th China International High-Tech Fair (CIHF) will be held from November 14 to 16, 2025, in Shenzhen, China. Mexican Chamber of Commerce in China (MEXCHAM)  will be represented by four companies (LEDEC Group, XPD Global, SLP Global, and Terra Regia) to participate in the event. You can find us at Booth 13-A40-3 in Hall 13. MEXCHAM cordially invites you to visit our booth, engage in discussions, and explore business opportunities together!

MEXCHAM continues building bridges between Mexico and China.

中国墨西哥商会将继续作为墨西哥与中国之间的桥梁，不断努力。
