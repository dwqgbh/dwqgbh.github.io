【新思想引领新征程】北京全力打造国际科技创新高地

[New Ideas Lead a New journey] Beijing is making every effort to build an international scientific and technological innovation highland

2025中关村论坛年会今天（3月31日）在北京闭幕，包括了人工智能、具身智能、量子信息等一批科技成果集中发布，标志着北京在国际科技创新中心的建设底座不断夯实。

The 2025 Zhongguancun Forum Annual Meeting closed in Beijing today (March 31), including a number of scientific and technological achievements including artificial intelligence, embodied intelligence, quantum information and other concentrated releases, marking Beijing's construction base in the international science and technology innovation center continues to consolidate.

北京依托丰富的科教资源优势，持续加强基础研究和关键核心技术攻关。在雁栖湖畔的怀柔科学城，37个重大科技基础设施与平台中，高能同步辐射光源已成功发出第一束光；在北京量子信息科学研究院，光声量子存储器存储时长创新世界纪录，达到了4035秒；在原始创新的脑机接口领域，“北脑一号”完成了国际首批柔性高通量半侵入式无线全植入脑机系统的人体植入。

Relying on its rich advantages in science and education resources, Beijing continues to strengthen basic research and research in key core technologies. In Huairou Science City beside Yanqi Lake, among 37 major science and technology infrastructures and platforms, the first beam of high-energy synchrotron radiation light source has been successfully emitted. At the Beijing Academy of Quantum Information Science, the storage time of photoacoustic quantum memory set a new world record, reaching 4,035 seconds. In the field of original and innovative brain-computer interface, "Beinao No. 1" completed the world's first flexible high-throughput semi-invasive wireless full-implant brain computer system for human implantation.

抓科技创新和产业创新融合，北京积极搭建平台，不断健全体制机制。2024年，北京人工智能核心产业营收突破了3000亿元。今年，北京将在海淀、朝阳、亦庄、京西等地加快布局智能算力产业生态，助推自动驾驶、商业航天、智慧物流等新兴领域加速科技创新。在中关村科技园区，这款由北京企业自主研发的原生机器人大模型日前取得新进展，已能完成筷子夹物、精准操作工具等超百种复杂操作。

Focusing on the integration of scientific and technological innovation and industrial innovation, Beijing has actively built platforms and constantly improved institutions and mechanisms. In 2024, the revenue of Beijing's artificial intelligence core industry exceeded 300 billion yuan. This year, Beijing will accelerate the layout of intelligent computing industry ecology in Haidian, Chaoyang, Yizhuang, Beijing West and other places to promote automatic driving, commercial space, smart logistics and other emerging fields to accelerate scientific and technological innovation. In Zhongguancun Science and Technology Park, the native robot model independently developed by Beijing enterprises has made new progress, and has been able to complete more than 100 complex operations such as chopsticks and precision operation tools.

截至目前，北京拥有独角兽企业115家，专精特新“小巨人”企业1035家，国家高新技术企业2.97万家，三类企业数量均居全国城市首位，已形成新一代信息技术、科技服务业、医药健康3个万亿级产业集群，以及智能制造与装备、人工智能等7个千亿级产业集群。

Up to now, Beijing has 115 unicorn enterprises, 1,035 specialized new "little giant" enterprises, 29,700 national high-tech enterprises, the number of three types of enterprises ranking first in the country, has formed a new generation of information technology, science and technology service industry, medicine and health three trillion industrial clusters. And intelligent manufacturing and equipment, artificial intelligence and other 700 billion industrial clusters.

今年，北京将围绕强化战略科技力量引领、中关村世界领先科技园区建设、教育科技人才一体化等方面，计划部署实施230项重点任务，并建设市级科技项目统筹管理系统，建立重点企业调度、重点政策措施、重大项目、重点任务等“四张清单”推进机制。

This year, Beijing will focus on strengthening the leadership of strategic science and technology forces, the construction of Zhongguancun's world-leading science and technology park, and the integration of education and science and technology talents, and plan to deploy and implement 230 key tasks, and build a municipal overall management system for science and technology projects, and establish a "four lists" promotion mechanism for key enterprise scheduling, key policies and measures, major projects and key tasks.

【促进民营经济高质量发展·权威访谈】广东：改革赋能 进一步开创民营经济发展新局面

[Promoting high-quality Development of Private Economy · Authoritative Interview] Guangdong: Reform enables the development of private economy to further create a new situation

广东民营经济活跃、总量规模大。推动民营经济健康发展、高质量发展有哪些真招实招？今天（3月31日）的《促进民营经济高质量发展·权威访谈》，来看总台记者对广东省委副书记、省长王伟中的专访。

Guangdong's private economy is active and its total size is large. What are the real moves to promote the healthy and high-quality development of the private economy? Today (March 31), "Promoting the high-quality development of the Private economy, authoritative interview", to see the Head Office reporter's interview with Wang Weizhong, deputy secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and governor.

今年，广东要在抓好政策保障和优化服务上下功夫。

This year, Guangdong should work hard to ensure good policies and optimize services.

聚焦民营企业关切问题，广东通过改革赋能，打造市场化、法治化、国际化一流营商环境。在市场准入方面，全面落实“非禁即入”，完善民营企业参与重大项目建设长效机制，支持民间资本参股投资核电、高铁、风电等重大基础设施项目。

Focusing on the concerns of private enterprises, Guangdong has created a market-oriented, law-based and internationalized first-class business environment through reform and empowerment. In terms of market access, we will fully implement the "entry if it is not prohibited", improve the long-term mechanism for private enterprises to participate in the construction of major projects, and support private capital to participate in major infrastructure projects such as nuclear power, high-speed rail, and wind power.

今年，广东将进一步强化企业科技创新主体地位，推动重大科技基础设施向民营企业开放，鼓励民营企业建设高能级创新平台，提高核心竞争力。大力实施优质企业梯度培育“十百千万”计划，即造就50家科技领军企业、100家链主企业、1000家制造业单项冠军企业、10000家专精特新企业，形成梯次发展的创新型企业集群。

This year, Guangdong will further strengthen the dominant position of enterprises in scientific and technological innovation, promote the opening of major scientific and technological infrastructure to private enterprises, encourage private enterprises to build high-level innovation platforms, and improve their core competitiveness. Vigorously implement the "ten million" plan of gradient cultivation of high-quality enterprises, that is, create 50 technology leaders, 100 chain master enterprises, 1,000 manufacturing individual champion enterprises, 10,000 specialized and special new enterprises, and form a cascade of innovative enterprise clusters.

中国制造业采购经理指数加快扩张

China's manufacturing purchasing managers' index expanded at a faster pace

中国物流与采购联合会、国家统计局今天（3月31日）公布3月份中国采购经理指数。其中，制造业采购经理指数继续环比上升，企业生产经营活动有所加快，景气水平继续回升。

The China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing and the National Bureau of Statistics today (March 31) released the China Purchasing Managers Index for March. Among them, the manufacturing purchasing managers index continued to rise month-on-month, the production and operation activities of enterprises accelerated, and the prosperity level continued to rise.

3月份中国制造业采购经理指数为50.5%，较上月上升0.3个百分点，连续2个月运行在扩张区间。

China's manufacturing purchasing managers' index was 50.5% in March, up 0.3 percentage points from the previous month, and running in the expansion range for two consecutive months.

从主要分项指数看，制造业产需两端均在加快扩张。其中，铁路船舶航空航天设备、计算机通信电子设备等行业生产指数和新订单指数均升至55%以上的较高景气区间，相关行业产需加快释放。

From the main sub-index, both ends of the manufacturing industry are accelerating expansion. Among them, the production index and new order index of railway, ship, aerospace equipment, computer communication electronic equipment and other industries rose to a higher boom range of more than 55%, and the production demand of related industries was accelerated.

从重点行业看，新动能较快上升，新质生产力加快发展。3月份装备制造业、高技术制造业采购经理指数分别为52%、52.3%，较上月分别上升1.2和1.4个百分点。其中，装备制造业新订单指数创下2023年4月以来的新高。

From the perspective of key industries, new growth drivers are rising rapidly, and the development of new quality productivity is accelerating. In March, the purchasing managers' indexes of equipment manufacturing industry and high-tech manufacturing industry were 52% and 52.3%, respectively, up 1.2 and 1.4 percentage points from the previous month. Among them, the new orders index of the equipment manufacturing industry hit a new high since April 2023.

从企业类型看，大型企业采购经理指数继续位于扩张区间，中小企业采购经理指数虽然运行在50%以下，但与上月相比景气水平均有不同程度回升，尤其是小型企业改善较为明显，采购经理指数较上月大幅上升了3.3个百分点。

From the perspective of enterprise types, the purchasing managers' index of large enterprises continues to be in the expansion range, and the purchasing managers' index of small and medium-sized enterprises, although running below 50%, has rebounded to varying degrees compared with the previous month, especially the improvement of small enterprises is more obvious, and the purchasing managers' index increased significantly by 3.3 percentage points compared with the previous month.

《公共安全视频图像信息系统管理条例》4月1日起施行

The Regulation on the Management of Public Safety Video Image Information System came into effect on April 1

《公共安全视频图像信息系统管理条例》4月1日起施行，要求政府加强统筹规划，避免重复建设，合理确定图像采集设备的安装位置、角度和采集范围。禁止在旅馆、饭店等客房或包间内部等隐私区域安装图像采集设备设施，明确公安机关加强监督检查、建立备案制度。

The Regulations on the Management of Public Safety Video Image Information System came into effect on April 1, requiring the government to strengthen overall planning, avoid repetitive construction, and reasonably determine the installation location, Angle and collection range of image acquisition equipment. It is forbidden to install image acquisition equipment and facilities in private areas such as rooms or private rooms in hotels and restaurants, and it is clear that public security organs should strengthen supervision and inspection and establish a filing system.

2025年国家助学贷款免息及本金延期偿还

In 2025, national student loans will be interest-free and principal repayment will be deferred

财政部、教育部、中国人民银行、金融监管总局今天（3月31日）发布通知，从今年1月1日起，对今年及以前年度毕业的贷款学生2025年内应偿还的国家助学贷款利息予以免除，免除的利息由中央财政和地方财政分别承担；本金经贷款学生自主申请，可延期1年偿还。

The Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Education, the People's Bank of China and the General Administration of Financial Supervision issued a notice today (March 31), starting from January 1 this year, students who graduated this year and those who graduated in the previous year will be exempted from the interest on national student loans that should be repaid in 2025. The exempted interest will be borne by the central and local governments respectively. The principal can be deferred for one year upon application by the student.

德国新型运载火箭“光谱”首次发射失败

The first launch of Germany's new launch vehicle Spectra has failed

由德国私营航天企业——伊萨尔航天技术公司研制的一枚“光谱”运载火箭30日在挪威安岛航天中心发射升空后不久坠毁爆炸，未造成人员伤亡。安岛航天公司称，正与火箭制造方共同调查事故原因。此次发射是“光谱”火箭的首次试飞，“光谱”为中小型运载火箭，能够将约1吨的载荷送入近地轨道。

Ashima Space said it was working with the rocket's manufacturer to investigate the cause of the accident. The launch was the first test flight of the "Spectrum" rocket, a small and medium-sized launch vehicle capable of carrying a payload of about 1 ton into low Earth orbit.
