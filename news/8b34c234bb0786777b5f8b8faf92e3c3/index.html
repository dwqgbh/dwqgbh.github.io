航天电器邀您共赴2024年慕尼黑国际电子元器件博览会(electronica 2024)

贵州航天电器股份有限公司 2024-11-09

01


展会介绍
Exhibition Profile




2024年11月12日，2024年慕尼黑国际电子元器件博览会（electronica 2024）将于德国慕尼黑国际展览中心拉开帷幕。作为全球电子行业的顶级盛会之一，慕尼黑国际电子元器件博览会每两年举办一次，是全球电子元器件及电子行业技术展示的重要平台。本届展会共涵盖17个展馆，吸引了全球超3000家参展商，展示范围涉及汽车、无线技术、医疗电子、新能源等众多前沿领域。展会预计将吸引超10万名专业观众。

On 12 November 2024, Electronica will kick off at the Munich International Exhibition Centre in Munich, Germany. As one of the top events in the global electronics industry, Electronica is held every two years and is an important platform for the global electronic components and electronics industry to showcase their technologies. This year's show covers 17 exhibition halls, attracting more than 3,000 exhibitors from all over the world, displaying a wide range of cutting-edge fields such as automotive, wireless technology, medical electronics, new energy, etc. The show is expected to attract more than 100,000 exhibitors. The exhibition is expected to attract more than 100,000 professional visitors.
