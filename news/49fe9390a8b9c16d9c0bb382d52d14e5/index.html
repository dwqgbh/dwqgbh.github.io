走进2022进博会|澳莆公司与您共话“绿色建筑材料工程竹的技术与未来”

altPlus澳莆 2022-11-09


2022年11月5日，以“新时代,共享未来”为主题的第五届中国国际进口博览会(China International Import Expo,简称进博会)在上海国家会展中心拉开帷幕,共有145个国家、地区和国际组织参展。11月7日，澳莆公司创始人林利群女士受邀参加由安永公司举办的“全球绿色建筑与技术的中国机遇” 专题研讨会，在进博会上传递“绿色建筑材料工程竹的低碳之声”。 

On November 5th, 2022, the 5th China International Import Expo with the theme of "New Era, Shared Future" opened in Shanghai National Convention and Exhibition Center, 145 countries, regions and international organizations participating in it in total. On November 7th, Mrs. Lin Liqun, alPlus’s co-founder, was invited to attend the round table of "China Opportunities for Global Green Building and Technology Adoption" held by Ernst &Young and delivered the "Low Carbon Voice of Structual Engineered Bamboo in Green Building Materials " at the Expo.





专题研讨会围绕 “绿色建筑和技术全球的发展情况以及在中国的发展前景”、“传统建筑行业实现绿色转型的路径与方法”、“绿色建筑与技术浪潮下的投资机遇”等热门话题，汇聚一众来自行业一线的专家与企业家共同探讨“碳中和”背景下的绿色建筑发展趋势及未来。林女士表示：作为竹材料研究和技术领域的全球最主要领导者之一，澳莆公司一直专注于通过集成生物材料的性能来重新定义商业、工业和基础设施领域的解决方案。近年来，随着我国推行绿色节能建筑的发展和“碳达峰、碳中和”的政策目标，大量木材被应用到建筑领域，相比木材而言，竹材作为一种生物质材料，生长周期短、可再生能力强、易降解，在生长过程中能存储二氧化碳改善环境，生产过程能耗低。从生产、加工、使用、废弃处理的全生命期来看，以竹代木更加节能环保，而澳莆公司从北美引进的先进的工程竹技术将为现今的市场注入新鲜的血液。 

The round table focused on such hot topics as "The global development of green buildings & technologies and their development situation in China", "The path and method of green transformation for traditional architectural industry", and "Investment opportunities in the wave of green buildings and technologies" . Mrs Lin said, as one of the worlds’ foremost leaders in private, bamboo research and technology, altPlus aims to standardize and accelerate the adoption of performance bamboo solutions and redefine the solutions in commercial, industrial and infrastructure fields. With the development of green & energy-saving buildings and the policy goal of "Carbon peak, Carbon neutral" in China, mass timber has been used in the construction field. Compared with wood, Bamboo, as a kind of biomass material, has short growth cycle, is rapidly renewable, and has high mechanical fiber properties. It can store carbon dioxide to improve the environment during its growth process, and has low energy consumption in the production process. Therefore, it is more energy saving and environmental protection to replace wood with bamboo, especially the advanced engineering bamboo technology brought by altPlus from North America will inject fresh blood into the current market. 






当讨论全球绿色建筑与技术在中国的运用过程中会面临哪些挑战的话题时，林女士谈到，在工程竹技术领域，我们面临着成本控制、标准规范与知识共享三大挑战，为了解决这些问题，过去几年中，我们加强了团队内部与当地政府和合作伙伴之间的沟通，近期我们与南京林业大学生物质材料国家工程研究中心和福建省邵武市政府合作，共建邵武工程竹材料生产基地，推进科研成果转化，以加快制造工艺的迭代更新，从而降低成本。另一方面，中国工程建设标准化协会批准了三部工程竹相关技术标准，分别是《工程竹结构设计标准》、《工程竹结构施工及质量验收标准》和《工程竹结构检测标准》，自2022年11月1日起施行。上述三部标准为世界首批针对工程竹结构的设计、施工、验收、检测的系列技术标准，有力地解决了因相关标准的缺失，严重制约工程竹结构的推广应用问题。有了这三部标准，我们更有信心抢占工程竹产业“制高点”，推动绿色可再生材料的全球应用。 

When discussing what challenges the global green building and technology adoption will face in China, Mrs. Lin said, in our area of expertise, we face the following three major challenges in technology adoption: Cost, Codes of standards and Knowledge sharing. To address these problems, we have strengthened the communication between our team and the local government, partners in the past few years, we cooperated with Shaowu Municipal Government, Fujian Province and National Engineering Research Center of Biomaterials (NERC) of Nanjing Forestry University to establish Engineered Bamboo Composite R&D facility. Promote the transformation of scientific research achievements to advance manufacturing processes, so as to reduce costs. On the other hand, China Association for Engineering Construction Standardization has launched three codes of standards related to engineering bamboo structures (EBC), they are Design Standards for EBC, Construction and Quality Acceptance Standards for EBC, and Testing Standards for EBC, which will be implemented from November 1st, 2022. With these three codes of standards, we are more confident to seize the peak of engineering bamboo industry and promote the global application of green renewable materials.  




关于知识分享的挑战，林女士表示，澳莆公司一直致力于发挥桥梁纽带作用，推进行业交流。进博会作为一个高水平对外开放的平台，不仅是商品和经济机会交流的平台，也是创意与智慧沟通的平台，我们期望在绿色产业领域全球最前沿的技术、创意通过进博会引入国内市场，结合澳莆公司本土化实践，一定会为全球客户提供更丰富多元的工程竹产品和技术解决方案，共创全新未来。 

Regarding the challenge of knowledge sharing, Mrs. Lin said that altPlus has always been committed to playing a role as a bridge to promote industrial knowledge exchanges, as a world-class open platform, the Expo is not only a platform for exchange of goods and economic opportunities, but also a platform for communication of creativity and wisdom. We hope that the world's most cutting-edge technology and creativity in the field of green industry will be introduced into  domestic market through the Expo. Combining with altPlus' practice, it’s certainly that we can provide more diversified engineering bamboo products and technical solutions for global customers to create a better future together.   



研讨会的最后，林女士重申了澳莆公司拥抱全球化机遇的信心，在绿色可持续发展的战略指引下，澳莆公司将继续发挥在创新技术和绿色低碳领域的强大优势，始终以客户需求为核心，不断拓宽对生物质材料的想象力，与更多合作伙伴一起积极探索绿色产业发展的新可能。 


At the end of the seminar, Mrs. Lin reasserted altPlus' confidence in embracing the opportunities of globalization. Under the strategy of green and sustainable development, altPlus will continue to give full play to the advantages in low-carbon technology fields, always focus on client’s needs, constantly broaden the imagination on biomass materials, and actively explore new possibilities for green industry development with more partners.
