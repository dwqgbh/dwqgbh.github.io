关于北京商务航空协会
北京商务航空协会成立于2016年12月，2017年3月经北京市民政局正式批准并颁发社会团体法人执照，是国内第一家专门以发展商务航空为主要目的的行业协会组织，是目前国内商务航空领域唯一一家具有全国性的、有产业代表性和影响力的社会团体组织。
协会将充分利用行业资源优势，以“政府主导 协会引领 企业参与”的创新合作模式，以发扬“前瞻思考 跨界衔接 行业先锋 协同发展”的工作精神，协助政府部门，联合广大会员单位发展商务航空相关产业，共同打造符合商务航空行业业态的营商环境，促进行业全面发展。
About China Business Aviation Association

  China Business Aviation Association was established in December 2016 and officially approved by the Beijing Municipal Civil Affairs Bureau in March 2017, which issued the association's social group legal person license. It is the first industry association organization in China dedicated primarily to the development of business aviation and currently the only nationwide, industry-representative, and influential social group organization in the domestic business aviation sector.

The association will fully leverage its industry resource advantages, adopting an innovative cooperation model of "government leadership, association guidance, and enterprise participation." By promoting a work spirit of "forward-thinking, cross-industry collaboration, industry pioneering, and coordinated development," the association will assist government departments and unite its member units to develop business aviation-related industries. Together, they will create a business environment that aligns with the characteristics of the business aviation industry and promote comprehensive development within the industry.
