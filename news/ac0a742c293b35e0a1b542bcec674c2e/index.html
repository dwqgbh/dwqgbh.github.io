华烁新能源产品闪耀2025汉诺威工业博览会

华烁高科新能源 2025-04-01

2025汉诺威工业博览会(HANNOVER MESSE 2025)的主题是“赋能工业可持续发展”，聚焦人工智能、能源转型等议题。

   The theme of this year's HANNOVER MESSE 2025 exhibition is "Empowering Sustainable Industrial Development", focusing on topics such as AI and energy transition.

作为服务于全球的新能源产品专业供应商，华烁高科参加本届汉诺威工业博览会，在12号展馆向世界各地区与国家的客户展示公司近年在新能源领域的科技创新与产品迭代，吸引并得到了海外诸多客户的认可与关注。

As a professional supplier of new energy products serving the global market, Huashuo Gaoke is participating in this exhibition. In Hall 12, we will showcase their recent technological innovations and product iterations in the field,It has attracted and received recognition and attention from many overseas customers.

华烁始终致力于提供一站式绿色智慧能源解决方案，公司重点展示行业领先的大功率液冷超充系统、智能充电机器人，以及全系列智能化充电产品，包括高效直流快充设备和智能交流充电设备等产品。公司还创新研发了工商业级智能光伏储能系统、绿色智能家居解决方案等，通过持续的技术创新，为海内外客户提供安全、可靠、高效、卓越的产品与服务，助力全球能源体系的数字化绿色转型。

Huashuo is always dedicated to providing one-stop green smart energy solutions.They will highlight industry-leading high-power liquid-cooled ultra-fast charging systems,intelligent charging robots,and a full range of smart charging products,including efficient DC fast-charging equipment and smart AC charging equipment,. The company has also developed the industrial and commercial PV energy storage system,green smart home solutions,etc. Through continuous technological innovation,they provide safe,reliable,efficient,and outstanding products and services to customers at home and abroad,aiding in the global energy transformation.

    大功率超级充电系统包括液冷主机和充电终端两部分，整机输出功率为360kW-960kW，充电终端数量可配置，单枪最大输出功率600kW。产品满足NACS, CHAOJI等标准，极速充电、超快补能，极大提升海内外新能源电动汽车用户的充电体验！

The high power supercharging system consists of a liquid-cooled host and charging terminal,with a total output power of 360kW to 960kW.The number of charging terminals is configurable,with a maximum output power of 600kW per charging port.It conforms to standards such as NACS,CHAOJI, etc.Ultra-fast charging and ultra-fast replenishment greatly improve the charging experience of new energy electric vehicle users at home and abroad.

智能充电机器人采用全新的产品形态架构，将充电桩、储能系统和智能移动机器人融合创新，可实现新能源汽车在多种复杂场景下的自动充电功能。满足IEC标准。

The smart charging robot adopts a new product form structure, integrating charging piles, energy storage systems,and smart mobile robots for innovative purposes,enabling automatic charging functions for new energy vehicles in various complex scenarios.It meets the standards of IEC.

工商业智能光伏储能系统利用园区的闲置屋顶进行光伏发电。

The industrial and commercial PV energy storage system uses the idle roof of the park to generate photovoltaic power.

该系统利用光伏和储能为园区提供日夜供电，有效降低电费账单。

The system utilizes photovoltaic and energy storage to provide electricity for the park day and night,and effectively reduces the electricity bill.

同时，园区还可申请并网进行短期电力交易，实现园区能源系统经济价值最大化。

At the same time,the park can apply for grid-connected short-term power trading to maximize the economic benefits of the park's energy system.

华烁高科将持续加强科技创新，积极拓展海外市场，致力于以清洁能源技术服务全球更多国家和地区的客户。公司正在寻求全球战略合作伙伴，代理商，在全领域进行商务合作，期待与您共同建设美好绿色未来！

Huashuo Gaoke continues to strengthen technological innovation and actively expands overseas markets,committed to serving customers in more countries and regions around the world with clean energy technology.The company is seeking global strategic partners and agents for business cooperation across all fields.
