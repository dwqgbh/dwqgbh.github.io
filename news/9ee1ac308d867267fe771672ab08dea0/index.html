INVITATION | CIIE 2026 Swiss Centers Online Webinar

瑞士中心 2026-04-07

2026进博会瑞士中心线上宣讲会

CIIE 2026 Swiss Centers Online Webinar

您是希望进入中国市场、或进一步拓展在华业务的瑞士企业吗？


瑞士中心诚邀您加入中国国际进口博览会瑞士馆，共拓中国商机。

Are you a Swiss company looking to enter or expand your products in the  Chinese market? Join the Swiss Pavilion at China International Import Expo (CIIE).





我们诚挚邀请您参加本次线上宣讲会，共同探讨入驻中国国际进口博览会（CIIE）瑞士馆，如何为企业解锁实实在在的在华增长机遇。本次活动特别邀请到中国国际进口博览局官方代表出席分享，带来权威解读。


自 2018 年首届进博会举办以来，Swiss Centers（瑞士中心）便一直作为瑞士商界参与进博会的官方组展机构。在上届进博会上，瑞士馆汇聚了 36 个食品饮料及消费品领域的瑞士品牌，充分彰显了中国市场对高品质瑞士产品的强劲需求。


We warmly invite you to join our upcoming webinar, where we’ll explore how  participating in the Swiss Pavilion at the China International Import Expo (CIIE) can help unlock real, tangible growth opportunities in China. We are pleased to announce that the CIIE Bureau will be joining us as guest speakers to share their  insights. 


Swiss Centers is the official organiser of Swiss business participation since the very first edition in 2018. At the last edition, the Swiss Pavilion brought together 36  Swiss brands in food & beverage and consumer goods, highlighting the strong  demand for high-quality Swiss products.





参会亮点 Why attend?

  • 深入了解作为全球顶级展会之一的进博会，其规模与战略价值

  • 掌握瑞士企业入驻瑞士馆的高效参与方式

  • 学习展前、展中、展后全周期最大化参展效益的实战策略

  • 聆听行业嘉宾一线经验与深度洞察

  • 现场问答环节，一对一解答您的疑问

  • Understand the scale and strategic importance of CIIE as one of the world’s  largest trade shows 

  • Learn how Swiss companies can successfully participate in the Swiss Pavilion 

  • Discover strategies to maximize your engagement before, during, and after the event 

  • Gain insights from guest speakers with first-hand experience 

  • Get your questions answered during a live Q&A session


适合群体 Who should attend?

本次线上宣讲会面向所有有意开拓或深化中国市场布局的瑞士企业及相关人士，不限规模。与我们一同启程，迈出拓展在华业务的关键一步。


This webinar is open to companies of all sizes and individuals interested in  exploring or strengthening their presence in the Chinese market. Join us andtake the first step toward expanding your business in China.
