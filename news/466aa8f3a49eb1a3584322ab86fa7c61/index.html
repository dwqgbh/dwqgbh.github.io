2025广交会,SENG CUCINE智造未来家居,定义全球高质生活新标准

每日财经 2025-04-29

近日，全球家居行业的焦点汇聚于第137届中国广交会。作为高端不锈钢定制家居行业的领军品牌，SENG CUCINE携社交厨房、智纳卫浴、智净阳台这三大核心空间定制方案亮相，展会现场产品得到广泛的认可和关注，国外客商咨询、洽谈场面异常火爆！
Recently, the global home furnishing industry turned its focus to the 137th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair). As a leading brand in the high-end stainless steel custom home furnishing sector, SENG CUCINE made a remarkable appearance with its three core customized spatial solutions: the Social Kitchen, Intelligent Storage Bathroom, and Intelligent Purity Balcony. The exhibited products garnered widespread recognition and attention, with international buyers flocking to inquire and negotiate, creating an exceptionally bustling scene at the booth.
SENG CUCINE拥有行业首家5G黑灯标杆工厂，生产车间全面引入AI技术，同时通过MES生产管理系统、AGV物流车、智能立体仓储等，实现生产、物流高度智能化和自动化，智能制造赋能产业极速发展。（官网：SENG CUCINE不锈钢定制家居）
SENG CUCINE boasts the industry’s first 5G-enabled “lights-out” benchmark factory, where AI technology has been fully integrated into the production workshops. By leveraging advanced systems such as MES (Manufacturing Execution System）， AGV logistics vehicles, and intelligent automated storage, the company has achieved highly intelligent and automated production and logistics, empowering rapid industrial advancement through smart manufacturing.
SENG CUCINE由意大利殿堂级设计师Enrico Tonucci领衔，国内顶尖设计师荟萃，引领不锈钢家居时尚风潮，产品覆盖中西厨空间系统、卧房系统、亲水系统、客餐厅社交系统、墙护系统、户外餐厨系统这六大空间系统。
Under the leadership of Italian master designer Enrico Tonucci and a team of China’s top designers, SENG CUCINE is setting trends in stainless steel home fashion. Its product portfolio spans six major spatial systems: Chinese & Western Kitchen Spaces, Bedroom Systems, Hydrophilic Systems, Living & Dining Social Systems, Wall Protection Systems, and Outdoor Kitchen Systems.
SENG CUCINE始终以用户为核心，致力于服务质量的建设，从前期服务、生成与试装、安装服务到售后保障，每一环节严苛至微，力求为每一位用户提供1V1专属理想服务。
Committed to a customer-centric philosophy, SENG CUCINE prioritizes service excellence at every stage—from pre-sale consultation, production and trial fitting, installation, to after-sales support—ensuring meticulous attention to detail and 1-on-1 personalized service for every client.
通过持续的设计创新、工艺升级、智能制造和全球化战略，SENG CUCINE正在重新定义高端不锈钢定制家居行业的标准，颠覆国际对中国智造的传统认知，提升全球用户居住舒适度和便利性，引领未来高质生活方式新潮流。
Through continuous design innovation, craftsmanship upgrades, intelligent manufacturing and globalization strategies, SENG CUCINE is redefining standards in the high-end stainless steel customized home industry, transforming traditional international perceptions of “Made in China,” while enhancing global users' living comfort and convenience to lead new trends in premium lifestyles.
（官网：SENG CUCINE不锈钢定制家居）
