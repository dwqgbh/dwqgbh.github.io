关于2022北京国际汽车展览会(AUTO CHINA 2022)停止举办的通知

北京国际汽车展览会 2022-11-10

尊敬的各参展商、各合作单位及各界观众：


鉴于国内疫情防控形势，北京国际汽车展览会联合办展各单位经与多方协商后决定，停止在2022年举办第十七届北京国际汽车展览会的计划，具体举办日期另行通知。


因展会停止举办而给各展商和相关单位及广大观众带来的诸多不便，我们深表歉意！


春日不迟，相逢终有时。衷心感谢国内外业界同仁和社会各界人士对北京国际汽车展览会的支持和关注！


特此通知。


中国国际贸易促进委员会汽车行业分会

中国机械国际合作股份有限公司

中国国际展览中心集团有限公司

中国汽车工程学会


2022年11月10日




Notice on the Suspension of

 AUTO CHINA 2022


November 10, 2022

 


Dear exhibitors, partners and visitors,


Facing severe challenge for the country’s epidemic prevention and control, the Organizing Committee of the 2022 (17th) Beijing International Automobile Exhibition (AUTO CHINA 2022), after a few of rounds of internal discussions among co-organizers, have decided not to hold the auto show in 2022, and we will announce the new specific holding date separately once available.

 

We apologize for the inconvenience caused to exhibitors, relevant parties and the audience due to the suspension of the exhibition!

 

Just like that the Spring may show up without delay, our reunion will eventually come true at the right time. We sincerely thank colleagues from the auto industry and people from all walks of life for their support to the Beijing International Automobile Exhibition.

 

Thank you for your attention.

 

 

China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Automotive Sub-Council

 

China National Machinery Industry International Co., Ltd.

 

China International Exhibition Center Group Corporation

 

China Society of Automotive Engineers
