Amisco, a manufacturer of micro medical electromagnetic navigation coils, has made a significant technological breakthrough in the field of micro medical electromagnetic navigation. It has successfully developed ultra-fine micro coil technology, compressing the coil diameter to 0.008mm-0.2mm, which is only 1/5 the diameter of a human hair. This ultra-fine coil adopts a high-precision winding process, enabling high-density coil winding while maintaining extremely high accuracy and reliability.

Application fields of micro-coils: vascular implant imaging, ear, nose and throat navigation sensors, electromagnetic navigation sensors, electromagnetic catheter position sensors, electrophysiological sensing coils, endoscope positioning coils.

Amisco社はマイクロ医療電磁ナビゲーションの分野で、成功的に超微細コイル技術を開発し、コイルの直径を圧縮0.008mm-0.2mm、人間の髪の毛の直径の1/5です。この極細コイルは高精度の巻線工程を採用しており、高い精度と信頼性を維持しながら高密度のコイル巻きを実現しています。
