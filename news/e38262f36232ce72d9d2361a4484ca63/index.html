临工智科邀您相约2025秋季广交会,共创智能制造新未来!

临工智科LGIM 2025-10-09

尊敬的客户与合作伙伴：
金秋十月，临工智科将盛装亮相第138届中国进出口商品交易会（广交会），为您展示最新一代移动机器人及智能物流解决方案。我们诚邀您莅临参观，共同探索工业4.0时代的无限商机。

  • 观展指南   Exhibition Guide

展会时间：10月15日-10月19日

Exhibition Dates: Oct 15–19

展会地点：广州 中国进出口商品交易会展馆

Exhibition Venue: China Import and Export Fair Complex, Guangzhou
展位号：20.1馆 D06-07 展位

Booth Number: Hall 20.1, Booth D06-07

  • 展品介绍  Exhibit Introduction

智能物流两款产品分别是智慧AMR和无人叉车，智慧AMR采用激光SLAM+二维码复合导航，运行灵活高效，该产线是智能物流和智慧工厂应用的典型案例，目前已在多家新能源企业落地应用，该款AMR采用自主研发的车辆集成控制器，运行稳定、调度灵活、轻巧节能、性价比高，广泛应用于汽车、3C、食品、医药等行业的智能产线、智能物流领域。

The two products of intelligent logistics are the intelligent AMR and the unmanned forklift. The intelligent AMR adopts laser SLAM + two-dimensional code composite navigation, which enables it to operate flexibly and efficiently. This production line is a typical case of the application of intelligent logistics and smart factories. Currently, it has been implemented and applied in many new energy enterprises. This AMR is equipped with a vehicle integrated controller independently developed by us, featuring stable operation, flexible scheduling, lightness, energy conservation, and high cost-effectiveness. It is widely used in the intelligent production lines and intelligent logistics fields of industries such as automobiles, 3C, food, and pharmaceuticals.

无人叉车采用自研控制系统，运行稳定，安全可靠，是目前在智能物流领域最先进的高科技产品。临工智科无人叉车可支持反光板和激光SLAM两种导航模式，目前最大可支持3吨负载的举升转运操作。同时可与机器人、流水线等设备联动协作，是厂内货物搬运及库内堆高、码垛等工作需求的“搬运神器”。

The unmanned forklift adopts a self-developed control system, ensuring stable operation, safety and reliability. It is currently the most advanced high-tech product in the field of intelligent logistics. The LGMG Robotics unmanned forklift can support two navigation modes, namely reflector navigation and laser SLAM navigation. At present, it can support lifting and transfer operations with a maximum load of 3 tons. Meanwhile, it can cooperate in with devices such as robots and assembly lines, making it a cooperate in linkage for meeting the requirements of tasks like in-factory cargo handling, as well as stacking and palletizing inside warehouses.

公司为国家级高新技术企业，先后获得山东省智能制造系统解决方案供应商、山东省新型研发机构、省级工业设计中心、山东省“专精特新”企业、山东省“制造业单项冠军”、山东省瞪羚企业、山东省“省长杯”工业设计大赛金奖、国家专精特新“小巨人”企业等。

The company is a national high - tech enterprise. It has successively been recognized as a supplier of intelligent manufacturing system solutions in Shandong Province, a new - type research and development institution in Shandong Province, a provincial - level industrial design center, a "specialized, refined, distinctive and innovative" enterprise in Shandong Province, a "single - item champion in manufacturing industry" in Shandong Province, a gazelle enterprise in Shandong Province, and has won the gold medal in the "Governor's Cup" Industrial Design Competition in Shandong Province. It has also been designated as a national "little giant" enterprise specializing in niche markets with its specialized, refined, distinctive and innovative products or services.
