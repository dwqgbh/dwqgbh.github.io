聚焦医疗设备网络安全,2026IHE大会将于3月27日重庆举行

华卫科技 2026-01-09


2026年迪拜国际医疗器械展（WHX DUBAI）将于2月9日至12日在迪拜展览中心举行。作为中东地区规模领先、影响力广泛的医疗行业盛会，预计将吸引全球上百个国家的数千家顶尖企业与数以万计的专业观众共襄盛举。



The World Health Expo (WHX DUBAI) 2026 is set to take place from February 9th to 12th at the Dubai Exhibition Centre. As a leading and influential healthcare industry event in the Middle East, it is expected to bring together thousands of top enterprises and tens of thousands of professional visitors from numerous countries worldwide.


在这场汇聚全球智慧的舞台上，华卫科技已是多年的“熟面孔”。多年受邀参展，标志着我们的产品实力与品牌影响力获得了国际市场的持续认可。公司旗下多款产品已通过 欧盟CE MDR认证、美国FDA认证 等全球主要市场权威准入，业务覆盖 全球170余个国家和地区。



On this global stage of innovation, Wuhan Huawei Technology is a seasoned participant. Our consecutive annual invitations underscore the sustained international recognition of our product strength and brand influence. Multiple product lines have obtained authoritative certifications for major global markets, including EU CE MDR and US FDA certifications. Our business now serves clients in over 170 countries and regions.


本次展会，华卫科技将携 医用功能性伤口敷料系列、个人护理系列、全新造口护理系列、医用膏贴系列 产品重磅登场。

我们诚挚邀请全球合作伙伴与客户莅临 HS5-F 展位，交流洽谈、共谋发展！



At this exhibition, Wuhan Huawei Technology will proudly present our Medical Functional Wound Dressing Series, Personal Care Series, Ostomy Care Series, and Medical Plaster Series.

We sincerely invite global partners and customers to visit us at Booth HS5-F. Let's connect, exchange ideas, and explore opportunities for mutual growth.


WHX DUBAI
