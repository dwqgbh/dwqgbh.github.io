中国进出口商品交易会(广交会)

CHINA IMPORT AND EXPORT FAIR (CANTON FAIR)

商务对话（中英对照）

场景1：问候与自我介绍（Greeting & Self-introduction）

A (Supplier, 张三): Good morning! Welcome to the 139th Canton Fair. I'm Zhang San from Guangzhou ABC Trading Co., Ltd.  

早上好！欢迎参加第139届广交会。我是广州ABC贸易有限公司的张三。  

B (Client, 美国客户): Good morning! I'm David Miller from New York. Nice to meet you.  

早上好！我是来自纽约的大卫·米勒。很高兴认识你。  

A: Nice to meet you too, Mr. Miller. Would you like to take a look at our product samples first?  

我也很高兴认识您，米勒先生。您想先看看我们的产品样品吗？  

B: Sure, that would be great.  

当然，那太好了。

---

场景2：展会与公司介绍（Exhibition & Company Introduction）

A: This is our booth for the 2026 Spring Canton Fair, focusing on smart household appliances and sustainable products.  

这是我们2026年春季广交会的展位，主要展示智能家电和可持续发展相关产品。  

B: Oh, your company has a strong presence here. How long have you been in this industry?  

哦，贵公司在展会的展示很有规模。你们进入这个行业多久了？  

A: We've been specialized in household appliances for over 15 years, with exports to more than 30 countries.  

我们专注家电行业超过15年，产品出口到30多个国家。  

B: That's impressive. I'm interested in your energy-efficient refrigerators.  

真厉害。我对你们的节能冰箱很感兴趣。

---

场景3：产品咨询与展示（Product Inquiry & Demonstration）

A: This is our latest model, with 30% lower energy consumption than traditional ones.  

这是我们的最新款，能耗比传统产品低30%。  

B: Could you show me how it works? And what's the warranty period?  

你能演示一下它的操作吗？保修期是多久？  

A: Of course. Let me demonstrate the smart temperature control. The warranty is 2 years.  

当然。我来演示智能温控功能。保修期是2年。  

B: The design is modern. Does it come in different colors?  

设计很现代。有不同颜色可选吗？  

A: Yes, we offer white, silver and black options to match different home styles.  

有的，我们提供白色、银色和黑色，适配不同家居风格。

---

场景4：价格与MOQ洽谈（Price & MOQ Negotiation）

B: What's your FOB Guangzhou price for 500 units?  

500台的广州离岸价（FOB）是多少？  

A: The unit price is $180 for orders over 300 units. Our MOQ is 200 units.  

订单量超过300台的话，单价是180美元。我们的最小起订量（MOQ）是200台。  

B: Can you give us a 5% discount if we increase the order to 1000 units?  

如果我们把订单量增加到1000台，能给5%的折扣吗？  

A: We can offer a 3% discount for 1000 units, which is our best price for this model.  

1000台的话我们可以给3%的折扣，这是这款产品的最低价了。  

B: That's acceptable. Let's discuss the delivery time next.  

可以接受。接下来我们谈谈交货时间吧。

---

场景5：样品与交付（Samples & Delivery）

A: We can send you a free sample within 3 days, but you'll need to cover the shipping cost.  

我们可以在3天内寄免费样品给您，但运费需要您承担。  

B: That's fine. How long does mass production take after we place the order?  

没问题。下订单后批量生产需要多久？  

A: It takes 45 days to finish production, and we can arrange shipment by the end of June.  

生产需要45天，我们可以在6月底安排发货。  

B: Perfect. We need the goods by mid-July for our summer promotion.  

太好了。我们需要在7月中旬收到货物用于夏季促销。

---

场景6：预约后续会面（Booking a Follow-up Meeting）

B: I'd like to discuss the contract details with your manager. Can we arrange a meeting tomorrow?  

我想和你们经理讨论合同细节。我们能明天安排会面吗？  

A: Sure. How about 10 a.m. at your hotel's business center?  

当然。上午10点在您酒店的商务中心怎么样？  

B: That works for me. I'll bring the draft contract with me.  

可以。我会带上合同草案。  

A: Great. I'll confirm the meeting with our manager and send you a reminder email later.  

好的。我会和经理确认会面，之后给您发提醒邮件。
