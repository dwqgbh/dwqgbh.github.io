CISMA2025（中国国际缝制设备展览会）即将于9月24日-27日在上海新国际博览中心隆重开幕！

本届CISMA以“智慧缝制赋能产业新质发展”为主题，作为全球规模最大、最具影响力的专业展会，这里汇聚了全球顶尖智慧与创新力量，引领行业开辟发展新领域新赛道、塑造发展新动能新优势。

布易科技将在E1-A41展位，期待与您携手，以数字化之力，智造时尚新未来！

CISMA 2025 (China International Sewing Equipment Exhibition) will grandly open at the Shanghai New International Expo Center from September 24th to 27th!

This year's CISMA, themed "Smart Sewing Empowers New-Quality Industrial Development," is the largest and most influential professional exhibition in the world. It brings together the world's top wisdom and innovation, leading the industry to explore new areas and tracks for development, and shaping new momentum and advantages.

Buyi Technology will be at booth E1-A41. We look forward to working with you to intelligently create a new fashion future with the power of digitalization!

ET产品


01

ET服装版型的数字化版权保护

服装企业的版型数据是企业的核心数字资产。ET为客户提供了可靠的服装版型版权保护机制，保护企业核心利益。同时，数字化版权的确立也让版型资源在供应链内安全传输，合作更安心。

Pattern data is the core digital asset of apparel companies. ET offers reliable copyright protection to safeguard these assets and ensure secure resource sharing across the supply chain, giving enterprises greater confidence in collaboration.


02

ET数字化柔性供应链

ET打通了设计到生产的数据壁垒，让生产与设计无缝对接，以ET线上+线下的模式实现数据闭环，降本增效，形成一个高效、透明且安全的系统环境，在确保数据的安全与准确的前提下，为客户提供精准的供应链管理。

ET connects design and production with a seamless data flow. Its online + offline model creates a closed-loop system that reduces costs, improves efficiency, and ensures transparency and security. With reliable data management, ET empowers precise and efficient supply chain control.


03

ET版型参数化技术

ET参数化的版型可以与部位尺寸结合，实现基于尺寸的版型驱动，只需调整关键参数即可快速生成新样版，通过ET的3D数据接口，还能够实现与3D仿真系统的数据交互。

通过ET智能版型库，版师可以自由选择需要参数化的对象，灵活高效地处理各种纸样数据。不仅提升了效率，还让版型更具适配性，轻松应对市场和多样化需求。

ET’s parametric technology links patterns with part dimensions, enabling rapid pattern creation by adjusting key parameters. Its 3D data interface also connects seamlessly with 3D simulation systems.
With the Intelligent Pattern Library, designers can flexibly parameterize and process diverse pattern data, boosting efficiency and adaptability to meet fast-changing market demands.


04

ETICM数字化版房信息系统

  • 通过ET数据接口收集数据

    Collect data through the ET data interface

  • 分析并形成数字化制版规范

    Analyze and generate digital platemaking specifications

  • 建立企业内部规范库

    Establish an internal specifications library

在服装制造领域，从依赖经验的传统制版模式向精准高效的数据驱动模式转型，已成为企业提升核心竞争力的关键路径。ETICM应运而生，致力于全方位赋能企业构建并应用企业级数字化制版规范，引领服装企业实现根本性变革。

In apparel manufacturing, shifting from experience-based to data-driven pattern making is key to competitiveness. ETICM helps enterprises build and apply digital pattern-making standards, driving true transformation in the industry.


05

基于AI的版型库管理及应用

AI在图像处理方面的卓越能力，结合到版型库和部件库技术，可以实现快速版型匹配或部件组合，提升制版效率。融合云端与线下灵活部署模式，为企业打造智能化、高效化、标准化的版房管理新生态。

ET以AI人工智能技术为驱动，结合ETCAD、ETPDM系统，构建从设计到生产的一站式数字化基础平台，助力企业迈向“快速反应，敏捷制造”的时代。

ET leverages AI image processing with pattern and component libraries to speed up matching and assembly, greatly improving pattern-making efficiency. With both cloud and offline deployment, it builds a smart, efficient, and standardized pattern room ecosystem.
Integrated with ETCAD and ETPDM, ET provides a one-stop digital platform from design to production, enabling agile manufacturing and faster market response.


06

ET云端自动排

ET提供具有超强计算力的云端自动排料服务，无需配置高昂的设备，无需安装，打开即用，只要一个账号，即可通过云端享受专业级排料服务，智能推荐排料时长，实现省料增效。

ET offers a cloud-based automatic nesting service with powerful computing performance. No hardware or installation is needed—just log in and start. With one account, users access professional-grade nesting anytime, with intelligent time recommendations to save materials and boost efficiency.
