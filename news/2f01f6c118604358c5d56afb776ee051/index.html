汇聚多元力量,共促公益创新:粤港澳大湾区国际公益中心在2025亚洲公益论坛隆重揭牌

亚洲联合文化艺术中心 2025-12-01


在2025亚洲公益论坛上开幕日，一项标志着粤港澳大湾区公益事业迈向新阶段的里程碑事件诞生——粤港澳大湾区国际公益中心正式揭牌成立。揭牌仪式多位来自政、企、慈善界的领袖人物共同见证了这一历史性时刻。On the opening day of the 2025 Asian Philanthropy Forum, a milestone event marking the new stage of philanthropy in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA) was achieved – the official inauguration of the GBA International Philanthropy Center. This historic moment was jointly witnessed by many leaders from the government, business, and philanthropy sectors at the unveiling ceremony.


仪式伊始，海南亚洲文化艺术基金会副理事长苏彤首先登台，详细阐述了海南亚洲文化艺术基金会发起单位和佛山市顺德区乐从镇人民政府联合成立粤港澳大湾区国际公益中心的战略背景与时代意义。他指出，该中心的构想源于对区域公益资源整合与国际化合作的迫切需求，旨在依托粤港澳大湾区的政策优势、经济活力与人文底蕴，打造一个集公益研究、项目孵化、资源对接与国际交流于一体的高端平台，以应对亚洲乃至全球面临的共同挑战。At the start of the ceremony, Su Tong, Vice Chairman of the Hainan Asian Culture and Art Foundation, took the stage first and elaborated on the strategic background and contemporary significance of establishing the GBA International Philanthropy Center through the joint efforts of the Hainan Asian Culture and Art Foundation (initiating organization) and the People's Government of Lecong Town, Shunde District, Foshan City. He pointed out that the concept of the center stems from the urgent need for integrating regional philanthropic resources and promoting international cooperation. It aims to leverage the policy advantages, economic vitality, and cultural heritage of the GBA to build a high-end platform integrating philanthropy research, project incubation, resource connection, and international exchange, so as to address common challenges faced by Asia and the world at large.


在随后的揭牌环节中，亚洲公益论坛联席主席、海南亚洲文化艺术基金会主席徐守振与顺德市乐从镇大墩村股份社余广祥理事长一同为“粤港澳大湾区国际公益中心”揭牌，象征着顶层设计与基层实践的强强联合，为中心的未来发展奠定了坚实的基础。In the subsequent unveiling session, Xu Shouzhen, Co-Chair of the Asian Philanthropy Forum and Chairman of the Hainan Asian Culture and Art Foundation, and Yu Guangxiang, Chairman of the Dadun Village Shareholding Cooperative in Lecong Town, Shunde District, jointly unveiled the plaque for the "GBA International Philanthropy Center." This symbolizes the powerful alliance between top-level design and grassroots practice, laying a solid foundation for the center's future development.


本次揭牌仪式嘉宾云集，规格之高彰显了各方对该中心的高度重视与期待。见证嘉宾包括：河南省政协原副主席、河南省慈善联合总会会长邓永俭；海南省人大常委会原副主任、海南省慈善总会会长陆志远；佛山市顺德区乐从镇人民政府镇长陈子荣，以及简一大理石原品牌总监、亚洲文化艺术基金会秘书长柯丽敏。现场同时更多重量级嘉宾参与了共同见证，预示着中心未来将获得跨地域、跨领域的广泛支持。The unveiling ceremony gathered a distinguished assembly of guests, and its high standard demonstrated the great attention and expectations from all parties for the center. The attending guests included:Deng Yongjian, former Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Chairman of the Henan Charity Federation; Lu Zhiyuan, former Deputy Director of the Standing Committee of the Hainan Provincial People's Congress and Chairman of the Hainan Charity Federation; Chen Zirong, Mayor of the People's Government of Lecong Town, Shunde District, Foshan City; and Ke Limin, former Brand Director of Gani Stone and Secretary-General of the Hainan Asian Culture and Art Foundation. Meanwhile, more heavyweight guests participated in the joint witness on-site, indicating that the center will receive extensive cross-regional and cross-sectoral support in the future.


粤港澳大湾区国际公益中心项目将聚焦于乡村振兴、乡邻中心、青少年发展、灾害救助、文化艺术公益及国际慈善合作等多个关键领域。中心将积极联动政府部门、慈善组织、企业及社会各界力量，积极筹备在中国大墩村举办粤港澳大湾区国际公益发展论坛，探索公益创新的新模式，致力于成为推动大湾区社会建设与可持续发展的核心引擎，并为亚洲公益事业的进步贡献“中国智慧”与“湾区方案”。The GBA International Philanthropy Center will focus on several key areas including rural revitalization, community centers, youth development, disaster relief, cultural and artistic philanthropy, and international charitable cooperation. The center will actively collaborate with government departments, charitable organizations, enterprises, and forces from all sectors of society, and actively prepare for the GBA International Philanthropy Development Forum to be held in Dadun Village, China. It will explore new models of philanthropy innovation, strive to become a core engine driving social construction and sustainable development in the GBA, and contribute "Chinese wisdom" and "Bay Area solutions" to the progress of Asian philanthropy.


该中心的成立是2025亚洲公益论坛的重要成果之一，不仅为公益组织提供了更为广阔的合作舞台，也展现了亚洲社会各界携手共进，共筑美好未来的坚定决心。The establishment of the center is one of the important achievements of the 2025 Asian Philanthropy Forum. It not only provides a broader cooperation platform for charitable organizations but also demonstrates the firm determination of all sectors of Asian society to work together for a better future.



机构介绍
亚洲联合文化艺术中心是艺术全球化合作平台，更是一个流动的思想场域与一个蓬勃的文化生命体。我们以 “溯千年文脉，塑当代美学” 为核心理念，构建一个由 “公益为魂、艺术为核、金融为翼” 的创新型国际文化艺术生态体系，旨在建设具有全球影响力的文化艺术枢纽。
