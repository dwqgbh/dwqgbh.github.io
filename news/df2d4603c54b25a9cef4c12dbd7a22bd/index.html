2025浙江义乌国际智能装备博览会今日开幕




Zhejiang Yiwu International Intelligent Manufacturing Equipment Expo 2025 Opens Today







11月20日，2025浙江义乌国际智能装备博览会（简称“装博会”）在国际博览中心启幕。本届展会为期三天（11月20日—22日），以“全球智能装备汇聚浙江 浙江智能科技点亮世界”为主题，重点展示我省和国内外高端装备制造业的新技术、新产品、新模式、新业态。


Zhejiang Yiwu International Intelligent Manufacturing Equipment Expo 2025 (hereinafter referred to as the "ME Expo") kicked off today, November 20, at Yiwu International Expo Center. Running for three days (November 20-22) under the theme "Global Intelligent Equipment Gathers in Zhejiang; Zhejiang's Intelligent Technology Illuminates the World", this year's ME Expo focuses on showcasing new technologies, products, models, and formats in the high-end equipment manufacturing industry from both within Zhejiang Province and internationally.

本届装博会现场设置4万平方米展示空间，设1个主题展区和6个专业展区、1场主题活动和N场系列活动。


The ME Expo features 40,000 square meters of exhibition space, divided into one thematic exhibition zone, six professional exhibition zones, one main event, and numerous supporting activities.




其中，主题展区围绕全球智能装备创新生态和浙江省高端装备创新成果，重点展示一批示范推广效应强、经济社会效应好的典型技术、典型产品和典型案例。六大专业展区则围绕浙江省特色优势产业和未来产业发展前沿，涵盖智能机器人、工业母机、新型农机装备、智能物流装备、现代纺织装备、高端塑料加工装备展区，全面勾勒出高端装备制造业的发展蓝图与创新路径。


The thematic exhibition zone centers on the global smart equipment innovation ecosystem and Zhejiang's high-end equipment innovation achievements, highlighting a range of typical technologies, products, and cases with strong demonstration and promotional effects, as well as significant economic and social benefits. The six professional exhibition zones, focusing on Zhejiang's characteristic advantageous industries and the frontiers of future industrial development, cover Smart Robotics, Industrial Machine Tools, New Agricultural Machinery Equipment, Intelligent Logistics Equipment, Modern Textile Equipment, and High-End Plastic Processing Equipment, comprehensively outlining the development blueprint and innovation path for the high-end equipment manufacturing industry.




展会广泛吸引了国际龙头企业、国内单项冠军、专精特新“小巨人”及产业链“链主”企业参展。三菱重工、富强鑫集团、杭叉集团、杭机股份、中捷缝纫、菲达环保等多家上市公司和行业领军企业，以及宇树科技、强脑科技、群核科技等“杭州六小龙”创新企业集中亮相本届装博会。


The Expo has attracted widespread participation from international industry leaders, domestic single-product champion enterprises, "Little Giant" (specialized, refined, characteristic, and innovative) enterprises, and industrial "chain master" enterprises. Numerous listed companies and industry leaders such as Mitsubishi Heavy Industries, Fu Chun Shin Machinery Manufacture Co., Ltd. (FCS), Hangcha Group, Zhejiang Hangji Machine Tool Co., Ltd., ZOJE Sewing Machine Co., Ltd., Zhejiang Feida Environmental Science & Technology Co., Ltd., as well as innovative companies of Hangzhou's "Six Little Dragons" like Unitree Robotics, BrainCo, and Manycore Tech Inc., are making a collective appearance at this ME Expo.



本届装博会精心组织策划了产销对接、行业交流、金融赋能、海外拓市等10多场主题鲜明、内容务实的配套活动。


The ME Expo has meticulously organized over 10 supporting activities with distinct themes and practical content, covering production-marketing matchmaking, industry exchanges, financial empowerment, and overseas market expansion. 



装博会现场还将发布2025国家单项冠军企业、“智造新势力”浙江智能装备优秀创新案例和浙江智能装备领军人物等，揭示产业趋势，引领行业前行。


The ME Expo will also release the 2025 National Manufacturing Single-Product Champion Enterprises List, the "Intelligent Manufacturing New Force" - Outstanding Innovation Cases of Zhejiang Intelligent Manufacturing Equipment, and recognize Leading Figures in Zhejiang's Intelligent Manufacturing Equipment sector, aiming to reveal industry trends and lead the way forward.
