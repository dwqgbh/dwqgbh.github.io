FACETS Sri Lanka 2025|南亚顶级宝石与珠宝展

斯里兰卡驻广州总领事馆 2024-12-27

璀璨盛会|Facets Sri Lanka 2025

斯里兰卡被誉为“宝石王国”，Facets Sri Lanka 2025是南亚地区最重要的宝石与珠宝展会之一。展会将展示斯里兰卡最珍贵的蓝宝石、红宝石及精美珠宝设计，吸引全球行业专家和宝石爱好者共襄盛举。


Sri Lanka, known as the "Gem Island," will host Facets Sri Lanka 2025, one of South Asia's premier gem and jewelry exhibitions. This event showcases Sri Lanka's finest sapphires, rubies, and exquisite jewelry, bringing together global experts and enthusiasts.
