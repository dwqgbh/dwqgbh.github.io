关于参加第22届中国—东盟博览会采访活动的通知

中国东盟博览会CAEXPO 2025-08-30

第22届中国—东盟博览会将于2025年9月17日至21日在广西南宁市举行。现将中外记者报名参加采访活动的有关事项通知如下。


一、报名时间


从本通知发布之日至2025年9月8日。


二、食宿及交通安排


记者到南宁采访，往返交通食宿费用自理。会议期间，组委会为记者在广西南宁市采访提供公共交通。经确认后的记者可入住由组委会推荐的酒店（沃笙酒店，地址：广西南宁市青秀区云景路29号，邮编：530025，电话：0771-2260888）。

2025年8月29日



Invitation to Media Coverage of

the 22nd China-ASEAN Expo


August, 2025


The 22nd China-ASEAN Expo (CAEXPO) will be held in Nanning, Guangxi from September 17th to September 21st, 2025. Details regarding the registration for media coverage of the event are as follows:


I. Registration Time


From the release date of this invitation to September 8th, 2025.


II. Accommodation and Transportation Arrangements


The round-trip transportation and accommodation expenses for media coverage in Nanning will be borne by the reporters. For the convenience of coverage, the reporters, upon approval, will stay in WO SHENG HOTEL recommended by the Organizing Committee (Address: No. 29, Yunjing Road, Nanning, Guangxi, China, 530025, Tel: +86-0771-2260888) and enjoy complimentary transportation services between WO SHENG HOTEL and the relevant event venues.


III. Registration Method


1. The Press and Publicity Department of the Organizing Committee is in charge of the registration.


For media from Taiwan region , please contact Mr. Xu Yaoyi, Tel: +86-771-8078676; Fax: +86-771-8078676;Mobile:+86-18077108648; E-mail:twmtbm@163.com.


For media from Hong Kong and Macao special administrative regions and international media, please contact Mr. Xie Tao, Tel: +86-771-5668524; Fax: +86-771-5646759；Mobile: +86-18978863925; E-mail:southrock@163.com(Please forward the scanned PDF copies of your passport and Mainland Travel Permit for Hong Kong and Macao Residents, along with your press card, to the designated email address).


2. Please complete the Confirmation Form of the 22nd CAEXPO and CABIS Media Coverage, and submit it to the designated contact via Fax. Additionally, please provide a color identification photo (in JPG format, with dimensions of 240x320 pixels and a file size under 50K, bareheaded) and send it along with the Confirmation Form to the designated contact via e-mail.


Your coverage of the 22nd CAEXPO is warmly welcomed.
