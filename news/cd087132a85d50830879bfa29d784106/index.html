2024东南亚包装工业博览会(WEPACK ASEAN 2024)

三信国际集团 SANSIN GROUP 2024-11-16

由励展博览集团主办的2024东南亚包装工业博览会（WEPACK ASEAN 2024）于2024年11月14-16日在马来西亚国际贸易与展览中心盛大举办。

WEPACK ASEAN 2024, hosted by Reed Exhibitions, will be held at the Malaysian International Trade and Exhibition Centre from 14 to 16 November 2024.

作为东南亚包装行业瞩目的盛事，WEPACK ASEAN 2024今年以12000平米展会规模、集聚200+东南亚和国内优质展商，以及5000+以东南亚为主的全球买家，呈现包装产业技术革新与时代新品。本届展会直击东南亚包装产业链，重磅打造非同凡响的盛会，通过展览、论坛、对接会、考察名企等丰富的活动，为东南亚包装行业人士提供一个深度交流碰撞的平台。

As a high-profile event in Southeast Asia's packaging industry, WEPACK ASEAN 2024 this year with 12,000 square meters of exhibition scale, gathered 200+ Southeast Asia and domestic quality exhibitors, as well as 5,000 + Southeast Asia-based global buyers, presenting the packaging industry technological innovation and new products of The Times. This exhibition directly attacks the Southeast Asian packaging industry chain, and creates an extraordinary event. Through exhibitions, forums, matchmaking meetings, visits to famous enterprises and other rich activities, it provides a platform for in-depth communication and collision for people in the Southeast Asian packaging industry.

本次展会三信国际集团携广州台盛纸箱机械有限公司、天津市永利奇纸品机械有限公司、浙江美丽华印刷材料科技有限公司、东莞科祺自动化设备有限公司等中国企业的设备和品牌亮相展会现场。

This exhibition, Sansin International Group with Guangzhou Taisheng carton Machinery Co., LTD., Tianjin Yongliqi Paper Machinery Co., LTD., Zhejiang Meilihua printing materials Technology Co., LTD., Dongguan Keqi Automation equipment Co., Ltd. and other Chinese enterprises of the equipment and brands appeared at the exhibition site.

此次展会在开展过程中，有众多东南亚当地纸箱厂、包装厂组团前来参观，现场采购气氛热烈。三信国际集团凭借优异的产品性能和优质的服务，吸引了来自世界各地的业内人士前来参观。现场人头攒动，商务洽谈、产品咨询，热闹非凡。

In the course of the exhibition, many Southeast Asian local carton factories and packaging factories came to visit, and the onsite purchasing atmosphere was warm. With excellent product performance and excellent service, Sansin International Group has attracted industry insiders from all over the world to visit. The scene is crowded, business negotiations, product consultation, lively.


2024东南亚包装工业博览会的圆满成功标志着东南亚的包装工业在持续的高速发展，为行业未来的进步奠定了坚实基础。作为参展商之一，三信国际集团在展会期间充分展示了产品优势，赢得了众多观众和合作伙伴的关注与认可。

The complete success of the 2024 Southeast Asia Packaging Industry Expo marks the continuous rapid development of the packaging industry in Southeast Asia, laying a solid foundation for the future progress of the industry. As one of the exhibitors, Sansin International Group fully demonstrated its product advantages during the exhibition and won the attention and recognition of many visitors and partners.
