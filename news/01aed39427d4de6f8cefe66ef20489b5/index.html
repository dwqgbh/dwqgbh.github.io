第139届广交会:境外采购商预登记与邀请函申请正式启动

亚欧非商旅会展 2026-02-27

点击蓝字 关注我们

2026年4月15日至5月5日，#第139届中国进出口商品交易会（#广交会）将在中国广州盛大举办。为进一步提升现场服务水平、提高参会效率，本届广交会于2026年2月14日正式开启境外采购商预登记工作，同时，官方邀请函申请也同步启动。

预登记：参会必经环节，畅享专属服务

所有计划线下参会的境外采购商，无论是曾多次到会的老采购商，还是首次线下参会的新采购商，均须提前完成预登记。这一要求旨在优化参会流程，确保广交会线下参会组织高效、有序。

提前完成预登记的采购商将享受多项专属服务。成功登记后，采购商可通过广交会官方邮件及时获取本届展会的丰富资讯，包括展会亮点、参展企业信息、展品详情以及现场活动安排等，为提前规划采购行程提供有力支持。到会后，采购商还可领取广交会纪念品，具体领取方式将在后续公布，为参会之旅增添一份惊喜。

预登记渠道便捷多样，采购商可通过广交会APP或广交会官网采购商服务系统完成登记。对于新采购商而言，在登记过程中还可同步提交境外采购商证申请，一站式完成多项手续，节省时间和精力。

邀请函：签证与行程的重要文件

邀请函作为众多采购商办理签证及安排行程的重要文件，其申请工作同样备受关注。与预登记同步启动的第139届广交会官方邀请函申请，为采购商提供了便利。

老采购商可登录采购商服务系统，在邀请函模块中提交并确认最新的个人及企业信息，轻松完成邀请函申请。首次参会的采购商则可在申请境外采购商证时一并申请邀请函，系统将在提交成功后自动生成邀请函。生成后的邀请函可直接在系统中查看、下载或打印，方便快捷，满足采购商的不同需求。

优化服务，提升参会体验

广交会持续优化线上预登记服务，不断提升境外采购商的参会体验。通过数字化工具的运用，广交会为采购商提供了更加高效、便捷的参会方式，确保采购对接与经贸交流能够高效务实开展。

在此，广交会诚邀全球采购商尽早完成预登记，充分利用广交会提供的数字化工具，为在广州举办的第139届广交会做好充分准备。期待与全球采购商相聚广州，共襄盛举，共创商机，共同推动全球贸易的繁荣发展。

The 139th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair) to be Grandly Held in Guangzhou, China from April 15 to May 5, 2026.

The 139th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair) will be grandly held in Guangzhou, China from April 15 to May 5, 2026. To further enhance on-site service levels and improve participation efficiency, the fair officially launched the pre-registration for overseas buyers on February 14, 2026, and simultaneously initiated the application for official invitation letters.

Pre-registration: A Must-do Step for Participation, Enjoying Exclusive Services

All overseas buyers planning to participate in the fair offline, whether they are veteran buyers who have attended multiple times or new buyers participating offline for the first time, are required to complete pre-registration in advance. This requirement aims to optimize the participation process and ensure efficient and orderly organization of offline participation at the Canton Fair.

Buyers who complete pre-registration in advance will enjoy a variety of exclusive services. After successful registration, buyers can receive timely information about this edition of the fair through official Canton Fair emails, including highlights of the fair, information about participating enterprises, details of exhibits, and arrangements for on-site activities. This provides strong support for buyers to plan their procurement itineraries in advance. Upon arrival at the fair, buyers can also receive Canton Fair souvenirs, with the specific collection method to be announced later, adding a pleasant surprise to their participation journey.

The pre-registration channels are convenient and diverse. Buyers can complete registration through the Canton Fair APP or the buyer service system on the official Canton Fair website. For new buyers, they can also submit an application for an overseas buyer certificate simultaneously during the registration process, completing multiple procedures in one go and saving time and effort.

Invitation Letter: An Important Document for Visa and Itinerary Planning

As an important document for many buyers to apply for visas and arrange their itineraries, the application for official invitation letters for the 139th Canton Fair has also attracted much attention. Launched simultaneously with pre-registration, the application for official invitation letters provides convenience for buyers.

Veteran buyers can log in to the buyer service system and submit and confirm their latest personal and enterprise information in the invitation letter module to easily complete the application for invitation letters. First-time participants can apply for an invitation letter when applying for an overseas buyer certificate, and the system will automatically generate an invitation letter after successful submission. The generated invitation letter can be directly viewed, downloaded, or printed in the system, which is convenient and fast, meeting the different needs of buyers.

Optimizing Services to Enhance Participation Experience

The Canton Fair continuously optimizes its online pre-registration services to constantly improve the participation experience of overseas buyers. Through the use of digital tools, the Canton Fair provides buyers with a more efficient and convenient way to participate, ensuring that procurement docking and economic and trade exchanges can be carried out in an efficient and pragmatic manner.

Here, the Canton Fair sincerely invites global buyers to complete pre-registration as early as possible, make full use of the digital tools provided by the Canton Fair, and be well-prepared for the 139th Canton Fair to be held in Guangzhou. We look forward to meeting global buyers in Guangzhou, jointly celebrating this grand event, creating business opportunities together, and jointly promoting the prosperity and development of global trade.
