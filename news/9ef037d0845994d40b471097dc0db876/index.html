艾利特医疗亮相2025年越南第32届国际医疗器械展览会携康复医疗创新产品深耕东南亚市场

艾利特医疗 2025-05-08


艾利特医疗推动康复医疗技术国际交流



今日新闻速递

2025年5月8日，越南首都河内市友谊文化宫迎来全球医疗行业盛会——第32届越南国际医药制药、医疗器械展览会（Vietnam Medi-Pharm 2025）。


作为越南卫生部主办、东南亚地区最具影响力的医疗专业展会之一，本届展会吸引了来自30余个国家和地区的450余家企业参展。深圳市艾利特医疗科技有限公司携多款康复医疗创新产品亮相，展示中国高端医疗器械的技术实力与市场竞争力。



聚焦康复医疗，多款核心产品首秀东南亚

艾利特医疗此次展出的核心产品包括冲击波治疗仪、睡眠仪、经颅电刺激仪等，覆盖疼痛管理、神经康复、睡眠健康等领域。其中，冲击波治疗仪采用非侵入式技术，通过精准聚焦的冲击波能量，有效缓解肌肉骨骼疼痛，适用于骨科、康复科及运动医学场景；子午流注穴位治疗仪应用电子脉冲技术替代毫针采用中医经络理论，模拟针灸技术治疗多种疾病。睡眠仪采用先进的CES微电流技术，实现睡眠质量实时监测与个性化干预方案输出，该产品已通过ISO13485、ISO9001、中国二类医疗注册证及CE、CFDA认证，填补了东南亚市场在睡眠健康管理领域的空白。

此外，艾利特医疗的经颅电刺激仪凭借其精准定位刺激技术与多模式治疗技术，成为神经康复领域的焦点，并获得多项发明专利，技术性能达到国际领先水平。



越南市场潜力释放，政策红利助推合作

越南医疗器械市场近年来呈现高速增长态势。数据显示，2023年越南医疗器械市场规模达20亿美元，年复合增长率超15%，且90%以上产品依赖进口。越南政府通过《投资法》修订及RCEP、EVFTA等自由贸易协定的实施，进一步降低进口关税，简化医疗器械注册流程，为中国企业拓展越南市场提供了政策保障。

艾利特医疗外贸负责人表示：“越南是东南亚新兴市场的核心，其医疗基础设施升级需求迫切。我们此次参展不仅希望展示产品，更期待与越南公立医院、区域经销商及政府采购部门建立深度合作，推动康复医疗设备的本土化应用。”


深耕技术创新，构建全球化服务体系

作为国家高新技术企业和深圳市“专精特新”中小企业，艾利特医疗始终以技术创新为核心驱动力。公司研发中心与生产基地位于深圳光明区，拥有近百项自主知识产权，产品涵盖物理治疗、运动康复、神经调控等八大领域，服务全球超5万家医疗机构。

为更好地服务东南亚市场，艾利特医疗已建立本地化服务团队，提供从产品注册、技术培训到售后维护的全链条支持。此次参展期间，公司还将与越南卫生部、医疗设备进口协会等机构展开对话，探讨医疗器械标准互认与临床应用合作。

Elite Medical Promotes International Exchange of Rehabilitation Medical Technology


On May 8, 2025, the Friendship Cultural Palace in Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, welcomed a grand event in the global medical industry - the 32nd Vietnam International Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices Exhibition (Vietnam Medi-Pharm 2025).


As one of the most influential medical professional exhibitions in Southeast Asia, hosted by the Ministry of Health of Vietnam, this exhibition attracted more than 450 enterprises from over 30 countries and regions. Shenzhen Elite Medical Technology Co., Ltd. made an appearance with a number of innovative rehabilitation medical products, demonstrating the technical strength and market competitiveness of high-end medical devices in China.
Focusing on rehabilitation medicine, multiple core products made their debut in Southeast Asia.


The core products exhibited by Elite Medical this time include: shock wave therapy device, sleep monitor, transcranial electrical stimulation device, etc., covering fields such as pain management, neurological rehabilitation, and sleep health. Among them, the shock wave therapy device adopts non-invasive technology, and through the precisely focused shock wave energy, it can effectively relieve musculoskeletal pain, which is suitable for scenarios in orthopedics, rehabilitation, and sports medicine; the Meridian and Zitong Acupoint Therapy Device applies electronic pulse technology to replace filiform needles, using the theory of traditional Chinese medicine meridians to simulate acupuncture technology for treating a variety of diseases. The sleep monitor adopts advanced CES micro-current technology to achieve real-time monitoring of sleep quality and output personalized intervention programs.


This product has obtained ISO13485, ISO9001, China's Class II medical registration certificate, as well as CE and CFDA certifications, filling the gap in the sleep health management field in the Southeast Asian market. In addition, the transcranial electrical stimulation device of Elite Medical has become the focus in the field of neurological rehabilitation with its precise positioning stimulation technology and multi-mode treatment technology, and has obtained a number of invention patents, with its technical performance reaching the international leading level.


The potential of the Vietnamese market is being released, and policy dividends boost cooperation:
The medical device market in Vietnam has shown a high-speed growth trend in recent years. Data shows that the scale of the medical device market in Vietnam reached 2 billion US dollars in 2023, with an annual compound growth rate of over 15%, and more than 90% of the products rely on imports. The Vietnamese government, through the revision of the Investment Law and the implementation of free trade agreements such as the RCEP and EVFTA, has further reduced import tariffs and simplified the registration process of medical devices, providing policy guarantees for Chinese enterprises to expand into the Vietnamese market.


The person in charge of foreign trade of Elite Medical said: "Vietnam is the core of the emerging market in Southeast Asia, and there is an urgent need for the upgrading of its medical infrastructure. Our participation in this exhibition not only hopes to showcase our products, but also looks forward to establishing in-depth cooperation with public hospitals, regional distributors, and government procurement departments in Vietnam, and promoting the localized application of rehabilitation medical equipment."


Deeply cultivating technological innovation and building a global service system
As a national high-tech enterprise and a "Specialized, Refined, Differential, and Innovative" small and medium-sized enterprise in Shenzhen, Elite Medical always takes technological innovation as the core driving force. The company's R&D center and production base are located in Guangming District, Shenzhen, with nearly a hundred independent intellectual property rights. Its products cover eight major fields such as physical therapy, sports rehabilitation, and neural regulation, serving more than 50,000 medical institutions around the world.
In order to better serve the Southeast Asian market, Elite Medical has established a localized service team to provide full-chain support from product registration, technical training to after-sales maintenance. During this exhibition, the company will also have dialogues with institutions such as the Ministry of Health of Vietnam and the Medical Device Import Association to discuss the mutual recognition of medical device standards and clinical application cooperation.
