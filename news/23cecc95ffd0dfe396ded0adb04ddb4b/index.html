国机海南诚邀您参加2025年中国国际进口博览会

国机海南 2025-11-03







在这秋意渐浓的美好时节，第八届中国国际进口博览会即将在上海隆重开幕。国机海南发展有限公司诚挚邀请您莅临食品及农产品展区8.1馆A3-02展台。

本届进博会上，我们重点呈现五大展区内容：非洲区、东南亚区、中亚南亚综合区、冷链冰鲜区及自有品牌专区，精选展示来自全球各地的特色农产品。现场更设有试吃试饮环节，欢迎您亲临品鉴。

国机海南团队恭候您的光临，期待与您相会，共绘合作新篇！




Invitation



Distinguished guests,dear partners, and colleagues,

As the autumn breeze grows stronger, the 8th China International Import Expo is about to open grandly in Shanghai. Sinomach Hainan Development Co., Ltd. sincerely invites you to visit our booth A3-02 in Hall 8.1 of the Food and Agricultural Products Exhibition Area.

At this year's Expo, we will focus on presenting five exhibition sections: the Africa Section, the Southeast Asia Section, the Central and South Asia Comprehensive Section, the Cold Chain and Fresh Section, and the Own Brand Section. We will carefully showcase characteristic agricultural products from around the world. There will also be on-site tasting sessions, and we welcome you to come and experience them in person.

The Sinomach Hainan team looks forward to your visit, and we are eager to meet you and work together to create a new chapter of cooperation!
