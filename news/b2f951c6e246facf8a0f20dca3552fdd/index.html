Brand Philosophy

品牌理念



EASDAR


深耕汽车 LED 照明领域 17 载，易事达是集研发、生产、销售于一体的专精特新企业。我们始终秉持“以专业引领行业，以体验创造价值”的经营理念，致力于成为具有全球竞争力的 LED 车灯制造商。凭借强大的自主研发能力、严苛的全链路品控体系与完善的全球化供应链，易事达的产品畅销美洲、亚洲、欧洲和大洋洲等多个国家和地区，收获了海内外客户的广泛信赖与认可。

With 17 years of deep commitment to the automotive LED lighting industry, Easdar has established itself as a specialized and innovative enterprise integrating R&D, production, and sales. Guided by our business philosophy of "leading the industry with professionalism and creating value through experience," we strive to become a globally competitive LED automotive light manufacturer. Powered by strong in-house R&D capabilities, a rigorous full-chain quality control system, and a well-established global supply chain, Easdar's products are now sold across the Americas, Asia, Europe, Oceania, and other regions, earning extensive trust and recognition from customers worldwide.




本届广交会，易事达带来多款首发新品与明星爆款，全方位展现 “智造” 的硬核实力，用前沿车灯技术，重新定义驾驶视野与安全：

At this year's Canton Fair, Easdar will unveil a range of debut new products and best-selling stars, showcasing our hardcore strength in "Intelligent Manufacturing" and redefining driving vision and safety with cutting-edge automotive lighting technology:



Product Highlights

新品亮点  


隐藏式中网灯：填补照明盲区，重塑行车视野

嵌入式设计完美融入汽车中网内部，有效补充原厂大灯的照明盲区，显著提升车辆在白天、低光环境下的存在感，同时大幅拓展前方、侧向照明范围，让每一次出行都更安心。

Concealed Grille Light: Fills lighting blind spots and reshapes your driving view.

Embedded seamlessly into the vehicle's grille, this light effectively compensates for blind spots in the factory headlights, significantly enhancing the vehicle's visibility during both daytime and low-light conditions. It also greatly expands forward and lateral illumination, making every journey more secure.

全系列雾灯透镜：极致光学，无惧复杂路况

航空铝材质灯体，内置高效散热系统与散热风扇，拥有IP67 级防水设计，全天候稳定运行。精准聚焦光线，减少散射，实现更规整的光型、更远的照射距离、更广的覆盖范围，尤其在雾天、雨雪天、无路灯路段，能显著改善视野，为夜间行车保驾护航。

Full Range Fog Light Lenses: Ultimate Optics for Conquering Challenging Road Conditions.

Crafted from aviation-grade aluminum, the lamp body features a built-in high-efficiency cooling system and a dedicated cooling fan, paired with an IP67-rated waterproof design for reliable all-weather performance. The precision-focused optics minimize light scatter, delivering a more uniform beam pattern, longer illumination distance, and wider coverage. Especially in fog, rain, snow, or on unlit roads, these fog lights significantly enhance visibility, providing reliable protection for nighttime driving.
