CIIE 进博会 2022 澳大利亚企业线上展示对接汇总 Summary of Online Business Matching

中国澳大利亚商会WestChina 2022-11-01

2022第五届中国国际进口博览会将于11月5日至10日在上海举办。澳大利亚企业将连续五年参展，产品类别涵盖澳大利亚食品饮料、消费品、营养保健、服务贸易四大板块。澳贸委继续通过微信小程序的进博会专题页，为中澳企业交流合作搭建平台。欢迎专业观众通过文内的小程序参与澳大利亚进博会线上商务展示对接，了解逾百家澳大利亚线上展示企业、品牌及其产品并开展合作交流。

The 5th China International Import Expo 2022 will be held in Shanghai from November 5th to 10th. Australian companies will participate in the exhibition for five consecutive years, covering four major sectors of Australian food and beverage, consumer goods, nutrition and health care, and service trade. Austrade continues to build a platform for exchanges and cooperation between Chinese and Australian enterprises through the special webpage of the WeChat Mini Programs for the Expo. Professional visitors are welcome to participate in the online business display matching of the Australian CIIE through the mini program in the article, learn about more than 100 Australian online display companies, brands and their products, and cooperate and exchange.
