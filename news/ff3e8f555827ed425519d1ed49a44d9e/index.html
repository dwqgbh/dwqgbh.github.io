精准检测,智造未来——华视集团携核心解决方案亮相2025 CIBF电池展!

Walvision华视智能 2025-05-21

华视集团

闪耀2025CIBF深圳电池展


精准检测，制造未来




2025年，第十七届中国国际电池技术交流会/展览会（CIBF2025）于5月15-17日在深圳国际会展中心举办，吸引超3200家行业领军企业参展，全面展示电池产业从材料创新到终端应用的全链条技术突破。


在这场新能源领域的巅峰盛会上，华视集团以“精准检测·智造未来”为主题，通过核心产品矩阵展示、新品全球首发、国际技术对话三大维度，向全球行业伙伴展现中国电池检测装备领域的创新实力。


In 2025, the 17th China International Battery Fair (CIBF2025) was held at the Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center from May 15th to 17th, attracting over 3,200 leading enterprises in the industry to showcase technological breakthroughs across the entire battery industry chain, from material innovation to end-use applications.


At this top event in the new energy field, Walvision Group, under the theme of "Precision Inspection · Intelligent Manufacturing for the Future," presented China's innovative capabilities in the battery inspection equipment sector to global industry partners in three dimensions: Matrix display of core products, First release of new products, and International technical exchange.



PART 01

产品矩阵：全周期的解决方案


南京华视智能：专注机器视觉系统等工业检测整体解决方案


1. CCD毛刺检测系统

高速全检能力：120m/min高速全检；微米级检测精度：检测重复性精度达0.006mm；智能自动化与模块化设计：双面，横/纵双向检测；自动避让，适应穿带；多场景适用性：尺寸可调，防尘遮光；合理利用空间，维护方便。


2. 高精密CT系列

一体化产品设计；最多11轴的多轴精密运动控制；强稳定性大理石架构；高安全性的多重防护措施；优于国标的辐射泄露率；自研三维重建算法；高自由度适配多场景CT检测需求。

Walvision (Nanjing Walvision Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd.): Specializes in industrial vision inspection systems and solutions.


CCD Burr Inspection System:

High-speed full inspection capability: 120m/min high-speed full inspection.

Micron-level inspection accuracy: Repeatability accuracy up to 0.006mm.

Intelligent automation and modular design: Dual-sided inspection, horizontal/vertical bidirectional inspection; Automatic avoidance, adaptable to threading; Multi-scenario applicability; Adjustable size, dustproof and shading; Space-efficient and easy to maintain.


High-Precision CT Series:

Integrated product design; up to 11-axis multi-axis precision motion control; High stability of marble structure; High safety of multiple protection; Radiation leakage within standards; Self-developed 3D reconstruction algorithm; Highly adaptable to diverse inspection needs.

无锡上川精密：专注涂布模头、挤压模头、五金模切及配套的供料系统、闭环控制系统的研发、设计和制造


1. 单/双层闭环涂布摸头

有效涂宽：200-2000mm；涂布厚度：200-2000mm；横向涂布一致性：±1.0%以内；兼容涂布速度：5-100m/min；兼容涂布工艺：间歇涂布、连续涂布、陶瓷涂布、双层涂布；定制化设计：流体仿真。


2. 五金模切

高耐磨，微变形模具取材， 高硬度钨钢刀口；MISUMI 精密六方导柱组件，使用寿命更长；刀板、模头板等部件加工精度公差控制到2μm；总预期寿命可达1500~2000万冲次，维护间隔时间为150~200万次；可定做钛合金、陶瓷以及特殊涂层以提高耐磨及稳定性。


Kamikawa (Kamikawa Precision Technology (Wuxi) Co., Ltd.): Specializes in the R&D, design, and manufacturing of coating slot dies, notching knives, feeding systems and closed-loop control systems.


Single/Double-Layer Closed-Loop Coating Slot Die:

Effective coating width: 200-2000mm; coating thickness: 200-2000μm; horizontal coating consistency: within ±1.0%; compatible coating speeds: 5-100m/min; compatible coating processes: gap, continuous, ceramic, and double-layer coating; customizable design with fluid simulation.


Notching knives:

High wear resistance and micro-deformation mold materials, high-hardness tungsten steel cutting edges; MISUMI precision hexagonal guide column components for longer service life; part processing precision tolerance controlled to 2μm; total expected lifespan of 15-20 million cycles, maintenance interval of 1.5-2 million cycles; customizable with titanium alloy, ceramic, and special coatings for enhanced wear resistance and stability.

常州锐奇精测：专注材料厚度/面密度一致性在线测量与控制解决方案


1. X射线全检面密度仪

100%抽检率：100%全幅面检测与监控，全数据存储与追溯；自动闭环调节：测量数据及时反馈，闭环调节更有效；助力去分容化；降低生产成本：一年可节省调机废料100万元以上；全质量闭环；0.4mm光斑：可实现鼓包、漏涂、颗粒线等涂布缺陷检测。


2. X-RAY成像检测系统

射线源：闭管微焦点源；探测器：平板探测器；成像系统：系统放大倍数5-50X；辐射防护：≤1μSv/h；行业应用：锂电池行业检测、电子半导体行业、3C数码总装检测、电子元件检测。


3. 3D线光谱轮廓仪

传感器：线光谱位移传感器；X轴测量范围：6~18mm；X轴分辨率：3~7μm；Z轴测量范围：0.7~6mm；Z轴重复精度：0.3μm~0.5μm；扫描速度：200-500mm/s ；行业应用：锂电池电池焊接缺陷检测、锂电池负极极片涂层激光划线与刻槽尺寸检测。


Reechi (Changzhou Reechi Precision Measuretech Co., Ltd.): Specializes in online measurement and control solutions for material thickness/surface density consistency.


X-ray Full-web Measurement Gauge:

100% detection rate: 100% full-web detection and monitoring, full data storage and traceability; automatic closed-loop adjustment: timely feedback of measurement data for more effective closed-loop adjustment; facilitates de-capacity sorting; reduces production costs: can save over 1 million yuan in debugging waste materials annually; full-quality closed-loop; 0.4mm spot size for detecting coating defects such as bulges, missed coatings, and particle lines.


X-Ray Imaging Inspection System:

X-ray source: closed-tube micro-focus source; detector: flat-panel detector; imaging system: magnification of 5-50X; radiation protection: radiation≤1μSv/h; industry applications: lithium battery industry inspection, electronic semiconductor industry, 3C digital assembly inspection, electronic component inspection.


3D Line Spectral Profilometer:

Sensor: line spectral displacement sensor; X-axis measurement range: 6-18mm; X-axis resolution: 3-7μm; Z-axis measurement range: 0.7-6mm; Z-axis repeatability: 0.3μm-0.5μm; scanning speed: 200-500mm/s; industry applications: lithium battery welding defect inspection, lithium battery negative electrode coating laser scoring and grooving size inspection.

新品发布：全周期的精度革命

此次，华视集团与官方携手，重磅推出了“涂布模头x面密度检测x视觉AI的精度革命”主题新品发布会。现场众多行业专家、客户代表及媒体人士亲临现场，共同见证了华视集团在涂布工艺全链路精度提升方面的前沿技术成果。


与此同时，华视集团还与高工锂电合作，开启了线上直播模式。这一举措打破了地域限制，吸引了超过5千人次的专业观众在线观看。直播过程中，观众互动频繁，弹幕刷屏不断，技术热点话题持续引发讨论，进一步扩大了华视集团的品牌影响力。


通过线下新品发布会的沉浸式体验与线上直播的裂变式传播，华视集团在CIBF展会期间实现了超百万级的曝光量，深度渗透行业社群。这场线上线下联动的盛宴，不仅彰显了华视集团在电池制造检测领域的技术领导力，更以卓越的传播效果，成为CIBF展会期间最具话题性与影响力的品牌事件之一。


During CIBF 2025, Walvision Group successfully attracted widespread attention from both online and offline audiences with its innovative technologies and strong brand influence.


Walvision Group, in collaboration with the official event organizers, launched the "Precision Revolution in Coating Slot Die, Surface Density Inspection, and Visual AI" product release event. Numerous industry experts, customer representatives, and media personnel attended the event in person to witness Walvision Group's cutting-edge technological achievements in enhancing the precision of the entire coating process chain.


Meanwhile, Walvision Group partnered with GGII (Gaogong Industry Research Institute) to initiate an online live stream, breaking space-time limitations and attracting over 5,000 professional viewers online. The live stream featured frequent audience interactions, a continuous barrage of bulleted comments, and lively discussions on technical hot topics, further expanding Walvision Group's brand influence.

This online-offline integrated event not only demonstrated Walvision Group's technological leadership in the battery manufacturing inspection sector but also became one of the most topical and influential brand events during CIBF2025.

国际交流：跨国检测技术合作


CIBF2025展会期间，华视集团的展台成为了国内外专业人士交流的热点。这里不仅汇聚了众多国内行业精英，更吸引了大量来自欧美、日韩、东南亚等海外国家的专业人员。


他们与华视集团的技术人员进行了深入的技术交流，共同探讨锂电池产业的前沿技术，并相约线下进一步探讨合作。华视集团凭借其在工业视觉检测系统、一致性在线测量与控制系统、挤压式涂布模头三大产品矩阵的深厚积累，向世界展示了其技术实力。
