时间：

2025年2月12日-15日10:00am-6:00pm

地点：

印度-新德里-印度世博中心

展位：B-2

Exhibition Name: Garment Technology Expo (GTE) India 2025

Time: 12-15 February 2025 10:00am-6:00pm

Venue: India - New Delhi - India Expo Centre

Booth: B-2


展会概要


印度纺织机械及面辅料展览会（Garment Technology Expo 简称GTE）是南亚最大、最全面的服装科技展览会。来自中国、日本、中国台湾、土耳其、孟加拉、巴基斯坦、美国、乌兹别克斯坦、迪拜、德国等世界各地500多家企业将集中展示各种服装机械、配件和支持服务，最新技术、新品发布和技术研讨。浙江衣拿智能科技股份有限公司（以下简称：衣拿智能）受邀参展。


Exhibition Outline:

India Garment Technology Expo (Abbreviation: GTE) is the largest and most comprehensive garment technology exhibition in South Asia. More than 500 companies from China, Japan, Taiwan, Turkey, Bangladesh, Pakistan, the United States, Uzbekistan, Dubai, Germany and other parts of the world will focus on displaying a variety of apparel machinery, accessories and support services, the latest technology, new product releases and technical seminars.INA INTELLIGENT TECHNOLOGY（ZHENGJIANG）CO.,LTD. (Abbreviation: INA SYSTEM) was invited to participate in the exhibition.


参展产品：衣拿智能整场规划解决方案
INA SYSTEM intelligent whole plant solutions.

衣拿智能作为智能制造领域的佼佼者，诚邀您莅临2025GTE，共同分享行业高效智能的整厂规划解决方案。现场将提供提供一系列数字化、高效、精准、个性化的解决方案，帮助企业用户们优化生产流程，提升产品与品牌竞争力！

INA SYSTEM, as a leading player in intelligent manufacturing, cordially invites you to the 2025 GTE to explore efficient and intelligent whole plant solutions together.A range of digitalized, efficient, precise, and personalized solutions will be provided on-site，to help business users optimise their production processes and enhance the competitiveness of their products and brands!
