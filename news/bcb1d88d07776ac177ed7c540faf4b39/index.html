2025广州开发区智能制造产业发展对接活动成功举办

华南美国商会 2025-09-11

AI赋能制造，黄埔汇聚全球新机遇


2025广州开发区智能制造产业发展对接活动成功举办

2025 Guangzhou Development District Intelligent Manufacturing Industry Development Summit Successfully Held

-AI Empowers Manufacturing as Huangpu Converges Global Opportunities-



（广州•黄埔，2025年9月10日）由广州开发区投资集团有限公司、广州开发区黄埔区具身智能机器人产业发展促进会、华南美国商会共同主办的“2025广州开发区智能制造产业发展对接活动”在广州黄埔区成功举行。


(Guangzhou • Huangpu, September 10, 2025) — The 2025 Guangzhou Development District Intelligent Manufacturing Industry Development Summit was successfully held in Huangpu District, Guangzhou. Jointly organized by Guangzhou Development District Investment Group Co., Ltd., Guangzhou Huangpu Embodied Intelligence Robotics Industry Promotion Association, and the American Chamber of Commerce in South China. 


本次活动以“智启黄埔新引擎，荟聚全球新机遇”为主题，聚焦“AI赋能·智造升级”，汇聚了众多智能制造领域的专家学者、世界500企业代表与行业精英，围绕智能技术驱动产业转型、国际合作机遇等核心议题展开深度交流，共同绘制智能制造新时代的共赢蓝图。


The summit was themed “Igniting Huangpu’s Innovation Engine · Converging Global Opportunities.” Focusing on “AI Empowerment · Upgrading Smart Manufacturing,” the conference gathered leading experts, Fortune Global 500 representatives, and industry elites to exchange insights on AI-driven industrial transformation and opportunities for international collaboration, jointly outlining a blueprint for the new era of intelligent manufacturing.


区领导，乔治·布什美中关系基金会尼尔·布什主席，华南美商会哈利·赛亚丁会长，开发区投资集团邬斌总经理等出席活动。


The summit was attended by district leaders, Neil Bush, Chairman of the George H. W. Bush Foundation for U.S.-China Relations, Harley Seyedin, President of AmCham South China, and Wu Bin, General Manager of Guangzhou Development District Investment Group, among others.


活动现场，广州开发区投资集团有限公司与华南美国商会签署产业投资促进合作协议，双方将在招商引资、产业研究、企业服务等领域建立长期合作机制，广州开发区投资集团有限公司将为美商会招引落户提供专项招商奖励金，携手导入全球优质产业资源，助力广州开发区打造智能制造产业高地，助力区域高水平开放与高质量发展。


At the summit, Guangzhou Development District Investment Group Co., Ltd. and the American Chamber of Commerce in South China signed an industrial investment promotion cooperation agreement. Under the agreement, the two sides will establish a long-term cooperation mechanism in areas such as investment promotion, industry research, and enterprise services. Guangzhou Development District Investment Group will provide special investment incentives to support AmCham South China in attracting projects to settle in the district, jointly channeling high-quality global industrial resources to help Guangzhou Development District build a leading hub for intelligent manufacturing and advance the region’s high-level opening-up and high-quality development.



大咖聚首，洞见智造前沿

Visionary Minds Meet, Exploring the Frontiers of Smart Manufacturing


乔治·布什美中关系基金会创始人及主席尼尔·布什指出面对第四次工业革命，任何国家或企业均难以独自应对供应链韧性、气候变化等全球挑战，“单打独斗”模式已不可行。


Neil Bush, Founder and Chairman of the George H. W. Bush Foundation for U.S.-China Relations, shared a keynote speech titled “Connecting Across the Pacific: Exploring Collaborative Models in the Smart Industry.” He emphasized that the Pacific should be a shared stage connected by digital bridges, smart concepts, and deep collaboration, rather than a geographical barrier. Facing the Fourth Industrial Revolution, no country or enterprise can single-handedly address global challenges such as supply chain resilience, climate change, and cybersecurity. 


他倡议三大智能协作模式：一是构建连接全球科创中心的“创新生态集群”，二是建立“行业专项联盟”，三是推动“监管沙盒”合作，呼吁全球企业家主动寻找跨境互补伙伴，通过连接、协作、共创，打造更智慧、更繁荣、更和平的共享未来。


He proposed three major models for intelligent collaboration: first, building “Innovation Ecosystem Clusters” that connect global science and technology hubs; second, establishing “Sector-Specific Alliances”; and third, promoting “Regulatory Sandbox” cooperation. He called on global entrepreneurs to actively seek cross-border complementary partners, and through connection, collaboration, and co-creation, jointly build a smarter, more prosperous, and peaceful shared future.




华南美国商会主席兼会长哈利·赛亚丁在题为《中国一直是再投资首选地》的主旨分享中指出，尽管全球经济存在不确定性，中国仍是全球最具战略价值的投资首选地。


Harley Seyedin, President of AmCham South China, delivered a keynote speech titled “China Remains the Top Destination for Reinvestment.” He emphasized that despite global economic uncertainties, China continues to be the most strategically valuable investment destination worldwide. 


据华南美国商会发布的《2025年华南地区经济情况特别报告》，61%的受访企业在2024年已实施在华再投资，会员企业未来三到五年在华利润再投资预算额预计增长33%，再投资额达145.9亿美元。


According to AmCham South China’s 2025 Special Report on the State of Business in South China, 61% of respondents had already reinvested in China. Member companies’ reinvestment budgets are projected to grow by 33% over the next three to five years, reaching USD 14.59 billion.


对于寻求区域总部的企业、瞄准高增长科技行业的投资者、寻求优质生活的家庭来说，最明智、最具战略意义的投资目的地不仅是中国，也不仅仅是广州——毫无疑问，是广州开发区、黄埔区。


For companies seeking regional headquarters, investors targeting high-growth technology sectors, and families pursuing a high-quality lifestyle, the most prudent and strategically significant investment destination is not only China, and not just Guangzhou—it is, without a doubt, Guangzhou Development District and Huangpu District.





思科、荣耀、毕马威等领军企业围绕智能制造与AI应用等前沿议题，分享创新实践与行业见解；微软、百事、联邦快递、斯睿特、顺威股份等企业代表也齐聚产业资源对接环节，以“AI赋能产业变革”为主题，聚焦人工智能在智能制造、智慧物流、消费品创新等领域的应用案例与实践经验，开展深度对接，共探数字化转型与高质量发展新路径。

产业投资促进合作，共享发展机遇

Promoting Industrial Investment Cooperation, Sharing Development Opportunities


广州开发区投资集团总经理邬斌介绍了广州开发区发展情况并作“做好产业赋能，打造全国一流科技产投集团”专题招商推介，表示将发挥产业投资，标准化、专业化的产业园区和产业服务团队等优势，在广州开发区共享发展机遇，并诚挚邀请加入广州开发区黄埔区具身智能机器人产业促进会。


Ms. Wu Bin, General Manager of Guangzhou Development District Investment Group, introduced the development of Guangzhou Development District and delivered a keynote investment promotion presentation titled “Empowering Industries to Build a Nationally Leading Science and Technology Investment Group.” He highlighted the district’s advantages in industrial investment, standardized and professional industrial parks, and dedicated service teams, emphasizing the opportunities for shared development within Guangzhou Development District. He also extended a sincere invitation to enterprises to join the Guangzhou Huangpu Embodied Intelligence Robotics Industry Promotion Association.




投资广州开发区，共赢未来




此次对接活动有效搭建了政府、企业、资本三方精诚合作、互利互惠的对接平台，进一步扩大了广州开发区作为粤港澳大湾区先进制造与对外开放核心平台的国际影响力，为全球企业投资粤港澳大湾区、布局智能制造提供了明确信号与务实合作路径。有效推动国际产业资源与黄埔发展新机遇深度融合。


The summit effectively established a high-level matchmaking platform for government, enterprises, and capital, enhancing the international influence of Guangzhou Development District as a hub for advanced manufacturing and openness in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area. It sent a clear signal and provided practical pathways for global enterprises seeking to invest in the region’s intelligent manufacturing sector, facilitating the deep integration of international industrial resources with new development opportunities in Huangpu.

关于广州开发区投资集团有限公司

广州开发区投资集团有限公司成立于1992年1月，总资产超700亿元，具有中诚信AAA国内信用评级和惠誉BBB+国际评级，合规管理体系通过国家、国际贯标认证，定位为区重大产业投资平台，以智能制造和先进制造、美容美妆和大健康、重大产业投资和资本运作、科技园区投资和运营服务为四大主业，是全球空调低压送风领域龙头企业和汽车电子门锁领域领先企业顺威股份（002676）的控股股东，万联证券、广州银行、鼎和财险的重要股东。投资赋能广汽埃安、滴滴自动驾驶、孔辉科技等约60家汽车产业链企业，投资培育国家级专精特新“小巨人”33家，助力广钢气体、广合科技等14家公司上市。现运营科技产业园区超160万方，打造了黄埔实验室、海丝知识中心、科教创新区、南方美谷等高端科教、科研、科创园区；创新“房东+股东+合作伙伴”科技企业成长陪伴模式，为导远科技、孔辉科技等科技企业高质量发展赋能，奋力打造全国一流的科技产业投资集团。


Guangzhou Development District Investment Group Co., Ltd. was established in January 1992 with total assets exceeding 70 billion yuan. It boasts a domestic credit rating of AAA from China Chengxin Credit Management Co. Ltd. and an international rating of BBB+ from Fitch Ratings and its compliance management system has been certified under both national and international standards. Positioned as a significant industrial-investment platform for the district, it focuses on four core businesses: Intelligent Manufacturing and Advanced Manufacturing, beauty Cosmetics and Healthcare, Significant Industrial Investment and Capital Operation, Sci-tech Park Investment and Operating Service. The Group is the controlling shareholder of Sunwill (002676), the global leade in low-pressure air-delivery systems for air conditioners and a frontrunner in automotive electronic-lock sets. It is also a major shareholder in Wanlian Securities, Bank of Guangzhou, and Dinghe Insurance.

The Group has invested in and empowered approximately 60 automotive-chain enterprises such as GAC AION, Didi Chuxing's autonomous driving division, and KH Automotive Technologies Co., Ltd.. It has nurtured 33 national-level “Little Giant” enterprises specializing in new technologies, and assisted 14 companies, including Guanggang Gas and DELTON TECHNOLOGY (GUANGZHOU)INC in going public.

The Group currently operates more than 1.6 million m2 of technology parks and has built flagship hubs including Huangpu Laboratory, Maritime Silk Road Knowledge Center, Science-Education Innovation Zone and Southern Beauty Valley. By innovating the “landlord + shareholder + partner” model, it empowers technological enterprises such as VectorBuilder, ASENSING Technology, and KH Automotive Technologies Co., Ltd. to achieve high-quality development, striving to become a first-class technology-industry investment group nationwide.

关于广州开发区黄埔区具身智能机器人产业发展促进会


为深入贯彻落实习近平总书记关于发展新质生产力的重要指示精神，积极响应2025年国务院政府工作报告中“建立未来产业投入增长机制，培育生物制造、量子科技、具身智能、6G等未来产业”的战略部署，扎实推进省、市、区各级党委政府关于现代化产业体系建设的系列工作要求，在广州市黄埔区工业和信息化局的指导下，由广州开发区投资集团有限公司牵头，联合工业和信息化部电子第五研究所、广东省检验检测认证研究院集团有限公司等多家行业权威机构与企业，共同发起成立了“广州开发区 黄埔区具身智能机器人产业发展促进会”（以下简称“促进会”）。

促进会经民政部门核准登记，促进会为具有独立法人资格的非营利性社会团体，业务主管单位为广州市黄埔区工业和信息化局。本会以“标准引领、技术协同、生态共建、人才培育”为宗旨，致力于构建具身智能机器人领域产业链上下游协同创新的高水平平台，通过整合产业资源、凝聚科研力量，推动关键技术研发、标准体系构建、成果转化与应用拓展，打造具有国际竞争力的产业集群。

目前，促进会已吸纳约90家成员单位，涵盖具身智能机器人核心企业、产业链关键环节单位、科研机构、生态合作伙伴及专业投融资机构，初步形成集产学研用于一体的产业生态。同时，促进会着力打造“开投·智行产业空间”具身智能特色产业园，重点聚焦具身智能机器人和智能网联新能源汽车两大产业链群，为区域未来产业集聚与高质量发展提供有力支撑。


In order to thoroughly implement President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping’s guiding instructions on fostering new‐quality productive forces and respond to the 2025 State Council Government Work Report—which calls for “establishing growth mechanisms for future industries and cultivating embodied intelligence, biomanufacturing, quantum technology and 6G”—the Promotion Association was officially established under the guidance of Bureau of Industry and Information Technology of Huangpu District. The initiative was led by Guangzhou Development district Investment Group and jointly launched with authoritative institutions and enterprises such as the Fifth Electronics Research Institute of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the Guangdong Inspection, Testing and Certification Research Institute Group.

Registered as a non-profit social organization with independent legal-person status and supervised by Bureau of Industry and Information Technology of Huangpu District, the Association is guided by the principles of “standards leadership, technology collaboration, ecosystem co-building and talent cultivation.” It aims to build a high-level platform for upstream and downstream collaborative innovation in the industrial chain of embodied intelligent robot. By integrating industrial resources and gathering scientific research strengths, it advances critical technologies, standard-system development, achievement transformation and application expansion to create an internationally competitive industrial cluster.

At present, the Association has absorbed about 90 member units, covering core enterprises in embodied-intelligent robot industry, key supply-chain partners, research institutes, ecosystem allies and specialized investment-and-financing institutions, and has formed an industry ecology integrating academia, research and application. Concurrently, it strives to develop the “GDDIG Zhixing Industry Space” embodied-intelligence industrial park, focusing on the two major industrial chain clusters of embodied intelligent robot and intelligent network connected new energy vehicles, so as to provide robust support for future industrial agglomeration and high quality regional development.

关于华南美国商会


华南美国商会是一个旨在促进美中两国双边经贸关系发展的无党派、非赢利性商业机构，由设在华盛顿特区的美国全国商会认可，于1995年成立。华南美国商会代表旗下的2300多家企业及个人会员，并由会员选举出完全独立的理事会进行机构管理。华南美国商会为在该地区经营的美国及国际企业提供有力、全面的支持。在过去的十年里，华南美国商会每年举办的各类活动包括：信息简介、主题研讨会、委员会会议及社交聚会等，超过1万名来自世界各地的政府官员和企业高管参与了相关活动。所有中国境内的美国全国商会均为独立管理，并代表各自所在区域内的会员企业。


The American Chamber of Commerce in South China (AmCham South China) is a non-partisan, non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating bilateral trade between the United States and the People’s Republic of China. Accredited in 1995 by the U.S. Chamber of Commerce in Washington D.C., AmCham South China represents more than 2,300 corporate and individual members, is governed by a fully-independent Board of Governors elected from its membership, and provides dynamic, on-the-ground support for American and International companies doing business in South China. Over the past ten years, AmCham South China has hosted on average each year more than 10,000 business executives and government leaders from around the world at its briefings, seminars, committee meetings and social gatherings. 

All American Chambers of Commerce within China operate independently and represent the member companies in their respective regions.
