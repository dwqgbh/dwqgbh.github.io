瑞士机床 - 很高兴再次来到上海!| 瑞士官方展团 @ 2024年中国数控机床展览会

瑞士驻沪总领馆 2024-04-07

2024中国数控机床展览会（CCMT）即将于2024年4月8日至12日在上海浦东新国际博览中心召开。时隔六年之后，瑞士企业以最新的制造技术再次在上海展示他们的产品和服务，从精密工具到测量仪器、自动化解决方案，当然还包括机床，将满足任何制造业对技术的渴求。

China CNC Machine Tool Fair 2024 (CCMT 2024) will take place from 8th to 12th April, 2024 at the Shanghai New International Expo Center. After a 6-year break, Swiss suppliers of state-of-the-art manufacturing will once again be showcasing their products and services live in Shanghai. The exhibition quenches technological thirst of every manufacturing expert. You will find all high-level manufacturing solutions, ranging from precision tools to measuring instruments, automation solutions and – of course – machine tools.

作为瑞士支柱产业之一的瑞士机床行业长久以来一直以其尖端科技和优良品质而享誉世界。该行业包括了大量全球领先的高质量产品制造商和服务企业，也为全球能源生产、交通运输、食品、医疗、建筑、制造和安全等行业提供支持。瑞士机床出口额排到世界前六，其中行业占比高达98%的主要是中小企业。

The Swiss machine tool industry, one of Switzerland's mainstays, is renowned worldwide for its cutting-edge technology and quality. The industry comprises a large number of the world's leading manufacturers of high-quality products and services, and also supports the global energy production, transport, food, medical, construction, manufacturing and security industries. Swiss machine tool exports rank among the top six in the world, with the industry accounting for up to 98 per cent of mainly small and medium-sized enterprises.


今年由瑞士技术产业协会（Swissmem) 组织、瑞士驻华大使馆贸易与投资处（Swiss Business Hub）支持的“瑞士馆”面积达 2300 平米，汇集了30多家主展商共计40多家瑞士制造商和品牌参展。

Organised by Swissmem and supported by Swiss Business Hub, the Swiss Pavilion this year covers 2,300㎡ with more than 30 main exhibitors presenting over 40 Swiss companies and brands.


瑞士机床以其卓越性、可靠性、创新性、广受赞誉和高性能表现为行业提供前沿优势，也为行业的发展提供了额外动力。在经历了数字化、VR 和 AR 的日新月异后，瑞士制造商已经做好了充分准备，迎接与当今制造现实相关的挑战。


Swiss machine tools offer a cutting-edge advantage in terms of excellence, reliability, innovation, acclaim and high performance as well as additional fuel for the way ahead of the industry.  Experiencing further transformation with digitalisation, VR and AR, Swiss manufacturers are fully up to it, taking on the challenges associated with today’s manufacturing realities.
