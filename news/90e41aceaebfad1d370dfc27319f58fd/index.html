广东旭科太阳能科技有限公司

 Exco Solar Technology Co.,Ltd..



广东旭科太阳能科技有限公司，国家高新技术企业，成立于2013年，位于广东佛山，是一家服务全球的专业光伏支架生产企业。2019年获得太阳能光伏支架的设计及生产ISO9001:2015国际质量管理体系认证。旭科太阳能秉承卓越和创新的理念，致力于为全球太阳能光伏电站提供经济安全的光伏支架解决方案及相关产品与服务，主营产品包括BIPV防水支架系统、户用光伏阳光房、车棚光伏支架系统、地面光伏支架系统、工商业屋顶光伏支架系统、跟踪支架系统、螺旋桩光伏支架系统，拥有多项发明专利和实用新型专利。目前已为全球30多个国家和地区的太阳能电站提供超过1 GW的专业支架产品和设计服务，并与国内外诸多知名光伏企业建立长期的战略合作伙伴关系，旭科太阳能已成为光伏电站建设的信心品牌。


Exco Solar Technology Co.,Ltd.

展位号：B13


Exco Solar Technology Co.,Ltd., a national high-tech enterprise, was established in 2013 and is located in Foshan, Guangdong, China. It is a professional photovoltaic mounting manufacturer serving the world. In 2019, it obtained the ISO9001:2015 international quality management system certification for the design and production of solar photovoltaic brackets. Exco Solar adheres to the concept of excellence and innovation and is committed to providing economic and safe photovoltaic mounting solutions and related products and services for global solar photovoltaic power stations. Its main products include household photovoltaic sunshades, carport photovoltaic mounting systems, and ground photovoltaic mounting systems,Industrial and commercial roof photovoltaic mounting system, tracking mounting system, BIPV, spiral pile, has a number of invention patents and utility model patents. At present, it has provided more than 1 GW of professional support products and design services for solar power plants in more than 30 countries and regions around the world, and has established long-term strategic partnerships with many well-known photovoltaic companies at home and abroad.
