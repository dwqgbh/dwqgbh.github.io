【展会预告】ETON 铱腾与您相约-2025印度纺织机械及面辅料展GTE

ETON 铱腾 2025-02-11


印度纺织机械及面辅料展览会（Garment Technology Expo 简称GTE）是南亚最大、最全面的服装科技展览会，本次展会铱腾将协同纺织机械行业在A14展位重磅亮相！


The Garment Technology Expo (GTE) is the largest and most comprehensive garment technology exhibition in South Asia. At this exhibition, ETON will make a grand appearance at Booth A14 together with the garment and textile machinery industry!

集缓存&分拣功能于一体式新款5000型系统

ETON's new 5000 type system with integrating buffering and sorting functions
