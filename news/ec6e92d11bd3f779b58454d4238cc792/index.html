2025汉诺威工业展会盛大开幕,聚焦工业转型与创新发展

青岛高科通信股份有限公司 2025-03-31

   当地时间3月30日晚，全球工业领域的年度盛会—2025汉诺威工业博览会在德国汉诺威会展中心盛大开幕。本届展会以“赋能工业可持续发展”为主题，吸引了来自不同国家和地区的参展商齐聚一堂，共同展示当今及未来的生产与能源供应解决方案，探讨工业领域的前沿技术与发展趋势。

  On the evening of March 30, local time, the annual event of the global industrial field - Hannover MESse 2025 was grand opened at the Hannover Exhibition Center in Germany. With the theme of "Empowering Sustainable Industrial Development", the exhibition attracted more than 3,800 exhibitors from about 60 countries and regions to showcase today's and future production and energy supply solutions, and explore cutting-edge technologies and development trends in the industrial sector.

   青岛高科在本次展会上展示了一系列自主研发的核心产品与创新解决方案。通过展示优质产品，增进了行业交流，积极吸引海外客户和产业合作伙伴。

  Qingdao Gaoke exhibited a series of independently developed core products and innovative solutions at this exhibition. Through the display of high-quality products, industry exchanges have been enhanced, and overseas customers and industrial partners have been actively attracted.

  展会期间，青岛高科的展位吸引了众多国际客户、行业专家以及合作伙伴的驻足交流。公司的技术团队与来自不同国家的企业代表就工业可持续发展领域的技术发展趋势、市场需求以及合作机会展开深入探讨。

  During the exhibition, Qingdao Gaoke's booth attracted many international customers, industry experts and partners to stop and exchange. The company's technical team conducted in-depth discussions with business representatives from different countries on technological trends, market demands and cooperation opportunities in the field of industrial sustainability.

   汉诺威工业博览会将持续至4月4日，在接下来的展会日程里，青岛高科通信将会与全球顶尖企业和专家的交流碰撞中，获取更多创新灵感与合作契机，进一步提升在国际市场的影响力。

  Hannover MESse will last until April 4, in the next exhibition schedule, Qingdao High-tech Communication is expected to get more innovative inspiration and cooperation opportunities in the exchange and collision with the world's top enterprises and experts, and further enhance its influence in the international communication market.
