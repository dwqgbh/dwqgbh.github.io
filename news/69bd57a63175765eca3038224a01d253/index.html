智造湾区,印领全域|美松集团 MASUNG 重磅亮相 2026 华南国际工业博览会

深极测SZGT 2026-06-12


当地时间6月9日，全球新能源行业极具影响力的 2026 欧洲电池展（The Battery Show Europe 2026） 在德国斯图加特会展中心隆重启幕。


作为中国锂电装备领域的创新标杆，深极测再度登陆这场国际行业盛会，携电池全生命周期测试系统与一站式智能制造解决方案强势参展，与全球锂电同仁深入交流、共话发展，全面展现中国锂电装备领域前沿技术与综合服务能力。




🔋 硬核亮相：全栈测试技术，守护电池品质

本次展会上，深极测带来的电池全生命周期测试设备，凭借突破性的性能参数，收获了海外观众与行业专家的一致好评。


  • 搭载0-2000V超宽电压测试范围，可全面覆盖各类电池全品类性能检测需求，从消费电子电芯到大型储能系统，实现全场景适配；

  • 在安全性、稳定性与测试精度上表现突出，拥有0.01%FS超高测量精度与≥99%能量回馈效率，从源头守护电池产品品质，为全球电池制造企业提供专业、可靠的一体化测试解决方案。

深度交流，共话智造





⚙️智造升级：全域解决方案，赋能高效生产

除测试设备外，深极测的一站式智能制造解决方案也成为本次展会的瞩目焦点。现场重点展示了模组 / Pack 自动化组装线、储能集装箱总成线等成熟落地方案，精准贴合新能源产业智能化、自动化升级趋势，助力各大制造企业实现高效生产、绿色低碳发展，充分彰显了深极测在锂电智能制造领域深厚的技术积累与自主创新实力。




🌍 全球服务：扎根中国，服务世界

依托深圳总部、东莞生产基地，深极测已搭建起辐射全球的服务网络：

  • 服务网点覆盖 7 个国家、15 座城市；

  • 国内西南、华中、华东、华南多点布局，国际亚太、欧洲区域服务站点全面落地；


深极测全系列产品已获欧盟 CE 认证，具备完整欧洲市场准入资质，可为欧洲各地客户提供从方案规划、设备供应到运维保障的全链条服务，携手拓展新能源产业布局。



Local time on June 9, The Battery Show Europe 2026, one of the most influential events across the global new energy industry, grandly kicked off at Messe Stuttgart, Germany. 

As an innovative benchmark in China’s lithium battery equipment sector, GEEKTEST once again participated in this world-class industry exhibition. We presented our full lifecycle battery testing systems and one-stop intelligent manufacturing solutions, engaged in in-depth exchanges with lithium battery professionals worldwide to explore industry development, and fully demonstrated the cutting-edge technologies and comprehensive service capabilities of China’s lithium battery equipment industry.


GEEKTEST’s full lifecycle battery testing equipment stood out for its groundbreaking performance parameters and won unanimous praise from overseas visitors and industry experts at the exhibition.

  • Equipped with an ultra-wide testing voltage range of 0-2000V, it fully supports performance testing for all types of batteries. It enables full-scenario application covering consumer electronic cells and large-scale energy storage systems.

  • Boasting outstanding safety, stability and testing precision, the equipment delivers ultra-high measurement accuracy of 0.01% FS and an energy feedback efficiency of no less than 99%. It ensures premium battery quality from the source and provides professional, reliable integrated testing solutions for battery manufacturers around the globe.


Beyond testing equipment, GEEKTEST’s one-stop intelligent manufacturing solutions also became a major highlight of the event. 

We showcased mature industrial solutions including automatic Module & Pack assembly lines and energy storage container assembly lines. These solutions are well-aligned with the intelligent and automated development trend of the new energy industry, assisting manufacturers in achieving high-efficiency production and green low-carbon operation. They also fully reflect GEEKTEST’s profound technological expertise and independent innovation strengths in lithium battery intelligent manufacturing.


Headquartered in Shenzhen with a production base in Dongguan, GEEKTEST has built a global service network with extensive coverage.

  • Our service outlets span 15 cities across 7 countries worldwide.

  • Domestically, we have established service presence in Southwest, Central, East and South China. Internationally, our service stations are fully operational across the Asia-Pacific and Europe.


All GEEKTEST products have obtained CE marking, holding complete qualifications for access to the European market. We are capable of providing European clients with full-chain services ranging from solution planning and equipment supply to operation and maintenance support, and join hands with partners to expand the layout of the new energy industry.
