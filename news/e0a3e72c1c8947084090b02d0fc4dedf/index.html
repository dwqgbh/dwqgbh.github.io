中国进出口商品交易会

全球瞩目，万商云集！2026中国进出口商品交易会（第139届广交会第三期）将于5月1日至5月5日盛大启幕，戴恩将携智慧康养创新产品亮相广交会智慧医疗专区，展现中国前沿健康科技，共筑“美好生活”创新底座。

Global attention, merchants from all over gathering! The 2026 China Import and Export Fair (the third phase of the 139th Canton Fair) will grandly open from May 1st to May 5th. Daneenon will showcase innovative smart health products at the smart healthcare section of the Canton Fair, presenting China's cutting-edge health technologies and jointly building the innovative foundation for "a better life".

China Import and Export Fair

(The third phase of the 139th Canton Fair）


时间：2026年5月1日-5月5日（第三期）

地点：广州广交会展馆

展位号：D区LF友谊大厅21.2C21-22

Time: May 1st - May 5th, 2026 (Phase 3)

Address: China Import and Export Fair Complex, Guangzhou

Booth: 21.2C21-22, LF Friendship Hall, Area D


第139届广交会第三期涵盖健康休闲、玩具及孕婴童、时尚、家用纺织品、文具5大板块21个展区，展览面积51.5万平方米，展位2.5万个。本期聚焦“美好生活”主题，紧扣全球消费者对健康品质、个性消费与情感陪伴的多元需求，集中展现中国企业、中国产品向“新”而行、向“绿”而进的升级趋势，为全球采购商拓宽优质选品视野，呈上一场提升生活品质的创新盛宴。

The third phase of the 139th Canton Fair covers 5 major sections including health and leisure, toys and baby care, fashion, household textiles, and stationery, with 21 exhibition areas and an exhibition area of 515,000 square meters, featuring 25,000 booths. This phase focuses on the theme of "Better Life", closely aligning with the diverse demands of global consumers for healthy quality, personalized consumption, and emotional companionship. It showcases the upgrading trend of Chinese enterprises and products moving towards "new" and "green", providing global buyers with a broader perspective for selecting high-quality products and presenting an innovative feast to enhance the quality of life.



本届广交会第三期聚焦健康品质、智能陪伴、绿色创新，智慧医疗专区全面扩容，戴恩在内的50家优秀企业集中展示AI诊断、手术机器人、智能康复、智能康养全链条创新生态，成为全球健康产业的“风向标”与“首发地”。秉持科技务实、感动常伴初心，戴恩以智能科技破解康复护理痛点，现场展出全系创新产品与解决方案，并预告全新企业赋能新品，向全球彰显中国智造的实力与温度。

The third phase of this Canton Fair focused on health quality, intelligent companionship, and green innovation. The smart healthcare zone was significantly expanded. 50 outstanding enterprises including Daien showcased the full chain of innovation ecosystem in AI diagnosis, surgical robots, intelligent rehabilitation, and intelligent elderly care, becoming the "trendsetter" and "first launch site" of the global health industry. Adhering to the principle of practical technology and constant inspiration, Daneenon used intelligent technology to solve the pain points in rehabilitation care. They exhibited a full range of innovative products and solutions, and announced new enterprise-enabled new products, demonstrating the strength and warmth of Chinese manufacturing to the world.
