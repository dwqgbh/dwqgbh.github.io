【5.14-5.17丨2025大连工博会】天津市骐峰机电设备有限公司

大连国际工业博览会 2025-04-18

展位号：C030-031

天津市骐峰机电设备有限公司

企业介绍

天津市骐峰机电设备有限公司始终专注于机械加工领域的产品供给，本着诚信和稳健的经营理念，骐峰得到了和众多世界优秀制造企业的合作机会，并先后建立了长期和谐的供销关系。

公司产品主要经营磨削产品（产品包含工业刷、刀具、砂轮、砂纸、砂带、磨头、珩磨条等等）、模具配件、设备机械电气备件、轻小型工位起重设备、电动葫芦、北京珞石机器人、埃尔森3D视觉、小羽飞刀提效软件、原点激光加工机床、高周波热炼感应淬火设备、美国道伯防锈产品、汉默欧测头、对刀仪、工业自动化生产线新作和改造、去毛刺专机设备；以及及工装夹具类和工事类项目等。

内部管理方面，骐峰一直致力于不断提高服务水平和增强企业竞争力。企业不断深入推动内部电子化管理和硬件建设，先后不断引进优秀的管理软件，目前整体公司核心管理环节全部使用ERP进行管理。

通过十余年的稳步发展和诚信服务，骐峰工业积累了丰富的汽车行业和制造业集成的经验，客户遍布全国，包括一汽红旗、一汽丰田、广汽丰田、东风日产、本田、广汽传祺、广汽新能源、吉利汽车、大众变速器、奥迪新能源、丹佛斯等企业，为汽车生产企业及相关零部件制造单位提供了优良的设备和服务。


Tianjin Qifeng Electromechanical Equipment Co., Ltd. has always focused on product supply in the field of mechanical processing. With a business philosophy of integrity and stability, Qifeng has been given opportunities to cooperate with numerous world-class manufacturing enterprises and has established long-term and harmonious supply and sales relationships.

The company's main products include grinding products (including industrial brushes, cutting tools, grinding wheels, sandpaper, sandbelts, grinding heads, honing bars, etc.), mold accessories, equipment mechanical and electrical spare parts, American Gaobo lifting equipment, Hitachi gourds, Beijing Luoshi Robotics, Elson 3D vision, aerospace omnidirectional mobile equipment, origin laser machine tools, high-frequency heat refining induction quenching equipment, American Dober rust prevention products New and renovated industrial automation production lines, as well as specialized deburring equipment; And also related to fixture and construction projects.

In terms of internal management, Qifeng has been committed to continuously improving service levels and enhancing corporate competitiveness. The enterprise is continuously promoting internal electronic management and hardware construction, and has continuously introduced excellent management software. Currently, all core management processes of the company are managed using ERP.

Through more than a decade of steady development and honest service, Qifeng Industry has accumulated rich experience in the integration of the automotive industry and manufacturing industry, with customers spread across the country, including FAW Hongqi, FAW Toyota, GAC Toyota, Dongfeng Nissan, Honda, GAC Trumpchi, GAC New Energy, Geely Automobile, Volkswagen Transmission, Audi New Energy, Danfoss and other enterprises, Provided excellent equipment and services for automobile production enterprises and related component manufacturing units.
