2025上海车展,汽车潮流风向标 Chinese Engineering Excellence At Auto Shanghai

Shanghai Spotlight 聚焦上海 2025-04-27





From April 23rd to May 2nd, 2025, the 21st Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition (Auto Shanghai 2025) is being held at the National Exhibition and Convention Center. 

Under the theme "Embrace Innovation, Win the Future," the exhibition spans over 360,000 square meters and brings together nearly 1,000 automotive and parts companies from 26 countries and regions. It serves as a key barometer for the development of China's — and indeed the world's — automotive industry.



本届车展首次将展期延长至“五一”黄金周，配合城市生活、文化、旅游、亲子娱乐等元素，打造“车展+消费+生活方式”的全新观展体验。观众可通过数字化渠道完成实名购票，持电子票实名入场；媒体注册全面电子化，新闻中心为全球媒体提供技术支持。为提升观展舒适度，主办方设置多处咨询台、特殊人群服务区、移动医疗站等贴心配套。


For the first time, the show will extend into China’s May Day holiday, integrating elements of urban life, culture, tourism, and family-friendly entertainment to offer a brand-new experience that blends automotive innovation with lifestyle consumption. 

The implementation of digital ticketing and real-name entry will enhance convenience for visitors, while the fully digital media registration and a comprehensive news center provide robust support to global press covering the event. Additional services, such as numerous information desks, accessibility zones, and mobile medical stations, ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone.



车展同期还举办“全球汽车领袖圆桌会议”“智能汽车技术论坛”“汽车半导体生态大会”等多场高规格行业会议，搭建全球汽车产业链对话与交流平台。


High-level industry events are held concurrently, including the Global Automotive Leaders Roundtable, the Intelligent Vehicle Technology Forum, and the Automotive Semiconductor Ecosystem Summit—creating a powerful dialogue and collaboration platform for the global automotive supply chain.



Industry Spotlight: Auto Shanghai Leads the New Global Automotive Landscape



在中国已成为全球最重要汽车市场的大背景下，2025上海车展不仅是新车发布的平台，更是未来出行趋势的展望窗口。奔驰、宝马、奥迪、大众、丰田、本田、特斯拉等国际主流品牌齐聚现场，而一众中国品牌如比亚迪、吉利、蔚来、小鹏、理想等也全面展示自主研发与智能制造的新成果。


Taken in the context of China becoming the world's most important automotive market, Auto Shanghai 2025 is not just a platform for launching exciting new vehicles, but also a window into the future of mobility. 

Leading global brands such as Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, Volkswagen, Toyota, Honda, and Tesla bring representatives to discuss their latest products and developments, while prominent Chinese manufacturers like BYD, Geely, NIO, XPeng, and Li Auto showcase their achievements in self-developed technology and intelligent manufacturing.



Brand Highlights


天元智能国民第一车东风纳米06即将开启预售
The Dongfeng Nammi 06, Tianyuan Intelligent's first national car, is set to start pre-sales



东风汽车旗下技术品牌“天元智能”迎来首款量产车--东风纳米06。该车集成“天元智驾和天元智舱"两大核心技术，真正从硬件、软件和场景感知三位一体重构智能纯电出行体验。官方定位其为“天元智能国民第一车”，充分体现东风对高性价比智能纯电市场的战略布局。



Dongfeng Motor's subsidiary technology brand "Tianyuan Intelligent" unveils its first mass-produced vehicle – the Dongfeng NAMMI 06, the first mass-produced model under its tech-forward "Tianyuan Intelligence" brand.


The vehicle integrates two core technologies – Tianyuan Smart Drive and Tianyuan Smart Cockpit – redefining intelligent EV experiences through a trinity of hardware, software, and contextual awareness. Positioned as the "People's First Intelligent EV" under Tianyuan Intelligence, this launch crystallises Dongfeng's strategic push into the high cost-performance smart electric vehicle market.



保时捷：以 “经典未来共驰” 

演绎豪华车新哲学

Porsche: Redefining Automotive Excellence Through "Timeless Innovation in Motion"



保时捷在 2025 上海车展以史上最大展车矩阵诠释 “未来源于传承”，全球限量 1500 台的 911 Spirit 70 作为 Heritage Design 系列第三款车型全球首发，将 70 年代复古美学与 T-Hybrid 高性能混动系统深度融合；同期推出的 Cayenne 劲驰版与逐梦版、纯电动 Macan 双版本，以中国专属车型矩阵覆盖全场景出行需求，搭配研发中心战略升级聚焦本土数智解决方案。



At the 2025 Shanghai Auto Show, Porsche unveiled its largest-ever exhibition lineup to date, showcasing its "Future Born from Heritage" philosophy. The global spotlight fell on the 911 Spirit 70 – limited to 1,500 units worldwide – marking the world premiere of the third model in the Heritage Design series. 

This collector's gem seamlessly blends 1970s retro aesthetics with the groundbreaking T-Hybrid high-performance hybrid system. Concurrently launched are the Cayenne Dynamic Edition and Dream Chaser Edition, alongside dual variants of the all-electric Macan – a China-exclusive vehicle portfolio engineered to address specific mobility scenarios. These offerings are synergistically paired with strategic enhancements to the R&D centre, prioritizing localized digital-intelligent solutions in a major effort from the brand.


宾利：奢华与性能的极致融合

Bentley: The Ultimate Fusion of Luxury and Performance



宾利品牌携全新阵容亮相2025上海车展，包括Continental GT Speed、Flying Spur Mulliner等标志性车型，展现其在超豪华市场的稳固地位。宾利在中国市场持续推进定制化战略，并在车展现场重点呈现可持续豪华内饰用材，强调环保与奢华的共融。



Bentley presented a full lineup at the show, including the Continental GT Speed and Flying Spur Mulliner, reinforcing its stature in the ultra-luxury segment. 


Emphasizing its tailored strategies for the Chinese market, Bentley also highlighted sustainable luxury materials in its interiors, merging elegance with environmental responsibility.



蔚来：三大品牌联袂亮相

诠释智能汽车“新三大件”

NIO: Three Brands United to Redefine the 'New Three Components' of Smart Vehicles



本届车展，蔚来首次以集团形式携三大品牌——NIO蔚来、ONVO乐道与Firefly萤火虫联袂参展。其中，NIO展出包括旗舰车型ET9在内的多款智能产品，并首次提出“智能汽车新三大件”概念：智能硬件、计算平台与智能算法，强调系统能力的整体构建将是下一阶段竞争核心。



For the first time, NIO appeared at the show as a group with its three brands: NIO, ONVO, and Firefly. Showcasing flagship models like the ET9, NIO introduced the concept of the "New Three Components" of smart vehicles, that being intelligent hardware, computing platforms and smart algorithms, asserting that holistic systems capability is the next core element of competitiveness in the industry. 



凯迪拉克：LOVE纯电家族全球首秀

Cadillac: Global Premiere of the LOVE Electric Family



凯迪拉克在车展期间举办“LOVE ELECTRIC FAMILY”发布会，三款全球首发车型：Lyriq锐歌、Optiq锐意、Vistiq锐境正式亮相，构成凯迪拉克纯电家族矩阵，全面传达其在电动化浪潮中的品牌态度。全新车型在续航、安全、智能座舱等方面实现重大突破，并延续凯迪拉克一贯的美式豪华基因。



Cadillac held the global premiere of its LOVE Electric Family, unveiling three new models: Lyriq, Optiq, and Vistiq. This pure electric lineup represents Cadillac's bold push into electrification, with significant advancements in range, safety, and intelligent cockpit systems—all while retaining its iconic American luxury DNA.


Unity 中国三大创新技术亮相上海车展 

赋能汽车产业数字化

Unity China Debuts Three Innovative Technologies at Shanghai Auto Show to Empower Automotive Digital Transformation



在 2025 上海国际车展，Unity 中国展示三大核心技术：与蔚来合作的数字孪生工厂实现制造全流程数字化，融合多模态交互与 AI 的 HMI 平行空间智慧座舱引领智能座舱 5.0，针对 “第四块屏幕” 的车载游戏生态开发多屏互动等专属形态。其已与 35 家全球车企合作，为 77 款量产车型提供解决方案，并通过联发科、高德等本土生态协作夯实技术底座，推动中国汽车产业从座舱到制造的全链条数字化升级。


At the 2025 Shanghai Auto Show, Unity China unveiled three key technologies: a digital twin factory with NIO for end-to-end manufacturing digitization, an HMI Parallel Space smart cockpit integrating multi-modal interaction and AI to lead the 5.0 era, and an in-vehicle gaming ecosystem targeting the "fourth screen" with exclusive experiences like multi-screen interaction. 

Partnering with 35 global automakers for 77 models, it strengthens local tech foundations through collaborations with MediaTek and Amap, driving full-chain digital upgrading in China's automotive industry from cockpits to manufacturing.


Brembo布雷博：智能制动科技再进化

Brembo: Smart Braking Technology Evolved



全球领先的高性能制动系统供应商Brembo本次展出多款前沿技术，包括面向新能源车型的轻量化制动系统及核心技术Sensify™智能制动系统。该系统通过电子控制与AI算法组合，实现制动精准响应与个性化调校，代表智能驾驶时代下“安全+性能”的制动发展趋势。



Global braking systems leader Brembo showcased a series of cutting-edge technologies, including lightweight solutions for EVs and its core innovation, the Sensify™ intelligent braking system. 


Powered by AI and electronic control, Sensify delivers precision response and customizable braking, setting new standards for safety and performance in the era of smart mobility.



New Exhibition Dimensions: From Auto Show to the Future Mobility Ecosystem



除了整车品牌的精彩亮相，2025上海车展还设立了涵盖自动驾驶、车联网、芯片系统、能源科技等领域的汽车科技与供应链展区。配套举办的全球汽车领袖圆桌会议、汽车关键技术论坛等20余场高规格活动，更将上海车展打造成产业链深度协同、全球汽车科技交流的重要平台。


Beyond automakers, Auto Shanghai 2025 features dedicated zones for autonomous driving, vehicle networking, chip systems, and energy tech. 


Over 20 premium forums and conferences—including the Global Automotive Leaders Roundtable and International Automotive Key Technology Forum—further establish the show as a key hub for collaborative innovation across the global mobility ecosystem.



Empowered by Technology, Enhanced by Experience—Auto Shanghai Reshapes the Global Automotive Ecosystem



回顾1985年首届举办至今，上海车展已成为全球汽车企业争相展示前沿技术和战略愿景的重要舞台。2025年，这一平台再次以开放、创新、专业的姿态，引领全球汽车产业拥抱变化、共赢未来。


Since its inception in 1985, Auto Shanghai has been a vital stage for the automotive industry’s technological progress and strategic vision. In 2025, it once again steps forward with openness, innovation, and professionalism—guiding the global auto sector toward transformation and shared success.
