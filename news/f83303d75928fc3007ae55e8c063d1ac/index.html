      Ningbo Newthink Motor Inc., which registered with a  capital of 20 million yuan, was established on March 9th, 2011. As a national high-tech enterprise, it specializes in the line with research, development, production, and sales of brushless motors and fans. The company occupies an area exceeding 3,000 square meters.

      

      The company specializes in the research,  enterprise development, product manufacturing, and sales of brushless motors, fans, and drive control systems. It holds ISO 9001 quality management system certification, and its products have obtained certifications including WTL, CE, UL, and RoHS.

      

      Since its establishment, the company has focused on the R&D, production, and application of brushless motors, fans, and drive control systems. It operates within the critical components (subassemblies) sector of Ningbo's Trillion-yuan Industrial Cluster, offering a comprehensiveproduct portfolio. 


      Its solutions are primarily deployed in industrial automation equipment such as intelligent sewing machinery and high-end spinning equipment; core intelligent logistics systems including automated storage systems and AGVs; medical devices; home appliances; industrial compact fume extraction and dust removal environmental equipment; and new energy sectors like fuel cells. 


      In 2022, the company passed the government acceptance inspection for Ningbo's digital workshop construction initiative.

世界热泵产业展览会 / 2026.9.16-18

世界热泵产业博览会（简称“WHPE”）是全球清洁供热领域极具影响力的专业展会，2026年9月16-18日将在广州·广交会展馆盛大启幕。大会立足数字孪生、庞大精准邀约数据等优势，辐射亚洲核心市场，联动全球产业资源，以“聚势热泵技术，赋能零碳转型”为核心定位，对接企业发布前沿新品、开拓海内外市场，亦助力行业人士洞析政策趋势、对接优质资源，WHPE都将成为不可错过的核心枢纽，为全球能源转型提供可落地、高价值的热能解决方案，打造热泵全产业链一站式商贸与创新平台。
