CMPF Investment Promotion

尊敬的全国摩托车、电动车及配件行业同仁：

Dear Friends from Motorcycle, Electric Vehicle, and Accessories Enterprises:

当外国友人来华政策持续优化，对外贸易口径不断扩大，摩托车产业南方国家布局加速推进，全球化协同发展浪潮势不可挡。承载千年商贸文明的岭南名城广州，正以粤港澳大湾区核心引擎之姿，续写开放包容的时代华章。正值产业发展的黄金机遇期，行业盛会如约启幕——第 92 届（2026秋）全国摩托车及配件展示交易会，定于 2026 年 11 月 10-12 日在广州保利世贸博览馆隆重召开！现正式面向全球行业同仁发出邀请，敬候海内外业界朋友齐聚珠江之畔，共享发展机遇，共拓全球市场！

As policies for foreign nationals visiting China continue to be refined, foreign trade channels are further expanded, the layout of the motorcycle industry in Global South countries is accelerating, and the wave of global collaborative development is irresistible. Guangzhou, a renowned city in Lingnan with a thousand-year history of trade and commerce, is writing a new chapter of openness and inclusiveness in the new era as a core engine of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area. Seizing this golden opportunity for industrial development, our industry event kicks off as scheduled – the 92nd (Autumn 2026) China Motorcycle and Parts Fair will be grandly held at the Guangzhou Poly World Trade Center Expo from November 10 to 12, 2026! We hereby extend a formal invitation to industry colleagues worldwide and sincerely look forward to gathering with friends from the industry at home and abroad on the banks of the Pearl River to share development opportunities and explore the global market together!

【黄金区位，外贸枢纽赋能】

广州作为国家重要中心城市、粤港澳大湾区核心引擎，立足国际性综合交通枢纽与千年商都、国际商贸中心优势，锚定"一点两地"全新战略定位，强化综合性门户功能，聚力打造国际消费中心、国际贸易中心、国际会展中心、国际产业链供应链中心，拥有空铁联运、江海联动的立体交通网络与全球高端要素集聚辐射能力，是链接国内国际双循环、拓展全球市场的核心门户与战略高地。依托“产业集群+数字贸易+国际物流”三位一体的创新型贸易生态，为中国摩配企业链接全球、畅通渠道、提升价值，共享全球化发展新红利。

【Strategic Location, Empowered by a Foreign Trade Hub】

As a major national central city and the core engine of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, Guangzhou leverages its advantages as an international comprehensive transportation hub, a millennial commercial capital and an international trade center. It adheres to its new positioning as a pivotal node and a dual-core base, strengthens its comprehensive gateway functions, and focuses on building an international consumption center, an international trade center, an international convention and exhibition center, and an international industrial and supply chain center. Boasting a three-dimensional transportation network integrating air-rail intermodal transport and river-sea combined transport, as well as the capacity to gather and radiate high-end global factors, Guangzhou is a core gateway and strategic highland connecting the domestic and international dual circulations and expanding the global market. Relying on an innovative trade ecosystem integrating "industrial clusters+digital trade+international logistics", it helps Chinese motorcycle parts enterprises connect with the world, smooth channels, enhance value and share the new dividends of global development.

【精准对接，全球商机直达】

当前，东南亚、非洲、中东、拉美等全球南方国家及地区已成为摩托车及配件进口需求最旺盛的增长极。当地摩托车保有量持续攀升、本土配套产能不足，对高性价比燃油配件、耐用易损件、电摩核心部件及改装用品形成刚性、持续、大规模进口需求，为中国摩配企业出海提供广阔增量空间。与此同时，全球市场对中国摩托车整车的需求同样迎来爆发式增长，中国整车凭借可靠品质、高性价比与快速迭代的电动化、智能化优势，在全球代步通勤、物流运输、休闲出行等场景广泛普及，出口呈现量价齐升、结构升级的良好态势，小排量实用车型与中大排量个性车型同步走俏，为中国摩企品牌全球化奠定坚实基础。

【Precise Matching, Direct Access to Global Business Opportunities】

Currently, Global South countries including Southeast Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and Latin America have emerged as the most dynamic growth poles for motorcycle and parts import demand. With continuously growing motorcycle ownership and insufficient local supporting production capacity in these regions, there is rigid, sustained, and large-scale import demand for cost-effective fuel parts, durable wear parts, core components for electric motorcycles, and modification accessories, providing vast incremental space for Chinese motorcycle parts enterprises to expand overseas. Meanwhile, the global market is also experiencing explosive growth in demand for complete Chinese motorcycles. Leveraging their reliable quality, high cost-performance, and rapid iteration advantages in electrification and intelligence, Chinese complete motorcycles are gaining widespread popularity in global scenarios such as commuting, logistics transportation, and leisure travel. Exports are showing a positive trend of both volume and price increases and structural upgrading, with practical small-displacement models and personalized medium-to-large-displacement models both selling well, laying a solid foundation for the globalization of Chinese motorcycle enterprises' brands.

【四十五年积淀，品牌口碑卓著】

全国摩配会自 1981 年创办以来，始终与中国摩托车产业同步发展，陪伴并见证了众多企业从成长到壮大的全过程，已成为国内积淀深厚、影响力广泛的行业品牌盛会。作为每年秋季固定在广州举办的行业旗舰展会，展会在延续 “全球买家精准对接”、“展贸一体化” 等经市场检验的成熟机制之上，深度依托广州作为国际商贸中心、国际性综合交通枢纽的独特优势，持续释放千年商都的开放势能与全球资源聚合效能，全力为企业搭建价值密度更高、效能更优的全球市场拓展枢纽，积极链接全球资源，打造一流服务生态。

【45 Years of Accumulation, a Prestigious Industry Brand】

Founded in 1981, the China Motorcycle and Parts Fair has always developed in step with China's motorcycle industry, accompanying and witnessing the entire growth process of numerous enterprises from their establishment to expansion, and has become a well-established and highly influential industry brand event in China. As a flagship industry exhibition held in Guangzhou every autumn, on the basis of continuing the market-proven mature mechanisms such as "precise matching of global buyers" and "integration of exhibition and trade", the fair deeply leverages Guangzhou's unique advantages as an international trade center and an international comprehensive transportation hub. It continuously unleashes the opening-up potential of this millennial commercial capital and the global resource aggregation effect, and strives to build a global market expansion hub with higher value density and efficiency for enterprises, and construct a first-class service ecosystem by connecting global resources.

【展会亮点】

· 超70,000㎡展览规模

· 吸引2000+优质参展商

【Fair Highlights】

·Over 2,000 high-quality exhibitors

·Over 50,000 professional buyers from China and more than 80 countries and regions

·Covering complete motorcycles, electric vehicles, bicycles and all categories of their parts and components

当前国际格局深刻调整，全球产业链加速重构，中国凭借稳定的产业基础、完善的供应链体系与持续扩大的对外开放优势，成为全球摩托车及配件行业最可靠的合作阵地与增长引擎。以人工智能为代表的新技术浪潮，正为产品研发、智能制造、跨境贸易与全球营销带来全新变革，为行业高质量发展注入强劲动能。第 92 届（2026秋）全国摩托车及配件展示交易会将继续立足粤港澳大湾区核心门户优势，汇聚全球资源、对接全球市场，助力企业抢抓 AI 赋能与全球南方市场爆发的双重机遇。我们真诚期待与海内外业界同仁相聚珠江之畔，共话行业新趋势、共享发展新红利、共拓全球新蓝海，携手共创中国摩托车及配件产业更加繁荣的未来！

Against the backdrop of profound adjustments in the international landscape and accelerated restructuring of the global industrial chain, China, with its stable industrial foundation, improved supply chain system and advantages of continuously expanded opening-up, has become the most reliable cooperation base and growth engine for the global motorcycle and parts industry. The new wave of technologies represented by artificial intelligence is bringing a brand-new transformation to product R&D, intelligent manufacturing, cross-border trade and global marketing, injecting strong impetus into the high-quality development of the industry. The 92nd (Autumn, 2026) China Motorcycle and Parts Fair will continue to base itself on the advantages of the core gateway of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, gather global resources, connect with the global market, and help enterprises seize the dual opportunities of AI empowerment and the booming Global South market. We sincerely look forward to gathering with industry colleagues at home and abroad on the banks of the Pearl River to discuss new industry trends, share new development dividends, explore new global blue oceans, and join hands to create a more prosperous future for China's motorcycle and parts industry!
