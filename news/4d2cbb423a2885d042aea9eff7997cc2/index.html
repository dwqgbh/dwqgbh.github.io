[ The 136th Canton Fair]

——  第136届进出口商品交易会  ——



爱屋安防 · 外贸


成都爱屋安防的外贸出口总额$500万美金，年增长率10%，公司主营业务安防类及特种类门窗，泄爆类门窗，工业智能门窗，及家用智能门窗等特殊的门窗安防产品。主要合作重要单位为军队，政府，公安，看守所，监狱等单位。公司未来出口市场方向将致力于特殊安防类门窗，目前公司技术团队正针对国际标准及市场需求积极研发统一标准的特种门窗。

Chengdu AiWu Security's foreign trade export revenue is $500,000 USD, with a growth rate of 10% per year. The company's main business is security-related and special types of windows and doors, explosion-proof windows and doors, industrial smart windows and doors, and home smart windows and doors, etc. The company's main cooperative parters are military, government, public security, detention centers, prisons, etc. The company's future export market direction will focus on special security-related windows and doors. Currently, the company's technical team is mainly developing standardized special windows and doors that meet international standards and market demands.





成都爱屋安防科技有限公司第二期展出，展位号：12.1K18

Chengdu AiWu Security Technology Co., Ltd. is exhibiting in Period 2, booth number: 12.1K18


广交会是展示新产品、寻觅新商机、了解新趋势的窗口，各企业纷纷展示新产品。广州琶洲国际会展中心热闹非凡、人潮涌动，每日数以万计的外商进入展馆，密集分布的展位间人流穿梭，采购商和参展商在其乐融融的交流洽谈中搭建起合作的贸易之桥。

The Canton Fair is a great chance for showcasing new products, seeking new business opportunities, and learning about new trends. Companies are displaying their new products at the exhibition. The Guangzhou Pazhou International Convention and Exhibition Center is bustling with activity, with people streaming in and out of the exhibition halls. Thousands of foreign buyers enter the exhibition halls every day, and people flow between the densely distributed booths. Buyers and exhibitors are building a bridge of cooperation through joyful exchanges and negotiations.

来自各国的客户对我们展出的产品都很有兴趣，我们的同事都很认真的向客户讲解产品。经过同事的介绍，客户了解产品之后，对我们的技术和产品的质量都很满意，都主动地添加联系方式，成为了我们未来的潜在客户。

Customers from various countries showed great interest in the products we displayed, and our colleagues were diligently explaining the products to them. After being introduced by colleagues, customers learned about the product and were satisfied with our technology and product quality. They actively added contact information and became potential future customers.


还有客户当场买下咱们的展品，直接用来作展厅的展品。

Some customers even bought our samples and they are going to and put samples directly as exhibits in their showrooms.

在此，我们满怀期待地祈愿第136届广交会能够继续发挥磁石般的吸引力，汇聚全球客商，为企业开拓市场、赢得订单添砖加瓦。

We sincerely hope with great expectation that the 136th Canton Fair will continue to exert its magnetic attraction, gather global merchants, and contribute to enterprises’ market development and order winning.
