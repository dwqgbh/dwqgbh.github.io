About Agri Vietnam 2026

“2026越南国际农业展 （Agri Vietnam 2026）” 将于 2026 年 6 月 10 日至 12 日 在 越南胡志明市西贡会展中心（SECC） 盛大举行。作为越南规模最大、影响力最强的农业展览会，本届活动将汇聚来自国内外的 农业生产商、经销商、农业科技企业与国际机构，共同搭建一个集 商务洽谈、技术交流与品牌展示 于一体的高端平台。


🌏 聚焦东盟市场，拓展区域合作新格局

Agri Vietnam 2026 特别聚焦 东盟农业市场开拓与区域合作，助力越南成为连接亚洲农业创新与国际市场的重要枢纽。

展会将汇聚来自 中国、泰国、马来西亚、印尼、柬埔寨及菲律宾 等国家的行业代表，共同探索：

✅ 智慧农业

✅ 精准灌溉

✅ 农业数字化

✅ 可持续农业价值链


通过促进区域农业科技共享、市场对接与投资合作，Agri Vietnam 2026 将成为推动东盟农业协同发展的重要平台。


💡 助力企业开拓东盟农业市场


展会期间，主办方将推出 商务配对会、投资推介会 与 农业科技论坛 等活动，全面展示东盟农业市场的新需求。

重点展示领域包括：

🌱 种业科技与生物育种

🚜 农业机械与自动化设备

💧 有机肥料与绿色投入品

🐟 水产养殖与畜牧管理

🥭 农产品加工与出口供应链


这将为参展企业提供面向东盟市场的全方位贸易与投资机会。


🌿 引领东盟农业的可持续未来


Agri Vietnam 2026 不仅是一场展会，更是 东盟农业创新、投资与合作的重要平台。

展会将推动区域农业供应链协同、促进资本与技术流动，并助力越南成为 区域农业创新与出口枢纽。


📢 加入 Agri Vietnam 2026，共探东盟农业新机遇！携手打造东南亚农业合作与可持续发展的新篇章！

Agri Vietnam 2026 will take place from June 10–12, 2026 at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), Ho Chi Minh City， Vietnam.


As Vietnam’s leading and important agricultural exhibition, it will bring together hundreds of manufacturers, distributors, agritech innovators, and international organizations, serving as a gateway for business networking, knowledge exchange, and sustainable agricultural cooperation.


🌍 ASEAN Market Expansion & Regional Cooperation


The 2026 edition emphasizes ASEAN agricultural market development and cross-border partnerships.

It will attract participants from Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Cambodia, and the Philippines, highlighting:


  • Smart Farming Technologies

  • Precision Irrigation & Automation

  • Digital Agriculture Systems

  • Sustainable Value Chains


Agri Vietnam 2026 will empower regional stakeholders to strengthen collaboration and jointly drive agricultural transformation and export competitiveness across ASEAN.


💼 Business & Investment Opportunities


The exhibition will feature B2B meetings, investor networking sessions, and technology forums to connect buyers and suppliers in fast-growing areas such as:


  • Seed Technology & Breeding

  • Agricultural Machinery

  • Organic Inputs & Fertilizers

  • Aquaculture & Livestock Management

  • Post-harvest Processing & Export Supply Chains

These sectors represent the core demand and innovation directions of Vietnam and the wider ASEAN market.


🌾 Shaping the Future of ASEAN Agriculture


Agri Vietnam 2026 is not only an exhibition — it is a regional catalyst for agricultural innovation and cooperation.


By fostering investment, technology exchange, and market connectivity, it positions Vietnam as a key agricultural gateway for the ASEAN region.


👉 Join Agri Vietnam 2026 to explore opportunities, partnerships, and innovations driving the future of ASEAN agriculture!

展馆介绍

About SECC

西贡会展中心（SECC）位于越南胡志明市东南部的西贡区，该会展中心建成于2014年，是越南南方地区最具规模的会展中心之一。西贡会展中心占地约 50,000平方米，拥有2个室内展示厅、序厅和会议厅及超过22000平米的室外展示区域，可同时容纳多场规模不同的活动。其中最大的展示厅面积达到9,000平方米，可容纳超过20,000人的参观者。 西贡会展中心已经成功举办了多项重要的国内、国际展览和会议活动，如越南食品展、越南国际旅游博览会、亚洲汽车配件展、中国-东盟媒体合作论坛等。这些活动吸引了众多海内外企业和观众参与，成为促进越南经济和文化交流的重要平台。会展中心地址：799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City  

Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), located in District 7, Ho Chi Minh City, is one of southern Vietnam’s largest and most modern venues. Built in 2014, it covers 50,000 sqm with two exhibition halls, a foyer, meeting rooms, and 22,000 sqm of outdoor space, hosting multiple events at once. The main hall spans 9,000 sqm, accommodating over 20,000 visitors.


SECC has hosted leading local and international exhibitions such as the Vietnam Foodexpo, Vietnam International Tourism Fair, and the China–ASEAN Media Cooperation Forum, serving as a key hub for trade, investment, and cultural exchange in Vietnam. 📍 Address: 799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu Ward, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City.
