中捷出征CONFEMAQ 2026,硬核智造圈粉南美市场

中捷缝纫机官方 2026-06-09


6月2—4日，阿根廷国际服装机械展（CONFEMAQ 2026）在布宜诺斯艾利斯La Rural会展中心举行。实力铸就焦点，中捷缝纫机成现场 “人气王”。

June 2–4 – The Argentina International Garment Machinery Exhibition (CONFEMAQ 2026) was held at La Rural Convention Center in Buenos Aires. Strength takes the spotlight – ZOJE sewing machines became the undisputed crowd favorite on site.








作为南美核心缝制设备专业盛会——本届展会联动EMITEX纱线展、SIMATEX面料展同期举办，打通纱线、面料、纺织机械、缝制设备到成品制造的全产业链闭环。

As a core professional event for sewing equipment in South America, this exhibition was held concurrently with the EMITEX Yarn Fair and SIMATEX Fabric Fair, creating a complete closed‑loop industrial chain from yarn, fabric, textile machinery, and sewing equipment to finished product manufacturing.



在本届展会上，A7100高速静音智能平缝机、B9500高速智能包缝机及线迹大师系列等多款核心智能装备，搭配全套智能缝制整体解决方案，全方位覆盖平缝、包缝、绷缝及自动化生产场景，为南美纺织服装产业智能化升级注入全新动能。

At this exhibition, a range of core intelligent equipment including the A7100 high-speed quiet intelligent lockstitch machine, the B9500 high-speed intelligent overlock machine, and the StitchMaster series, together with a complete set of intelligent sewing solutions, comprehensively covered flat-sewing, overlocking, interlocking, and automated production scenarios, injecting new momentum into the intelligent upgrading of South America’s textile and garment industry.



中捷与当地拥有95年历史的资深代理商联合亮相，依托其深耕阿根廷及南美市场的渠道与服务体系，中捷的智能缝制方案获得了本土客户的高度评价。


意向客户覆盖阿根廷、智利、秘鲁等国，涵盖服装代工、工业缝纫线等关键领域，圆满完成本次南美之旅。

ZOJE made a joint appearance with a local agent boasting 95 years of history. Leveraging the agent’s deeply established distribution and service networks in Argentina and the broader South American market, ZOJE’s intelligent sewing solutions received high praise from local customers.

 Prospective clients covered countries such as Argentina, Chile, and Peru, spanning key sectors including garment OEM and industrial sewing thread, bringing this South American journey to a successful close.




在全球纺织产业格局重构的当下，南美市场凭借独特的发展魅力与巨大的增长潜力，成为全球缝制产业布局的新焦点。而阿根廷作为南美第二大经济体，纺织服装产业基础雄厚，当地企业正加速向智能化、高效化升级，对高品质、高性能的智能缝制设备需求愈发迫切。

Amid the ongoing restructuring of the global textile industry, the South American market, with its unique development charm and substantial growth potential, has become a new focal point for global sewing industry players. Argentina, as the second‑largest economy in South America, has a strong textile and garment industrial base. Local enterprises are accelerating their transition toward intelligence and efficiency, creating increasingly urgent demand for high‑quality, high‑performance intelligent sewing equipment.



多年来，中捷始终坚持全球化发展战略，与当地资深代理商两代携手、深度合作，深耕阿根廷及南美区域市场，熟悉当地产业政策、生产需求与工艺痛点，持续迭代产品技术、优化服务体系，如今已成为南美市场备受信赖的智能缝制解决方案合作伙伴。


未来，中捷将持续立足技术创新，坚守品质初心，持续深耕全球市场，积极把握海外产业机遇，以更优质的产品、更完善的解决方案、更专业的服务，赋能全球缝制产业高质量发展，与海内外客户携手共赢未来！

For many years, ZOJE has consistently adhered to its global development strategy, working hand in hand across two generations with its experienced local agent in deep collaboration. By cultivating the Argentine and broader South American markets, we have gained an in-depth understanding of local industrial policies, production needs, and process pain points. Through continuous product technology iteration and service system optimization, ZOJE has now become a trusted partner for intelligent sewing solutions in the South American market.

Looking ahead, ZOJE will continue to base itself on technological innovation, uphold its original commitment to quality, keep deepening its presence in global markets, actively seize overseas industry opportunities, and empower the high‑quality development of the global sewing industry with better products, more complete solutions, and more professional services – working hand in hand with customers at home and abroad for a win‑win future.
