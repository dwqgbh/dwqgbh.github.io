孟加拉驻昆明总领事穆罕默德·卡勒德出席2025国际货博会开幕式并致辞

国际货博会 2025-11-11

2025年11月7日，由中国物流与采购联合会、中国国际海运网主办的2025年第二十届中国国际货运产业链博览会开幕式宁波举行，孟加拉人民共和国驻昆明总领事馆总领事穆罕默德·卡勒德（Mr. Mohammed Khaled）出席并致辞。

总领事穆罕默德·卡勒德在致辞中介绍孟加拉国是物流投资极具前景之地，其经济快速增长，物流行业稳步增长且潜力巨大。地理位置优越，是南亚与东盟间门户，多港口及交通建设将大幅降低物流成本。政府重视物流发展，政策优惠，成本低廉。孟中关系紧密，合作基础良好。在此背景下，孟中在物流多领域合作可互利共赢，推动区域发展。


在2025第二十届中国国际货运产业链博览会上致辞

孟加拉人民共和国驻昆明总领事馆 总领事 穆罕默德·卡勒德（Mr. Mohammed Khaled）

2025年11月7日 浙江·宁波

Mr. Kang Shuchun, Founder and Chairman of the WIFFA International Freight Forwarders Credit Cooperative Platform, and CEO of ShippingChina.com

Mr. Cui Zhongfu, Chief Economist of China Federation of Logistics & Purchasing (CFLP)

Members of Diplomatic and Consular Corp

Distinguished Guests, Ladies and Gentlemen

Very Good Evening

WIFFA国际货运代理信用合作平台创始人、中国国际海运网CEO康树春先生

中国物流与采购联合会总经济师崔忠付先生

外交及领事团成员

各位来宾，女士们，先生们

晚上好


WIFFA Expo

It is indeed a privilege to be here with you in this opening ceremony of 20th China International Freight Forwarding Alliance Expo 2025 in Ningbo. I thank the organizers (China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing and shippingchina.com) for giving me opportunity speak a few words.

很荣幸出席在宁波举行的2025年第20届中国国际货运产业链博览会开幕式。感谢主办方中国物流与采购联合会和中国国际海运网邀请出席大会并致辞。

WIFFA Expo

As this occasion brought us here together with many of the leaders of the logistics sector, I take the privilege to portray Bangladesh as one of the most prospective destinations for investors, who are looking for new frontiers of logistics sector development. 

这次活动让我们与物流业的众多领导人齐聚一堂。我很荣幸地介绍，孟加拉国是寻求物流业发展新领域的投资者最具前景的目的地之一。

WIFFA Expo

Bangladesh is one of the fastest growing economies in Asia, experienced a steady growth of 6–7% over the last 15 years and projected to become the 26th largest economy in a decade! Its logistics industry is expanding steadily, driven by strong export-import growth and infrastructure investments. Freight and logistics services account for a significant share of the national economy, supporting nearly every sector—from manufacturing and agriculture to e-commerce. But this hardly depicts the underlying prospect of logistics industry in Bangladesh.

孟加拉国是亚洲增长最快的经济体之一，过去15年里，其经济一直保持着6%-7%的稳定增速，预计十年内将成为全球第26大经济体。在进出口强劲增长以及基础设施投资不断加大的推动下，孟加拉国的物流业正稳步扩张。货运和物流服务在国民经济中占据很大比重，几乎为制造业、农业到电子商务等各个行业提供支撑。然而，仅靠这些还难以充分描绘出孟加拉国物流业的潜在发展前景。

WIFFA Expo

Bangladesh is strategically located between South Asia and Southeast Asia and Its position makes it a gateway between South Asia and ASEAN, aligning with the BCIM Corridor and China’s Maritime Silk Road vision. This geographically advantageous position particularly location of its major sea ports and deep-sea ports made it a natural hub in the greater region.

孟加拉国地处南亚与东南亚之间的战略要冲，是连接南亚和东盟的门户，这与孟中印缅经济走廊以及中国“海上丝绸之路”的愿景高度契合。凭借这种得天独厚的地理优势，尤其是其境内主要海港及深海港口所处的优越位置，孟加拉国成为了更大区域范围内的天然枢纽。

WIFFA Expo

Expanded capacity of Chittagong port, construction of two deep sea-ports in Matarbari and Payra, modernization of Mongla port with completion of Padma Bridge and Karnafuli tunnel and many Expressways connecting north to south and east to west domestically, and adequate rail and road connectivity with India and presence of similar infrastructure to be connected with Myanmar, Bangladesh is set to witness an era of seamless road, rail, and port connectivity—cutting travel times, reducing congestion and enabling faster cargo flow and ultimately reducing logistics costs by up to 35%. The rise of e-commerce and f-commerce (social media-based businesses) is creating huge demand for urban and regional logistics solutions. E-commerce turnover reaching to nearly a billion USD, with local companies like Pathao, E-Courier, and Paperfly leading logistics innovation. Warehouse demand is estimated to increase by 50 million square feet by this year, especially near ports and airports. Yet, bonded warehouse access remains limited mainly to the RMG (Ready-Made Garments) sector—leaving great potential for diversification.

随着吉大港运力不断扩大的推进、马塔巴里与帕耶拉两个深水港建设的开展、蒙拉港现代化改造的进行，以及帕德玛大桥和卡纳夫里隧道的建成投用，再加上国内连接南北和东西方向的众多高速公路的建成、与印度铁路和公路的连通，以及与缅甸类似基础设施的建成，孟加拉国将迎来公路、铁路和港口无缝连接的新时代。这一系列变化将缩短货物运输时间、减少拥堵、加快货物流通，最终使物流成本降低35%。
与此同时，电子商务（包括基于社交媒体的业务）蓬勃兴起，对城市和区域物流解决方案产生了巨大需求。目前，孟加拉国电子商务交易额已接近10亿美元，Pathao、E-Courier、Paperfly等本土企业引领着物流领域的创新发展。预计到今年，仓库需求将增加5000万平方英尺，尤其是在港口和机场附近。不过，当前保税仓库的准入仍主要局限于成衣（RMG）部门，这为物流行业的多样化发展留下了巨大潜力。

WIFFA Expo

At present the logistics costs is Bangladesh is 15–25% of product value —signaling large efficiency and profit potential for professional logistics operators. And the government has set a target to make logistics a “thrust sector” under the Perspective Plan 2041 and Logistics Policy 2023. Besides, Bangladesh government’s PPP framework and favorable FDI policies makes it an ideal investment destination for Chinese logistics and freight enterprises aiming to expand in South Asia. Cheap land, labor and energy cost in Bangladesh makes it further attractive.

目前，孟加拉国的物流成本占产品价值的15%-25%，这对于专业物流运营商而言，意味着巨大的效率提升空间和利润增长潜力。政府在《2041展望计划》和《2023物流政策》中明确提出，要将物流业打造成为“重点产业”。此外，孟加拉国政府推行的公私合营(PPP)框架以及优惠的外商直接投资(FDI)政策，使其成为中国物流和货运企业在南亚地区进行业务拓展的理想投资目的地。再加上该国廉价的土地、劳动力和能源成本，更是进一步增强了其吸引力。

WIFFA Expo

In this backdrop, by combining strategic geography, policy reform and foreign investment, and by taking advantage of demographic divident, Bangladesh aims to build new logistics infrastructure with reduced logistics costs, higher quality and competitiveness, and become a South Asian logistics hub by the early 2030s. in this backdrop, Chinese and Bangladeshi collaboration can drive mutual benefits — boosting regional trade efficiency, supply chain resilience and industrial growth across the Belt and Road region.

在此背景下，孟加拉国将借助自身战略性的地理位置，通过推进政策改革、吸引外国投资，并充分利用人口红利，大力建设新的物流基础设施。这一系列举措旨在降低物流成本，提升物流服务质量和整体竞争力，进而在2030年代初成为南亚的物流中心。与此同时，中孟两国开展合作能够实现互利共赢，有助于提升“一带一路”沿线区域的贸易效率、增强供应链韧性并推动产业增长。

WIFFA Expo

Over the last 50 years, Bangladesh-China relations has consolidated to a comprehensive strategic cooperative partnership. China is now Bangladesh’s biggest trading and development partner. Bangladesh is also a major partner of China under BRI, pledging USD 40 billion in infrastructure financing, including USD 26 billion for logistics and connectivity projects. In this favourable situation, Chinese investors can partner Bangladesh in port modernization, building inland terminals and Feeder shipping & coastal transport, special economic zones (SEZs), Invest in logistics technology, smart warehousing, and cold chain management, support training and digital platforms for freight management and data analytics and participate in developing multimodal logistics hubs connecting China–Bangladesh–India–Myanmar.

I invite you all to explore the potential.

I wish the conference a great success.

Thank you all. 

过去50年来，孟中关系不断深化，已巩固发展为全面战略合作伙伴关系。如今，中国是孟加拉国最大的贸易与发展伙伴，而孟加拉国也是中国“一带一路”倡议下的主要合作对象。孟加拉国承诺向中国提供400亿美元基础设施融资，其中260亿美元将用于物流和互联互通项目。

在这样有利的形势下，中国投资者可与孟加拉国携手合作，在多个领域开展项目，包括港口现代化建设、内陆码头建设、支线航运与沿海运输发展、经济特区建设等；还可以投资物流技术、智能仓储和冷链管理领域；支持货运管理与数据分析培训，搭建数字平台；参与建设连接中国、孟加拉国、印度和缅甸的多式联运物流枢纽。

在此，我诚邀各位去探索其中蕴藏的巨大潜力。

预祝本次会议取得圆满成功！

谢谢大家！


WIFFA Expo
