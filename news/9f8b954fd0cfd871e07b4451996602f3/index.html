巴基斯坦代表团参加2025年上海车展

CPEC 2025-04-27


巴基斯坦代表团参加2025年上海车展。[供图给《瓜达尔邮报》]

 

长安巴基斯坦公司4月24日表示，其在2025年上海车展上创造了历史，派出了由50名代表组成的最大规模代表团参加该活动。它还补充说，这也是巴基斯坦有史以来最大规模的代表团。

 

该公司表示，代表团包括长安汽车的经销商和团队，这凸显了巴基斯坦在长安全球战略中的重要性。

 

代表团还参加了2025年长安汽车海外合作伙伴会议，会上宣布巴基斯坦合资企业是长安“浩瀚海洋计划”全球愿景的战略组成部分。据说，代表团还通过试驾体验了长安的车型系列，并参观了该品牌的最新创新成果、数字智能工厂和全球研发中心。

 

与此同时，巴基斯坦萨兹加尔工程公司与中国长城汽车的合资企业哈弗巴基斯坦公司的经销商和团队成员代表团也参加了上海车展。

 

哈弗巴基斯坦公司称，长城汽车巴基斯坦公司在2025年长城汽车年度颁奖典礼上赢得了10个奖项。萨兹加尔公司营销副总经理阿布扎尔·巴特表示：“在来自80个国家的参赛者中，这是第二高的获奖数量。”

 

中国上汽集团与巴基斯坦JW经济特区集团的合资企业MG汽车巴基斯坦公司也单独表示，其在2025年上海车展上被任命为IM品牌的官方经销商。

 

第二十一届上海国际汽车工业展览会（2025年上海车展）于4月23日至5月2日在上海举行。

Officials and dealers of China-Pakistan joint-venture automakers are participating in Shanghai Auto Show 2025 in remarkable numbers.


Changan Pakistan said on April 24 that it made history at the Shanghai Auto Show 2025 by bringing the largest delegation of 50 representatives to the event. It is also the largest in Pakistan’s history, it added.


The delegation, which included Master Changan’s dealers and team, reinforced Pakistan’s significance in Changan’s global strategy, the company said.


The delegation also participated in the 2025 Changan Automobile Overseas Partners Conference, where the Pakistan venture was announced as a strategic part of Changan’s global vision under the Vast Ocean Plan. The delegation also experienced Changan’s lineup through test drives and explored the brand’s latest innovations, its digital intelligence factory and Global R&D Centre, it said.


Meanwhile, a delegation of dealers and team members of Haval Pakistan, a JV of Pakistan’s Sazgar Engineering and China’s Great Wall Motors, also participated in the Shanghai Auto Show.


GWM Pakistan won 10 awards at the GWM Annual Awards 2025, Haval Pakistan said. "It is the 2nd highest tally among contestants from 80 countries", according to Abuzar Butt, DGM Marketing at Sazgar.


MG Motors Pakistan, a JV between China’s SAIC Motors and Pakistan’s JW SEZ Group, said separately that it was appointed as an official distributor for IM Brands at the Shanghai Auto Show 2025.


The 21st Shanghai Int’l Automobile Industry Exhibition (Auto Shanghai 2025) is underway in Shanghai from April 23 till May 2.
