邀请|​2022对接进博会上海外商投资促进交流会

上海商务 2022-10-11


2022年是中国迈入建设社会主义现代化国家新征程的重要一年，也是中国国际进口博览会举办的五周年。值此第五届中国国际进口博览会召开之际，上海市商务委员会与宝山区人民政府将于2022年10月20日共同举办 "2022年第五届中国国际进口博览会投资促进与对接会"。


2022 is an important year for China to enter into a new journey of building a modern socialist country and also the fifth anniversary of the China International Import Expo(CIIE). On this occasion, Shanghai Municipal Commission of Commerce, together with Baoshan District People's Government, will co-host the "2022 Investment Promotion and Matchmaking Conference for the 5th China International Import Expo" on October 20, 2022.
