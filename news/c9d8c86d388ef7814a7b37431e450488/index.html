关于我们

About us

川上服装设备（中国）有限公司为日本在华独资企业，总部主要制造生产智能化服装机械设备。设备除广泛应用在服装领域，更扩展到航空、汽车、医疗、家具、家纺等特殊领域。

Kawakami China is a wholly-owned subsidiary of a Japanese company in China, primarily engaged in the manufacturing of intelligent automatic spreading machinery and CAM. In addition to its extensive applications in the garments industry, the machine has also expanded into specialized fields such as aviation, automotive, medical, furniture, and home textiles.

用好的产品回馈客户，做一个可信赖有价值的公司是我们的宗旨和理念。

Our aim and philosophy is "Give back to our customers with good products. Be a reliable and valuable company!"


展馆交通指南

Transportation Guide


▌01 机场路线 By air

▼深圳宝安国际机场--(城际铁路)--厚街站

  Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport -(Intercity Railway)- Houjie Station 
  直接打车约1小时
  Take a taxi directly for about 1 hour
▼广州白云国际机场--广州南站--(高铁)--虎门火车站--(地铁2号线)--展览中心站
  Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport - Guangzhou South Railway Station -(High speed train)- Humen Railway Station -(Subway Line 2) -Exhibition Center 

  直接打车约1.5小时

 Take a taxi directly for about 1.5 hours


▌02 虎门站/东莞站 By subway

到达虎门高铁站或东莞火车站之后，可直接乘坐地铁2号线，在“展览中心”站下车，从B1出口出站‌。

After arriving at Humen Railway Station or Dongguan Railway Station, you can directly take Line 2, get off at "Exhibition Center" station, and exit from Exit B1.


▌03 穗深城际铁路 By train

乘坐穗深城际铁路到达“厚街站”，步行约600米（10分钟）即可到达展馆‌。

Take the Guangzhou-Shenzhen Intercity Railway to Houjie Station, and walk about 600 meters (10 minutes) to reach the exhibition hall.


▌04 出租车/网约车 By taxi

可以直接定位到“广东现代展览中心”或“东莞厚街嘉华大酒店”‌。

You can directly locate "Guangdong Modern Exhibition Center" or "Regal Palace Hotel".


▌05 自驾 Self driving

导航搜索“广东现代国际展览中心”，按导航行驶。

Search for "Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Center" on the navigation and follow it to drive.
展馆具体地址：广东省东莞市厚街镇家具大道1号
Address: Jiaju Avenue No.1, Houjie, Dongguan, Guangdong
✅从广州/深圳方向过来的商客，可通过广深高速公路厚街立交出口或S3广深沿江高速，沿S256省道或博览大道可直达展馆‌。
Customers departing from Guangzhou or Shenzhen can take exit toward Houjie Street on the Guangzhou-Shenzhen Expressway (G4) or the S3 Guangzhou-Shenzhen Coastal Expressway, and directly reach the exhibition hall along S256 Provincial Highway or Bolan Avenue.
