展会邀请 | 中南神箭与您相约2025第138届广交会

中南神箭集团 2025-09-19

2025年10月23日-27日，万众瞩目的2025第138届广交会将在广州的中国进出口商品交易会展馆盛大开幕。中南神箭实业集团致力于成为全球建筑模板一站式解决方案综合服务商。本次展会，我们将向海内外客户展示公司主营的覆膜板、红模板、覆塑模板、PVC实心模板、商业胶合板、家具板、防滑板、木工字梁等明星产品。我们期待您的到来，共洽发展合作，共绘建筑模板行业新篇章！
From October 23 to 27, 2025, the highly anticipated 138th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair) in 2025 will grandly open at the China Import and Export Fair Complex in Guangzhou. Zhongnan Shenjian Industrial Group is committed to becoming a comprehensive service provider offering one-stop solutions for global construction formwork. At this exhibition, we will showcase our star products to domestic and international customers, including film-faced plywood, red formwork plywood, plastic-faced formwork, PVC solid formwork, commercial plywood, furniture panels, non-slip panels, and wooden I-joists. We look forward to your visit as we explore opportunities for development and collaboration and jointly compose a new chapter in the construction formwork industry!
