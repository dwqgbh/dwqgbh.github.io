How does the DGNB German Sustainable Building system become a systematic tool for the zero-carbon transformation of buildings? How can green finance and sustainable building materials unleash innovative momentum through whole-industry-chain collaboration?


This forum delves deeply into the globally leading DGNB certification system, demonstrating how it functions as a systematic tool to integrate green financial resources, activate industry-chain collaboration, and empower building material innovation.


For the first time, DGNB joins forces with the C3 Future Building Conference to host a sustainability forum at the Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Centre. Johannes Kreissig, CEO of DGNB, will attend in person to share the latest developments of DGNB in EU and Germany, and will formally announce the strategic cooperation plan between DGNB and the Shenzhen Green Building Association. The forum will also gather pioneering representatives from China Construction Eighth Engineering Division, Jiang & Associates Architecture, Broadwell Technology, A+E Design, Shenzhen Institute of Tianjin Univerisity and other sectors. Together, they will explore diverse innovative topics concerning sustainable building industry development, fostering a new collaborative and mutually beneficial industrial ecosystem.

关于我们
