中国智造赋能全球畜牧!广州广兴集团亮相2026年Agravia俄罗斯国际畜牧展!

广州广兴集团 2026-01-23














点击蓝字，关注我们

    2026年1月21-23日，备受瞩目的2026年俄罗斯Agravia国际畜牧展在莫斯科盛大启幕。广州广兴集团携全套智能化畜禽饲养设备亮相，以硬核技术实力惊艳全场，圆满完成本次国际展会之旅。

    From Jan 21th to 23th,2026,the highly anticipated AGRAVIA 2026, Russia's International Animal Husbandry Exhibition grandly opened in Moscow.Guangzhou Guangxing Group showcased its full range of intelligent livestock and poultry equipment,impressing the audience with technological strength and successfully concluding its journey at this international exhibition.


FINE PRODUCT · GLOBAL SERVICE

   作为国内畜牧设备领域的领军企业，广州广兴集团精准聚焦市场需求，带来了成套自动化养鸡设备、养猪设备、全自动鸡蛋分级包装机及环保发酵罐设备等核心产品，为规模化养殖场提供一体化智能解决方案，充分展现了“中国智造”在畜牧设备领域的技术沉淀和创新实力。

     As a leading group of Chinese livestock equipment enterprises ,Guangzhou Guangxing Group precisely focuses on market demands.It presented core products including complete sets of automated layer and pig breeding equipments,fully automatic egg collecting machines,and eco friendly fermentation tank systems.These offerings provide integrated intelligent solutions for large scale breeding farms, fully demonstrating the technological expertise and innovative capabilities of“Chinese Intelligent Manufacturing” in the field of livestock equipments.

    展会期间，广州广兴集团展位前人头攒动，前来咨询洽谈的专业观众络绎不绝。广兴团队与各国意向客户进行了深入交流，详细介绍了产品的技术特点、应用场景及成功案例，现场演示了设备的操作流程与核心优势。

    During the exhibition, the booth of Guangzhou Guangxing Group was crowded with people, and professional visitors came in an endless stream for consultation and negotiation. The Guangxing team had in-depth exchanges with potential customers from various countries, detailedly introducing the technical characteristics, application scenarios, and successful cases of the products, and demonstrated the operation process and core advantages of the equipment on site.

创造精品 · 服务全球

    期间，广州广兴集团副总裁周晓华先生受邀接受了本次展会主办方的采访，周晓华副总裁表示，Agravia展会是欧亚地区农业全产业链的重要平台。此次参展，广州广兴集团带来了成熟的智能养殖解决方案，致力于提供从设备供应到售后支持的全链条服务，助力俄罗斯畜牧产业高质量发展。

    During the event, Mr. Zhou Xiaohua, Vice President of Guangzhou Guangxing Group, was invited for an interview by the exhibition organizer. Mr. Zhou stated that the Agravia exhibition is a crucial platform for the entire agricultural industry chain across Eurasia. By participating this time, Guangzhou Guangxing Group brought mature intelligent breeding solutions,committed to providing full chain services from equipment supply to after sales support, thereby contributing to the high quality development of Russia livestock industry.


FINE PRODUCT · GLOBAL SERVICE

    此次参加俄罗斯国际畜牧展，是广州广兴集团践行“创造精品·服务全球”经营理念的体现，不仅展示了广州广兴集团的技术实力与品牌影响力，更借助展会平台精准对接欧亚市场资源，进一步拓宽了国际合作渠道。

    Participating in Russia International Livestock Exhibition embodies Guangzhou Guangxing Group operational philosophy of “FINE PRODUCT · GLOBAL SERVICE” Serving the Globe”.It not only showcased the group’s technological prowess and brand influence but also leveraged the exhibition platform to precisely connect with market resources in Eurasia, further broadening channels for international cooperation.

    广州广兴集团将以2026年Agravia俄罗斯国际畜牧展为契机，深入研判欧亚市场需求，不断提升产品的国际竞争力，以优质的产品与服务赋能全球畜牧产业升级，让“广兴智造”走向更广阔的国际舞台，在全球畜牧设备市场书写更加精彩的篇章！

    Guangzhou Guangxing Group will take the AGRAVIA 2026, Russia's International Animal Husbandry Exhibition as an opportunity to delve into the demands of the Eurasian market, continuously enhance the international competitiveness of its products, and empower the global livestock industry upgrade with high quality products and services. Let “Guangxing Intelligent Manufacturing” stride onto a broader international stage and write an even more brilliant chapter in the global livestock equipment market!
