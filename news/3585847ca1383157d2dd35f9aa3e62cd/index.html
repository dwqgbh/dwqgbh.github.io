Air Corporation有限责任公司一家科技密集型企业，生产用于各行各业的复杂空气净化系统Aircorp。该设备可有效净化供水和排水设施、农业、公共餐饮以及工业场所的空气，去除有害和恶臭物质。

Air Corporation有限责任公司拥有自己的生产基地，提供全方位的服务，从空气净化单元的设计、设备生产、安装、维护到测量，应有尽有。Air Corporation有限责任公司在运营的各个阶段提供全方位的支持。

Air Corporation有限责任公司持续发展的原则，努力在其活动中将对环境的损害降至最低，秉承创造一个更清洁，更安全的世界的原则。

Aircorp AQUA LI CN

The AIRCORP AQUA hydrodynamic air purification unit is designed to purify air from extremely high concentrations of harmful and foul-smelling substances, such as hydrogen sulfide (up to 1200 mg/m3), a mixture of natural mercaptans, ammonia, nitric and hydrochloric acids, and others.


MFA TECH is a developer of innovative fire extinguishing products for preventive suppression of fires at early stages. The company manufactures microencapsulating fire extinguishing agents and fire extinguishing materials. Fire extinguishing materials combine detection and fire suppression into a single unit and protect lithium-ion batteries (EV, ESS, UPS, telecom etc.) and electrical equipment in 24/7 mode. They are installed inside the battery and suppress fire at the initial stage, in a few seconds.

MFA TECH has a unique technology for microencapsulation of fire extinguishing agents and other substances. It’s a «Green Tech». Products and solutions of the company effectively solve fire safety problems where the use of standard fire extinguishing methods is impractical or impossible.

Exhibit：


Microcapsules with FC-5-1-12

When exposed to heat, the microcapsules break down and release a fire-extinguishing agent that puts out the fire



Fire extinguishing material

When exposed to open fire or temperatures above 120 degrees, the microcapsule develops an overpressure that ruptures its shell. This triggers a sudden release of fire-extinguishing agents, effectively extinguishing the flames.

Fomos-Materials company offer precision elements, blocks, sections and wafers manufactured, using top technologies, from industrial crystals of Lanthanum-gallium silicate (LGS), Lanthanum Gallotantalate (LGT), Calcium gallotantalum silicate (CTGS), Lithium Niobate (NL), Lithium Tantalate (TL), Calcium Molybdate (MK) and Gallium gadolinium-aluminum garnet activated by cerium ions (GGAG:Ce). Solving “bottleneck” problems, we have possibility to change raw materials matrices to tune crystal properties according to customer’s needs. Fomos has very serious scientific background and many accomplished independent research and development projects. Our facilities based in Moscow, which is a big advantage, because we do not have a lack of electricity, materials and employees.

Exhibit：


Lanthanum-gallium silicate

A piezoelectric crystal of the gallogermanate family with a symmetry class of 32 and a chemical formula of La3Ga5SiO14. It is produced using the Chochralski method. Langasite has excellent thermal, piezoelectric, and dielectric properties and is resistant to chemical exposure.


Lanthanum-gallium tantalate

A crystal with a calcium-gallogermanate structure, point symmetry group 32, chemical formula: La3Ga5.5Ta0.5O14. The method of production is the Chochralski method. Langatate is a piezoelectric crystal that lacks hysteresis, pyroelectric, and ferroelectric effects. LGT is not hygroscopic, and the temperature stability of its piezoelectric coefficients allows it to be used as a sensitive element for high-temperature sensors based on the direct piezoelectric effect.


Calcium-gallotantalate silicate

A crystal of the lanthanide group, with a point symmetry group of 32. The chemical formula is Ca3TaGa3Si2O14. It is obtained using the Chochralski method.


Sensitive elements in a plastic case

EO1 PКУС SR24 Module EO1

PKUS SR24 SKO Module

PКУС СР24 Модуль ЭО2
