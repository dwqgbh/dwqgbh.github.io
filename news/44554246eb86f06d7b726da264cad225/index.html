展会介绍

About ChemExpo Vietnam 2026

越南国际化工产业博览会（ChemExpo Vietnam 2026）将于 2026 年 6 月 10–12 日在越南胡志明市西贡展览会议中心（SECC）举行。作为东南亚化工行业的重要专业平台，展会汇聚石化、精细化工、特种化学品、橡胶、塑料、涂料、油墨及农化等全产业链相关企业，展示最新工艺技术、材料研发及智能制造解决方案。


        越南作为东南亚经济增长最快的国家之一，化工市场需求持续旺盛，制造业、建筑、农化及包装行业对基础化学品和高附加值特种化学品的进口依赖大幅增加，同时该地区正积极推进绿色化学、循环经济及生物基材料应用，产业结构升级明显。越南及东盟国家正成为中国化工企业出口与投资的重要战略市场，为中国企业提供原料供应、技术输出和本地生产合作机会。


ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 不仅为参展商提供展示产品和技术的窗口，也为行业专家、投资者及经销商提供专业交流、市场开拓及战略合作的平台。展会将成为企业捕捉市场机遇、了解东南亚化工产业趋势以及推动中越化工产业合作的重要桥梁。

ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 will be held from June 10–12, 2026, at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC) in Ho Chi Minh City. As a key platform for Southeast Asia’s chemical industry, the event will gather enterprises from petrochemicals, fine and specialty chemicals, rubber, plastics, coatings, inks, and agrochemicals to showcase cutting-edge technologies, new materials, and smart manufacturing solutions.


Vietnam’s rapidly growing economy drives strong demand for chemicals, with rising imports of both base and specialty products for manufacturing, construction, agrochemicals, and packaging. The region is also accelerating its transition toward green chemistry, circular economy, and bio-based materials, creating new cooperation and investment opportunities for Chinese enterprises.


ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 will serve as a dynamic platform for showcasing innovations, networking with industry leaders, and exploring market trends and partnerships across the ASEAN chemical sector.

展馆介绍

About SECC

西贡会展中心（SECC）位于越南胡志明市东南部的西贡区，该会展中心建成于2014年，是越南南方地区最具规模的会展中心之一。西贡会展中心占地约 50,000平方米，拥有2个室内展示厅、序厅和会议厅及超过22000平米的室外展示区域，可同时容纳多场规模不同的活动。其中最大的展示厅面积达到9,000平方米，可容纳超过20,000人的参观者。 西贡会展中心已经成功举办了多项重要的国内、国际展览和会议活动，如越南食品展、越南国际旅游博览会、亚洲汽车配件展、中国-东盟媒体合作论坛等。这些活动吸引了众多海内外企业和观众参与，成为促进越南经济和文化交流的重要平台。会展中心地址：799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City

The Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), located in District 7 of Ho Chi Minh City, is one of southern Vietnam’s largest and most modern venues. Completed in 2014, it spans 50,000 sqm, featuring two indoor halls, a conference area, and 22,000 sqm of outdoor space. The largest hall covers 9,000 sqm, accommodating over 20,000 visitors. SECC has hosted major international events such as Vietnam Foodexpo, Vietnam International Travel Mart, and the China–ASEAN Media Cooperation Forum, serving as a key hub for Vietnam’s economic and cultural exchange. Address: 799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu Ward, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City.
