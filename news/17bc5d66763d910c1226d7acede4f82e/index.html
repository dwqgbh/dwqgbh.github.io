汇川技术亮相2025德国测试展,驱动全球汽车测试新未来

汇川技术动态 2025-05-15

随着全球汽车行业向电动化快速迈进，对先进测试解决方案的需求日益增长。电机、控制器、电池包以及动力总成已成为新能源汽车的核心部件，这为制造商和测试服务商带来了新的机遇与挑战。

As the global automotive industry rapidly moves towards electrification, the demand for advanced testing solutions is increasing. The motor, controller, battery pack, and powertrain have become the core components of new energy vehicles, bringing new opportunities and challenges to manufacturers and testing service providers.


汇川技术多年来深耕新能源汽车领域，将在5月20日举行的2025德国测试展上亮相，展示其最新的汽车测试产品方案。此次展会将成为汇川展示全球领先测试产品的绝佳平台，也彰显了其致力于创新与高性能设备的承诺。

Inovance Technology has worked hard in the field of new energy vehicles for many years, and will be unveiled at the Automotive Testing Expo Stuttgart 2025 on May 20th to show its latest automotive test product scheme. This exhibition will become an excellent platform for Inovance to display the world's leading test products, and also demonstrate its commitment to innovation and high-performance equipment.


当下，汽车测试已成为保障电动车安全性、可靠性和性能的关键环节。高速测试、动力总成测试及电池包模拟对精确控制、快速数据处理和强大测试能力提出了更高要求。现阶段，行业面临的挑战包括：如何在极端测试环境下保持精度、如何降低差速和扭矩波动、如何优化设备空间和能源利用效率。

At present, automobile testing has become a key link in ensuring the safety, reliability, and performance of electric vehicles. High speed testing, powertrain testing, and battery pack tester have higher requirements for precise control, fast data processing, and powerful testing capabilities. At present, the challenges faced by the industry include: how to maintain accuracy in extreme testing environments, how to reduce differential and torque fluctuations, and how to optimize equipment space and energy utilization efficiency.


针对这些挑战，汇川技术开发了一系列多传变频器、电池模拟器及SiC DCDC模块，专为汽车测试场景而设计。

In response to these challenges, Inovance Technology has developed a series of multi-drive converters, battery simulators and SiC DCDC modules, which are specially designed for vehicle test scenarios.



此外，汇川技术还推出了液冷多传变频器，将AFE整流、逆变器及SiC DCDC模块集成于单一冷却回路中。这一系统显著降低了空气中的散热量、噪音水平，并具备更高的IP防护等级。

In addition, Inovance Technology also launched a liquid cooled multi-drive converter, integrating AFE rectifier, inverter and SiC DCDC module into a common cooling circuit. This system significantly reduces heat dissipation and noise levels in the air, and has a higher level of IP protection.


汇川技术的测试解决方案目前已广泛应用于全球多个市场，其电池模拟器凭借可靠的产品设计和口碑，大幅提升测试结果的可重复性，缩短测试时间，帮助客户显著降低测试成本。

Inovance Technology has always been committed to providing high-quality and reliable test equipment for global automobile manufacturers and test service providers to improve operational efficiency and product performance.


诚邀行业专家、合作伙伴于5月20日莅临2025德国测试展Hall 10，1748展位，深入了解其全球领先的汽车测试解决方案，共同探讨未来合作机遇。让我们携手，共同推动汽车测试行业向更高品质、更高效率迈进。

We sincerely invite industry experts and partners to visit Hall 10, Booth 1748 of the Automotive Testing Expo Stuttgart 2025 on May 20th to gain a deeper understanding of its globally leading automotive testing solutions and explore future cooperation opportunities together. Let's work together to promote the automotive testing industry towards higher quality and efficiency.
