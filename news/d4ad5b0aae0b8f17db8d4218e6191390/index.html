CAM代表參加「2025粵港澳大灣區民航青年交流項目」

澳門國際機場訂閱號 2025-07-10

澳門國際機場專營股份有限公司（CAM）代表參加由中國民用航空中南地區管理局和香港民航處主辦、國泰航空承辦的「2025年粵港澳大灣區民航青年交流項目」，活動於2025年6月29日在深圳啟動，並於7月5日在成都圓滿結束。本次交流項目旨在促進粵港澳大灣區民航青年交流與合作，拓寬視野，增進對港澳和內地民航業發展的了解，推動區域民航業協同發展。

來自粵港澳大灣區各民航企事業單位四十餘名青年代表參加了此次活動，包括參訪騰訊、大疆、深圳寶安國際機場、珠海翔翼航空技術有限公司、中國航空工業集團、中國民用航空第二研究所及中國民用航空飛行學院等知名企業和機構，深入了解民航業最新科技應用及發展趨勢。在項目啟動儀式上，CAM機場營運部機場營運員李敏怡女士作為澳門青年代表發言，分享澳門民航業及澳門國際機場發展現況。

此次交流項目弘揚愛國愛港愛澳崇高精神，為青年提供互動平台，助力其把握粵港澳大灣區民航業發展機遇。通過交流，青年代表不僅加深對三地航空業獨特性的理解，更促進粵港澳民航資源互補與長效合作機制的構建，為區域民航業高質量發展凝聚青年力量。

新聞來源：澳門國際機場


CAM Representative Participated In The “2025 Greater Bay Area Civil Aviation Youth Exchange Program”

Representative from Macau International Airport Company Limited (CAM) participated in the “2025 Greater Bay Area Civil Aviation Youth Exchange Program”, an event co-hosted by the General Administration of Civil Aviation of China (CAAC) Central South Regional Administration and the Civil Aviation Department of Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (CAD), and organized by Cathay Pacific. The program was launched in Shenzhen on 29th June 2025 and concluded successfully in Chengdu on 5th July 2025. The exchange program aimed to promote interaction and cooperation among civil aviation youth in the Greater Bay Area (GBA), broaden their horizons, enhance their understanding of the civil aviation industry’s development in Hong Kong, Macau, and the Mainland of China, and promote the coordinated development of regional civil aviation.

Over 40 young representatives from various civil aviation enterprises and institutions in the GBA participated in the event. They visited renowned companies and organizations such as Tencent, DJI, Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport, Zhuhai Xiang Yi Aviation Technology Co., Ltd., the Aviation Industry Corporation of China, the Second Research & Institute of CAAC, and Civil Aviation Flight University of China. These visits provided participants with in-depth insights into the latest technological applications and development trends in the civil aviation industry. At the opening ceremony of the program, Ms. Mandy Lei, Airport Operations Officer of the Airport Operations Department of CAM, spoke as a youth representative of Macau, shared updates on the development of civil aviation industry in Macau and Macau International Airport.

This exchange program promotes the noble spirit of patriotism and love for Hong Kong and Macau, providing a platform for interaction among young professionals and allowing them to seize the opportunities in the development of the civil aviation industry. Through the program, the youth representatives not only deepened their understanding of the unique characteristics of the aviation industry among Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macau, but also facilitated the complementarity of civil aviation resources and the establishment of a long-term cooperation mechanism in three regions. This effort aims to gather the strength of the youth for contributing the high-quality development of the regional civil aviation industry.
