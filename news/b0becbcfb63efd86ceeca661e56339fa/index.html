聚势·共生·向新 | 迪培思2026:行业自办展的时代答卷

AUDLEY奥德利数码 2025-12-30



引言：一场属于喷印人的“觉醒宣言”


当行业站在转型与突破的十字路口，2026年3月10日-12日，第33届迪培思广州国际广告标识及喷墨打印展将以“行业自办展”的全新姿态，在广交会展馆D区掀起一场关于技术革新、生态共建的浪潮。这不仅是展会的迭代，更是中国喷印行业从“单兵作战”迈向“协同共生”的里程碑。作为深耕行业多年的参与者，河南印都深知：唯有开放共享、技术共融，方能应对全球市场的激流。


Introduction: An "Awakening Manifesto" for Printing Professionals

As the industry stands at the crossroads of transformation and breakthrough, the 33rd DIBIZ Guangzhou International Advertising, Signage and Inkjet Printing Exhibition will make its debut as an "Industry Self-Initiated Exhibition" at Area D of the Canton Fair Complex from March 10 to 12, 2026, sparking a wave of technological innovation and ecological co-construction. This is not only an iterative upgrade of the exhibition, but also a milestone for China's printing industry to move from "individual combat" to "synergistic coexistence". As a long-term participant deeply rooted in the industry, Henan Yindu firmly believes that only through open sharing and technological integration can we calmly navigate the turbulent waters of the global market.




一、行业自办展：打破壁垒，重塑价值



首次由业内同行联合发起的“行业自办展”，承载着打破垄断、回归本质的初心。

规模升级：双展联动（广告标识+纺织印花），10万㎡展区、超1000家展商、12万+专业观众，覆盖从设备研发到终端应用的全产业链。

生态重构：拒绝“流量收割”，聚焦“技术赋能”——取消传统广告位竞价，增设“创新技术展区”“解决方案工作坊”，让设备性能、环保指标、智能化水平成为核心竞争力。

全球链接：联动5大洲17城海外推广，依托广交会“超级枢纽”，为中小企业打通“出海”通道，让中国智造与全球需求无缝对接。


I. Industry Self-Initiated Exhibition: Breaking Barriers, Reshaping Value

For the first time initiated by ndustry peers, the "Industry Self-Initiated Exhibition" bears the original mission of breaking industry monopolies and returning to the essence of the industry.

Scale Upgrade

Dual exhibition linkage (Advertising & Signage + Textile Printing), creating a 100,000-square-meter super exhibition area, gathering more than 1,000 high-quality exhibitors and 120,000+ professional visitors, covering the entire industrial chain ecology from equipment R&D, consumables production to terminal application.

Ecological Reconstruction

Rejecting "traffic exploitation" and focusing on "technology empowerment" – eliminating the traditional advertising space bidding model, adding an "Innovation Technology Zone" and "Solution Workshops", making equipment performance, environmental indicators and intelligence level the core evaluation criteria for enterprises' core competitiveness.

Global Linkage

Jointly carrying out overseas promotion in 17 core cities across five continents, relying on the "super hub" advantage of the Canton Fair, opening up efficient "going global" channels for small and medium-sized enterprises, and realizing the seamless connection between "Made in China" and global market demand.



二、河南印都：以技术突围，做行业“破局者”



面对行业“内卷”，河南印都选择以技术深耕回应挑战：
环保革新：推出低VOCs排放数码喷墨设备，响应国家“双碳”战略，助力客户通过绿色认证；
智能升级：优化打印精度和稳定性，降低设备故障率，降低运维成本；

我们的承诺：
不做“价格屠夫”，只做“价值创造者”；
不搞“闭门造车”，与同行共筑技术护城河。

II. Henan Yindu: Breaking Through with Technology, Being an Industry "Game-Changer"

Faced with the predicament of industry "involution", Henan Yindu chooses to respond to market challenges with in-depth technological research and development, and bravely acts as a pioneer in breaking the situation of the industry:

Environmental Innovation

Launching low-VOCs emission digital inkjet equipment, actively responding to the national "Dual Carbon" strategy, helping customers successfully obtain green certification and seize the opportunity in the environmental protection track.

Intelligent Upgrade

Continuously optimizing printing precision and equipment stability, effectively reducing equipment failure rate, significantly lowering operation and maintenance costs for customers, and improving production efficiency.

Our Commitment

We will not be a "price cutter" engaging in blind price reduction, but only a "value leader" creating value; we will not engage in closed and rigid "isolated innovation", and are willing to join hands with peers to build a technological moat and create a sound industry ecology.



三、致行业：选择比努力更重要


在信息爆炸的时代，参展不仅是“展示”，更是战略选择：

对品牌：直面全球采购商，用实力替代“广告轰炸”；

对从业者：与头部企业对话，捕捉趋势，规避盲目投资；

对行业：拒绝低价竞争，推动标准制定，让良币驱逐劣币。


河南印都的观察：

2025年海外观众增长26.5%，印证“中国智造”全球认可度；

72家喷墨设备厂商集体“抱团”，证明行业共识已至临界点。


III. To the Industry: Choice Matters More Than Effort

In the era of information explosion and fierce competition, participating in exhibitions is not only a window for enterprises to show their strength, but also a strategic choice related to long-term development:

For brands: Face global buyers directly, replace ineffective "advertising bombardment" with strong strength, and effectively enhance the international influence of the brand;

For practitioners: Conduct in-depth dialogues with leading industry enterprises, accurately grasp technological trends and market opportunities, and avoid blind investment risks;

For the industry: Reject low-price vicious competition, jointly promote the formulation of industry standards, and realize a sound development pattern of "good money driving out bad money".


Henan Yindu's Observation

Overseas visitors increased by 26.5% year-on-year in 2025, confirming that the global recognition of "Made in China" continues to rise; 72 inkjet equipment manufacturers collectively "develop in solidarity", marking that the industry consensus has reached a critical point, and coordinated development has become an inevitable trend.




四、邀您同行：共赴一场约定


2026年3月10日，广交会展馆D区，我们期待与您共同书写：

技术派：见证白墨烫画抖粉机、UV系列打印机等新款产品如何助力个性化定制服务；

实干家：在“供需对接会”上，找到降本增效的“最优解”；

破局者：加入“绿色喷印倡议”，为行业可持续发展按下加速键。


IV. Inviting You to Join: Embarking on an Appointme

On March 10, 2026, at Area D of the Canton Fair Complex, we look forward to writing a new chapter of the industry with you:

Technologists: Witness how cutting-edge equipment such as DTF printer and powder shaker and UV series printers empower personalized customization services and unlock new industry scenarios;

Practitioners: Accurately match resources at the "Supply-Demand Matching Conference", find the "optimal solution" for cost reduction and efficiency improvement, and achieve business quality improvement and upgrading;

Game-Changers: Join the "Green Printing Initiative", promote the green transformation of the industry with a sense of responsibility, and press the accelerator for sustainable development.




结语


行业寒冬，唯有抱团取暖；市场寒冬，更需向光而行。迪培思2026，不是一场展览，而是一次行业集体觉醒。河南印都愿与诸君携手，以技术为矛，以共识为盾，共同定义喷印行业的下一个十年。

3月10日，广州见！


Conclusion

In the industry winter, only by uniting can we resist the cold; in the market changes, we need to move towards the light to see the dawn. DIBIZ 2026 is by no means an ordinary industry exhibition, but a collective awakening of the industry to unite consensus and seek common development. Henan Yindu is willing to join hands with colleagues in the industry, take technology as a spear and consensus as a shield, to jointly define the next golden decade of the printing industry. 

See you in Guangzhou on March 10!

 公司简介 Company Profile

河南印都数码科技有限公司，是一家从事数码打印设备研发、生产及销售一体的国家高新技术认定企业。致力于为全球客户提供更高性价比的“中国智造”喷印设备与技术方案。


河南印都成立河南省数码喷墨打印工程技术研究中心，申请通过数码打印设备相关专利100余项。先后获得国家高新技术企业、国家级专精特新“小巨人”企业、河南省制造业单项冠军、“绿色工厂”等称号。产品已通过国家3C认证。代理商及服务网点遍布全国34个省、市地区。产品远销至欧美、中东、东南亚等几十个国家及地区。


Henan Yindu Digital Co., Ltd is a national high-tech certified enterprise , engaged in the R&D production and sales of digital printing equipment and committed to providing global customers with more cost-effective "Wisdom in China" printing equipment and technical solutions.


Henan Yindu Digital Co., Ltd established Henan inkjet printer engineering technology research center and applied for more than 100 patents related to digital printing equipment. We has obtained the titles of National High-tech Enterprise, National “Little Giant”Enterprise, Henan Province Manufacturing Industry Single Champion, "Green Factory", etc. The products have passed the national 3C certification. Our domestic agents are located in 34 provinces and cities. The products are exported to dozens of countries and regions such as Europe, the United States, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia.
