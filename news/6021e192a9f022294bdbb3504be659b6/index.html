即將截止|誠邀港澳特別行政區參與「2025年粵港澳大灣區教育數位化轉型」案例第二輪徵集活動 港澳專版

广二师粤港澳教育数字化研究基地 2025-12-14







🌱  為促進粵港澳大灣區教育數位化領域的學術交流與實務合作，回應《粵港澳大灣區發展規劃綱要》中推動教育協同發展的倡議，本中心現誠邀港澳地區相關教育同仁參與「2025年粵港澳大灣區中小學教育數位化轉型案例徵集」活動，共同推動區域教育數位化轉型的創新與實踐經驗的廣泛分享。

🌱To promote academic exchange and practical collaboration in digital education within the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, and in response to the educational cooperation initiatives outlined in the Outline Development Plan for the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, our center cordially invites education professionals from Hong Kong and Macao to participate in the 2025 Greater Bay Area Case Collection on Digital Transformation in Primary and Secondary Education. This initiative aims to foster innovation and widespread sharing of practical experiences in regional digital transformation of education.



Objectives
01.活動宗旨

本次活動以「數智賦能·協同創新」為主題，旨在發掘港澳與灣區內地中小學在教育數位化領域的優秀實踐案例，通過學術交流與成果展示，促進大灣區基礎教育數位化的高質量發展。入選案例將獲邀參與本年度粵港澳大灣區教育數位化轉型研討會進行分享交流，並獲得中心合作專案優先推薦資格。


Under the theme “Intelligent Empowerment · Collaborative Innovation,” this campaign seeks to identify outstanding practical cases in digital education from schools in Hong Kong, Macao, and the mainland parts of the Greater Bay Area. Through academic exchanges and result presentations, we aim to advance the high-quality development of digitalized basic education in the region. Selected cases will be invited to be presented at the annual Greater Bay Area Digital Education Seminar this year and will receive priority consideration for cooperative projects with the Center.





粵港澳大灣區教育數位化發展研究中心

Guangdong–Hong Kong–MacaoGreater Bay Area Educational Digital Development Research Center



About the Organizers
02.中心簡介

粵港澳大灣區教育數位化發展研究中心（GBA-EDRC）設於廣東第二師範學院，是區域內聚焦教育數位化轉型的省級重點研究平臺。中心彙聚了超50名跨學科研究人員，並協同粵港澳中小學共建“教育數位化實踐共同體”，圍繞“理論-監測-政策”三維框架開展研究，已產出多項重要成果，為政府決策提供了有力支持，正逐步成為連接灣區、面向國際的研究樞紐。


Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area Educational Digital Development Research Center (GBA-EDRC)is established at Guangdong University of Education. As a provincial-level key research platform in the region focusing on digital transformation of education, the Center brings together over 50 interdisciplinary researchers and collaborates with primary and secondary schools across the Greater Bay Area to form an “Educational Digital Practice Community”.  Operating within a “Theory-Monitoring-Policy”tripartite framework, GBA-EDRC has produced multiple significant outcomes, providing substantial support for government decision-making, and is evolving into an internationally oriented research hub connecting the Greater Bay Area.



Thematic Scope

03.案例主題範圍


案例可圍繞以下方向提交：

  • 區域數位化推進案例：教育政策規劃、數字基礎設施建設、數據治理與應用等；

  • 學校整體實踐案例：智慧校園建設、教學與管理創新、教師專業發展等；

  • 課堂教學創新案例：數位化教學設計、人工智慧教育應用、跨學科融合及評估改革等；

  • 教育研究類案例：數位化轉型調研報告、模式構建及政策建議等學術成果。


Cases may address, but are not limited to, the following areas:

  • Regional Digitalization Initiatives: Education policy planning, digital infrastructure development, data governance and application;

  • School-Wide Practices: Smart campus construction, innovation in teaching and management, teacher professional development;

  • Classroom Teaching Innovations: Digital instructional design, AI-assisted teaching, interdisciplinary integration, and assessment reform;

  • Education Research Outputs: Research reports on digital transformation, model development, policy recommendations, and other academic achievements.




 Submission Subjects

04.提交細則


1.案例須為原創，未曾在公開發行刊物發表，且不涉及版權爭議；

2.中英文稿件皆可，建議字數3000–6000，歡迎附圖片、圖表等輔助說明；

3.請提交Word文檔，檔案命名方式：「單位名稱-作者姓名-案例主題」；

4.提交請電郵至：gbaedu_digital@outlook.com，郵件主旨請注明：「港澳數位化案例-單位名稱」；

5.截止日期：2025年12月15日。

6.入選結果將於2025年12月下旬

通過電子郵件通知相關單位及作者，本次案例徵集不收取任何費用。


1.Cases must be original, unpublished in any publicly distributed publication, and free of copyright disputes;

2.Submissions in both Chinese and English are accepted. Recommended length:3000–6000 words. Illustrations, charts, and other supporting materials are encouraged;

3.Please submit in Word format. Name the file as: “Institution Name-Author Name-Case Title”;

4.Email submissions to:gbaedu_digital@outlook.comwith the subject line: “Hong Kong/Macau Digital Case-Institution Name”;

5.Deadline: December 15, 2025;

6.Selection results will be notified via email to the relevant institutions and authors by late December 2025. There are no fees for participation.
