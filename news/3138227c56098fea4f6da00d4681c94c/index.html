“移动工厂”开进宁波高交会:宁诺创新技术破解秸秆处理难题

诺丁汉大学灯塔研究院 2025-09-22

下滑查看中文版


"Mobile factory" showcased at Ningbo High-Tech Fair: UNNC innovation offers solution to crop waste disposal

The Ningbo High-Tech Achievements Transaction Fair, a key event of the 2025 Zhejiang Ningbo Talent and Technology Week, opened on 20 September. Under the theme “Gathering Technology in Ningbo, Creating the Future Together”, this year’s fair aims to deepen the integration of high-quality scientific innovation resources with Ningbo’s local economic development. As a key research part of the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC), the China Beacons Institute was invited to showcase a remarkable innovation—the MPS200 Mobile Straw Pyrolysis System—a technology set to revolutionise traditional agricultural waste disposal.


This seemingly simple mobile unit is, in fact, a “portable processing factory”.  It can be driven directly into fields to convert straw on-site into high-value bio-oil and biochar, fundamentally transforming conventional straw disposal methods. Dr Dongbing Li, Associate Professor in Green Chemicals and Energy at the China Beacons Institute and lead developer of the technology, explained that the core strength of the system lies in its ability to achieve “on-site conversion, turning waste into treasure”.


Specifically, the equipment features a modular design, allowing rapid deployment—much like “building blocks”. It can complete processing in one field and relocate to continue operations elsewhere the following day. An intelligent control system further enables “multiple functions in one machine”: it can automatically adapt to process straw from different crops such as rice, wheat, and corn, and self-adjust to the optimal treatment parameters.


Beyond its technical breakthroughs, the system also delivers significant economic and environmental benefits. It is estimated that a single unit can generate bio-oil worth approximately RMB 3 million annually, with a projected payback period of three years. The on-site processing model helps farmers save over 60% in straw transportation costs. Environmentally, the system’s emissions are well below national standards, and processing one tonne of straw equates to reducing carbon dioxide emissions by 1.2 tonnes.


“This technology genuinely helps farmers turn straw from a ‘burden’ into a ‘profit’,” said Dr Li, “It represents a practical application of technology benefiting agriculture, enhancing the resource utilisation of agricultural waste while achieving both economic and environmental gains.”


As one of China’s first pilot cities for innovation, Ningbo continues to promote the digital and green transformation of its manufacturing sector. Dr Li emphasised, “Our mobile processing system not only addresses the challenge of straw disposal but also aligns with Ningbo’s strategic focus on high-end equipment manufacturing and new energy industries. We are building not just a machine, but a comprehensive solution for agricultural waste resource utilisation.”


At the fair, the technology attracted strong interest from numerous environmental protection companies, energy firms, and investment institutions. According to Dr Li, the system is currently in the technical verification stage, and the team is actively developing a new-generation product to meet emerging market demands.


Dr Li added, “Nurtured by Ningbo’s vibrant innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem, we are confident in achieving the industrial application of this technology. It will inject new momentum into Ningbo’s green development and rural revitalisation, offering a replicable, scalable ‘UNNC solution’ for China and the global market.”

“移动工厂”开进宁波高交会：

宁诺创新技术破解秸秆处理难题


9月20日，2025中国浙江·宁波人才科技周重要活动——宁波市高新技术成果交易洽谈会（以下简称“高交会”）开幕。本届高交会以“甬汇科技，‘甬’创未来”为主题，旨在推动优质科创资源与宁波地方经济发展深度融合。作为宁波诺丁汉大学（简称“宁诺”）科研的重要组成部分，灯塔研究院受邀参展，并带来了一项引人注目的创新成果——MPS200移动式秸秆热解系统模型，这项技术有望改变传统农业废弃物处理方式。

高交会现场


据悉，这台看似简约的移动设备模型，实则是一个“可移动的加工工厂”。它能够直接驶入田间地头，将秸秆就地转化为高附加值的生物油和生物炭，从根本上革新了传统秸秆处置模式。该技术负责人、灯塔研究院绿色化工副教授李冬兵博士介绍到，该系统的核心优势在于“就地转化、变废为宝”。


具体而言，设备采用模块化设计，如“搭积木”般可快速拆装和灵活转运，今日收割完毕，明日即可转场继续生产。智能控制系统更赋予其“一机多用”的能力：可自动适应水稻、小麦、玉米等不同作物秸秆，并调节至最优处理工艺。


MPS200移动式秸秆热解系统模型


除了技术突破，该设备还展现出显著的经济与环境效益。据估算，单台设备年产生物油价值可达300万元，预计三年内可回本并盈利。就地处理模式帮助农民节省超60%的秸秆运输成本。在环保方面，这套系统的排放远低于国家标准，每处理1吨秸秆相当于减少1.2吨二氧化碳排放。


李冬兵博士（左一）在现场参与直播讲解


“这项技术真正帮助农民将秸秆从‘负担’转为‘收益’，是实现科技惠农的有力实践，”李冬兵博士表示，“它不仅提升了农业废弃物的资源化利用水平，更实现了经济与环保的双赢。”


作为国家首批创新型试点城市，宁波持续推动制造业数字化与绿色化转型。李冬兵博士强调：“我们的移动处理系统不仅破解了秸秆处置难题，更与宁波高端装备制造和新能源产业发展方向高度契合。我们正在构建的不只是一套设备，而是一整套农业废弃物资源化解决方案。”


李冬兵博士（右一）为现场观众讲解


高交会现场，多家环保企业、能源公司及投资机构对该技术表现出浓厚兴趣。据悉，该技术目前已进入技术验证阶段，团队正积极推进新一代产品研发，以响应市场新需求。


李冬兵博士补充说到：“在宁波优质的创新创业土壤中，我们有信心实现该技术的产业化落地，为宁波绿色发展及乡村振兴注入新动力，并向全国乃至全球输出可复制、可推广的‘宁诺方案’。”
