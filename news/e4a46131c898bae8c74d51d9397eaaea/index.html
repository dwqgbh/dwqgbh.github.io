润邦股份亮相2025粤港澳大湾区智慧港口暨智慧港航展览会,共绘港航未来新图景

润邦股份 2025-05-21


近日，粤港澳大湾区（广州）智慧港口暨智慧港航展览会在广州保利世贸博览馆隆重举行。润邦股份（股票代码：002483）以135㎡重磅展位亮相这场全球港航领域盛会，携旗下GENMA(GENMA-KALMAR)、KOCH及RAINBOWCO三大品牌创新成果，全方位展示智慧港口全场景解决方案，与全球320余家领军企业共绘行业高质量发展蓝图。

智慧赛道领跑者：全矩阵产品诠释港航未来

本次展会以"走智慧赛道，引行业发展"为主题，润邦股份紧扣行业脉搏，围绕“智能化、绿色化、高效化”集中呈现三大核心领域突破：



GENMA智能物料搬运：GENMA致力于面向全球市场，为各类工矿企业、港口码头、矿山堆场和船舶海工等领域的客户提供如移动式港口起重机（MHC）、氧化铝气力式卸船机、埋刮板卸船机等专业且高品质的物料起重搬运解决方案。

GENMA-KALMAR依托对KALMAR百年技术体系的深度传承与自主创新，展出轮胎式龙门起重机（RTG）、岸边集装箱起重机（STS）等明星产品，其模块化架构与自动化解决方案已服务全球40余国，助力智能码头建设。



KOCH散料专家：整合德国近80年散料技术积淀与FLSmidth散料尖端工艺（2023年收购），推出桥式抓斗卸船机、堆取料机、皮带输送机等核心设备，以"全产业链覆盖+数字化管理"重构散料绿色智能装卸生态。



RAINBOWCO海洋工程：展示海上风电安装平台、PSV、全回转起重船等高端海工装备，多项技术填补国内空白，彰显海上新能源装备领域的"中国智造"实力。



智造矩阵支撑，夯实产业根基

润邦股份依托润邦重工、润邦重机、润邦智装、润邦工业、润邦海洋五大智造集群，构建"研发-制造-服务"一体化生态。各基地通过数字化管理与精益制造，为全球客户提供定制化高端装备。此前，广东省政府领导曾莅临展位，对其技术实力与行业贡献给予高度评价。



作为连续多年蝉联展会"明星展商"的行业标杆，润邦股份将持续深化"智能化、绿色化、高效化"战略，以本次粤港澳大湾区（广州）智慧港口暨智慧港航展览会为窗口，与全球伙伴共探港航产业升级新路径，为打造"海上新广东"注入强劲动能。


From May 14 to 16, 2025, the 5th Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (Guangzhou) Smart Port and Shipping Exhibition was grandly held at the Poly World Trade Center in Guangzhou. Jiangsu Rainbow Heavy Industries Co., Ltd. (short for RAINBOWCO; Stock Code: 002483), made a high-profile appearance at this global port and shipping industry event with an impressive 135 m² booth. Featuring innovations from its three core brands - GENMA (GENMA-KALMAR), KOCH, and RAINBOWCO - the Company presented comprehensive smart port solutions for all operational scenarios. Alongside over 320 global industry leaders, RAINBOWCO helped shape a vision for high-quality industry development.

Leading the Smart Revolution: Full Product Portfolio Defining the Future of Ports and Shipping

Aligned with the exhibition's theme of “Embracing Smart Pathways, Driving Industry Development”, RAINBOWCO highlighted cutting-edge breakthroughs in three key areas: intelligence, sustainability, and operational excellence.

GENMA-Intelligent Material Handling: GENMA is dedicated to serving the global market and providing an array of professional and high-quality material handling solutions for various industrial and mining enterprises, ports and wharfs, mining stockyards, and offshore engineering enterprises, including mobile harbor cranes (MHC), alumina pneumatic ship un-loaders and scraper ship un-loaders. The GENMA-KALMAR series inherits KALMAR’s century-old technological expertise and integrates independent innovation. It features flagship products such as rubber-tyred gantry cranes (RTG) and ship-to-shore cranes (STS). With modular architectures and automated solutions deployed in over 40 countries, GENMA continues to advance the construction of smart terminals worldwide.

KOCH - Expertise in Bulk Material Handling: With nearly 80 years of German bulk material technology expertise and advanced handling processes from FLSmidth (acquired in 2023), KOCH presented key equipment including grab ship un-loaders, stacker reclaimers and belt conveyors. KOCH integrates full industry chain coverage and digital management to reshape a green and intelligent ecosystem for bulk cargo handling.

RAINBOWCO Offshore Engineering Equipment：Showcasing high-end offshore engineering equipment, including wind power installation platforms, PSVs and full-rotation crane vessels, RAINBOWCO has mastered multiple technologies that fill domestic gaps, demonstrating the strength of "Chinese intelligent manufacturing" in offshore new energy equipment.

Smart Manufacturing Network: Strengthening Industrial Foundations

Leveraging five major manufacturing clusters - Jiangsu Rainbow Heavy Industries Co., Ltd. (RHI), Nantong Rainbow Heavy Machineries Co., Ltd. (RHM), Nantong Rainbow Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd. (RIE), Jiangsu Rainbow Industrial Equipment Co., Ltd. (RIC), and Nantong Rainbow Offshore & Engineering Equipment Co., Ltd. (ROC), RAINBOWCO builds an integrated “R&D-Manufacturing-Service” ecosystem. Through digital management and lean manufacturing, these facilities deliver customized, high-end equipment to clients around the world. The Company’s technological expertise and contributions to the industry have previously earned high praise from leaders of the Guangdong Provincial Government during exhibition visits.

As an industry benchmark and multi-year “star exhibitor” at the event, RAINBOWCO will further deepen its strategies of "intelligentization, greening, and efficiency". Using the 5th Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (Guangzhou) Smart Port and Smart Port Exhibition as a pivotal platform, RAINBOWCO will collaborate with global partners to explore new ways to upgrade the port and shipping industry, injecting powerful momentum into building "A New Maritime Guangdong" and beyond.
