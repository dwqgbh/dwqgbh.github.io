2024秋季广交会开幕在即

/ 2024秋季广交会/



FASHION



/



      2024年10月31-11月4日，森马服饰旗下成人服饰森马与儿童品牌巴拉巴拉将首次参加广交会，暨第136届中国进出口商品交易会秋交会。


       2024年，公司旗下两个品牌总共进驻全球13个国家及地区，含中国香港在内的国际市场已落地百家门店。


      此次，两大品牌联合启动了全球招商加盟计划，为全球合作伙伴提供了一个难得的商业机遇，旨在寻找志同道合的合作伙伴，共同开拓国际市场。







      公司将为国际合作伙伴，提供全方位的支持，包括品牌授权、市场分析、店铺设计、运营指导等，并提供多种形式的合作方式，包括国家代理、单店加盟、线上分销和批发等，确保国际合作伙伴能够快速融入品牌体系，从而在目标市场取得成功。


      在3.1G07&08展位， 森马品牌将以其户外系列服饰为亮点，展示其对功能性与时尚性的完美结合，满足消费者对户外活动的舒适性与耐用性需求；而巴拉巴拉品牌则以充满创意的IP及图案T恤系列为主打，通过生动有趣的卡通形象和鲜明的色彩搭配，展现品牌对儿童服饰趣味性和个性化的追求。




我们期待在展位上与您会面，

共同开启一段美好的商业旅程。

让我们携手共创，

让世界见证中国服饰品牌的力量！


/


Semir Apparel are excited to announce that it will be participating for the first time in the 136th China Import and Export Fair Autumn Session from October 31 to November 4, 2024. 


The group’s two core brands, Semir adult comfort fashion and Balabala children's wear, will both be represented at the fair. 


The Semir Group has established a retail footprint in over 13 countries and regions in 2024 with 100 stores and by participating in the Canton fair aims to showcase their business model and for potential global investors and entrepreneurs to join them on their international expansion journey.


Semir and balabala has built its current success on providing comprehensive support for international partners, including brand authorization, market analysis, store design and operational guidance. The business model offers various forms of cooperation including, national agency, single-store franchising, online distribution and wholesale ensuring that international partners can quickly integrate into the brand system to deliver success in the market.


Located at booths 3.1G07&08, the Semir brand will highlight its outdoor series of clothing, showcasing the perfect combination of functionality and fashion, meeting consumers' demands for comfort and durability in outdoor activities. Meanwhile, the Balabala brand will focus on its creative IP and patterned T-shirt series, displaying the brand's pursuit of fun and personalized children's clothing through lively and interesting cartoon images and bright color combinations.


We look forward to meeting you at the Fair for initial discussions on starting a fruitful business journey together. Let's join hands to create and let the world witness the strength of Chinese apparel brands!
