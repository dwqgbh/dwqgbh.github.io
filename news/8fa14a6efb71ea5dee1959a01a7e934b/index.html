2025九州汽车生态博览会

Shenzhen International Smart obility, Auto Modification
and Automotive Aftermarket Services Ecosystems Expo 2025


2月28日-3月3日，备受行业瞩目的九州汽车生态博览会，将在深圳国际会展中心（宝安）盛大开幕。作为亚洲唯一囊括整车发布、前装配套、后装服务、汽车文化的汽车生态展贸平台，九州展已成为中国汽车行业人每年必来的行业盛会。


From February 28 to March 3, 2025, the Shenzhen International Smart Mobility, Auto Modification, and Automotive Aftermarket Services Ecosystems Expo, widely known as the Jiuzhou Auto Ecology Expo, will take place at the Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center in Bao'an. As the only automotive ecology exhibition and trade platform in Asia that includes vehicle launches, original equipment (OE) parts, aftermarket services, and automotive culture, the Jiuzhou Expo has established itself as an essential annual event for professionals in the Chinese automotive industry.

展会开幕已进入倒计时阶段，富怡在此诚挚邀请您参加2025深圳国际智慧出行、汽车改装及汽车服务业生态博览会，届时我们将携富怡全自动双头垫板式冲孔机及富怡全自动冲绣缝一体机华丽亮相，共同见证富怡在九州汽车生态展的完美呈现!


As the countdown to the exhibition's opening begins, Richpeace cordially invites you to attend the Shenzhen International Smart Mobility, Auto Modification, and Automotive Aftermarket Services Ecosystems Expo 2025. During this event, we will proudly showcase our Richpeace Automatic Dual-Head Plate-Mount Perforation Machine and Richpeace Automatic Perforation+Embroidery+Sewing Machine. Join us to witness the impressive presentation of Richpeace at the Jiuzhou Auto Ecology Expo!
