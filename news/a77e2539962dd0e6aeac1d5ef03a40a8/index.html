智利汽配展2025已成为南美地区领先的汽车售后市场展会

IFEMA 2025-03-18


智利汽配展2025已成为南美地区领先的汽车售后市场展会

智利领先的汽车行业展会——智利汽配展（Motortec Chile）将于2025年6月26日至28日在圣地亚哥展览中心Risco中心第四次举办。预计来自15个国家的共150家公司将参展，在12,000平方米的展览空间（相较于2024年的8,000平方米）上展示其产品，并预计将吸引约7,000名专业观众。这些数字证实，该展会现已成为南美地区汽车售后市场领域的领先展会。该展会将成为该行业创新和发展的重要平台。

空间更加宽敞的智利汽配展2025将推出专注于技术创新和车辆性能的新展区。此外，还将设立全新的全路况和户外车辆展区，这一创新举措预计将吸引该市场细分领域的爱好者。

与往届一样，展会将为车间和该行业的专业人士举办全面的活动计划，包括汽车机械和技术最新趋势的现场演示和会议。

该展会的关键举措之一是买家特邀计划，该计划吸引了来自整个伊比利亚美洲的企业家和决策者。会议日程安排将促进参展商与潜在客户之间的会面，开辟新的商业机会。

智利汽配展还将于2025年4月23日至26日在马德里汽配展现场Z3E08展位（3号和5号展厅之间）。作为南欧地区该行业的主要商务平台，智利展会将借此机会加强与国际汽车售后市场的联系。


Motortec Chile 2025 has become established as the Southern Cone’s leading aftermarket trade fair 

The trade fair will feature products from 150 companies and will be visited by around 7,000 trade professionals 

Madrid, 17 February 2025. The leading automobile industry trade fair in Chile, Motortec Chile, will be held for the fourth time from 26 to 28 June 2025 at the Centro de Exposiciones Espacio Risco in Santiago. A total of 150 companies from 15 countries are expected to participate, displaying their products on 12,000 m2 of exhibition space (as opposed to 8,000 m2 at the 2024 edition) and will welcome approximately 7,000 trade visitors. These numbers confirm that this is now the leading fair for the automobile aftermarket sector in the Southern Cone. The trade fair will be a key platform for innovation and development of this industry.

A more spacious Motortec Chile 2025 will launch new areas focused on technological innovation and vehicle performance. What’s more, there will be an all-new space for all-road and outdoor vehicles, an innovation that is expected to attract fans of this market segment.

As in earlier editions, a comprehensive programme of activities will be held for workshops and professionals in the sector, including live demonstrations and conferences on the latest trends in automotive mechanics and technology.

One of the trade fair's key initiatives is its Guest Buyer Programme, which attracts entrepreneurs and decision-makers from all over Ibero America. A meeting schedule will foster get-togethers between exhibitors and potential customers, opening up new business 

Motortec Chile will also be present at the upcoming edition of MOTORTEC 2025 from 23 to 26 April 2025, on Stand Z3E08 (between Halls 3 and 5). Its presence as the main business platform for this sector in Southern Europe will enable this Chilean fair to strengthen its connections with the international automotive aftermarket industry.

More information at www.motortecchile.com
