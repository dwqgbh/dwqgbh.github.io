技术驱动,创新引领:大牧人亮相巴基斯坦国际家禽博览会

大牧人 2025-10-24

Technology-Driven, Innovation-Led: Big Herdsman Shines at Pakistan International Poultry Expo

导言

2025年10月23日，为期三天的巴基斯坦国际家禽博览会在卡拉奇展览中心隆重开幕。作为中国畜禽养殖装备领域的领军企业，大牧人盛装亮相这一南亚地区最具影响力的行业盛会，向来自巴基斯坦及周边国家的专业观众展示了中国制造的创新实力。

On October 23, 2025, the three-day Pakistan International Poultry Expo grandly opened at the Karachi Expo Center. As a leading enterprise in China’s livestock and poultry farming equipment industry, Big Herdsman made a stunning appearance at this highly influential South Asian industry event, showcasing the innovation strength of Made in China to professional visitors from Pakistan and surrounding countries.

Part 01

市场深耕：把握巴基斯坦畜牧业机遇

Deep Market Cultivation – Seizing Livestock Opportunities in Pakistan

巴基斯坦是一个发展中国家，农业是该国的支柱型产业，这一经济结构为畜牧装备制造业提供了广阔的市场空间与良好的市场潜力。本次参展，大牧人带来了专门为南亚地区气候条件研发的智能化养殖设备，包括通风系统、温控设备和自动化喂料装置，这些产品精准契合了当地市场需求。
Pakistan is a developing country where agriculture serves as a pillar industry. This economic structure provides broad market potential and favorable opportunities for livestock equipment manufacturers.At this expo, Big Herdman presented smart farming equipment specially developed for the climatic conditions of South Asia, including ventilation systems, temperature control equipment, and automated feeding devices — all precisely aligned with local market demands.

Part 02

产品亮点：创新科技精准适配本地需求

Product Highlights – Innovative Technologies Tailored to Local Needs


针对巴基斯坦高温干燥的气候特点，大牧人重点展出了智能化环境控制系统。该系统能够根据舍内外温度、湿度变化，自动调节通风量和降温强度，有效缓解高温天气对家禽生长的影响。展出的自动化喂料设备采用了节能设计，在保证喂料精准度的同时，大幅降低了能耗。

Considering Pakistan’s hot and dry climate, Big Herdsman highlighted its intelligent environmental control system. This system automatically adjusts ventilation volume and cooling intensity based on indoor and outdoor temperature and humidity fluctuations, effectively mitigating the negative impact of high temperatures on poultry growth.In addition, the showcased automated feeding system features an energy-saving design, which ensures feeding precision while significantly reducing energy consumption.

Part 03

展会现场：专业交流赢得客户认可

On-Site Interaction – Professional Exchange Wins Customer Recognition


展会期间，大牧人展区迎来了密集的人流，前来咨询和洽谈的客商络绎不绝。工作人员以专业的知识和耐心的态度，为每一位客商详细介绍产品特点，并结合实际情况提供有针对性的解决方案。

通过现场演示和深入交流，大牧人不仅展示了产品的卓越性能，更收集了众多宝贵的市场一线反馈，为后续产品优化和技术创新提供了重要参考。

Throughout the exhibition, the Big Herdsman booth attracted heavy foot traffic and a steady stream of visitors seeking consultations and cooperation. Our professional team explained the technical features of each product in detail and provided targeted solutions based on clients' actual farming needs.
Through live demonstrations and in-depth exchanges, Big Herdsman not only showcased the superior performance of its products, but also gathered valuable front-line market feedback, which will serve as an important reference for future product optimization and technological upgrades.


结语

此次参展不仅是一次产品展示，更是大牧人深耕巴基斯坦市场的重要举措。我们与当地合作伙伴紧密配合，为客户提供从设备安装到售后维护的全方位服务保障。未来，大牧人将一如既往地坚持以科技创新为驱动，以客户需求为导向，为全球畜牧业发展提供更多中国智慧和中国方案。
This exhibition was not only a platform for product display but also a key step in Big Herdsman's long-term commitment to cultivating the Pakistani market. By working closely with local partners, we are dedicated to offering comprehensive service solutions — from equipment installation to after-sales maintenance. Looking ahead, Big Herdsman will continue to uphold technology-driven innovation and customer-oriented principles, providing smart Chinese solutions for global livestock development.
