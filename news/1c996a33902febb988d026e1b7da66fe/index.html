轻院公寓（广州）介绍



轻院公寓（广州）位于广州市海珠区主干道上，具体地址为新港西路152号广东轻工职业技术大学大院内，毗邻中大国际轻纺城、中山大学、南海海洋研究所等，交通非常便利。轻院公寓环境幽静，景色优美，书香四溢，让人在繁华国际大都市仍然可享受一份难得的宁静和闲适。

Qingyuan Apartment (Guangzhou) is located on a main thoroughfare in Haizhu District, Guangzhou. Its specific address is within the compound of Guangdong Industry Polytechnic University, 152 West Xingang Road. Adjacent to Guangzhou International Textile City, Sun Yat-sen University, and the South China Sea Institute of Oceanology, it has highly convenient transportation around the clock.

The apartment features a tranquil environment, pleasant scenery, and a strong academic atmosphere, offering a sense of peace and leisure even in the bustling international metropolis.

Address of Accommodation:
Qingyuan Apartment, (Sun Yat-sen University Branch, Guangzhou International Textile City)
1st Floor, Building 33, Guangdong Industry Polytechnic University, 152 West Xingang Road, Haizhu District, Guangzhou, Guangdong Province.

轻院公寓（广州）餐饮配套


轻院公寓共享广轻大校园内的三个大型餐厅，餐厅供应早中晚餐，有粤式炒菜、面食、湘菜、烧腊等，菜色自选，荤素可自由搭配。可提供定制自助餐、围餐、特色餐等服务，丰俭由人。

Dining  Services of Qingyuan Apartment (Guangzhou):


Qingyuan Apartment shares three large campus dining halls of Guangdong Industry Polytechnic University.

The dining halls provide breakfast, lunch and dinner, featuring a wide variety of options including Cantonese stir-fried, noodles, spicy Hunan cuisine, and Cantonese roasted meat.

Guests may select dishes freely and combine meat and vegetables according to their personal preference.

Customized catering services are available, including buffet and special themed meals, with flexible pricing to meet different needs.
