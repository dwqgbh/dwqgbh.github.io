Rittal威图亮相2024中国国际工业博览会: 创新技术助力中国智能制造

德国海外商会联盟 2024-10-11


Rittal威图亮相2024中国国际工业博览会：

创新技术助力中国智能制造

Rittal Showcases Innovation in Smart Manufacturing at CIIF 2024



📅 日期: 2024年9月24日

📅 Date: September 24, 2024

📍 地点: 中国国际工业博览会 (CIIF) 威图展台5.1H-B05

📍 Location: Rittal Booth 5.1H-B05, China International Industry Fair (CIIF)

2024中国国际工业博览会（CIIF）上，Rittal威图以其创新技术和工业解决方案再次成为焦点。展会期间，德国海外商会联盟·大中华区高级总监Marcus Wassmuth亲临现场，和威图亚太区首席运营官兼中国代理总经理Michael Galler 进行了专访。访谈中，Michael Galler详细分享了威图在中国的成功经验，以及其产品和战略如何在中国市场获得广泛认可。


At the 2024 China International Industry Fair (CIIF), Rittal showcased its innovations and industrial solutions, solidifying its leadership in the fields of Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing. During the event, Marcus Wassmuth, Senior Director of AHK Greater China, came to the site and sat down for an insightful interview with Michael Galler, COO of Rittal Asia and Acting Managing Director of Rittal China. Galler shared Rittal's key successes in China, emphasizing how the company’s products and strategies have been widely recognized in this crucial market.

左 Left: Michael Galler; 右 Right: Marcus Wassmuth

威图在中国的市场定位与成就

Rittal’s Market Positioning and Achievements in China

当Wassmuth问到威图在中国取得的里程碑成就时，Galler提到威图多年来一直深耕中国市场，致力于推动本地化研发与生产。其产品广泛应用于中国的汽车制造、机械制造、新能源、新基建和数据中心等领域。通过参与北京大兴国际机场、特斯拉中国工厂和国家大数据产业基地 等重大项目，威图在中国市场的行业影响力持续扩大。


When Wassmuth asked about Rittal's key achievements in China, Galler mentioned that over years, Rittal has continued to focus on localized development in China, contributing significantly to industries such as automotive, machinery manufacturing, new energy, new infrastructure, and data centers. Through involvement in major projects like the Beijing Daxing International Airport, Tesla’s China Gigafactory, and the National Big Data Industry Base, Rittal has significantly expanded its influence in the Chinese market.

威图的双轮战略：聚焦中国与创新驱动

Rittal’s Dual Strategy: China Focus and  Innovation-drivenGrowth


威图通过优化供应链、拓展新行业、推出更贴合中国需求的产品，以及重塑成本结构，确保其产品能够准确匹配市场需求。同时，威图实施了精益管理，提升了生产效率。Galler强调，威图通过创新技术，不仅满足了客户对可持续发展的需求，还助力中国制造业向绿色低碳转型。


Rittal has optimized its supply chain, entered new industries, developed customer-oriented products for China, and restructured cost efficiencies to ensure that its products precisely meet market demands. Additionally, the company has successfully implemented lean management, which has increased production efficiency.

Galler emphasized that through technological innovation, Rittal not only meets its customers' sustainability goals but also contributes to China’s broader transition toward low-carbon industrial growth.




丨

VX25机柜系统：智能制造的驱动核心

VX25 Enclosure System: Driving Smart Manufacturing

丨

访谈的另一焦点是威图的旗舰产品VX25机柜系统。当问到VX25技术创新方面的新进展以及如何通过技术创新来满足中国市场的特定需求时，Michael表示：“我们始终与中国市场的需求高度契合。通过模块化设计和智能化集成，VX25不仅能够优化生产流程，还将帮助企业提升运营效率。”这款产品将成为推动中国智能制造的重要力量，为中国企业在全球市场竞争中提供持续的技术支持。


A key highlight of the interview was Rittal’s VX25 Enclosure System. When asked about the latest developments in VX25 technological innovation and how Rittal meets the specific needs of the Chinese market through technological innovation, Galler emphasized that Rittal is highly aligned with the needs of the Chinese market and that the modular design and intelligent integration of the VX25 Enclosure System help customers optimize production processes and enhance operational efficiency. He expressed his firm belief that this product will become a key driver in China’s smart manufacturing field, with Rittal providing ongoing technical support for Chinese enterprises in the global competition.

威图的前瞻与承诺

Rittal’s Vision and Commitment

Michael Galler总结道，威图始终坚持通过技术创新和本地化战略深耕中国市场，不断推动智能制造和工业数字化转型。如其所说，威图在中国取得的这些成就，展示了其对中国市场的承诺和持续投资，也进一步巩固了其在全球工业解决方案领域的地位。


Summing up the interview, Galler affirmed that Rittal remains committed to the Chinese market through cultivating its deep localization strategy and innovation-driven growth, continuously driving industrial digital transformation and smart manufacturing. In that regard, Rittal’s milestone achievements in China reflect its dedication and continuous investment in this crucial market, reinforcing its leading position in industrial solutions.
