“瑞士制造”@2024中国数控机床展览会

瑞士驻沪总领馆 2024-04-10

中国数控机床展览会（CCMT）于2000年在上海创办，每逢双数年举办，是中国国际机床展览会（CIMT）的姊妹展，受疫情影响，2020年、2022年CCMT停办两届，CCMT2024是相隔6年后的再度举办。这也是瑞士企业在时隔6年后，以最新的制造技术再次在上海展示他们的产品和服务。

China CNC Machine Tool Fair (CCMT) was founded in Shanghai in 2000 and is held every two years as a sister show of China International Machine Tool Show (CIMT). Due to the pandemics, CCMT was suspended in 2020 and 2022. This year it is being held after a gap of 6 years. Swiss companies are showcasing again state-of-the-art manufacturing in Shanghai.

对于任何来自中国各地的制造专家来说，CCMT是他们结识来自瑞士和世界其他国家的技术合作伙伴的“必看”展会。今年，瑞士机床行业在CCMT上的表现尤为突出，令人印象深刻。位于W5 展厅的 "瑞士馆 " 面积达 2300 平米，汇集了30 多家主展商共计40 多家瑞士制造商和品牌参展。

CCMT is a "must see" for any manufacturing experts to meet their technology partners from Switzerland and the rest of the world. This year, the Swiss machine tool industry has a particularly impressive presence at CCMT. With 2,300㎡, more than 30 main exhibitors in the «Swiss Pavilion» in Hall W5 and over 40 Swiss manufacturers and brands present during CCMT 2024.

4月9日瑞士驻上海总领事邵凯文亲临展会现场巡馆并和瑞士参展商交流沟通，当晚他出席了“瑞士之夜”招待晚宴并致辞。总领事说， 等待6年CCMT重返上海，是值得的。瑞士机床产业代表了瑞士制造的顶尖水平，也是瑞士一个重要的出口产业，是瑞士卓越、创新、精密和可靠的形象大使。

On 9 April, Sacha Bachman, the Consul General of Switzerland in Shanghai visited the Swiss Pavilion at CCMT. In the eventing, he attended the "Swiss Evening" reception and delivered a speech. The Consul General said that it was well worth the wait for CCMT to return to Shanghai after six years. The Swiss Machine Tool Industry represents the best of Swiss capabilities  and a vital export sector for Switzerland. lt is a successful Ambassador for Swiss excellence, innovation, precision and reliability.

此次瑞士官方展团由瑞士技术产业协会（Swissmem) 组织，该协会是代表瑞士技术产业中小型企业和大型企业的领导性协会。协会有效地为会员企业提供广泛的关系网络，并在数字化方面为他们提供支持，同时倡导创业产业中心，并大力投资于面向未来的青年培训和高级培训，以此强化协会企业在本土和国际的竞争能力。

This year the Swiss Pavilion @ CCMT is organised by Swissmem, the leading association for both SMEs and major corporations in the Swiss technology industry. The association provides member companies with an extensive network and support them in digitalisation. It focuses on enhancing the competitiveness of its members domestically and internationally by the effective promotion of innovation and the heavy investment in future-oriented training for young people.
