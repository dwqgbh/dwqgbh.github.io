走进无锡:2025医疗器械创新展与企业参访

洹盈咨询RPConsulting 2025-10-04

     2025年8月21日至23日，我们来到江苏无锡参观2025医疗器械创新展，并受邀参加了“江苏无锡商机圆桌会谈与现场参观”活动。来自国内外的企业代表齐聚一堂，共同探讨医疗器械与健康产业领域的合作新机遇。本次活动由无锡市贸促会主办，在无锡滨湖区和无锡外商会的支持下举行，旨在推动产业交流与跨国合作。

      在参访过程中，我们来到无锡国际生命科学与科技园，深入了解园区在生物医药、医疗器械孵化和科研服务方面的布局，切身感受到这里完善的产业生态与政策优势。随后，代表团走进健适医疗（Genesis MedTech (Wuxi) Co., Ltd.），其集研发、制造、临床培训于一体的现代化设施让人印象深刻。最后一站，我们拜访了费森尤斯卡比华瑞制药（Fresenius Kabi Huarui Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd.），对其在临床营养和药品研发生产上的创新实践有了更直观的认识。通过与园区和企业代表的交流，我们真切感受到当地产业集群的活力，以及开放包容的合作氛围。

      这趟参访之旅，不仅让我们获得了许多宝贵的一手观察机会，也为未来的合作带来了新的启发和可能。展望未来，我们希望以展会平台为纽带，进一步深化交流合作，推动跨境合作与技术落地，让创新成果走进更广阔的医疗健康市场。

From August 21 to 23, 2025, we were invited to participate in the “Wuxi Business Roundtable & Site Visits,” joining Chinese and international enterprises in Wuxi to explore new opportunities in the fields of medical devices and healthcare. Hosted by CCPIT Wuxi with the support of Wuxi Binhu District and WuxiCham, the event aimed to strengthen industrial exchange and international cooperation.

During the visits, we explored the Wuxi International Life Science and Technology Park, learning about its ecosystem for biopharma incubation, medtech R&D, and policy support. The delegation then visited Genesis MedTech (Wuxi) Co., Ltd., whose integrated facilities for research, manufacturing, and clinical training left a strong impression. The final stop was Fresenius Kabi Huarui Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd., where we gained insights into its expertise in clinical nutrition, R&D, and production innovation. Through exchanges with park and company representatives, we experienced first-hand Wuxi’s thriving industrial clusters and its welcoming, collaborative environment.

          

This experience provided us not only with valuable first-hand insights but also with inspiration for future collaboration. In the future, we hope to leverage the exhibition as a platform to strengthen partnerships, foster cross-border exchange and technology implementation, and bring innovative achievements into broader healthcare markets.
