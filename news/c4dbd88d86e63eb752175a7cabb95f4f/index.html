关于延期举办第二十三届(2022)中国国际工业博览会的通知

自动化网 2022-11-18

各有关单位：

为切实保障广大参展商、观众及相关人员的身体健康，确保展会效果，经研究决定，原定于2022年11月30日至12月4日在国家会展中心（上海）举办的第二十三届中国国际工业博览会延期至2023年举办。

由衷感谢各参展商、观众朋友及相关单位的理解和支持！

中国国际工业博览会组委会办公室

2022年11月15日

The 23rd China International Industry Fair Extension Notice

Dear exhibitors, visitors and all relevant parties:

In order to secure the benefits and health of all exhibitors and visitors, and ensure the exhibition effect, the organizing committee of China International Industry Fair has taken prudent consideration after discussion and hereby makes the following announcement.

The 23rd China International Industry Fair, originally scheduled to be held on November 30-December 4, 2022 at National Exhibition and Convention Center（Shanghai）, will be postponed to the year of 2023.

We deeply appreciate the understanding and support from exhibitors, visitors and all relevant parties!

The Organizing Committee Office of CIIF

November 15, 2022
