关于第二十三届中国国际工业博览会举办日期的通知

为深入推进第二十三届中国国际工业博览会（以下简称本届工博会）各项工作，提振制造业信心，促进实体经济发展。经研究决定，本届工博会于2023年9月19日-23日在国家会展中心（上海）举办。

本届工博会将以“碳循新工业、数聚新经济”为主题，为四海宾朋构筑新桥梁，为全球展商打造新盛会。衷心感谢大家对本届工博会的大力支持和倾情参与，期待今年9月与您相聚上海!


 中国国际工业博览会组委会办公室

                         2023年3月6日

Notice on the date of China International Industry Fair 2023

March 6 2023


To further promote the preparations of the 23rd China International Industrial Fair (CIIF 2023), boost the confidence of the manufacturing industry and promote the development of the real economy. The organizing committee of CIIF will make the following announcement. The 23rd China International Industry Fair will be held on September 19–23 2023 at National Exhibition and Convention Center (Shanghai).


With the theme of “Digital Economy & Industrial Decarbonization”, CIIF 2023 will build a new bridge for friends around the world and create a new grand event for global exhibitors. We sincerely thank you for the strong support and enthusiastic participation in CIIF, and look forward to meeting you in Shanghai in September this year. 


The Organizing Committee Office of CIIF



来源：中国工博会
