展会直击丨小航带您走进2024北京国际汽车展览会

中航光电 2024-04-26


4月25日-27日，第十八届北京国际汽车展览会在中国国际展览中心（朝阳馆）盛大举行，作为汽车行业备受瞩目的行业盛会，汇集了全球汽车行业的企业和专家在此相遇、分享、探讨，共同推动行业发展。

From April 25th to 27th, AUTO CHINA 2024 has been grandly held at China International Exhibition Center. For its high professional and authoritative reputation, it has been regarded as an essential platform for enterprises and experts to meet, share, and discuss.

中航光电作为互连行业创新发展的推动者，致力于为客户提供更可靠、更专业的全链路互连方案，在此次展会上带来新能源汽车整车互连解决方案，包括车载动力系统、充换电系统以及智能网联系统。

Promoting innovation and development in the interconnect industry, JONHON is committed to providing customers with more reliable and professional interconnect solutions. At this exhibition, JONHON has brought a full-link interconnect solutions for Electrical Vehicle, such as vehicle power system, charging & battery swap system, intelligent network system products.



展会现场，中航光电技术专家代表公司参加“新能源汽车连接技术大会”，并发表《车载智能网联高速互连问题探讨》为主题的演讲。
Product Manager of JONHON, has represented the company in the "EV Connection Technology Conference" and delivered a speech on the theme of "Exploration of High speed Interconnect in Vehicle Intelligent Network system".
