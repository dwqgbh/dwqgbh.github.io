南博会“孟加拉国日”开幕式暨孟中投资贸易促进活动圆满举行

拓富仕云南管理有限公司 2026-06-28

南博会“孟加拉国日”开幕式暨

孟中投资贸易促进活动圆满举行

Bangladesh Day and Bangladesh-China Investment and Trade Promotion Event Successfully Held

2026年6月12日，在由云南省人民政府和商务部共同主办的第十届中国—南亚博览会上，针对该次南博会主题国为孟加拉国而精心组织的核心配套经贸交流活动——“孟加拉国日”开幕式暨孟中投资贸易促进活动在昆明索菲特酒店圆满举办。该次活动由孟加拉国驻昆明总领事馆、云南省商务厅主办，云南国际经贸文化合作中心（以下简称“国合中心”）承办，拓富仕国际集团（云南）、昆明中央法务区的南亚东南亚投资权益维护中心、南亚东南亚国际安保联盟秘书处为会议支持单位。

On June 12, 2026, the Bangladesh Day and Bangladesh-China Investment and Trade Promotion Event—a core supporting economic and trade exchange activity specially organized for Bangladesh as the theme country of the 10th China-South Asia Expo (CSAE) co-hosted by the People's Government of Yunnan Province and the Ministry of Commerce—was successfully held at Sofitel Hotel in Kunming. The event was hosted by the Consulate General of Bangladesh in Kunming and the Department of Commerce of Yunnan Province, organized by the Yunnan Center for International Economic, Trade, and Cultural Cooperation (hereinafter referred to as "the center"), and supported by TOFFS international group limited (Yunnan), the Rights and Interests Protection Center for Investments in and from South and Southeast Asia and the Secretariat of the International Security Alliance for South and Southeast Asia (ISASSA) of the Kunming Central Legal-services District.

当日上午，开幕式由孟加拉国驻华大使纳兹穆尔主持，孟加拉国商务部长坎达克率主题国代表团，与云南省副省长刘勇及云南省外办、商务厅、贸促会的领导共同出席并分别致辞。

That morning, the opening ceremony was presided over by Bangladeshi Ambassador to China Nazmul. Bangladeshi Minister of Commerce Khondaker led the theme country delegation, joining Vice Governor Liu Yong of Yunnan Province and leaders from the Yunnan Provincial Foreign Affairs Office, Department of Commerce, and Council for the Promotion of International Trade, who all delivered speeches respectively.

孟加拉国投资发展局执行主席（国务部长级）乔杜里、孟加拉国出口促进局副主席兼首席执行官哈桑·阿里夫、孟加拉国驻华大使纳兹穆尔、孟加拉国驻华使馆商务参赞齐亚·拉赫曼，孟加拉国驻昆明总领事穆罕默德·卡勒德分别在上午下午的会议上作主旨发言，全面介绍本国投资政策与机遇、产业与产品的优势和竞争力，期盼依托云南区位优势深化中孟经贸合作。

Executive Chairman (State Minister rank) of the Bangladesh Investment Development Authority (BIDA) Chowdhury, Vice Chairman and CEO of the Bangladesh Export Promotion Bureau Hasan Arif, Bangladeshi Ambassador to China Nazmul, Commercial Counsellor of the Bangladeshi Embassy in China Zia Rahman, and Consul General of Bangladesh in Kunming Muhammad Khalid delivered keynote speeches at the morning and afternoon sessions respectively, comprehensively introducing their country's investment policies and opportunities, as well as the advantages and competitiveness of their industries and products, expressing the hope to deepen China - Bangladesh economic and trade cooperation by leveraging Yunnan's geographical advantages.

本次活动共有110家中孟两国企业、近250名专业客商参会，与会企业及客商收获满满。

A total of 110 enterprises from China and Bangladesh and nearly 250 professional merchants attended the event, with participants gaining substantial benefits.


此次活动是国合中心服务对外开放、助力云南的辐射中心建设的务实举措。下一步，国合中心将持续优化跨国经贸服务，为共建“一带一路”、构建周边命运共同体贡献力量。

This event was a pragmatic measure by the center to serve opening-up and assist in Yunnan's construction as a gateway. Going forward, the center will continue to optimize cross-border economic and trade services and contribute to the joint construction of the “Belt and Road” and the building of a community with a shared future with neighboring countries.
