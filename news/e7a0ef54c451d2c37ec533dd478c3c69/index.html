智能娱乐出海新篇章,音创亮相2026年春季广交会

华运物流集团 2026-04-17

2026年春季第一期广交会盛大启幕，作为中国外贸的“晴雨表”与“风向标”，这场盛会汇聚全球商机，深圳市华运国际物流（Chinatrans）携北美、拉美两大核心团队联合参展，全力开拓全链条物流门到门新赛道。

The first phase of the 2026 Spring Canton Fair kicked off grandly . Known as the "barometer" and "vane" of China's foreign trade, this grand event gathers global business opportunities. Shenzhen Chinatrans International Logistics (Chinatrans) participated in the exhibition jointly with its North America and Latin America core teams, striving to develop a new track of end-to-end door-to-door logistics.

本次参展，华运聚焦港到门、DDP到门、海运，中欧铁路、跨境电商等核心业务，整合美洲双区域资源，打通国际运输、清关、仓储、末端配送全环节，与海内外买卖家面对面洽谈，量身定制高效物流解决方案，精准破解跨境物流痛点。

In this exhibition, Chinatrans focused on core businesses including port-to-door, DDP door-to-door, shipping, China-Europe Railway Express and cross-border e-commerce. It integrated resources from the two American regions, opened up the entire process of international transportation, customs clearance, warehousing and last-mile delivery, held face-to-face negotiations with global buyers and sellers, customized efficient logistics solutions, and accurately solved cross-border logistics pain points.

展会现场亮点十足，幸运大转盘互动游戏成功点燃氛围，吸引大批客商驻足参与、咨询洽谈。依托成熟的海外网络与稳定舱位优势，华运高效对接全球需求，累计接待海内外客商超百人次，达成多项合作意向，进一步夯实中国与美洲地区的物流通道优势，以专业服务赋能全球贸易。

The exhibition site was full of highlights. The Lucky Wheel interactive game successfully ignited the atmosphere, attracting a large number of merchants to stop, participate and consult. With mature overseas networks and stable shipping space advantages, Chinatrans efficiently connected global needs, received more than 100 domestic and foreign merchants, reached multiple cooperation intentions, further consolidated the logistics corridor advantages between China and the Americas, and empowered global trade with professional services.
