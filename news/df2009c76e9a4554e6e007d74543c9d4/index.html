厦门金鹭与您相约DMP2020大湾区工博会

厦门金鹭切削工具 2020-11-20


2020年11月24- 27日，DMP大湾区工博会（第23届DMP国际模具、金属加工、塑胶及包装展）将于深圳国际会展中心（新馆）举行。展示规模达32万㎡，聚焦于针对不同行业的数字化、智能化、个性化创新技术及解决方案。厦门金鹭携GPT6100系列钢材高效车削刀片、GHDS双螺旋内冷浅孔钻、SH360系列高硬钢加工立铣刀、D918S 高性能钢用麻花钻等产品亮相此次盛会，敬请莅临E4馆-2A21展位。


From November 24 to 27, 2020, Great Bay Area Industrial Expo(the 23rd DMP international mould, metal processing, plastic and packaging exhibition) will be held in Shenzhen International Convention and Exhibition Center (New Hall). The exhibition covers an area of 320000 square meters, focusing on digital, intelligent and personalized innovative technologies and solutions for different industries. GESAC will bring GPT6100 series steel efficient turning insert, GHDS double screw internal cooling shallow hole drill, SH360 series high hard steel processing end mill, D918s high performance steel twist drill and other products. Welcome to E4-2A21 exhibition stand.



主推产品速览
Overview of Main Products



GPT6100系列钢材高效车削刀片

GPT6100 series high efficiency steel turning insert



采用独创的GE.FORCE技术，通过融合高硬表面层、高韧涂层技术和先进的后处理技术，搭配专用硬质合金基体，使刀片具有更高的耐磨性和韧性。


Using unique GE.FORCE Technology, combined the integration of high hard surface layer, high toughness coating technology and advanced post-treatment technology with special cemented carbide matrix, the insert has higher wear resistance and toughness.


GHDS双螺旋内冷浅孔钻

GHDS double-helix internal cooling shallow hole drill


采用双螺旋内冷结构设计，具有较高的刀体强度和良好的排屑性能。其产品阵容包括2~5倍径，刀具直径范围Ø14mm~Ø40mm，产品系列齐全。


The double -helix internal cooling structure design has high strength and good chip removal performance. Its product lineup includes 2 ~ 5 times diameter, tool diameter range of 14mm ~ 40mm, complete product series.


SH360系列高硬钢加工立铣刀

SH360 series high hardness steel endmill



SH360系列高硬钢加工立铣刀，采用超细晶硬质合金基体，搭配我司全新一代高硬钢K涂层，并通过高精密磨削工艺管控，保证该系列刀具的批量稳定生产，实现高精度、高质量、长寿命的刀具性能要求。


SH360 series high hardness steel endmill adopts ultra-fine grain cemented carbide matrix, matching with our new generation of high hardness steel K coating, and through high-precision grinding process control, ensure the batch and stable production of this series of tools, and realize the performance requirements of high-precision, high-quality and long-life tools.


D918S高性能钢用麻花钻

D918S series high performance steel twist drill



D918S基于D918和D938进行系列升级，适用于钢、铸铁的钻削加工，重点解决断续切削和冷却条件差等恶劣工况加工难题。广泛运用于汽车行业的凸轮轴、连杆、门铰链及轮毂单元等典型零部件加工。


D918s is a series upgraded based on D918 and D938. It is suitable for drilling processing of steel and cast iron. It focuses on solving machining problems such as intermittent cutting and poor cooling conditions. It is widely used in the processing of typical parts such as camshaft, connecting rod, door hinge and hub units in automobile industry.
