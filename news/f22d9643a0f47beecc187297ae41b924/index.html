Light + Building 2026 Frankfurt | LTECH Looks Forward to Meeting You in Germany!

全球照明与建筑技术领域盛会：2026 法兰克福国际照明展（Light + Building）将于3 月 8 日 - 13 日在德国法兰克福国际展览中心盛大启幕。这场横跨照明、智能建筑、智能家居的行业盛宴，是全球从业者洞察行业趋势、建立合作的关键平台。


深耕智能照明控制领域 23 载的雷特科技，此次将携完整照明智控产品体系与一站式照明智控解决方案亮相，以 “技术创新 + 生态互联” 双轮驱动，在法兰克福展览中心8.0 D50展位，向全球客户展示品牌实力。


The global lighting and building technology event: Light + Building 2026, the Frankfurt International Lighting Fair, will grandly open from March 8 to 13 at the Frankfurt International Exhibition Center in Germany. This cross-industry feast spanning lighting, intelligent buildings, and smart home is a key platform for global professionals to gain insights into industry trends and establish collaborations. 

LTECH with 23 years of specialization in the field of intelligent lighting control, will make a strong appearance this time with its complete intelligent lighting control product system and one-stop intelligent lighting control solutions, driving forward with the dual engines of "technological innovation + ecosystem interconnection", and showcasing the brand strength to global customers at booth 8.0 D50 in the Frankfurt Exhibition Center.
