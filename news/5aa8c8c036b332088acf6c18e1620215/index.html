越南主流媒体如何评价 ChemExpo Vietnam 2026？

What Does the Press Say About ChemExpo Vietnam 2026?



作为越南及东盟地区化工产业的重要专业展会，ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 正持续获得来自主流媒体与行业权威平台的高度关注。多家越南全国性报纸与产业媒体围绕展会规模、发展定位及其在越南化工与工业体系中的作用，进行了系统、深入的报道与分析。


包括越南《工贸报》（Cong Thuong）、《投资报》（Dau Tu Newspaper）、VietnamNet、《劳动者报》（Nguoi Lao Dong）、《越南企业》（Doanh Nghiep Viet Nam）、《管理者》（Nha Quan Ly）等媒体一致指出，ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 在以下方面发挥着关键平台作用：


🔹 推动化工产业在政策法规与 ESG 可持续框架下的规范化与高质量发展

🔹 协助企业在区域一体化与国际竞争加深背景下提升综合竞争力

🔹 构建企业—投资机构—技术专家—监管部门多方对话与合作的专业平台



🔗 媒体支持如何强化展会与越南及东盟化工产业的连接？


密集而多维度的媒体报道，使 ChemExpo Vietnam 不仅是一个 “展示窗口”，更成为政策导向、产业趋势与市场需求交汇的公共议题平台。
这类曝光有效放大了展会在以下层面的产业链接价值：

    • 将越南化工产业升级议题置于国家发展战略与东盟区域合作语境中

    • 帮助国际企业快速理解越南在绿色化工、基础化工、新材料与应用端市场的真实需求

    • 提升展会作为进入越南及东盟化工市场首选平台的行业认知度与公信力



📌 给参展商与国际买家的营销与参会建议


对参展商而言：

✔ 在展前与展中积极引用主流媒体报道内容，用于官网、社媒与客户邀约，强化品牌背书

✔ 围绕 “合规、安全、低碳、应用场景” 设计展示主题，更贴合越南市场关注重点

✔ 主动参与论坛、对话活动，提升在产业与政策层面的可见度


对国际买家与投资方而言：

✔ 将 ChemExpo Vietnam 视为集中获取产业信息、政策信号与优质供应商的高效平台

✔ 通过 B2B 配对与现场交流，深入了解本地企业在成本、交付与技术适配方面的实际能力

✔ 结合媒体报道线索，重点关注具备长期合作潜力的越南及区域企业

ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 continues to attract strong attention from leading national newspapers and industry-focused media in Vietnam. A series of in-depth articles have examined the exhibition’s scale, development direction, and its strategic role within Vietnam’s chemical and broader industrial ecosystem.


Reputable publications such as Cong Thuong, Dau Tu Newspaper, VietnamNet, Nguoi Lao Dong, Doanh Nghiep Viet Nam, and Nha Quan Ly, among others, have highlighted ChemExpo Vietnam 2026 as a key platform for:


🔹 Advancing the chemical industry in line with regulatory frameworks and ESG standards

🔹 Supporting enterprises in strengthening competitiveness amid deeper regional and global integration

🔹 Facilitating connections among businesses, investors, technical experts, and regu

latory authorities



🔗 How Media Support Strengthens Industry Linkages


Extensive media coverage positions ChemExpo Vietnam as more than an exhibition — it functions as a convergence point for policy direction, industry trends, and market demand.


This visibility reinforces the exhibition’s role in:


  • Embedding Vietnam’s chemical industry development within national strategies and ASEAN cooperation frameworks

  • Enabling international companies to better understand Vietnam’s demand for green chemicals, basic chemicals, new materials, and downstream applications

  • Enhancing ChemExpo Vietnam’s credibility as a gateway platform to the Vietnamese and ASEAN chemical markets

For International Buyers & Investors:

✔ Use ChemExpo Vietnam as an efficient platform for market intelligence, policy insights, and supplier sourcing

✔ Conduct targeted B2B meetings to assess local capabilities in cost structure, delivery, and technical adaptability

✔ Identify long-term partnership opportunities based on insights highlighted in media and on-site engagement
