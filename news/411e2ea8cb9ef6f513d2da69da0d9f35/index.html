公司介绍



珠海承宇新材料公司是一家以数码喷墨技术为依托，能为客户提供数码喷墨应用方案与一站式服务公司。公司自成立以来，始终坚持开拓创新、诚信务实的经营原则，为客户提供从喷墨墨水使用、打印设备调试到数码喷墨应用方案的全方位服务，以及基于强有力的技术团队，为客户解决产品应用及产品售后的技术问题。

主营范围包括烫画墨水、热升华墨水、活性墨水等纺织墨水及打印设备；各类UV墨水及办公桌面墨水。公司在竞争中求生存，在创新中求发展，在专业细致的服务中求认同。 

做专业的、永续经营的，有价值的产品与服务；目标集聚，品质第一，持续创新，快捷满足客户需求，是我们服务客户的遵旨。

Zhuhai Chengyu New Materials Company is a company that relies on digital inkjet technology to provide customers with digital inkjet application solutions and one-stop services. Since its establishment, the company has always adhered to the business principles of exploration and innovation, integrity and pragmatism, providing customers with comprehensive services from inkjet ink use, printing equipment debugging to digital inkjet application solutions. Based on a strong technical team, we solve technical problems related to product application and after-sales for customers.

Our main business scope includes textile inks and printing equipment such as hot stamping ink, sublimation ink, and reactive ink; Various UV inks and office desktop inks. The company seeks survival in competition, development in innovation, and recognition in professional and meticulous services.

Develop professional, sustainable, and valuable products and services; Target aggregation, quality first, continuous innovation, and quick satisfaction of customer needs are our guiding principles in serving customers.

Welcome new and old customers to negotiate and cooperate at any time.
