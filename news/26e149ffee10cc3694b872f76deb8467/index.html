展会介绍

Exhibition Introduction





义乌工博会将在“世界超市-义乌”隆重举办，展会面积超过 40000 平方米，将来自全国优秀企业600家，集中展示各种泵阀、 机床与自动化相关设备及配件;橡塑产品与设备;印刷与包装设备;胶带与薄膜产品及技术等工业制造品类产品，多年来已成为业界国际贸易成交的重要商贸平台。

The Yiwu Industrial Expo will be grandly held in "Yiwu, the World's Supermarket". With an exhibition area of over 40,000 square meters, it will gather 600 outstanding enterprises from across the country. These enterprises will focus on displaying various industrial manufacturing products,including pump valves, machine tools.automation-related equipment and accessories rubber and plastic products and equipment; printing and packaging equipment; adhesive tape, film products and related technologies, etc. Over the years, the expo has become an important trade platform for international trade transactions in the industry.

Transportation Routes




Transportation Routes

机场--国际博览中心 

Airport - International Expo Center

可乘坐机场专线1号线直达银都华悦酒店后(全程2元)，打车至国际博览中心(全程10元)

乘坐出租车，时间约为30分钟(全程约50元)

You can take the Airport Express Line 1 directly to Yindu Huayue Hotel (the whole journey costs 2 yuan), and then take a taxi to the International Expo Center (the whole journey costs 10 yuan).

It takes about 30 minutes by taxi (about 50 yuan for the whole journey).


火车站一国际博览中心

Railway station - International Expo Center

乘公交车804路至会展体育中心站下车，步行约20分钟到展馆;乘坐出租车，时间约为30分钟(全程约50元)

Take Bus No. 804 and get off at the Exhibition and Sports Center Station. Walk for about 20 minutes to reach the exhibition hall. It takes about 30 minutes by taxi (about 50 yuan for the whole journey).


国际商贸城客运中心一一国际博览中心

International Trade City Passenger Transport Center - International Expo Center

步行至国际商贸城站乘坐405路内环，在国际博览中心站下车(全程1.5元);出租车约为10分钟车程(全程约15元)

Walk to the International Trade City Station and take the Inner loop Bus No. 405. Get off at the International Expo Center Station (1.5 yuan for the whole journey). The taxi ride takes about 10 minutes (the whole journey costs approximately 15 yuan).
