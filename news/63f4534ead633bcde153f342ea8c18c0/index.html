企业简介（Company Profile）

    宁波精华电子科技股份有限公司创立于2008年，2015年挂牌新三板，深耕汽车电子领域十八年，主营汽车前大灯调光电机、步进电机等汽车执行器的研发生产，是大众、比亚迪、长城、吉利等中外车企二级供应商，也为法雷奥、马瑞利等全球照明系统龙头企业配套。公司先后获评国家专精特新小巨人、国家高新技术企业、国家知识产权优势企业等多项荣誉，在技术创新、智能制造等方面形成核心优势。

  Founded in 2008, Ningbo Jinghua Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. was listed on the National Equities Exchange and Quotations (NEEQ) in 2015. With 18 years of deep cultivation in the automotive electronics industry, we specialize in the R&D and manufacturing of automotive actuators, including headlamp leveling motors and stepper motors. We are a Tier 2 supplier to renowned domestic and international automakers such as Volkswagen, BYD, Great Wall Motors and Geely, and also provide supporting products to global leading lighting system manufacturers including Valeo and Marelli. The company has been successively awarded honors such as National Specialized, Sophisticated, Unique and New "Little Giant" Enterprise, National High-Tech Enterprise and National Intellectual Property Advantage Enterprise, establishing core advantages in technological innovation and intelligent manufacturing.
