巴基斯坦顶级珠宝亮相中国2025年进博会

CPEC 2025-11-07

巴基斯坦珠宝业闪耀全球，珍稀宝石工艺亮相第八届中国国际进口博览会

 

巴基斯坦顶尖宝石工艺亮相上海第八届中国国际进口博览会（CIIE），该国珠宝业正走向全球舞台。今年的焦点当属巴基斯坦知名品牌WINZA——其展出了经吉尼斯世界纪录认证、重达3051克拉的全球最大宝石级锂辉石。

 

这枚产自巴基斯坦北部的非凡宝石，彰显了该国在奢侈品出口领域日益增长的潜力。WINZA秉持“从矿山到杰作”的运营模式，确保从开采到设计的每一个环节，都融合传统工艺与现代精准技术。

 

锂辉石以其柔和的粉紫色调闻名，如此超大尺寸且净度极佳的锂辉石实属罕见。它在进博会的首次亮相，成为巴基斯坦奢侈品贸易的里程碑，进一步巩固了与中国高端市场的联系。

 

WINZA的传承跨越一个多世纪，源于乔杜里家族精湛的宝石工艺。据中国经济网报道，该公司全程掌控采矿、切割、制作等所有环节，确保产品的真实性与创意自主性——这一模式深受重视产地溯源与品牌故事的中国消费者青睐。

 

行业专家指出，巴基斯坦珠宝商与中国设计师的合作正重新定义跨境工艺。WINZA的成功印证了这一转变：七年间，其中国市场份额从20%升至50%以上，年均增长率达30%。

 

自中巴自由贸易协定第二阶段实施以来，巴基斯坦宝石出口增长迅猛。除珠宝外，进博会巴基斯坦馆还汇集了20余家企业，展出纺织品、大理石及手工艺品，标志着该国贸易正从原材料出口向高端奢侈品牌转型。

 

这枚锂辉石的展出不仅是一次展品亮相，更代表着巴基斯坦正式跻身全球奢侈品领域。它承载着文化传承、精湛工艺与贸易外交的内涵，彰显了民族自豪感与国际社会的认可。


“Pakistan’s jewellery industry is shining globally as the nation’s finest gemstone craftsmanship debuts at the 8th China International Import Expo (CIIE) in Shanghai. The spotlight this year falls on WINZA, a renowned Pakistani brand presenting the world’s largest gem-quality kunzite , a 3,051-carat crystal officially recognised by Guinness World Records.


The remarkable gemstone, sourced from northern Pakistan, highlights the country’s growing potential in luxury exports. WINZA’s “mine-to-masterpiece” model ensures every step, from extraction to design, reflects tradition blended with modern precision.


Kunzite, known for its delicate pink and lavender tones, rarely appears in such impressive size and clarity. Its debut at CIIE marks a milestone in Pakistan’s luxury trade, strengthening ties with China’s high-end market.


WINZA’s legacy spans over a century through the Chaudhry family’s mastery in gem artistry. As reported by China Economic Net, the company oversees all stages mining, cutting, and crafting  ensuring authenticity and creative control. This model appeals to Chinese consumers who value both provenance and story.


Industry experts note that collaboration between Pakistani jewellers and Chinese designers is redefining cross-border craftsmanship. WINZA’s success reflects this shift in seven years, its Chinese market share rose from 20% to over 50%, with annual growth averaging 30%.


Since the second phase of the Pakistan-China Free Trade Agreement, gemstone exports have grown rapidly. Beyond jewellery, Pakistan’s pavilion at CIIE showcases more than 20 companies offering textiles, marble, and artisanal goods symbolising a clear shift from raw materials to refined luxury brands.


Kunzite’s showcase is more than an exhibit , it represents Pakistan’s entry into the global luxury arena. The gemstone embodies a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and trade diplomacy, reflecting national pride and international recognition.”
