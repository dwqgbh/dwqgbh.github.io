佛山市德龙信环保设备有限公司
Foshan Delongxin Environmental Protection Equipment Co., Ltd.
公司简介 | Company Profile
佛山市德龙信环保设备有限公司是一家专注环境污染治理二十年的老牌企业，凭借自主研发和定制化服务优势，致力于为全球客户提供专业、高效的解决方案及技术咨询服务。
Foshan Delongxin Environmental Protection Equipment Co., Ltd. is a well-established enterprise with 20 years of expertise in environmental pollution control. With strong capabilities in independent R&D and customized services, the company is committed to providing professional and efficient solutions, along with technical consulting services, to clients worldwide.

全球化战略 | Global Strategy
多年来，公司坚持以科技创新为驱动，以品质与服务为核心，不断积累丰富的行业经验与技术成果，赢得了国内外客户的广泛信赖。
Over the years, Delongxin has adhered to technology-driven innovation and a quality- and service-oriented philosophy, gaining extensive industry experience and earning the trust of both domestic and international customers.
秉承全球化品牌战略目标，德龙信积极拓展国际市场，整合先进环保技术与优质资源，努力为客户提供更加全面、高效的环保产品和完善的配套解决方案，助力全球环境治理与可持续发展。
In line with its global brand strategy, Delongxin actively expands into international markets, integrating advanced environmental technologies and premium resources. The company strives to deliver more comprehensive and efficient products, along with complete supporting solutions, contributing to global environmental management and sustainable development.
