ASIAN CHAMBER OF COMMERCE (ACC)
Houston, Texas | Est. 1990
🌐 www.asianchamber-hou.org




Building Bridges Between Asia and Houston — The Energy Capital of the World

For more than 35 years, the Asian Chamber of Commerce (ACC) has served as the leading voice for Houston’s Asian business community, creating meaningful connections between local, national, and international partners.

Our mission is to connect, inspire, and grow by fostering leadership, economic opportunity, and cross-cultural collaboration.

 

Why Houston?


  • 4th largest city in the U.S. — one of the most diverse and globally connected

  • Home to 5,000+ Asian-owned businesses

  • Global hub for energy, healthcare, logistics, and innovation

  • Strategic gateway for trade and investment between Asia and the Americas


Signature Programs & Events


Our programs strengthen business growth, leadership, and global partnerships.


  • Spirit of Entrepreneurship Awards
    Honoring outstanding Asian-owned businesses and visionary leaders.

  • Asian Chamber Foundation’s Business Conference & Lenders Matchmaking
    Connecting small businesses with lenders, resources, and experts for growth.

  • Asian Chamber Foundation’s Business Expo & Luncheon
    Showcasing innovation, community partnerships, and corporate opportunities.

  • Asian Chamber Foundation’s Rising 10 Awards
    Recognizing ten fast-growing Asian-owned businesses making a regional impact.

  • Asian Restaurant Series
    Celebrating culinary diversity and supporting Houston’s vibrant restaurant community.

  • Executive Business Program
    Empowering business leaders with high-level professional development and mentorship.

  • Trade Expo & Luncheon
    Fostering international partnerships and trade expansion between Asia and Texas.
