孟加拉国际纺织暨制衣机械展


Dhaka Int'l Textile & Garment Machinery Exhibition


开展时间：2025年02月20日—23日

Date: Feb. 20-23, 2025

展馆地址：孟加拉达卡国际会展中心(ICCB)

Address: International Convention City Bashundhara (ICCB) Dhaka, Bangladesh

Richpeace Booth: HALL 6-241

展会盛况 

    Exhibition Highlights    

2025年2月20日，孟加拉国际纺织暨制衣机械展在孟加拉达卡国际会展中心(ICCB)隆重开幕。这里，汇聚了全球纺织行业的精英与先锋，共同探索纺织科技的未来趋势，分享创新成果，共谋行业发展新篇章。

On February 20, 2025, the Dhaka Int'l Textile & Garment Machinery Exhibition commenced with great fanfare at the International Convention City Bashundhara (ICCB) in Dhaka, Bangladesh. This event served as a platform for industry leaders and innovators from the global textile sector to come together to explore future trends in textile technology, share groundbreaking achievements, and collaborate on shaping new horizons for industry development.

展会上，上工富怡的展台成为了众多目光的焦点。我们收到了来自世界各地的客户赞誉，其中不乏一些使用我们版房设备超过10年的老客户。他们的设备，历经时间的考验，依然稳定运行，见证了上工富怡对品质的执着追求与匠心独运。这份信任与支持，是我们不断前行的强大动力，也是我们持续创新、追求卓越的源泉。

At the exhibition, the Richpeace booth captured the spotlight, drawing the attention of numerous visitors. We received praise from customers around the globe, including several loyal clients who have been using our pattern room equipment for over a decade. Their equipment, having withstood the test of time, continues to operate reliably, demonstrating Richpeace's steadfast commitment to quality and innovation. This trust and support serve as a powerful motivation for us to continue progressing and are the foundation of our ongoing innovation and pursuit of excellence.

随着全球纺织产业的快速发展，孟加拉作为服装出口市场的佼佼者，对纺织设备的需求日益多元化。本次展会，上工富怡展出了多款功能全面的绣花机，包括亮片绣、散珠绣、缠绕绣、毛巾绣和平绣等，满足了客户对多样化生产的迫切需求。这些创新产品，不仅提升了生产效率，更为客户的产品竞争力提供了强有力的支撑，助力他们在激烈的市场竞争中脱颖而出。

With the rapid development of the global textile industry, Bangladesh has emerged as a leading player in the apparel export market, resulting in a growing demand for diverse textile equipment. At this exhibition, Richpeace showcased a range of versatile embroidery machines, including sequin, bead, chenille, towel, and flat embroidery machines, addressing the urgent needs of customers for varied production capabilities. These innovative products not only enhance production efficiency but also significantly bolster customers' competitiveness, enabling them to stand out in a fiercely competitive market.

展会在如火如荼进行中，欢迎新老客户和各界朋友莅临富怡展位HALL 6-241参观交流，了解更多富怡产品和最新技术成果!

The exhibition is still in full swing, and we warmly welcome new and old customers, as well as friends from all walks of life, to visit the Richpeace booth at HALL 6-241 for exchanges and to learn more about our products and latest technological achievements!
