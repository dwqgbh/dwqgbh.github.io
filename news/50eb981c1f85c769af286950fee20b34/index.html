会议主题

本次会议围绕“人工智能赋能低碳岩土与城市更新建设”主线，聚焦2026年岩土工程前沿热点，围绕AI岩土融合、大数据数字孪生、低碳固碳材料、地下智能建造、十五五城市更新五大方向展开深度交流，重点剖析岩土工程技术在粤港澳大湾区城市更新、轨道交通、地下管网、水利大坝、海绵城市等重大工程领域的创新应用模式，为青年岩土工作者提供学习和交流平台。会议涵盖但不限于以下专题内容：

1、AI与大数据在岩土工程中的应用

2、低碳岩土材料与固碳技术

3、地下空间智能建造与全生命周期健康监测

4、极端气候下城市基础设施韧性与防灾减灾

5、城市更新时代下大湾区地下空间开发新进展与挑战

Forum Topics

The theme of this forum is "AI Empowered Low-Carbon Geotechnics and Urban Regeneration" , focusing on the cutting-edge frontiers of geotechnical engineering in 2026. In-depth exchanges will be carried out across five core research orientations: integration of AI and geotechnical engineering, big data and digital twin technologies, low-carbon and carbon sequestration materials, intelligent underground construction, and urban renewal under the 15th Five-Year Plan. It will thoroughly analyze innovative application models of geotechnical technologies in major engineering sectors within the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, including urban renewal, rail transit, underground pipe networks, hydraulic dams and sponge cities. Serving as a learning and communication platform for young geotechnical practitioners, the conference will cover thematic topics including but not limited to the following:

1. Applications of AI and Big Data in Geotechnical Engineering

2. Low-Carbon Geomaterials and Carbon Sequestration Technologies

3. Intelligent Construction and Life-Cycle Health Monitoring of Underground Space

4. Urban Infrastructure Resilience and Disaster Prevention/Mitigation under Extreme Climate Conditions

5. New Advances and Challenges in Underground Space Development in the Greater Bay Area under the Urban Renewal Era
