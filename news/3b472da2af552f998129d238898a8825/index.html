跨越山海·智启未来 | TOMELY 闪耀2025泰国东盟建筑及技术博览会

通利大理石瓷砖 2025-05-10


2025年4月29日至5月4日，全球建材行业目光聚焦泰国曼谷——东盟地区规模最大、影响力最广的东盟建筑及技术博览会（Architect’25）盛大启幕。作为中国高端建材品牌的领军者，通利大理石瓷砖以“破界前瞻·智绘远景”为主题重磅亮相，向东南亚乃至全球市场展现中国智造的实力与魅力。
From April 29 to May 4, 2025, the global construction and building materials industry turned its attention to Bangkok, Thailand, as the Architect’25—the largest and most influential event of its kind in the region—officially commenced. As a leading Chinese high-end building materials brand, TOMELY Marble Tiles made a grand appearance under the theme “Breaking Boundaries, Envisioning the Future”, showcasing the strength and innovation of Chinese smart manufacturing to Southeast Asia and the global market.


以匠心深耕，铸就行业标杆


Crafting Excellence, Setting Industry Benchmarks



通利大理石瓷砖始终以“永远做最好的产品”为使命，深耕建材领域十三载，凭借国家级研发中心、超百项技术专利及全球领先的全智能生产线，成为行业技术创新的风向标。
Guided by its mission to “always make the best products,” TOMELY has dedicated 13 years to the building materials industry. Leveraging its national-level R&D center, over 100 technical patents, and globally leading intelligent production lines, the brand has become a beacon of technological innovation.

design patent

▷ 部分产品外观认证专利展示

此次参展，通利品牌的“皇家绿”产品格外引人注目。皇家绿是通利1:1复刻巴西奢石，并结合新工艺技术精心打造而成，逼真的纹理将天然大理石的深邃与灵动展现得淋漓尽致，每一道脉络都仿佛在诉说着大自然的鬼斧神工，彰显了通利在大理石瓷砖领域的深厚造诣。
At the expo, TOMELY’s “Royal Green” series stole the spotlight. This product line meticulously replicates Brazil’s luxurious natural stone using advanced craftsmanship and innovative techniques. Its hyper-realistic veining captures the depth and vitality of natural marble, with every line echoing the artistry of nature—a testament to TOMELY’s mastery in marble tile design.

通利展位



精湛工艺与创新设计的完美结合


Fusion of Artistry and Innovation


通利一直专注大理石瓷砖领域，致力于将精湛工艺与创新设计相融合。在此次展会上，通利不仅展示了对传统工艺的传承与创新，更体现了对现代设计理念的深刻理解和运用，让观众感受到了中国品牌在国际市场上的强大竞争力，也传递出中国陶瓷文化与现代工艺的独特魅力，进一步提升了中国制造在国际市场上的形象和影响力。
TOMELY has long focused on blending exquisite craftsmanship with cutting-edge design in the marble tile sector. At Architect’25, the brand not only highlighted its heritage of traditional techniques but also demonstrated a profound understanding of contemporary design. This synergy allowed visitors to witness the formidable competitiveness of Chinese brands on the global stage while celebrating the unique charm of Chinese ceramic culture and modern innovation.

跨越国界，共鉴非凡魅力


Bridging Borders, Sharing Excellence



通利大理石瓷砖在海外展览的征程中，不断书写着属于自己的荣耀篇章。在这个全球设计与建筑艺术交融的舞台上，通利大理石瓷砖成为了展览独特的焦点，吸引着众多国际友人前来品鉴通利品牌非凡魅力，共同开启一场关于美与品质的探索之旅。期待在未来，通利能够继续在国际舞台上绽放光彩，为全球消费者带来更多优质的产品和服务。
Throughout its global exhibition journey, TOMELY Marble Tiles continues to carve out a legacy of excellence. As a standout at this convergence of global design and architectural artistry, the brand attracted international visitors to experience its extraordinary creations, sparking a shared journey of beauty and quality. Looking ahead, TOMELY is poised to further illuminate the global stage, delivering exceptional products and services to consumers worldwide.
