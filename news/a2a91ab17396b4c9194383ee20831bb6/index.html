2022深圳国际复合材料工业技术展览会延期举办

中国国际复合材料展览会 2022-03-11


尊敬的参展商、观众及各界同仁：


　　深圳市近期新冠疫情严重，按照深圳市疫情防控的要求，为保障参会者的健康和最广泛的利益，在向深圳市政府相关主管部门请示并与深圳会展中心充分沟通、听取各方意见后，组委会经慎重考虑决定：


　　原定于2022 年3 月30 日-4 月1 日在深圳会展中心举办的“2022 深圳国际复合材料工业技术展览会”延期举办。在深圳相关区域疫情稳定后，组委会将另行公布新的举办日期，并将通知到每一位已预登记的参展商及专业观众。


　　对于此次展会时间调整给您带来的不便，我们深表歉意！从始至终，参展商、观众和合作伙伴等所有参与者的健康和安全是我们的首要关切。延期举办是当前疫情背景下的最优选择，符合展商、观众和行业的最广泛利益。我们将全力以赴，继续积极推进展会各项工作，为参展商及观众提供更好的参展服务和观展体验。在此期间，参展商报名及观众预登记系统正常开放。


　　最后，再次感谢各位参展商、观众朋友及合作伙伴一如继往的厚爱、理解和支持！


深圳国际复合材料工业技术展览会组委会

2022 年3 月11 日




Important Notice

on the Postponement of CCE Shenzhen 2022


In view of the grim COVID-19 pandemic broken out in Shenzhen over past weeks, subject to local regulations on pandemic prevention and control, following sustained efforts to seek instructions from authorities, raise discussions with venue operators and solicit opinions from all aspects and deliberative consideration, the organizing committee of CCE Shenzhen 2022 has decided hereby that:

 

CCE Shenzhen 2022, pending to be held from March 30th- April 1st, 2022 at Shenzhen Convention & Exhibition Center, will be postponed. New dates, after the subsiding of local pandemic, will be publicized and informed to every pre-registered exhibitor and professional visitor.  

 

We feel deeply sorry for the inconvenience arising from the reschedule. Our primary concern has always been the health and safety of all the visitors, exhibitors, partners and other attendees. In face of current pandemic situation, we believe the postponement of CCE Shenzhen 2022 is the best option and complies with the most extensive benefits of exhibitors, professional visitors as well as the composite industry. All-out efforts will be continued to advance the preparation of CCE Shenzhen 2022, so as to ensure better service to and participating experience of both exhibitors and visitors. During this period, our pre-registration system will stay open for exhibitors and visitors as usual. 

 

Once again, our heartfelt gratitude goes to every exhibitor, visitor and partner, for the sustained attention, understanding and support given to us!


The Organizing Committee 

of CCE Shenzhen
