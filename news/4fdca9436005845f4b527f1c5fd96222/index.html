[Last Call] 2022进博会德企名录 German Company Directory

德中工商服务平台 2022-09-21

《进博会德企名录》收录了第五届中国国际进口博览会德国联合展团展商的信息，包括公司商标、展位位置、公司介绍以及产品或者服务介绍。该名录将通过各地方政府、进博会地方交易团和德国海外商会联盟营销渠道分发到全国各地。


The German Company Directory lists German pavilion exhibitors of the 5th China International Import Exhibition (CIIE), incl. company logo, booth location, company profile, and product or service introduction. This directory will be distributed all over China, incl. local governments, local VIP Buyers of CIIE, and through the AHK marketing channels.

目前,《进博会德企名录》是今年中国国际进口博览会上唯一能获得的德国企业名录。

Currently, the German Company Directory is the only obtainable directory for German companies exhibiting at this year’s CIIE.

无论您是否为德国展团展商，您都可通过以下两种途径加入此名录：

Whether you are the German pavilion exhibitor or not, you can join this directory in the following two ways:


①  进博会德企地图导览 （嵌入《德企名录》手册内的单独地图册）

Guide Map (separate map embedded in the German Company Directory）for German exhibitors at CIIE


只需1,000人民币即可在手册的导览图上标记您的展位位置，通过AHK官方渠道获得全面的曝光！

Mark your booth location in the Guide Map of this directory and get comprehensive exposure through our AHK network for only 1000 RMB!

② 唯一进博会德企名录广告机会

Advertisement opportunities in the only German Company Directory at CIIE

广告位置将按先到先得的原则分配。

The advertisement placement will be assigned according to a first-come, first-serve principle.

地图导览以及广告订购截止日期: 

9月30日

The deadline for joining 

the Guide Map and booking the ADs: September 30
