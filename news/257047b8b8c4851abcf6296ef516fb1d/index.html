工控明灯：秉持公正，洞察行业动态

Industrial control beacon: uphold fairness and gain insight into industry trends

    自2020年10月31日发布第一篇文章以来，"工控明灯"已经走过了四个年头。作为一个致力于观察和分析工控行业动向的平台，我们始终坚持站在第三方的视角，力求公正，绝不偏袒任何品牌。

   Since the first article was published on October 31, 2020, "Industrial Control Light" has gone through four years. As a platform dedicated to observing and analyzing the trends of the industrial control industry, we always adhere to the perspective of a third party, strive for fairness, and never favor any brand.

     为了保持公正性，过去几年里，我们拒绝了许多来自工控行业的商业推广。商业利益虽诱人，但为了不影响我们对行业的独立观察与分析，我们选择了一条艰难但更有价值的道路。正因如此，我们得以在信息泛滥的时代，仍能为行业内的朋友们提供真实而可靠的内容。

    In order to maintain impartiality, we have rejected many commercial promotions from the industrial control industry in the past few years. Although commercial interests are tempting, in order not to affect our independent observation and analysis of the industry, we have chosen a difficult but more valuable path. Because of this, we are able to provide real and reliable content to friends in the industry in an era of information overload.

     与此同时，我们还积极分享打假信息，帮助工控圈的朋友们降低被骗的风险。在工控领域，市场环境复杂，产品和信息的真假难辨。我们希望通过持续的努力，为行业的健康发展贡献一份力量。

     At the same time, we also actively share anti-counterfeiting information to help friends in the industrial control circle reduce the risk of being deceived. In the field of industrial control, the market environment is complex and it is difficult to distinguish the authenticity of products and information. We hope to contribute to the healthy development of the industry through continuous efforts.

第二十四届中国国际工业博览会（简称“工博会”）即将于9月24日至28日在中国国家会展中心（上海）盛大启幕。

    The 24th China International Industry Fair (hereinafter referred to as "CIIE") will be grandly opened at the China National Exhibition and Convention Center (Shanghai) from September 24 to 28.

本届工博会以“新质领航，数字赋能”为主题，不仅是对当前全球工业发展趋势的深刻洞察，更是对未来工业智能化、数字化转型的一次全面展望。本届工博会设9大专业展，规划面积30万平方米，预期超过3000家展商参展。

The theme of this year's CIIE is "New Quality Leading, Digital Empowerment", which is not only a profound insight into the current global industrial development trend, but also a comprehensive outlook on the future industrial intelligence and digital transformation. This year's CIIE has 9 major professional exhibitions with a planned area of 300,000 square meters, and more than 3,000 exhibitors are expected to participate.
