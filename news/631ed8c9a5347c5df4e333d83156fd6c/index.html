SmartGen展会 | 众智科技亮相2026土耳其国际工业展览会

众智SmartGen 2026-06-12



2026年6月10日至13日，备受瞩目的土耳其国际工业展览会在伊斯坦布尔博览中心盛装启幕。本届展会吸引全球 800 + 企业、60,000 + 专业观众参与。众智科技借势发力，既是对设立土耳其伊斯坦布尔工厂的战略呼应，更是深耕中东欧、辐射欧亚市场的重要一步。








众智科技作为中国发电机组控制系统领域的领军企业，携全系列智能控制解决方案亮相展会，包含新一代发电机组控制器HGM6120P-4G、市电并联控制器HGM9520P、智能电源切换系统IPTS80、增程控制系统HCU4320、分体式柴油发动机控制系统主控模块HMC9170和显示模块MMU10、电子调速器SCU100、混合能源控制系统HMU7、蓄电池充电器BACM2440等。全方位展示在工业自动化及混合能源领域的技术实力。





众智科技诚挚邀请各方客户、合作伙伴与行业专家莅临展位，共同探讨工业自动化与电力控制领域的前沿技术，进一步巩固并拓展合作商机。众智科技期待以优质的产品与高效的服务，为欧亚乃至全球能源智能控制贡献力量，在这片连接欧亚大陆的热土上继续书写高质量发展的新篇章！






众智科技展位号：H100

展会时间：2026年6月10-13日

展会地点： 伊斯坦布尔博览中心

热烈欢迎大家莅临现场交流指导！

Exhibition | Smartgen Debuts at the 2026 Turkey International Industry Fair


From June 10 to 13, 2026, the highly anticipated Turkey International Industry Fair opened grandly at the Istanbul Expo Center. This year's event attracted over 800 exhibitors and more than 60,000 professional visitors worldwide. Riding the wave of opportunity, Smartgen’s participation not only echoes its strategic move to establish a factory in Istanbul, Türkiye, but also marks a major step in deepening its presence in Central and Eastern Europe while reaching out to the broader Eurasian market.








As a leading player in China’s genset control system industry, Smartgen unveiled its full range of intelligent control solutions at the fair, featuring next-generation products including the HGM6120P-4G generator controller, HGM9520P mains parallel controller, IPTS80 intelligent power transfer system, HCU4320 range-extender control system, HMC9170 master module and MMU10 display module of the split diesel engine control system, SCU100 electronic speed controller, HMU7 hybrid energy control system, and BACM2440 battery charger. The exhibits showcased Smartgen’s technical strengths across industrial automation and hybrid energy applications.




Smartgen sincerely invites customers, partners, and industry experts to visit our booth. Let’s explore cutting-edge technologies in industrial automation and power control, and work together to strengthen and expand cooperation opportunities. With high-quality products and efficient services, Smartgen looks forward to contributing to intelligent energy control across Eurasia and beyond, and to continuing its journey of high-quality development on this dynamic land connecting two continents.
