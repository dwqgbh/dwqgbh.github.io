意大利航空航天企业协会参加2025年亚太地区航空维修展(MRO Asia-Pacific 2025)

IAN 2025-09-19

Andrea Spiriti, President of the Italian Aerospace Network (IAN), alongside Jayjay, IAN’s Business Developer, attended MRO Asia-Pacific 2025, showcasing Italy’s aerospace expertise on a regional stage. Their presence highlighted IAN’s commitment to fostering international collaborations, exploring innovative solutions in maintenance, repair, and overhaul, and connecting Italian aerospace companies with key stakeholders across the Asia-Pacific aviation market. The participation also reinforced IAN’s role as a bridge between Italian industry leaders and global partners, driving growth and knowledge exchange in the sector.

安德烈亚·斯皮里蒂（Andrea Spiriti），意大利航空航天企业协会（IAN）主席，以及IAN商务拓展经理Jayjay出席了2025年亚太地区航空维修展（MRO Asia-Pacific 2025），在区域舞台上展示了意大利航空航天的专业实力。他们的出席凸显了IAN致力于促进国际合作、探索维护、维修和大修（MRO）领域创新解决方案，并将意大利航空航天领域的公司与亚太地区关键利益相关者连接起来的承诺。这一参与进一步强化了IAN作为意大利行业领导者与全球合作伙伴之间桥梁的角色，推动了行业增长与知识交流。

MRO Asia-Pacific 2025 serves as a cornerstone event for the aviation maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) industry, providing a strategic platform for global and regional stakeholders to converge. The expo is instrumental in showcasing technological innovations, facilitating knowledge exchange, and fostering strategic partnerships that enhance operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability in aviation. By bringing together airlines, MRO providers, manufacturers, and suppliers, the event not only drives business development but also strengthens collaboration across the Asia-Pacific aerospace sector, reinforcing the region’s position as a hub for aviation excellence.

2025年亚太地区航空维修展（MRO Asia-Pacific 2025）是航空维护、维修与大修（MRO）行业的核心盛会，为全球及区域利益相关者提供了战略性交流平台。该展会在展示技术创新、促进知识交流以及推动提升航空运营效率、安全性和可持续性的战略合作方面发挥着重要作用。通过汇聚航空公司、MRO服务提供商、制造商及供应商，该活动不仅推动了业务发展，也强化了亚太航空航天领域的协作，进一步巩固了该地区作为航空卓越中心的地位。

MRO Asia-Pacific 2025 attracted a diverse lineup of industry leaders and innovators across the aviation maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) sector. Notable exhibitors included Boeing, Airbus, Rolls-Royce, SIA Engineering Company, ST Aerospace, AAR Corp, FL Technics, MacroAsia Corporation, and Chromalloy, among others. The event also featured the Singapore Pavilion, coordinated by the Association of Aerospace Industries (Singapore), showcasing the capabilities of over 30 Singapore enterprises. Key figures such as Elodie Berthonneau, Vice President Asia-Pacific at Lufthansa Cargo, and Simon Cheok, Executive Panel Speaker, contributed to discussions on industry advancements. Their presence underscored the expo's role as a central hub for shaping the future of aviation in the Asia-Pacific region.

2025亚太航空维护、维修和大修 (MRO) 展览会吸引了众多航空维护、维修和大修 (MRO) 领域的行业领袖和创新者。知名参展商包括波音、空客、劳斯莱斯、新航工程公司、新科宇航、AAR 公司、FL Technics、MacroAsia公司和 Chromalloy 等。由新加坡航空工业协会(Association of Aerospace Industries (Singapore)) 协调的新加坡展团也参加了此次展会，展示了30多家新加坡企业的实力。汉莎货运航空亚太区副总裁 Elodie Berthonneau和执行小组发言人 Simon Cheok 等重要人物参与了关于行业进步的讨论。他们的出席凸显了该博览会作为塑造亚太地区航空业未来发展中心的地位。

The Italian Aerospace Network (IAN) is a leading industry association dedicated to promoting the excellence and innovation of Italy’s aerospace sector on a global scale. Its core mission is to connect Italian aerospace companies, from manufacturers to service providers, with international partners, fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and business opportunities. IAN serves as a strategic bridge between Italy’s aerospace industry and global markets, supporting its members in expanding their presence, advancing technological innovation, and strengthening competitiveness in the rapidly evolving aviation and aerospace landscape.

意大利航空航天企业协会（IAN）是领先的行业协会，致力于在全球范围内推广意大利航空航天领域的卓越与创新。其核心使命是将意大利航空航天企业——从制造商到服务提供商——与国际合作伙伴连接起来，促进合作、知识交流及商业机会。IAN充当意大利航空航天产业与全球市场之间的战略桥梁，支持其成员扩大国际影响力、推动技术创新，并在快速发展的航空航天领域提升竞争力。
