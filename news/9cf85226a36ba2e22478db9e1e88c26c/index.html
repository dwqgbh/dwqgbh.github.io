关于“2025年大湾区商事调解员培训(学时互认第二期)”报名截止的通知

横琴国际商事调解中心HICMC 2025-12-17

关于“2025年大湾区商事调解员培训(学时互认第二期)”报名截止的通知


致各调解员及商事调解领域同仁：


衷心感谢各位对本中心与广州国际商贸商事调解中心联合主办的“2025年大湾区商事调解员培训(学时互认第二期)”的关注和支持！本次培训自启动报名以来，得到了业界同仁的积极响应，报名人数已远超预期并提前达到上限。为确保培训质量，经合办单位共同审慎研究，现决定提前停止接受报名。


对于未能成功报名的同仁，我们致以诚挚的歉意。本中心后续将持续推出商事调解专业培训活动，敬请关注本中心微信公众号及官方网站。


再次感谢各位同仁对本中心的信任与支持！


横琴国际商事调解中心

2025年12月17日


Notification on Early Closure of Registration for "2025 Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area Commercial Mediator Training (Phase II: Training Hours Mutual Recognition Program)"


To All Mediators and Colleagues in Commercial Mediation:


We extend our sincere gratitude for your strong interest and support for the "2025 Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area Commercial Mediator Training (Phase II: Training Hours Mutual Recognition Program)," co-organized by HICMC and GICMC. Since its launch, the program has received an overwhelming response from industry peers. Registration has now reached full capacity well ahead of schedule, exceeding our anticipated numbers.


To ensure the quality and effectiveness of the training, we have made the careful decision to close registration earlier than originally planned.


We sincerely apologize to those who were unable to secure a place in this program. HICMC remains committed to offering professional development opportunities in commercial mediation and will organize similar programs in the future. For updates, please follow our official WeChat account and visit our website.


Thank you once again for your continued trust and support.


Hengqin International Commercial Mediation Center
December 17, 2025


示范条款

本合同之各方当事人均同意将因本合同引起的或与本合同有关的任何争议，提交横琴国际商事调解中心，按照申请调解时该中心现行有效的调解规则进行调解。经调解后如达成调解协议，各方均应认真履行该调解协议所载之各项内容。


The Parties hereby agree that any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall be submitted to Hengqin International Commercial Mediation Center for mediation conducted in accordance with its Mediation Rules in effect at the time of applying for mediation. If a settlement agreement is reached after mediation, each party shall duly perform all obligations set forth in the agreement.
