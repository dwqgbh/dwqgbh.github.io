台州中港机械设备公司

台州中港机械设备有限公司位于浙东滨海城市，是一家集专业研发、制造、销售于一体的救生设备、玻璃钢救生船艇企业，是目前省内名列前茅的知名生产企业之一，拥有国内外先进的生产检测设备。产品畅销国内外，深得广大用户的好评和信赖。

本公司占地面积约6660平方米，拥有一支高素质的管理技术队伍；本公司通过中国船级社ISO9001：2015质量管理体系、职业健康安全管理体系和环境管理体系认证，所生产的产品经中国船级社CCS、浙江船舶检验局ZC、法国船级社BV、俄罗斯船级社RMRS、欧盟船级社EC认证，满足1974年国际海上人命安全公约及其修正案、国际救生设备规则（LSA）和国际海事组织MSC.81（70）救生设备试验建议等。

Located in a coastal city of East Zhejiang, Taizhou Zhonggang Machine Equipment Co., Ltd. is a company that integrates professional R&D, manufacturing and sales of life-saving equipment and GFRP (glass fiber reinforced plastic) lifeboats. It is currently one of the top well-known manufacturing enterprises in the province, with production testing facilities that are advanced both at home and abroad. Our products are well sold domestically and internationally and have won the praise and trust of our customers.

Our company covers an area of 6,660 square meters and has a highly qualified managerial and technical team. The company has passed the ISO9001:2015 quality management system, occupational health and safety management system, environmental management system certification and our products are approved by CCS, ZC, BV, RMRS and EC, and meet the requirements of the 1974 amendments to the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea and the International Life Saving Appliance Code (LSA) and the IMO MSC.81 (70) test resolution.

01

主营业务

本公司主导产品有：玻璃钢救生/救助艇、快速救助艇、全封闭（普通/耐火）救生兼救助艇、自由降落式救生艇、重力倒臂式吊艇架、液压倒出式吊艇架、平台式吊艇架、自由降落救生艇降放装置、单臂救助艇/筏降放装置、快速救助艇降放装置、电动救生/救助艇绞车、液压回转起重机、玻璃钢工作艇等。

The company’s leading products include: FRP open type lifeboat/rescue boat, fast rescue boat, fully enclosed (common/fire-protected) lifeboat and rescue boat, free-fall lifeboat, gravity luffing arm davit, hydraulic luffing arm davit, platform davit, free-fall lifeboat launching appliance, single-arm rescue boat/liferaft launching appliance, fast rescue boat launching appliance, electrical life/rescue boat winch, hydraulic slewing crane, FRP workboat, etc.

02

经典案例

目前，公司销售网络已分布全球，在韩国、印尼、秘鲁、新加坡等国家拥有海外服务站点。从业期间为多家国内外石油钻井平台、发电平台，各大船厂以及学校教育培训中心提供救生设备。同时，本公司还拥有全面的售后服务体系，拥有中国船级社供方认可证书和巴拿马船级社检修资质认可证书，能为客户及时提供翻新升级、检修、检测一站式服务。

Currently, the company's sales network spans the globe, with overseas service stations established in countries including South Korea, Indonesia, Peru, and Singapore. During its operations, the company has supplied lifesaving equipment to numerous domestic and international oil drilling platforms, power generation platforms, major shipyards, and educational training centers. Additionally, the company maintains a comprehensive after-sales service system, holding supplier approval certificates from the China Classification Society and the Panama Classification Society. This enables the company to provide clients with timely, one-stop services including refurbishment and upgrades, maintenance, and inspections.

关于海博会

中国海洋经济博览会（以下简称“中国海博会”）是我国唯一的国家级海洋经济展会，被广泛誉为“中国海洋第一展”。中国海博会自2019年落户深圳以来，已成功举办五届，吸引了全球一大批优秀企业、高校、研究机构前来参展，已成为中国海洋经济发展的重要风向标和促进海洋产业交流合作的重要平台。2025中国海博会定于10月28日-30日在深圳国际会展中心（宝安新馆）举办，届时将邀请相关部委和地方政府、央企、院士专家、行业领袖、国际机构、外商代表等出席。本届中国海博会以“数智深蓝·共创未来”为主题，将全面聚焦海洋能源、深海采矿、海洋智能装备、海洋电子信息及数据、海洋现代服务业、海洋生物医药、滨海旅游等新兴领域。
