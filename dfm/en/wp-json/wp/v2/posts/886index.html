\uff0aExhibition Activities\uff0a<\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

The 1st Marker Layout Competition<\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

To meet the market needs, in addition to the popularization of application of CAD\/CAM system in clothing industry, promotion of this system application is necessary so as to increase the competitiveness of suppliers. In the competition, the participants should complete the marker layout within a period. This definitely offered an idea exchanging platform for the technicians as well as exhibiting the modernized production technique. With multitudinous participants and their marvelous technique, the competition was so keen that attracted a large number of audiences.<\/p>\n

The 1st Em~Touching \u2013 International Embroidery Design Competition<\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

With the rapid growth of embroidery design and technology, this textile technology is widely used in the clothing industry. \u201cEm-Touching\u201d aimed at collecting the most touching pieces revealing the creativity of participants and the extraordinary embroidery techniques. More experts of embroidery design were then excavated, and the quality of embroidery design can be further enhanced. On the other hand, it aimed at enhancing the exhibitors\u2019corporate reputation as well as promoting their brand-names and further consolidate the corporate status in the industry. Over 40 designs were collected and this definitely proved the enthusiasm of the participants. All splendid and excelling designs demonstrated the brand new embroidery technique and the creativity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/a>\uff0aFree Bus Facilitates Buyers\uff0a<\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n

The exhibition hall situated in the middle of the Pearl River Delta, where only an hour should be spent in traveling to and from other important cities of the Pearl River Delta. With the favourable transportation facilities, free shuttle bus service was arranged between the exhibition centre and other important cities, which include the South China Region such as Hong Kong, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhuhai, Zhongshan, Foshan, Huizhou, Junan, Shunde, Panyu, Zencheng and so on, so as to assemble all experts from the textile and clothing industry.<\/p>\n
