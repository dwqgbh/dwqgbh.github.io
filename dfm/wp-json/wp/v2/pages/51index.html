About Organizers:<\/strong><\/span><\/h4>\n
\n

Paper Communication Exhibition Services<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

Established in 1985, Paper Communication Exhibition Services (Paper Communication) has been engaging in organizing trade exhibitions, publishing industrial magazines, as well as providing printing and design services.\u00a0 Since its establishment, Paper Communication has organized a number of industrial exhibitions. Today, Paper Communication has become one of the leading exhibition organizers in Hong Kong and South China region.<\/p>\n

Paper Communication organizes over ten exhibitions in Hong Kong and Mainland China every year with themes spanning from machinery, plastics & packaging, textile & clothing, footwear machinery & material, printed circuit board. Exhibitions such as machinery & material, plastics & packaging, textile & clothing are some of the world-renowned events. In addition, Paper Communication has organized a number of Vietnam and Malaysia machinery exhibitions. The Vietnam industry exhibitions have already become the major trading events in Vietnam. In recent years, more industrial markets have been explored and developed in Cambodia and Myanmar.<\/p>\n

Paper Communication aims to become a one-stop exhibition organizer, providing impeccable services and creating business opportunities for exhibitors. Paper Communication currently has strategic partners in Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Italy and Spain as well as being firmly established as a major exhibition organizer in Asia. Paper Communication will become the leading exhibition organizer in Asia, building up the largest exhibition and trading platform in Asia.<\/p>\n
