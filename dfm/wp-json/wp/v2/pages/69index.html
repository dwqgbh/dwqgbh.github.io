      \n4. Privacy Policy Statement From The Organisers<\/b><\/p>\n

      \u3000\u3000The Organisers take individual privacy seriously. The Organisers will comply with applicable personal data protection laws, including applicable PRC laws and regulations on personal data protection.<\/div>\n

      \u3000\u3000(1)\tInformation collection<\/u><\/p>\n

      \u3000\u3000In order to provide better service to the Visitor and to fulfil the purposes set out in the next paragraph, the Organisers may collect certain information when the Visitor registers with them or communicates with them. In the process, the Organisers may also collect personal information of individual contact persons of companies or their employees. As stated in the registration form, some of the requested information is mandatory and some of them the Visitor may provide voluntarily. If specific mandatory information is requested but not supplied, the Organisers may be unable to provide the requested service. The Organisers only retain personal data for so long as it is necessary for fulfilling the purposes set out in the next paragraph.<\/div>\n
      \u3000\u3000In order to comply with the health and safety precautions implemented by the 2020 coronavirus disease contact tracking requirements, the organiser may collect the identification data of exhibition visitors additionally (may include ID cards\/passports (including the numbers and a scanned copy of the document of the People\u2019s Republic of China Exit-Entry permit for travelling to and from Hong Kong and Macau, the Mainland travel permit for Taiwan residents)). Such additional identification data will be stored in the organiser\u2019s database within a reasonable time after you visit the exhibition. If required by law or legally required by the government agencies, the organiser may share your personal information (including any additional identification data) for the purpose of tracing contacts of the 2020 coronavirus disease. Without such identification data, the organiser will not be able to process your exhibition pre-registration.<\/div>\n
