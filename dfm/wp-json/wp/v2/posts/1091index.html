The 9th China (Dongguan) International Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair is scheduled on 5-8 March. Hosting 200 International Exhibitors makes the fair crucial.<\/p>\n

Hosted by Paper Communication Exhibition Services, “The 9th China (Dongguan) International Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair” (DFM 2008), scheduled on 5-8 March 2008 at Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Centre, will feature the most marketable technology & materials of the footwear industry. With the support from China Leather & Footwear Industry Research Institute, Hong Kong Synthetic Leather and Metal Material Suppliers Association & The Federation of Hong Kong Footwear, it is believed that this show must be the most remarkable one.<\/p>\n

A display of the most outstanding footwear technology encompasses the machinery, components, CAD\/CAM, synthetic leather and so forth. Exhibitors worldwide like Eyko Machinery Ltd., Adler Sewing Machinery Co., F. Alberti (Asia) Ltd., HD Shoe Machinery, Shoe-Making Machinery, Typical Sewing Machine, Velmont Machinery and so on will showcase the high standard exhibits and the latest trend and development of the industry to visitors.<\/p>\n<\/td>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n<\/tbody>\n<\/table>\n

The key feature of the show, \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d, will display the splendid and diversified footwear designs shortlisted from the Hong Kong Footwear Design Competition, revealing the prominent creativity and standard of Hong Kong designers as well as the competitiveness of Hong Kong footwear design industry. The informative technical seminars by professionals will look into the concerned topics in the market.<\/p>\n

Located at the centre of Pearl River Delta, the exhibition centre benefits from the excellent location and comprehensive transportation network. To further provide our visitors more value added services, free shuttle bus is scheduled connecting all important footwear manufacturing locations, including Hong Kong, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhuhai, Zhongshan, Foshan and so on.<\/p>\n

Now, the organizer is already at work preparing for the show and expecting to serve 20,000 visitors within 4 days.<\/p>\n
