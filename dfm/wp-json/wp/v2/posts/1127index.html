\u201cChina (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d greatly supports \u201cFootwear Design Competition\u201d organized by the Federation of Hong Kong Footwear and the Hong Kong Trade Development Council. The organizer, Paper Communication Exhibition Services, understands the importance of excavating footwear design experts and enhancing the quality of design and creativity.<\/p>\n

As the previous years, \u201cThe 10th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d will specially host \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d displaying the pieces of winners by featuring the most splendid designs of Hong Kong designers.<\/p>\n

The result of this year\u2019s competition was announced on 13 January. Judging this year was based on creativity, fashion aesthetics and ease of design production. On top of the six categories: Children; Sports; Men\u2019s Shoes; Ladies\u2019 Boots; Ladies\u2019 Sandals; and Ladies\u2019 Shoes, a new category last year, Ladies\u2019 Shoes and Bags, was brought to you. We are sure that visitors will find things new and fresh.<\/p>\n

To learn more about the pavilion, you are most welcome to visit the official website: https:\/\/www.dfmshow.com<\/a><\/p>\n
