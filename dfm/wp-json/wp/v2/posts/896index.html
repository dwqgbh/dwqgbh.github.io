Sewing Technology in Automotive Industry Pavilion<\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

With the rapid growth of sewing technology in automotive industry, a newly launched Sewing Technology in Automotive Industry Pavilion brought the awareness of visitors on the importance of this application by exhibiting the most developed and advanced technology. The pavilion was successfully held with the great support from Pfaff Industrial Hong Kong Co., Ltd., Juki (Hong Kong) Limited and Duerkopp Adler Trading (Shanghai) Co., Ltd.<\/span><\/p>\n

\uff0aFree Shuttle Bus Services\uff0a<\/strong><\/h3>\n

The exhibition hall situated in the middle of the Pearl River Delta, where only an hour should be spent in traveling to and from other important cities of the Pearl River Delta. With the favourable transportation facilities, free shuttle bus service was arranged between the exhibition centre and other important cities, which included the South China Region such as Hong Kong, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhuhai, Zhongshan, Foshan, Huizhou, Junon, Shunde, Panyu, Zencheng and so on, so as to assemble all experts from the textile and clothing industry.<\/span><\/p>\n
