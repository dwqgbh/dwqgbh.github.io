\u201cThe 15th<\/sup> China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Textile & Clothing Industry Fair (DTC 2014)\u201d and \u201cThe 15th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d (DFM 2014), two renowned clothing & footwear industry fairs in Dongguan, will be staged at GD Modern International Exhibition Center on April 1-4, 2014. State-of-the-art clothing & footwear machineries and technologies of the world will be exhibited in the fairs, through which exhibitors and buyers can explore business opportunities.<\/p>\n

The renowned DTC and DFM attract over 350 exhibitors, coming from China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, India, Germany, Korea, Italy, the US, France, Switzerland and more. The two fairs gather elites from the clothing & footwear industries. In response to market change and customers\u2019 requirements, exhibitors display automatic production machineries that save human resources and increase production efficiency.<\/p>\n

Exhibitors include\uff1aSewing Machines: Siruba\u3001Gemsy\u3001Riverstone\u3001Golden Wheel\u3001Cheung Hing\u3001Zoje\u3001Hashima\u3001Kawata\uff1bKnitting: Kyang\u3001Jakob Mueller\uff1bEmbroidery & Laser: Tajima\u3001SWF\u3001Han\u2019s Laser\u3001Autowin\u3001Zhan Yi\u3001Hefeng\u3001CBL\uff1bCAD\/CAM: Lectra\u3001Gerber\u3001Kawakami\u3001Oshima\u3001Yin\u3001Topcut-bullmer\u3001PGM. Exhibits include ironing equipment, material and machinery. It is the best opportunity to purchase production machinery and equipment.<\/p>\n

During the exhibition, related activities including \u201cThe 8th Em~Touching International Embroidery Design Competition\u201d, \u201cThe 8th Marker Layout Competition\u201d and seminars will be held again.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe 15th China (Dongguan) Int’l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d (DFM 2014) will be staged at the same time. Exhibits include footwear machinery, production line, vamp production, packaging machine, leather processing machine, leather, synthetic leather and more. Exhibitors include: Eyko\u3001Sunrise\u3001Luxin\u3001Jia Long\u3001Chin-ei\u3001Youli\u3001Qifeng\u3001Jinyuelai\u3002<\/p>\n

\u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d is the highlight of DFM every year. This year, \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d will showcase the awarded pieces of \u201cThe Hong Kong Footwear Design Competition\u201d organized by The Federation of Hong Kong Footwear \uff081970\uff09. The competition has six categories: Men\u2019s Shoes, Ladies\u2019 Boots, Ladies\u2019 Sandals, Ladies\u2019 Shoes, Children\u2019s Shoes, Ladies\u2019 Shoes and Bags. Over a thousand entries were received. Ms Cheung Siu Fong, student of School of Continuing and Professional Studies, CUHK, is the winner of \u201cLadies\u2019 Sandals\u201d and \u201cLadies\u2019 Shoes and Bags\u201d, as well as the \u201cOverall Winner\u201d. The piece awarded with \u201cBelle Overall Winner\u201d and \u201cLadies\u2019 Shoes and Bags Gold Prize\u201d is \u201cDragon Gate\u201d, which was inspired by \u201cDragon Gate\u201d of \u201cFinca Guell\u201d designed by famous Spanish architect Antoni Gaudi. Ms Cheung uses metal, snake leather and crystal beads to show the beauty of \u201cDragon Gate\u201d. As for the children\u2019s shoes, lego and clown are some of the inspirations, while the ladies\u2019 boots are inspired by Hong Kong housing and men\u2019s shoes are based on the design of warriors\u2019 shoes. These shoes let visitors from around the world to know more about Hong Kong design. These creative designs also promote Hong Kong to be an international design centre.<\/p>\n

In recent years, some of the manufacturers of the Pearl Delta region set up their production facilities in provinces outside Guangdong. The trend brings buyers from Jiangxi, Guangxi, Hunan, Hubei, Jiangxu, Fujian to visit the fairs. Overseas visitors coming from Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Cambodia are also on the rise. In response to this, the organizer offers a two-night free accommodation for buyers from overseas and outside Guangdong, who visit the fairs for the first time.<\/p>\n

Besides, the organizer provides free transportation services for companies and buyer groups. For the convenience of visitors, they can pre-register online. To visit the fairs, they just need to show the confirmation letter and get a badge at the registration counter. Let\u2019s pre-register online.<\/p>\n

About the organizer<\/strong><\/p>\n

Established in 1985, Paper Communication Exhibition Services (Paper Communication) has been engaging in organizing trade exhibitions, publishing industrial magazines, as well as providing printing and design services. Since its establishment, Paper Communication has organized a number of industrial exhibitions. Today, Paper Communication has become one of the leading exhibition organizers in Hong Kong and South China region.<\/p>\n

Paper Communication organizes over ten exhibitions in Hong Kong and Mainland China every year with themes spanning from machinery, plastics & packaging, textile & clothing, footwear & shoe making machines, art & antiques, pet accessories. Exhibitions such as machinery & material, plastics & packaging, textile & clothing are some of the world-renowned events. In addition, Paper Communication and its strategic partners organize Bangladesh International Textile & Garment Industry Exhibition, Cambodia International Textile & Garment Industry Exhibition, Myanmar International Textile & Garment Industry Exhibition and Vietnam International Textile & Garment Industry Exhibition. As an experienced event organizer, Paper Communication has organized various exhibitions, conferences and promotion campaigns, helping exhibitors to explore business opportunities. Paper Communication has built up a comprehensive network in the industry.<\/p>\n
