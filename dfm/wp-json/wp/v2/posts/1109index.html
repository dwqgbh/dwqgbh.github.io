Being a professional exhibition organizer, Paper Communication always strives for providing the most effective and efficient trading platform to exhibitors and customers, as well as bringing you a fruitful purchasing tour.<\/p>\n

As the previous years, the organizer of \u201cThe 9th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d will specially host \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d displaying the pieces of winners by featuring the most splendid designs of Hong Kong designers.<\/p>\n

\u201cChina (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d greatly support \u201cFootwear Design Competition\u201d organized by the Federation of Hong Kong Footwear and the Hong Kong Trade Development Council. The organizer understands the importance of excavating footwear design experts and enhancing the quality of design and creativity.<\/p>\n

The result of this year\u2019s competition was announced. Judging this year was based on creativity, fashion aesthetics and ease of design production. On top of the six categories: Children; Sports; Men\u2019s Shoes; Ladies\u2019 Boots; Ladies\u2019 Sandals; and Ladies\u2019 Shoes, a new category, Ladies\u2019 Shoes and Bags, was brought to you. We are sure that visitors will find things new and fresh.<\/p>\n

To learn more about the pavilion, you are most welcome to visit the official website: www.dfmshow.com<\/a><\/p>\n
