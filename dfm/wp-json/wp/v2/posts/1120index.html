Hosted by Paper Communication Exhibition Services, \u201cThe 10th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d (DFM 2009), scheduled on 4-7 March 2009 at Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Centre, will feature the most marketable technology & materials of the footwear industry. With the support from China Leather & Footwear Industry Research Institute, Hong Kong Synthetic Leather and Metal Material Suppliers Association & The Federation of Hong Kong Footwear, it is believed that this show must be the most remarkable one.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
\n

Global Brands Gathering<\/strong><\/h4>\n

A display of the most outstanding footwear technology encompasses the machinery, components, CAD\/CAM, synthetic leather and so forth. Exhibitors worldwide like Eyko Machinery Ltd., Sunrise Machinery (HK) Co., Ltd., Luxin Shoe Machines and so on will showcase the high standard exhibits and the latest trend and development of the industry to visitors.<\/p>\n

Diversified Events at DFM 2009<\/strong><\/h4>\n

The key feature of the show, \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d, will display the splendid and diversified footwear designs shortlisted from the Hong Kong Footwear Design Competition, revealing the prominent creativity and standard of Hong Kong designers as well as the competitiveness of Hong Kong footwear design industry. The informative technical seminars by professionals will look into the concerned topics in the market.<\/p>\n<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n<\/tbody>\n<\/table>\n
