Hosted by Paper Communication Exhibition Services, “The 10th China (Dongguan) International Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair” (DFM 2009), scheduled on 4-7 March 2009 at Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Centre, featured the most marketable technology & materials of the footwear industry in the four-day show. The support from The Federation of Hong Kong Footwear, China Leather & Footwear Industry Research Institute & Hong Kong Synthetic Leather and Metal Material Suppliers Association made this show must be the most remarkable one.<\/p>\n

254 exhibitors experienced this important business and communication platform this year. This 4-day show attracted 33,000 visitors. From the feedback of visitors, this fair was significant and evaluated as a world-class show. It was also recognized as a must-go annual fair in the purchase tour of textile & clothing equipments.<\/p>\n

A display of the most outstanding footwear technology encompassed footwear machinery & components, synthetic leather machinery, leather chemicals, bag\/luggage machinery, injection machinery, CAD\/CAM and so forth.<\/p>\n

As the previous years, the organizer of \u201cThe 10th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair\u201d specially hosted \u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion\u201d displaying the pieces of winners by featuring the most splendid designs of Hong Kong designers. On top of the six categories: Children; Sports; Men\u2019s Shoes; Ladies\u2019 Boots; Ladies\u2019 Sandals; and Ladies\u2019 Shoes, a new category, Ladies\u2019 Shoes and Bags, was brought to you. Visitors surely found things new and fresh.<\/p>\n

The 11th China (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair (DFM2010) will be on 29 March \u2013 1 April 2010<\/strong>. See you there!<\/p>\n
