\u3000\u3000\u201cChina (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Textile & Clothing Industry Fair 2018 (DTC 2018)\u201d, \u201cSouth China Int\u2019l Sewing Machinery & Accessories Show\u201d and \u201cChina (Dongguan) Int\u2019l Footwear Machinery & Material Industry Fair 2018\u201d (DFM 2018) will be staged at GD Modern International Exhibition Center on March 27 – 30, 2018.\u00a0 State-of-the-art clothing & footwear machineries and technologies of the world will be exhibited in the fairs, through which exhibitors and buyers can explore new business opportunities.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000The renowned DTC and DFM attract over 300 exhibitors<\/strong>, coming from China, Japan, Germany, Italy, US, Switzerland, Hong Kong, Taiwan and many other places. Following last year\u2019s success, Hong Kong Apparel Machinery Association<\/strong> joins hands with Hong Kong Productivity Council again and set up the \u201cHong Kong Energy Saving Apparel Machinery Pavilion<\/strong>\u201d, as a part of the Cleaner Production Partnership Programme. Hong Kong Productivity Council will organize two half-day seminars, promoting energy-saving machineries to the clothing industry.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000Not only a platform to showcase the products, the fairs are also good opportunities for industry insiders to exchange their ideas. Elites and experts will gather at the fairs and participate in a number of events including \u201cChina Sewing Machinery Association Conference 2018 & the 10th<\/sup> Summit of China Sewing Machinery Industry\u201d, \u201cSigning Ceremony of Bangladesh Int\u2019l Sewing Machinery & Accessories Fair 2019\u201d, \u201cInaugural Meeting of New Generation Entrepreneurs of Guangdong Sewing Machinery\u201d and \u201cInauguration Ceremony of Guangdong Shoe Machinery Association\u201d.<\/strong><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000DTC\u2019s exhibitors include<\/span><\/strong>\uff1asewing machines<\/strong><\/span>: Juki, Siruba, Golden Wheel, Strobel, Supreme, Groz-Beckert, Yaho, Juita, Hashima, Baoyu, Maqi; knitting<\/strong><\/span>: Kyang Yhe, Jakob Mueller, Hsiang Chuan, Giu Chun, Tongxiang, HF ENGG, People Trust, Julibao; embroidery & laser<\/strong><\/span>: Tajima, Han\u2019s Yueming Laser, GBOS Laser, Dahao Technology, Autowin, Zhan Yi, Hefeng, CBL, Richpeace, YiBoda, Xinsheng, Jiaxiu, Qichuang, Shenshilei, Zhuji Leye, Shengming, Jingwei, Yuelong, Zhuji Chuangda; CAD\/CAM\/ERP<\/strong><\/span>: Yin Group, Topcut-bullmer, Kawakami, Morgan, Integrated Opimization Software, East System; automation<\/strong><\/span>: Dahao Technology\u3001Powermax. Exhibits also include automation solutions, ironing equipment, material and machinery<\/strong><\/span>. In addition to state-of-the-art machineries and technologies, this year\u2019s fair newly adds \u201cantique sewing machine showcase\u201d.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000DFM\u2019s exhibitors <\/span><\/strong>including Eyko, Mingling, Luxin, JYL, Teseo, Tianfeng, Qifeng, Qiyu, Cheng Feng, Meng Cheng, East Systems, Teng Hong, Taiwei, Feiyue will showcase footwear machinery, production line, vamp production, packaging machine, leather processing machine, leather, synthetic leather and more. In response to the market needs, flyknit knitting machine manufacturers such as Cixing, Lian Xing, Feng Fan, Jia Wei, Dream Star, Rong Rong Fei Hu, Qianlong, Maxima, Taiwan Eagle will exhibit their products. According to the manufacturers, the new technology not only creates shoes uppers, but also capable of producing accessories.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000During the exhibition, a number of activities such as \u201cMarker Layout Competition<\/strong>\u201d, \u201cProduction Scheduling Competition<\/strong>\u201d and seminars will be held. Mr Peter Mangione<\/strong> will give a seminar on \u201cTrump’s China ‘trade policy\u201d. Don\u2019t miss out.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000\u201cHong Kong Footwear Design Pavilion<\/strong>\u201d is the highlight of DFM every year. Awarded pieces of \u201cThe Hong Kong Footwear Design Competition\u201d organized by The Federation of Hong Kong Footwear (1970) are on display. These shoes let visitors from around the world know more about Hong Kong designers\u2019 creativity and potential.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000In recent years, some of the manufacturers of the Pearl Delta region set up their new production facilities in provinces outside Guangdong. The trend brings buyers from Jiangxi, Guangxi, Hunan, Hubei, Jiangxu, Fujian to visit the fairs. Overseas visitors coming from Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Cambodia and India are also on the rise. In response to this, the organizer offers a two-night free accommodation for buyers from overseas and outside Guangdong, who visit the fairs for the first time.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n

\u3000\u3000Dongguan metro line 2 opened in May, 2016. Take metro line 2 and get off at Exhibition Center station (exit B), Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Centre is right there. The convenient transportation largely shortens the travel time for people to get to the exhibition centre, attracting even more buyers from other provinces.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
