Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center
Address：No.1 Zhancheng Rd, Fuhai Street, Baoan District, Shenzhen, China
Tel：0755-32937926
Website：www.shenzhen-world.com/
Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center is located in Shenzhen Airport New Town, which lies at the top of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area and in the center of the Pearl River Delta and Guangdong Free Trade Zone.
Shenzhen World will reach 500,000 m2 of indoor exhibition space once Phase 2 is complete, becoming the largest exhibition venue in the world. Phase 1 covers an area of 1.21 million m2 with a gross floor area of 1.6 million m2, delivering 400,000 m2 of purpose-built and flexible exhibition and event space.
The venue is designed with an organizing spine to connect 19 halls arranged symmetrically along a central corridor. The venue features two main registration lobbies directly connected to the subway, two VIP entrances to the west, plus the grand South Entrance. The two-level central corridor moves visitors efficiently from North to South with the use of people movers and travellators.
Shenzhen World features state-of-the art exhibition complex integrating exhibition, conference, activity (competition, entertainment), catering, shopping, office and service.
The dual-level underground parking lot provides 9,500 parking spaces for private cars, with 300 VIP parking spaces and 1,000 for trucks above ground. The dedicated truck marshaling area also boasts 1,500 parking spaces. Totally it provides over 15K toll parking spaces.

Transportation To Shenzhen World
From Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport
3 km to Shenzhen Airport T4; 7 km to Shenzhen Airport T3, and then take Metro Line to the venue within 30 mins.

From Highway (self-driving)
Served by 2 highways, S3 (Yanjiang Hw), G4 (Guangzhou-Shenzhen Hw) and 1 expressway (Haibin Blvd).
Guangzhou：S3 (Yanjiang Hw)
Dongguan：Beijing-Hong Kong-Macau Hw ↔ Guanglong Hw ↔ S3 (Yanjiang Hw)
Huizhou：Yongguan High Speed ↔ Guanfo Hw
Zhongshan：Guang’ao Hw ↔ Guanfo Hw ↔ S3 (Yanjiang Hw)
Hong Kong：Tuen Mun Hw ↔ S3 (Yanjiang Hw)
Macau：Guang’ao Hw ↔ S3 (Yanjiang Hw)
Remarks：The vehicles other than Shenzhen licensed vehicles need to apply for temporary pass if entering the city proper of Shenzhen in the restricted period.

 

From Shenzhen Metro Station
Metro Line 11 Station:
Futian → Chegongmiao → Hongshuwan South → Houhai → Nanshan → Qianhaiwan → Bao’an → Bihaiwan → Airport → Airport North → Fuyong → Qiaotou → Tangwei → Ma’an Hill → Shajing → Houting → Songgang → Bitou
Fuyong and Tangwei station on line 11 currently in operation is within 6 km away from the venue; shuttle bus will be arranged between Tangwei and Venue to facilitate the transportation. Shuttle bus provided by Shenzhen City,
Taxi from Fuyong station to venue is around 25 mins.
Metro Line 12 Station: (Direct Access to the Venue)
Fuyong → Fuhaixi → Shenzhen World → Shenzhen World North
Metro Line 20 Station: (Direct Access to the Venue)
Airport North → Shenzhen World South → Shenzhen World → Shenzhen World North → Convention & Exhibition City
Metro line 20 will directly serve the venue with two metro stations, Shenzhen World and Shenzhen World North. Line 20 connects Shenzhen World with the Shenzhen Airport with only one metro station in between and offers transfers to Line 11 and the Guangzhou-Dongguan-Shenzhen intercity railway.
Shenzhen Metro Station website: www.szmc.net/szmc_en

 

From Guangzhou-Shenzhen-Hong Kong (high-speed railway)
From Guangzhou
Guangzhou South Station → Shenzhen North Station → Shenzhen Metro: take line 5 to Qian Haiwan → interchange line 11 to Airport North Station and then transfer to Metro Line 20 to Shenzhen World and Shenzhen World North.
From Hong Kong
Hong Kong West Kowloon Station → Shenzhen Futian Station → Shenzhen Metro: take line 11 to Airport North Station and then transfer to Metro Line 20 to Shenzhen World and Shenzhen World North..
From Shenzhen Station (Luohu)
Shenzhen Metro take line 1 to Chegongmiao Station → interchange line 11 to Airport North Station and then transfer to Metro Line 20 to Shenzhen World and Shenzhen World North.
From Dongguan
By Guangzhou-Dongguan-Shenzhen Intercity Railway is recommended.
