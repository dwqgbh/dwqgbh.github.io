2019 Guangdong International Robot and Intelligent Equipment Exposition, 21st China Dongguan International Mould, Metalworking, Plastics & Packaging Exhibition, 3D Printing Show, DMP Hardware Show, hosted by Dongguan Government, organized by东莞市工业和信息化局, Houjie Government, Paper Communication Exhibition Services, co-organized by China Machinery Industry Federation, were held at GD Modern International Exhibition Center, Houjie, Dongguan on May 8-11, 2019.
–
As one of the largest machinery exhibition in South China region, the show used 5 exhibition halls, taking up 45,000sqm with 2,384 booths, featuring high precision machineries and equipment.
–
The exhibition was divided into different zones: sheet metal & laser; metal-cutting tools, toolings & mould parts; mould & metal-working machinery, plastics & packaging; robots, 3D printing; plastic packaging & auxiliary equipment; Korea Pavilion, Taiwan Pavilion, Engineering Plastic Zone.
–
883 exhibitors came from Japan, Korea, Germany, the US, Singapore, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong. Local and international big names of automation and intelligent equipment included GSK, CATO, Harvest Star, Dachuan, Borunte etc. The show attracted 67,000 local and foreign visitors.
Related activities include: The 5th China Korea Robots Forum, Initiation Ceremony of 2019 DiDi Award (Dongguan Cup), Industry 4.0- Application of Nuclear Energy Conference, Annual Meeting of Dongguan Machinery Association, 2019 Big Data and AI seminar.
–
Gathering Well-known Manufacturers From Around the World
Machine Tools Exhibitors: Erowa from the Swiss; Metrol, Mitutoyo from Japan; Beijing Jingdiao and Bystronic DNE。Plastics and Packaging Exhibitors: Star Seiki from Japan, Multitech and Borunte.
–
Exhibitors & buyers came from around the world
Exhibitors & buyers came from: Japan, Korea, Italy, the US, Germany, Swiss, the UK, Austria, Sweden, Singapore, France, Israel, Turkey, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong and more.
–
Number of exhibiting companies and where they came from:
There were totally 883 exhibitors, including China 836 companies (Dongguan, other Guangdong area and other provinces), 23 from Hong Kong SAR, 10 from Taiwan, 2 from Japan, 11 from Korea, 1 from the US.
–
Overseas companies participated through their offices in China:
Japan 7, Germany 3, USA 1, Switzerland 1, Austria 1, Singapore 1, Austria 1, HKSAR 1, Taiwan 1, altogether 17.
–
Featuring Latest Robots and Intelligent Equipment
DMP gathered world-renowned machine manufacturers such as Star Seiki, THK, Korea Association of Robot Industry, Hua Zhong CNC, GSK, showcasing state-of-the-art machines and technologies of the world. The latest robots and intelligent equipment were highlights of the show.
–
Well-known Brands and Manufacturers Visited the Show
22 association, enterprises and institutes with altogether 1048 people visited the show. These groups included Xiamen Die & Mould Industry, Quanzhou Die & Mould Industry Association, Kin Long Hardware Products Co., Ltd., TCL, Guangzhou Panyu Polytechnic, Lianguan Group, Dongguan Maisto Industries Co., Ltd., Wellmei, Dongguan Intelligent Manufacturing Industry Association, Guangdong Die & Mould Industry Association, Minjia, Guangdong Robotics Association, Sinomach Intelligence Technology Co., Ltd., Dongguan Hardware Mechanical & Electric Chamber of Commerce, Guangdong Polytechnic Normal University, overseas buyer groups, Dongguan University of Technology.
Buyers of previous DMP came from: China, Macau, HK, Taiwan, Japan, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, Korea, US, Germany, Russia, Thailand, Mexico, Turkey, UK, Chile, Brazil, Australia, India, Peru, Philippines, Switzerland, Ukraine, Venezuela, Vietnam, Lao, Nigeria, Cote d’Ivoire, Mali, Congo, Ethiopia, Uganda, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, Austria, Spain, Italy, Iran, Saudi Arabia, Tunisia, France, Canada, Belgium and more.
