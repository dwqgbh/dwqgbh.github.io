About DMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo
　　DMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo is the most influential and largest event with widest coverage organized by Paper Communication Services.
　　As an integrated mega industrial event, 2023 DMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo used 12 exhibition halls of Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center with an exhibition area of 240,000 sqm, exhibiting CNC machine tools, robots & automation, machine tools accessories, intelligent manufacturing systems, precision moulding, die-casting, precision measuring, mould manufacturing, new metal materials, showcasing new technologies, new trends and integrated solutions, acting as a platform for manufacturers to explore business opportunities.

Highlights
① Showcasing new technologies & new trends
　　Gathering “big names”, showcasing over 200 world / Asia first launched technologies and intelligent solutions covering green technology, smart manufacturing solution and new materials.
② Germany Pavilion
　　Showcasing the latest and most iconic German integrated solutions.
③ Red pockets Activity
　　“On-site purchase to win million red pockets” was an activity to enhance business matching. Exhibitors who have signed a contract of over RMB 5000, they could enter a “Red Pocket lucky draw” and have a chance to win the most RMB 50,000, the least RMB 500.
④ Ten industrial travel routes
　　During the expo, ten industrial routes were organized, taking over a hundred visitors to visit big names in the industry. This news on XINHUANET attracted nearly a million readers.
