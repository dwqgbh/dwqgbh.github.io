2018 \u201cGuangdong International Robot & Intelligent Manufacturing Expo\u201d (\u201cGIRIE\u201d), \u201c20th China Dongguan International Mould, Metalworking, Plastics and Packaging Exhibition\u201d(\u201cDMP\u201d), \u201cSouth China International Sheet Metal & Laser Show\u201d, \u201cthe 8th International (Dongguan) Metal Casting Show\u201d, \u201c3D Printing Show\u201d, \u201c14th Dongguan International Electro-plating, Surface Finishing & Coating Show\u201d, \u201c2018DMP Hardware & Tools Show\u201d were held at Guangdong Modern International Exhibition Center, Houjie, Dongguan, Guangdong, China on November 27-30, 2018.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
As one of the largest machinery exhibition in South China region, the show used 8 exhibition halls, taking up 124,000sqm with 7,181 booths, featuring high precision machineries and equipment and holds 34 seminars. This year, 3D Printing HK Pavilion, Fitmi\u2019s Industry 4.0 Smart Factory Demonstration, Dongguan Industrial Park Zone, Hardware & Tools Exhibition, were newly added.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
The exhibition was divided into different zones: sheet metal & laser; metal-cutting tools, toolings & mould parts; mould & metal-working machinery, plastics & packaging; robots, 3D printing and Industrial 4.0; plastic packaging & auxiliary equipment; die-casting & automation, electroplating, surface finishing & coating; special pavilions: Fitmi\u2019s Industry 4.0 Smart Factory Demonstration & seminars, 3D Printing HK Pavilion, Dongguan Industrial Park Zone, Korea Pavilion, Taiwan Pavilion, Lubricant & Chemicals Show, Engineering Plastic Zone.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
1,675 exhibitors came from Japan, Korea, Germany, the US, Italy, France, Israel, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong. Local and international big names of automation and intelligent equipment included GSK, CATO, Topstar, Borunte, etc. The show attracted 120,000 local and foreign visitors.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
Gathering Well-known Manufacturers From Around the World<\/strong><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
Machine Tools Exhibitors:<\/strong> HAAS from the US, Emag from Germany, Roeders, Tornos Blum, Exeron; Renishaw from the UK, Erowa from the Swiss; Sodick, Fanuc, Mitsubishi, Mitutoyo, Yamazen from Japan, Beijing Jingdiao, Bystronic DNE, Han\u2019s Laser, Zeiss, Accretech and more.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
Plastics and Packaging Exhibitors:<\/strong> Arburg from Germany; Piovan from Italy; Wittmann Battenfeld from Austria, Fanuc, Star Seiki, Sumitomo, Toyo, JSW, Yushin, Kawata, Matsui, Harmo from Japan; Haiten, Topstar, Multitech, Fomtec and more.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
International (Dongguan) Metal Casting Show:<\/strong> Buhler from the Swiss, Hishinuma from Japan, LK, Glory, Yan Hing, Chit Shun, Kashui, Shun King just to name a few.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
Concurrent Events & Seminars<\/strong><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
During the exhibition, 34 events and seminars were held such as The 2018 Int\u2019l Advanced Manufacturing Industry (China Guangdong) Summit, The 9th International Conference on Mould Materials and Parts Application, The 3rd Dongguan International Cutting Tool Festival, 3D Printing Mould & Precision Casting Forum.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n

There were totally 1,675 exhibitors<\/strong>, including China 1535 companies (Dongguan, other Guangdong area and other provinces), 78 from Hong Kong SAR, 40 from Taiwan, 13 from Korea, 6 from Japan, 1 from Italy, 1 from France and 1 from Israel.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n

Featuring Latest Robots and Intelligent Equipment<\/strong><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
DMP gathered world-renowned machine manufacturers such as Wittmann Battenfeld from Austria, Hardinge from the US, Fanuc, Sodick, JSW, Star Seiki, Yushin, Mitsubishi & THK from Japan, Cato, Alfa, GSK, Topstar, showcasing state-of-the-art machines and technologies of the world. The latest robots and intelligent equipment were highlights of the show.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
