 <\/p>\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
\u3000\u3000In order to promote advanced manufacturing technology and industrial development of Guangzhou-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area, Paper Communication Exhibition Services organized the \u201cDMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo\u201d at Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center (Baoan) on November 24-27, 2020. The expo showcased high-end machineries, automation, smart factory solutions, robots, 3D printing, industrial internet, auto manufacturing & parts, mask & protective equipment, nuclear applications, new materials, metalworking and plastic machines.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000Exhibitors came from Japan, Korea, Italy, Germany, Singapore, France, Switzerland, Austria, Sweden, US, China, China Hong Kong, China Taiwan. The expo had an exhibition area of 192,000 sqm, gathering more than 1,460 exhibitors and covering high-end manufacturing enterprises. The expo attracted over 150,000 visitors from more than 10 countries and regions.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
\u3000\u3000The Greater Bay Area is not only a region covering the world’s high-end manufacturing and manufacturing of intelligent equipment, but also a trendsetter of the world’s emerging industries and advanced manufacturing, attracting the world’s attention. “DMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo” strives to create an international platform for intelligent manufacturing and technology exchange, together with exhibitors to explore the Greater Bay Area. For more information, please visit www.dmpsz.com ; dmpshow.com or WeChat dmpshow.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n<\/table>\n\n <\/p>\n
