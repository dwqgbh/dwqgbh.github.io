 <\/p>\n\n\n\n\n\n
In order to promote advanced manufacturing technology and industrial development of Guangzhou-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area, Paper Communication Exhibition Services organized the \u201cDMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo\u201d at Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center on November 26-29, 2019. The expo showcased high-end machineries, automation, smart factory solutions, robots, 3D printing, nuclear applications, metalworking and plastic machines. Exhibitors came from Japan, Korea, Italy, Germany, Singapore, France, Malaysia, Switzerland, UK, Austria, Sweden, Holland, US, Australia, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan. The large scale expo had an exhibition area of 200,000 sqm, gathering more than 1,848 exhibitors and covering high-end manufacturing enterprises. The expo attracted over 132,790 visitors from more than 100 countries and regions.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
–<\/span><\/td>\n<\/tr>\n
The Greater Bay Area is not only a region covering the world’s high-end manufacturing and manufacturing of intelligent equipment, but also a trendsetter of the world’s emerging industries and advanced manufacturing, attracting the world’s attention. “DMP Greater Bay Area Industrial Expo” strives to create an international platform for intelligent manufacturing and technology exchange, together with exhibitors to explore the Greater Bay Area. For more information, please visit www.greaterbayexpo.com or WeChat dmpshow.<\/td>\n<\/tr>\n<\/table>\n\n <\/p>\n
