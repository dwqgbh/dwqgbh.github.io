]]> Art Hong Kong Expo 2025 https://www.paper-com.com.hk/en/blog/arthk2025/ Wed, 17 Jul 2024 10:30:42 +0000 https://www.paper-com.com.hk/?p=9284 　　The organizing committee of Art Hong Kong Expo & Paper Communication Exhibition Services will hold the 3rd Art Hong Kong Expo from 30 May to 2 June 2025 at AsiaWorld-Expo in Hong Kong. The Expo will include the 4th Belt & Road National Art & Culture Expo & the 7th Hong Kong Int’l Art & Collectibles Fair, showcasing Chinese paintings and calligraphy, bronzes, ceramics, antiques, gold and silverware, jades, lacquerware, jewellery, sculpture, modern art, photography, embroidery and installation art. A series of seminars, performances, competitions and other exciting activities will be held concurrently.

　　The 3rd Art Hong Kong Expo has successfully attracted the participation of companies and artists from many countries and regions around the world. Until now, the confirmed exhibitors and artists come from countries and regions including China, Pakistan, the Philippines, Tanzania, Vietnam, Senegal, Iran, Benin, etc. This reflects the outstanding international influence of Art Hong Kong Expo.
