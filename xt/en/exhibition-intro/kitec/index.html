Kowloonbay International Trade & Exhibition Centre (KITEC)

Kowloonbay International Trade & Exhibition Centre (KITEC)
Address：1 Trademart Drive, Kowloon Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong
Tel：852-26202222
Website：www.kitec.com.hk

 

Take MTR & Free shuttle bus to KITEC
Get off at Kowloon Bay Station(MTR). Then take free shuttle bus (near by Hang Seng Tower) to KITEC, fast and convenient!
● From Shenzhen：Take MTR – Lo Wu Station / Lok Ma Chau Station → Kowloon Tong Station → Kowloon Bay Station (Exit A). Then take free shuttle bus (near by Hang Seng Tower) to KITEC.
► Lo Wu Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station Approx every 53 mins. Lok Ma Chau Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station Approx every 57 mins.
► Adult Octopus HKD 42.5 / Child and Elderly Octopus HKD 21.6. Adult Single Journey Ticket HKD 46.5 / Child and Elderly Single Journey Ticket HKD 23。
● From Hong Kong International Airport：Take MTR – Airport Station → Tsing Yi Station → Lai King Station → Prince Edward Station → Kowloon Bay Station (Exit A). Then take free shuttle bus (near by Hang Seng Tower) to KITEC.
► Airport Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station Approx every 52 mins.
► Adult and Elderly Octopus HKD 65 / Child Octopus HKD 32.5
► Adult Single Journey Ticket：Airport Station ←→ Tsing Yi Station about HKD 70. Tsing Yi Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station about HKD 11.5. Total Expense HKD 81.5.
► Child Single Journey Ticket：Airport Station ←→ Tsing Yi Station about HKD 35. Tsing Yi Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station about HKD 5. Total Expense HKD 40.
► Elderly Single Journey Ticket：Airport Station ←→ Tsing Yi Station about HKD 70. Tsing Yi Station ←→ Kowloon Bay Station about HKD 5. Total Expense HKD 75.
